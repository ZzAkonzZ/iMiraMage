Chapter 479:

Plu, with her injured body, stood in front of Shirone.

Mirka, who ranked second among the 72 ranks of fairies, exuded a different kind of aesthetic power compared to angels.

While the beauty of angels was grand and reverent, Mirka's beauty came from a charming, fairy-tale-like quality.

Of course, such facts were of no importance to Plu.

She was barely clothed, and the pain from her burns was clawing at her brain through her nerves in real-time, but the mental strength of a professional mage unerringly created a Spirit Zone.

"Tch, I knew it. I had a bad feeling."

Plu's gaze turned to Feope, who was standing beside Mirka.

Was she feeling guilty?

Feope kept her head bowed, which only made Plu angrier.

The revered fairy Mirka approached with a stern face.

"A lowly human group disturbing heaven and tormenting countless subjects. Even now, receive the aura and seek forgiveness. Then leave this world cleanly."

"Feope."

Ignoring Mirka, Shirone called out Feope's name, causing her to flinch and slowly raise her head.

Her large eyes were filled with deep fear.

Seeing the fear of the harm Shirone might suffer, Shirone said nothing more.

It wasn't that she didn't suspect Feope. She had simply erased such doubts from her mind.

Because she…

'She was willing to die for me.'

The scene of Feope willingly sacrificing her life for Shirone in Jebul still lingered in Shirone's mind.

'No, that's not it.'

Shirone suddenly realized.

Perhaps, deep down, she had wanted this.

A battle with heaven was inevitable, and in the end, the two of them would become enemies.

And with this, the feelings they had for each other were withdrawn.

"Feope."

Feope still didn't respond.

She was just waiting for Shirone's words with fearful emotions or perhaps a glimmer of hope.

As the power amplification function of the Vajra Armor activated, Shirone's robe fluttered fiercely.

"I will kill you."

As if to firmly root her true feelings, Shirone's words sent a cold murderous intent into the fairies' minds.

Feope also felt a chill from Shirone's killing intent.

But her face soon turned cold as well, and thus their hearts closed off from each other.

"Do it if you can. I'm not the same as before."

Feope had indeed become stronger.

When Shirone first came to heaven, Feope was the youngest among the fairies, but now she held a mid-level position among the 72 ranks.

The reason for her rapid rise in status was largely due to her relationship with Shirone.

As the rebellion that started in Shamain accelerated, the Ministry of Internal Affairs valued Feope, and she rapidly improved her skills by learning from fairies with high mental stages.

Thus, she couldn't turn her back on the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

When Shirone returned to heaven, Feope's heart raced more than anyone else's, but in the end, she chose the fairies of heaven who had accepted her.

"Arrest Shirone."

As Mirka extended her hand, dozens of fairies flew in from behind.

Shirone and Plu stepped back, assessing the situation around them.

The best plan was to get to Araboth as quickly as possible, but the fairies surrounding them were not to be underestimated.

Even without exchanging glances, Plu's energy was conveyed.

They needed a timing and a trigger to escape this place.

"Wait a moment!"

At that moment, Deina, who had lost her sanity, knelt before Mirka.

"I am the great immortal. Please spare me! They tried to kill me!"

Mirka looked at Deina with disdain.

The reason Anke La was wary of humans was because they had the ability to actively seek death.

When humans began to actively seek death, they could exhibit something beyond human.

But Deina had lost humanity's greatest strength after gaining immortality.

Of course, not all immortals were as weak as her.

In fact, members of the Ten Thousand-Year Council, who were over ten thousand years old, were known to have transcended even the cycle of life and death.

"Foolish creature. What greatness could a being like you possess? For the crime of defiling Ra's grace, you too shall be executed."

Execution.

The word pierced Deina's mind, a word she had never considered in a thousand years, and her avatar screamed again.

The fear of death, which she hadn't felt in a thousand years, completely shattered her already half-mad mind.

"Nooooo!"

Deina mustered all her strength to draw upon her magic.

The magic of a mad person resonated with her mind, soaring endlessly, but without reason, it lacked purpose.

Even so, a powerful atmospheric flow enveloped the surroundings.

"No! You die!"

As Deina extended her hands to cast ancient magic, her neck twisted with a crack.

Feope, the spiral fairy, used her powerful spiral force to quickly rotate Deina's neck, completely tearing it from her body.

As Deina's shocked face soared into the air, Shirone and Plu simultaneously realized.

'Now!'

Their bodies turned into light and shot into the air as Mirka shouted.

"Chase them! They must be caught in Shehakim!"

Araboth was the most sacred area of heaven where Anke La resided.

For humans to infiltrate from the fairies' perspective was an unprecedented disgrace in the history of the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

Even though it happened suddenly, the fairies moved without hesitation.

Dozens of fairies chased after Shirone, and at the same time, fairies flew up from all directions in Shehakim like flocks of birds.

This confirmed that the core personnel of the Ministry of Internal Affairs were watching Shehakim.

"Feope…"

There was no doubt that Feope was a key figure in this incident.

Their identities had already been exposed, and with all the fairies chasing them, the only option left was to infiltrate Araboth by the shortest route.

"Shirone, go ahead!"

Plu, injured, couldn't keep up with Shirone's flying speed.

Thinking that at least one person had to escape, she stopped flying and turned around.

Numerous fairies were charging at her.

Activating the Dawkins Algorithm, she dodged the fairies' magic and ignited flames.

Shirone, flying vertically, spotted Plu struggling in the distance and deployed Photon Cannons in all directions.

But even he was being swarmed by at least a hundred fairies.

Though not as advanced as angelic abilities, the fairies' magic, imbued with their unique essence, was sufficiently threatening.

Even with Armand's body enhancement abilities, it was impossible to dodge all the attacks, and as Ringer's Skin activated, his robe transformed into a heavy, ultra-strong metal.

"Hmph!"

Unable to bear the weight, Shirone spread his wings of light through his artificial brain.

As the wings of light whipped the air with a bang, his body shot forward dozens of meters in an instant.

The sky flashed with rainbow-colored magic, and the fairies, gathering and dispersing repeatedly, meticulously drove Shirone into one spot.

Not knowing how far he had flown or where he was, Shirone desperately defended against the fairies' attacks.

At that moment, there was a bang from where Plu was, and her body was seen falling to the ground.

"Senior!"

As Shirone tried to rush out, the sleeve of his right arm twisted rapidly, almost breaking his arm.

"Ugh!"

As the robe tightened to the point of tearing, Ringer's Skin activated, transforming it into ultra-strong metal.

Only after the rotation stopped did Shirone look ahead.

Feope, with fierce eyes, was using her power against him.

"Ugh!"

Feope poured all her strength into the spiral force.

Her magic had indeed become incredibly powerful, but even so, Ringer's Skin didn't budge.

'Senior Plu.'

Plu had already disappeared from the scene.

All he could do now was hope she had survived.

"Feope!"

Gritting his teeth, Shirone extended Armand's tentacles.

Two tentacles appeared and were immediately caught in the spiral force and exploded, but Kensei's absolute restoration ability quickly created new tentacles.

As the whip-like afterimages flew in, Feope, unable to withstand it, retreated.

At the same time, the fairies who had brought down Plu all charged at Shirone.

Plu's enemy.

Flames rose in Shirone's chest, but the cold rationality of a mage quickly extinguished them.

But that didn't mean he had forgotten his anger.

"Capture him! It's okay to kill him!"

As the fairies surrounded Shirone and cast their magic, Shirone's eyes flashed open.

'Scatter Movement!'

Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang!

A massive flash of light spread in eight directions.

As one fairy failed to dodge and fell, the flash of teleportation sharply turned and rushed there.

The scatter spread countless times, and in an instant, the sky became a web of Photon Cannon flashes.

The power of the Photon Cannons, amplified by Galtomic's magic, was on a different level, and most of the fairies were busy dodging.

Feope stared blankly at the sight of numerous comrades falling.

The power was on a different level from when they had been tormented by the fairies while blocking the Elixir of Life in Zone 73.

Especially since the fairies present were all powerful spirits positioned in the middle ranks of the 72 ranks, unlike back then.

"Follow him! Don't stop!"

Even as their comrades were being blown apart, the fairies continued to fly in endlessly.

Shirone, with his wings of light fully spread, engaged in an aerial battle with the fairies using dazzling movements.

"Damn it…"

The fairies, belatedly noticing the red dots on their foreheads, gritted their teeth.

As the homing photon cannons equipped with seekers fired, the fairies surrounding Shirone scattered.

However, the homing photon cannons relentlessly pursued the fairies, and in the distant sky, the sound of explosions and dying screams echoed.

'Even with this many fairies, they can't stop me?'

According to the Ministry of Internal Affairs' report, Shirone's combat power was such that a unit of 65th-rank fairies could handle him when he blocked the Elixir of Life. But now, even dozens of 40th-rank fairies were at risk of being annihilated.

'I'm going to Araboth!'

Shirone flapped his wings of light to their maximum and increased his speed.

The weaker-willed fairies gradually fell behind, and finally, none could keep up with him.

'Pathetic creatures.'

Just as Shirone thought he had won the speed battle, a fairy's voice echoed in his mind.

Mirka, the second-ranked fairy, flew in with a blue flash and blocked his path.

Shirone deployed 16 homing photon cannons and fired them all at her.

As the flashes curved and flew towards her, she extended her hand.

Unregulated Form - Razor of Truth.

Thin, thread-like blades sparkled around Mirka at incredible speed, intercepting all the homing photon cannons.

Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang!

Shirone halted abruptly, raising his wings.

'What is this…?'

Mirka, seemingly unaffected by the impact, still had her hand extended and her wings fluttering.

'She didn't block them. It's some kind of ability.'

A fairy's durability is never strong.

Even a second-ranked fairy couldn't stand unscathed after being hit by sixteen photon cannons enhanced by magic amplification.

"This makes it clear."

Mirka, looking sternly at the confused Shirone, spoke solemnly.

"There is no variable by which you can defeat me."
