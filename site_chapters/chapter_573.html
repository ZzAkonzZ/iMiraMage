Chapter 573:

As steam-like mist rose from Frings' body, Dorothy canceled her victory pose and assumed a defensive stance.

'Hickory, defense mode 100%.'

Feeling an unnatural, twisted hostility, Dorothy instinctively activated her algorithm.

"Dorothy, you're a strange girl."

The moment she saw Frings' eyes as he stood up, Dorothy's soul screamed in terror.

It was an unprecedented coldness.

"You seem like you have no thoughts, but you're actually thinking a lot. Tell me, how scared are you right now?"

Dorothy bit her lip until it bled.

"You're actually terrified, aren't you? Afraid that I'll kill you. Well, you're always scared."

"I'm… not thinking anything."

"Really? Did you think I'd let it go if you said you weren't thinking anything?"

Frings floated and spread his hands.

'Ice Demon.'

Dozens of hideous ice pillars shot out from his back like spider legs.

'I'm going to die. I'm going to die.'

Dorothy's body trembled.

"Your expressionless face is a mask to hide the scared child inside. Is an adult's body that fascinating?"

As the president of the Hide-and-Seek Research Group, Frings had seen through Dorothy's life.

"Tell me, what are you imagining? What expression are you making?"

Dorothy's lips quivered.

"If you won't tell me…"

Frings' mouth twisted grotesquely.

"I'll pull the child inside you out."

Dorothy's mind went blank, and her body shivered as if struck by lightning.

-Mimesis algorithm activated. Emergency escape.

As the shock hit the magician's brain, Hickory switched to automatic mode, grabbed Dorothy, and fled into the forest.

"…"

Crash!

The spikes of the Ice Demon shattered and fell to the ground.

"Heh heh."

Covering his face with his hands, Frings slowly reined in his killing intent.

"Too bad. I wanted to see you scream."

Returning to his sly expression, he grabbed the Scramble and called out "White," completing a Triumvirate (○●○).

'At least I have a decent hand now.'

Checking his Master Card, he pushed through the bushes, muttering,

"Now, who should I destroy next?"

"Huff, huff!"

Deep in the forest, Dorothy leaned against a tree trunk, panting heavily.

Hickory, now out of the mimesis algorithm, looked at her with a smiling face, but it no longer felt comforting.

"Ugh!"

Completely overwhelmed by Frings' chilling presence, she hadn't even thought of stealing the Scramble and had just fled.

"What do I do…"

Dorothy collapsed weakly, tears streaming down her face.

"I've been found out."

She had been exposed.

The child hiding in an adult's body.

Hershey checked his Master Card and looked at Shirone's current hand, ○●○●ⓡ.

'Strange.'

It was a question that had lingered since he lost to Shirone in a Kang the previous day.

He had accepted Shirone's Kang while holding ●●●○ (Dictator) because all of Shirone's cards were ⓡⓡⓡⓡ.

Dictator was the second strongest hand in the fourth stage, so there was no reason to call Pao.

But the result was ○●○● (Democracy).

Of course, opening four Random Cards to get an equal ratio of White and Black was statistically correct.

'No, probability doesn't work like that.'

It felt too probabilistic, making it seem improbable.

'And what Kaiden said yesterday bothers me.'

Shirone had also chosen Random for his fifth card.

'It feels like he's not afraid of a dead hand. If he builds up wrong, he'll have to destroy his own hand in the sixth stage.'

Both sides had already experimented with calling Kangs on each other to destroy hands.

'Does he think hiding one card is more important than destroying a hand, even with Democracy revealed?'

Having finished his thoughts, Hershey sent out a gathering signal.

His current hand was ●● (Heretic). It was a hand that could be destroyed for experimentation without regret.

'Tomorrow, I'll eliminate Shirone from the game.'

Scramble Royal, Day 3.

At the Scramble summoning location, Shirone encountered Kaiden again.

'Of all times…'

Kaiden's hand was ○○○ (Religion).

He had chosen one White (○) and opened two Random (ⓡⓡ) cards to form the hand.

'It's a difficult hand to develop.'

So, it wasn't a situation Shirone was entirely happy about.

If Kaiden called a Kang, Shirone's hand, ●○●○● (Revolution), would be revealed, raising suspicion.

"This is enough."

Kaiden stood his Cross Sword vertically and said,

"Today, I'll kill you."

Shirone felt somewhat relieved by Kaiden's attitude, showing no intention of calling a Kang.

'In a way, it's impressive.'

Though their dispositions were different, Kaiden's stubbornness reminded him of Rian.

"Fate of the Red Cross."

Kaiden placed his palm on the sword and muttered,

"Guide my talent."

As the blade glowed red-hot, Shirone's eyes widened.

'That's…'

"Mana absorption."

As the flames on the sword blazed, Kaiden charged.

'A magic swordsman!'

A title only someone like Kaiden, who was in the top class of the graduation class, could earn.

"I don't know what secrets you're hiding…"

The Cross Sword slashed horizontally.

"I'll bury them with your death."

A massive cloud of flames surged like a tidal wave, engulfing the area where Shirone stood.

"Ugh!"

It was a technique of a magic swordsman, never before shown at school.

"Burn! Burn!"

Each swing of Kaiden's sword sent a 6-meter-high cloud of flames spinning and advancing.

The radius was too wide to dodge without teleportation, and a bolt of lightning struck above Shirone's head as he retreated.

'Lightning magic?'

The Cross Sword, charged with lightning, struck the ground vertically.

Magic Sword Technique - Lightning Impact.

Blue lightning spread across the ground like a spider web, causing Shirone's body to convulse.

'Time Reversal!'

Simultaneously, his sequence of exercises entered the realm of awe.

One second earlier.

Magic Sword Technique - Lightning Impact.

As the lightning spread, Shirone leaped into the air, and Kaiden gritted his teeth.

'I can't understand this.'

That feeling again.

No matter how skilled a mage, Schema should dominate in the realm of physical and neural techniques.

And the battle had been progressing accordingly.

'What's going on!'

No matter how much he thought, he couldn't find a reason because nothing was wrong.

"In that case…!"

Kaiden's face twisted like a wolf's as he prepared his ultimate attack.

"Mana enhancement!"

The Cross Sword vibrated, emitting powerful high-frequency waves.

It was a type of sound magic from the Kaiser series.

'He can use all kinds of magic?'

He was a different kind of genius from Fermi.

Kaiden swung his sword at Shirone, who wore a bewildered expression.

"Can you dodge sound?"

Boom!

The shockwave devastated the surroundings, but Shirone emerged unscathed, breaking through the shockwave.

'How?'

It wasn't space.

He had dodged through time.

'I have to do it.'

As the incarnation of the Light Angel merged with Shirone, Kaiden finally understood the nature of his intuition.

'That's it. It's not luck. It's definitely a technique.'

As the Photon Cannon compressed in Shirone's hand, golden afterimages spread like a fan from his body.

For the first time, Kaiden, who had been relentlessly attacking, took a step back in front of Shirone.

As Shirone closed the distance with teleportation and swung the hand holding the Photon Cannon, Kaiden rotated the Cross Sword 180 degrees to defend his side.

"Huh?"

To his surprise, Shirone was approaching from the opposite side.

"Damn it!"

Kaiden, pushing his Schema abilities to the limit, defended at terrifying speed, but Shirone twisted his direction again.

It wasn't magic altering the trajectory but a natural movement as if predetermined, striking Kaiden's mind with a startling insight.

'Could it be… time?'

As soon as he thought it, Kaiden's arm moved at a frightening speed.

"Ugh!"

Block, block, block again.

In a split second, Kaiden changed his defensive stance seven times, a speed no mage could mimic, but Shirone's Photon Cannon steadily closed in on the undefendable spot.

'This feels like…'

At the end of his thoughts, Kaiden was stunned.

'Infinity.'

It felt like an infinite number of Shirones were closing in.

Crash!

The Photon Cannon struck his side, and he heard his ribs crack.

"Ugh!"

The impact sent Kaiden flying ten meters into a wall.

"Ugh!"

With broken bones, even Schema's regenerative abilities couldn't make him combat-ready immediately.

'I have to take his Master Card!'

Shirone rushed towards Kaiden.

Kaiden, struggling to stand, collapsed to one knee as his legs gave out.

'Knock him out.'

As Shirone grasped the Photon Cannon, Kaiden shouted with a twisted face.

"Shirone, Kang!"

Ruru's voice came through as an electric signal.

"Kaiden has called a Kang."

If he didn't choose within one second, his cards would be destroyed.

"Kang!"

Kaiden's cards were destroyed, and Shirone's hand, ●○●○● (Revolution), was revealed.

In the brief moment when Shirone had to respond to Kaiden's Kang, Hershey's team seized the opportunity for a counterattack.

"Now!"

As Hershey shouted and charged, Anchal, Eden, and Pisho appeared from all directions.

'Eden?'

Shirone, prioritizing stopping Eden from collecting cards, quickly turned towards the Scramble.

As he grabbed the Scramble, Hershey shouted,

"Stop him before he opens it!"

Anchal called a Kang.

Her hand was ○○●○○ (Emperor).

It was a higher hand than Revolution, and she left one unopened ⓡ card, which was destroyed.

-Unopened Random Cards cannot influence or be influenced by the game.

This fact had already been experimentally verified, so without needing to check the Master Card, Hershey, Eden, and Pisho shouted simultaneously,

"Shirone! Kang!"

If everyone called a Kang, Shirone couldn't harm anyone.

'It's over! You're out!'

It was the so-called "Kill Shirone" strategy.

"You have lost the Kang. You cannot harm Shirone until the next Scramble summoning."

"What?"

Hershey, thinking there must be something wrong with his hearing, checked his Master Card.

'Annihilation (●○●●○●).'

"Wait a minute. Why is this…?"

Kaiden, clutching his side, said,

"He controls time. That's his ability."

It felt as if reality had been erased and a new reality had been born.

While you couldn't feel the reset in the realm of thought, the physical evidence of the cards proved it was true.

'Time? Time, you say?'

It was the first time Hershey had such a dumbfounded expression, but he quickly regained his composure and shouted,

"Retreat! Fall back!"

As everyone fled, Shirone targeted Kaiden.

Though injured, it was also a moment to release the pent-up frustration he had been harboring.

At that moment, Eden rushed in and blocked Shirone's path to the still-immobile Kaiden.

'Saint Barrier!'

As the Spirit Zone formed a radiant barrier, Shirone, recalling Iruki's explanation of Eden's abilities, frowned.

'I need more power.'

Gritting his teeth, he activated Immortal Function, and the Photon Cannon shook as if dancing with madness.

Eden, feeling a chill, bit her lip and prepared to withstand the attack.

'Photon Cannon!'

A nearly 1-meter-wide flash struck the Saint Barrier.

The sphere, which hadn't budged against explosive magic, dented like cotton and then burst out the other side with a bang.

"Ahhhhh!"

As Eden, her omnipotence shattered, clutched her head and curled up on the ground, everyone who had been fleeing turned in shock.

"Eden's…"

Her defensive magic had been breached.

It was a sight no one in the graduation class had ever seen.
