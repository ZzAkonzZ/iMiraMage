Chapter 822: Smile, Smile (2)

Royal Cemetery

The resting place of the silent was now filled with countless screams.

Lights were turning on one by one in the houses, but no one dared to come outside.

"Charge! Charge!"

Xenia urged them to keep moving forward.

'It's not far.'

The intense aura was pulling the instincts of the Silver Born, making her hair stand on end.

"Argh! You bastards!"

Every time Fowler swung the Mega Gun around, the sound of flesh tearing came from the cloud-like smoke.

"Die, human!"

Even though Vesica was the lowest rank among purebloods, their power was on a different level compared to hybrids.

"Power pack!"

As the effects wore off, Katain quickly prepared the drug and injected it into the hunters' necks.

Their heads spun, and soon the world seemed slower than before.

"Let's go!"

With the pain halved, the hunters wielding metal weapons swung their weapons and cleared a path.

After a bloody battle, they reached the graves of the greats, where the bodies of the drained guards lay.

'Suction.'

Simply sucking blood doesn't turn one into a vampire.

"More blood is needed."

An old man with an umbrella stood on a tombstone.

"You will suffice."

"Do you think you'll get away with this? You've killed the caretakers of the royal cemetery."

No matter how strong vampires are, humans are the species that rule the world with the power of the sun.

Such reckless actions were unprecedented, making Xenia uneasy.

"Do you know the misery of a people who have lost their king?"

"What are you talking about?"

"Hiding in the shadows, the humiliation of having the world stolen by weaklings like you ends today."

Xenia's hair stood on end.

'What, what is this?'

An immense aura was emanating from one of the tombstones behind the Umbrella Man.

"A Lord?"

The drained bodies, the blood bank, it all made sense now.

The Umbrella Man smirked.

"Today, our king will be resurrected."

"Run!"

Seeing the tomb shake, Xenia shouted, but it was already too late.

"Argh!" As Fowler fired the Mega Gun, the Umbrella Man quickly soared into the sky.

"What, what is that?"

Like gas erupting, black smoke shot up tens of meters from the cracks in the tomb.

"That makes two."

Two of the three Lords were being resurrected.

"Ha ha ha! Ha ha ha ha!"

A woman's laughter, so piercing it felt like it would tear eardrums, echoed as the smoke gathered.

As it fell to the ground like a meteor, the earth rippled, and finally, a beautiful naked woman stood there. The Umbrella Man landed beside her and knelt.

"Greetings to Agnes, the ruler of blood."

Only Lords could enter a state of suspended animation by draining all moisture from their bodies.

To awaken from this state, the blood of 300 humans was needed.

"A Lord..."

It was a powerful aura that neither Nike nor Rian could sense.

It was simply the law of nature.

The identity of the Lord, inherited from Jinma, was at the pinnacle of the biological pyramid from birth.

"Damn it..."

Fowler's eyes bulged with blood vessels, and veins stood out on Katain's temples.

They were like rabbits facing a lion, but these rabbits had lost their fear to anger.

"I'll kill you!"

Their hearts, which couldn't be comforted by anything, finally found a breakthrough.

As the hunters charged head-on, Agnes extended her white fingers.

"Blood Control."

The remaining blood from her awakening rose and formed a massive sphere above her head.

The blood, cooled by pressure, became a sword in her hand.

"It's been two thousand years."

As she leaped towards the hunters, her body passed them and landed far away.

"Ugh!"

The blood sword evaporated at an incredible speed, leaving wounds on the hunters' bodies.

"Blood Control."

As she crossed her hands, blood spurted from their bodies like fountains.

"Faster! Move faster!"

Hearing the series of explosions in the city, Benoff led the special vampire task force in a carriage.

"Damn it..."

When they arrived, it looked like a typhoon had passed, with all the buildings collapsed.

Boom!

Hearing a distant explosion, Benoff turned his head.

"What, what is that..."

Two long shadows clashed freely between the sky and the ground.

"Ugh!"

Rian's body, torn to shreds, pushed through Nike's whip.

In the high-speed battle where cover was irrelevant, Rian swung his Mind Strike.

'Must cut!'

As he struck with the conviction of his limits, Nike's shoulder was sharply severed.

'Incarnation doesn't work.'

As long as couldn't be destroyed, the semi-spiritual body was just material.

'But it doesn't die.'

With rapid regeneration, Nike's body quickly recovered, and he charged at Rian head-on.

"Yaaah!"

In the place where flesh was cut, torn, and blood rained down.

'How far can I go?'

Rian wondered how much a living being could be destroyed.

At that moment, his body was torn apart.

"Yaaah!"

Divine Transcendence.

Even in that moment, moved, and Nike's body was shattered into dozens of pieces.

It was a fleeting moment.

Benoff heard a bang.

From hundreds of meters away, he saw the two shadows collide and explode like fireworks.

"Captain, is that..."

Can a person explode like that?

"What are you doing! Move out!"

Even Benoff was stunned, but he needed someone to vent his emotions on.

"Rian..."

It was probably Rian.

"Haaah."

Rian and Nike's bodies, torn to pieces, were tangled together.

With his nose cut off, Rian turned his gaze to Nike.

In the sky, , having lost its target, still danced.

With only his head remaining, Nike looked up at the sky.

"You won." Nike, once a heretic hunter, had questioned the essence of life long ago.

"You went further."

Nike's body gathered into black smoke, and a bat struggled to move.

"You..."

Rian's severed hand grabbed Nike's face.

Even under the pressure of his eyes, Nike smiled bitterly and spoke.

"Since we abandoned God, there is no mercy for us."

Only endless pain remained.

"Only those who reach the end can kill God."

-Smile, Smil.

As the annoying hallucination echoed again, the bat sank its fangs into Rian's neck.

"Ugh!"

As the prion from the injection spread, Rian's half-recovered body convulsed wildly.

"...Advance."

As the bat disintegrated, Nike's body turned to black ash and scattered on the ground.

"Rian! Rian!"

Arriving at the scene, Benoff stopped ten meters away.

"R-Rian?"

Rian, who had risen unsteadily, regenerated his last remaining right hand and turned his face.

With a face devoid of humanity, his teeth clenched, he looked like a beast.

"Get a grip!"

"Ugh!"

As inexplicable pain consumed his body, Nike's memories flooded in.

"Damn it! Everyone, stay back!"

Fearing he might turn into a vampire, Benoff drew his sword and watched the situation.

-Me. Beyond the physical me.

The hallucination of Smile echoed.

-How far can you go?

"Haaah..."

Steam from burning cells escaped through Rian’s clenched teeth.

-To the end. Only when every smallest piece of me is destroyed... Burn, regenerate, and burn again.

In the process of expelling Nike's prion, Rian surpassed the limits of biological collapse.

-That is the true me. Ogent.

"Argh!"

As Rian spread his upper body and roared, a powerful gust blew the soldiers away.

"Damn it! What is this!"

Divine Transcendence - Idea.

-Smile!

As every cell twisted into the form of a Yacha, ancient memories flooded Rian’s mind.

"Smile."

As those memories mixed with Nike's remaining thoughts, Rian’s head snapped.

"I must save Smile."

As he kicked off the ground and charged, the buildings in front of him collapsed.

"What is happening?"

Benoff, who had been blown away by the gust, rubbed his head and looked at where Rian had disappeared.

The mansion of the tycoon Miguel.

One of the most successful nobles in Rodenin, Miguel was a Vesica vampire.

"Welcome, my Lord."

Agnes, accompanied by the Umbrella Man, arrived at the mansion and dropped the pale hunters to the ground.

"Is Kanoa's resurrection complete?"

"The blood is about two-thirds full, but it's still insufficient. He will awaken from his slumber around midnight tomorrow."

"You, you bastard..."

The only conscious one, Xenia, raised her head, but she was too weak from blood loss to move.

Agnes, coldly staring at her, turned towards the underground and gave an order.

"Bring them. He needs the Silver Born."

The Vesica vampires carried the hunters one by one and boarded the elevator.

They descended a full three kilometers underground to a massive vampire hideout.

"You've awakened, Agnes."

Another Lord, Benedict, greeted her.

"Where is Jinma?"

Being disturbed from slumber was extremely unpleasant, but if Jinma had given the order, it was different.

"He is waiting for you."

In the deepest part, Jinma Faust waited in a place with eight giant glass spheres.

Anyone who had been to heaven would recognize the elixir of life, but to Xenia, it was just terrifying.

"Kill me! Just kill me!"

It would be better to die than to become a lab experiment.

"Calm down. You're not the main subject of this experiment."

While the Vesicas placed the hunters into glass spheres one by one, some people began to wake up.

"Damn it! What is this!"

Ignoring them, Faust asked the Umbrella Man,

"What happened?"

"Nike went. He may not kill him, but the injection will be successful."

Xenia asked,

"What are you planning to do with us?"

"Not much. You're just fertilizer. To reach the end of life."

"The end of life?"

Faust's gaze pierced through ten thousand years as he looked up at the ceiling, arriving in the past.

"There was a human named Ogent."

"Smile... Smile..."

Rian destroyed everything in his path as he raced towards Miguel's mansion.

His eyes saw the scenery, but his brain was flooded with countless scenes it couldn't analyze.

As if emerging from a tunnel, all the scenes pushed out of his view, revealing the landscape of heaven.

Led by memories, he crossed the sea and passed through forests, arriving at an ancient village.

A man with long black hair turned with a beautiful smile.

"Smile."
