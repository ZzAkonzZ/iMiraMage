Chapter 1078: Raid Control (4)

When Shirone arrived in Democracy, he received shocking news from Mafia's group.

"What? You can only attempt one myth-level hunting ground per day?"

Sona explained, "Yes. The system resets at midnight. You can try the same hunting ground multiple times, but the 'Moon's Incubation' event is only available after midnight."

Without this restriction, most users would flock to myth-level hunting grounds.

"Usually, parties try one place, and if it doesn't work out, they move to regular hunting grounds."

Mafia added, "Anyway, we have to wait until midnight to go to the Moon's Incubation. Why not contact Golden Wheel now…"

"See you then."

Cutting him off, Shirone entered the Mid Gear charging station.

'I need to wait until midnight.'

Staying outside with them would only lead to pointless arguments.

"Charge, please."

Paying with the credit card, Shirone entered Mid Gear, and the wires connected.

'It'll start as soon as I wake up.'

It would be a moment for Shirone, but time was still passing outside.

Mafia's group, waiting outside the charging station, couldn't contain their frustration.

"Damn! We're just following him around. What if he changes his mind later?"

Mafia said, "Let's post on the bulletin board. Title it 'Legendary Item Acquired.' It's the top guild in East Country, so they can't scam us. It's minimal insurance."

Sona said, "I'll post it. I have the recording."

As she updated the video, DexGamsung turned to Mafia.

"I didn't expect him to equip a legendary item without hesitation. The guild must be seriously backing him. We can't afford mistakes in the Moon's Incubation."

"Do you really think another legendary item will drop? Let's stick with him for a day and then formally report to Golden Wheel."

"It feels off. Too much luck. I checked the bulletin board on the way, and there are rumors he's using some bug. The admin meeting cleared him, but still."

"Hmm."

Mafia thought for a while.

"Let's do this."

The rest of the conversation was through communication, and their attitudes changed.

Then Sona said, "Wow, guys, check the bulletin board."

Her video had over 1,300 views in just five minutes.

'Good.'

If Yahweh2 was scamming them, at least public opinion would be on their side.

Clank, clank.

Engines roared continuously in a massive factory 500 meters underground.

The sky was pitch black, and the background was invisible despite the sparks flying everywhere.

Boss Creature Manufacturing Plant.

An area not updated in High Gear, it was the main stage for Admin Number Seven.

"Phew! This never ends."

Turning off his torch, he stood up from welding an unfinished mechanical monster.

His machine was specialized for factory work, with NO.7 marked on the shoulder guard.

"Such peculiar tastes."

Pulling up documents in augmented reality, he reviewed the portfolio provided by the Operator.

"How am I supposed to meet the new update date? She plays around every day."

Machines completed here went through the test server before official updates.

The test server mainly corrected errors, a mind-numbingly repetitive task.

Yet he stayed up all night because…

"Am I a pervert?"

The Operator's immersion in facing his creations was unmatched.

'How did I get involved with her?'

It started with curiosity, then he was captivated by the Operator's exceptional design skills.

'That's why it's more frustrating.'

Seeing himself swayed by a teenage girl's fantasies mirrored his reality.

'I ran away.'

Number Seven was an undercoder addict.

Finding it hard to deal with people (especially women), he had lived here for so long he couldn't remember when it started.

"Ugh, whatever. I'll finish this and quit. I'll find a place outside."

Breaking news.

A message appeared in Number Seven's augmented reality.

"What now? I'm busy."

As an admin duty, he turned off the torch and opened the bulletin board.

"Wow, over 10,000 views?"

He watched the video without moving until it ended.

"What?"

It was revealed in the admin meeting that Yahweh2 had special senses for time and space.

But even considering that, this case was hard to understand.

'Obtained Michelangelo on the first try?'

It was possible.

But the problem was that Yahweh2 had been surrounded by controversy.

'Log search.'

Numerous signals in the undercoder's language flashed across his retina.

'It's not a bug. No traces of illegal programs. It's clearly a probabilistic result. But…'

The signal parade suddenly stopped.

'This section. Couldn't he have dealt with Ergos faster? The delay is slightly longer than the average user's reaction. It's like he's aiming for something.'

Number Seven looked up sharply.

"Is that possible?"

Probability is probability.

Even knowing the random number algorithm at the designer's level, it's still luck.

'I couldn't match it even if I tried. How does he know the timing for specific results?'

He muttered again.

"Pattern."

Creating perfect randomness is nearly impossible.

'With infinite dice rolls, symmetry breaks, and individuality forms. What we call the designer's intention. The unconscious, even deeper.'

His face grew serious.

'But reaching that level requires processing an amount of information comparable to the universe. This can't be calculated. Unless there's a quantum computer in his head… huh?'

His thoughts paused.

'…What form would that computer take? Probably not logical computation. More like… yes, intuition! A sense for specific outcomes.'

Number Seven checked the logs again.

'The firewall wasn't breached. So it's not magic. But also…'

It is magic.

'Damn, it was wordplay. If realizing something in reality is magic, it applies here too. He's adapting to High Gear at an incredible speed.'

If this hypothesis was true, the real world couldn't be free from simulation either.

What is a perfect world?

Recalling the original purpose, Number Seven downloaded all logs related to Yahweh2.

'Who are you?'

It wasn't an admin's authority.

Ascension Guild

After Mafia's group posted the video on the bulletin board, the most outraged were the members of the Ascension Guild.

Codename Taedaecheon bit down on his electronic cigarette.

"The Sanctuary of Ergos? That's controlled by Ascension's third team! How did Yahweh2 get in without a West Country pass?"

Codename Yagwi added, "There's another issue. Michelangelo dropped. Our guild has only managed to get two of those items despite our efforts, and now one has gone to East Country."

Codename RubyMix spread her arms in disbelief.

"According to the report, they used a West Country Free Pass. That's managed by Ascension's first team. It means there's a traitor among us."

Taedaecheon's single eye lit up.

"Impossible. We've fought fiercely against East Country in the Solar War. No one would sell out for galaxies. Who would throw away their pride for that?"

"But the results speak for themselves. And Yahweh2 is a member of Golden Wheel, East Country's top guild. This is a serious issue."

Yagwi asked, "So who is the traitor?"

"You all know."

In the silence, RubyMix spoke.

"Little Witch. She's the newest member and always acts unpredictably in the Solar War. She's never made a mistake, but she always keeps us on edge."

Taedaecheon stroked his chin.

"I thought she was just crazy at first. Her skill overshadows her personality. We need to confirm this."

Yagwi asked the Operator, "What do you think? Honestly, you're the most suspicious of Little Witch."

She chose silence.

"Alright, let's find her without delay. If she's a traitor, she'll pay."

As Taedaecheon spoke, the door opened.

"Did you call for me?"

It was Little Witch.

"I thought I heard my codename outside. What's going on? The atmosphere seems tense."

"You… you…"

They spoke indirectly due to lack of evidence, but everyone had already made up their minds.

'She's the traitor.'

As Taedaecheon bit his cigarette, Little Witch approached the Operator.

"Do you have something to say to me?"

After exchanging glances for a while, the Operator finally spoke softly.

"…No."

Then she walked towards the door.

"I'll be back shortly. Something came up."

"Hmm."

As Little Witch watched her leave, she turned back to face them with a sigh.

"Little Witch."

Three guild members were blocking her path.

"It's best to be honest. Did you give Yahweh2 the West Country Free Pass?"

"Yes, I did. Why?"

"What!"

RubyMix shouted.

"Do you know what you've done? You gave a legendary item to an enemy! And you call yourself Ascension?"

"Oh, it's about balance. The Operator is too strong. Honestly, without her, we'd have lost to East Country long ago. What's the point of winning so disgracefully?"

Taedaecheon drew his mechanical greatsword.

"So you're finally showing your true colors. Now that we know you're an East Country spy, it won't end well for you."

"Oh? What are you going to do?"

"We recorded your confession. Kneel and dismantle all your parts. We'll make sure you can't set foot in High Gear again. Even you can't handle three rankers."

Yagwi and RubyMix also drew their weapons.

"Anyway."

Casually passing between them, Little Witch sat on a sofa and crossed her legs.

"This is why you're failing. You need to make it entertaining. Is it fun if only you hog everything?"

"Are your last words done?"

Little Witch formed her hand into a gun shape and aimed it at them, making them stop.

"…What are you doing?"

"A gun. This is a shooting simulation game. Don't come closer, or I'll shoot."

"Heh heh heh."

As Taedaecheon's shoulders shook with laughter, his eyes lit up with electricity.

"Crazy…"

As he raised his greatsword and took a stance, Little Witch bent her elbow and made a sound.

"Pew!"

Taedaecheon flinched.

But nothing happened, and RubyMix and Yagwi looked at each other in confusion.

Little Witch tilted her head.

"Huh? Why didn't it work?"

"You… you…!"

Feeling humiliated for flinching even momentarily, Taedaecheon charged with a roar.

"You little…!"

As his greatsword infused with booster energy aimed for Little Witch's head…

"Pew!"

"Gah!"

Taedaecheon convulsed as if electrocuted and collapsed on the floor.

"Hey! Taedaecheon!"

RubyMix and Yagwi rushed over but saw him disappear before they could touch him.

'He disconnected forcibly.'

Turning with a chilling feeling, they saw Little Witch pointing her finger gun at her mouth.

"What do you think? Isn't my gun awesome?"
