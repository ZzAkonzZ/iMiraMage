Chapter 1127: Triggers (3)

Ashur returned.

The two-dimensional form trapped in the transparent panel materialized as the glass shattered.

"Ikael!"

He immediately drew his sword, ready to fight, but the situation was different from what he expected.

'What's going on…'

The angels' bodies were emitting black smoke as if they were being scorched by fire.

Shirone was the first to be seen, but Ashur focused on the fiery figure next to Ikael.

'Amy.'

The fact that the conceptual beings, the angels, were burning meant that a transcendent fire was at work.

'The Idea of Fire.'

As far as Ashur knew, the only being who had reached that level was the incarnation of the sun, Anke Ra.

'Nane swallowed that dream.'

Recalling that Amy had received help from Buddha, Ashur roughly grasped the situation.

'She has also reached the end. If that's the case, no angel can approach her.'

All angels were born from the sun.

The flames disappeared from Amy's body, and she regained her human form, panting.

"Whew."

While complete combustion was in progress, she was invincible, but the aftermath was severe once the fire was extinguished.

Shirone approached with teleportation.

"Amy, are you okay?"

Instead of answering, Amy smiled, a small thrill in her eyes.

'It's certainly an incredible power.'

Shirone had also blown away angels with the Hand of God, but this time it was on a different level.

'The Idea is the essence. There is nothing that Amy cannot burn.'

"Shirone."

As Ashur approached, Shirone asked.

"What happened to Wizard?"

"She made contact with the target. She asked us to trust her. By now, it should be settled."

It was a battle with Satan.

"I see."

It would be a lie to say he wasn't worried, but he hadn't trained her weakly enough to treat her like a child.

'We have no choice but to trust Wizard now.'

At that moment, Satiel's voice was heard from afar.

"Uriel."

Then a shockwave hit, and Satiel flew in, clutching Uriel's neck.

"Don't interfere with me! Even you will be annihilated…!"

Satiel, who was hit in the face by the paradise scepter, couldn't finish her words and flew sideways.

She dug her fingers into the ground 20 meters away and bared her teeth in a crouched position.

"You…"

Ikael spoke.

"Stop it."

As always, her voice had a special resonance.

"This is not the time to fight. The world is heading towards its end. We must prepare for the final moments."

"It's all because of you!"

Satiel, who had kicked off the ground, rushed at Ikael with her fist.

"How dare you betray the angels!"

When Ikael grabbed her hand, the judicial halo Nostalgia was activated.

"Die!"

As the power of disintegration began to crumble her palm like sand, Ikael spread her holy light.

"Ataraxia."

As her presence amplified, even Satiel's Nostalgia lost its light and weakened.

"Ugh!"

Nevertheless, Satiel did not back down.

"Ikael, you hypocrite. You just need our power for Shirone."

"That might be true."

"You betrayed us! So…"

"But aren't you the same?"

The moment Satiel saw Ikael's cold eyes, she instinctively shut her mouth.

"Goffin is no longer here."

"…What are you talking about?"

"No matter what means you use, you can't meet him, and the only thing left is your memory."

Satiel's teeth ground together.

"If you extinguish even the anger burning now, you won't be able to feel him anymore."

"Shut… up."

"Isn't that why you're fighting?"

"Kill!"

The moment the archangel's order was given, all the ordinary angels flew up, ready to perish.

As Shirone blocked Ikael, Uriel rushed towards the ordinary angels.

"Ragnarok."

The moment the paradise scepter struck the ground, a massive white lightning surged up like a reverse current.

The roar wasn't the problem, but the shockwave that shook the holy light froze the angels.

Ikael muttered with a surprised expression.

"Uriel."

"Don't be mistaken."

Still blocking the angels, he slowly turned his head.

"This is not a decision for you. If I have a heart, it is surely murderous intent, but…

It's strange.

Because I can destroy everything in the world, I am an existence that cannot be touched even more.

'Destroying Ikael is easy.'

The only problem was whether he could handle himself after she was destroyed.

At that moment, all the angels turned their heads.

"Huh?"

Then Ashur, and the next moment, the human Amy and Shirone realized.

"Something is coming."

It was clear that it wasn't material.

'A certain feeling.'

A chilling sensation was creeping in.

The Zodiac.

The astrologers looked at the mechanical device floating above the pentagram drawn on the floor.

'How sorrowful.'

The essence of the world was contained within the metal.

King Kitra of Paras, with brilliant blue light emanating from his eyes, gave the order.

"Welcome the new world."

Having received the baptism of humanity at the Pyramid of Truth, he briefly saw the essence of God.

When he came to his senses, he was outside the pyramid, and his emotional illness was completely cured.

"The cause is meaningless."

Currently, besides Kitra, those who had contacted the Pyramid of Truth were scattered around the world.

"Major Adjustment."

If reset means system initialization, Major Adjustment means changing the system itself.

The result was…

The ground shook.

Shirone looked at the pyramid structure rising beyond the horizon.

"What is that?"

It was as huge as the Pyramid of Truth in Paras.

"Shirone!"

Ikael shouted.

"The time waves keep coming. They are changing the laws of this world."

"Time waves?"

The angels were the first to realize it.

"Those things are being erected all over the world. We can't see everything with our oversight, but there are already more than eight."

Shirone bit his lip.

'Ancient civilization.'

Evidence of an outer world created when nothing existed in the universe.

'Are they intervening directly? For what purpose?'

While Shirone was lost in thought, the ordinary angels flocked to Satiel.

"Archangel, what on earth is that…?" Satiel remained silent.

Even if they were archangels, there was no information about things established before their existence.

'No, even if they knew, it would be the same.'

OOPArts disrupts the information of all approaching beings with a magnetic field.

"What is about to happen?" The Zodiac reported.

"Your Highness! We have entered the coordinates. However, once we change fate, it cannot be undone."

The reason for speaking was that even the astrologers were terrified.

'This is madness. It's a different issue from the world map or the extinction of humanity.'

The world as they knew it would disappear.

"Proceed."

"Ugh."

Some astrologers shed tears of pure fear, but Kitra remained calm.

'If I can become a god.' Losing his head was a trivial matter.

"Major Adjustment."

As the astrologers operated the machine, the spherical metal began to spin rapidly.

Blue light exploded from Kitra's eyes.

"Ooooooh!"

Beyond the veil of the now pale view, something faint seemed to shimmer.

'Oh, god.' The angels were busy observing the state of the world with their oversight.

'This doesn't make sense.'

The terrain in the areas where the pyramids were erected had completely changed, and no living creatures were visible.

"Shirone, this is serious. Right now…"

As Ikael was about to speak, the nearest pyramid was enveloped in electricity.

"Ugh!"

The holy lights of the angels shook violently, and eventually, someone screamed.

"Aaaaah!"

Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang!

The angels whose holy lights exploded fell to the ground like puppets with their strings cut.

The angels' faces turned pale, and Shirone met Ikael's eyes.

'No way?'

Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang!

With half the angels already down, Satiel bit her lip tightly.

"Archangel! Ugh!"

When the holy light of the angel next to her exploded, her shoulders twitched involuntarily.

'Is this the end?'

The chain reaction of explosions skipped Satiel and continued to the angel on the opposite side.

And so…

"All of them?"

In the place where the ordinary angels had disappeared, only Satiel, Ikael, and Uriel remained.

'Why are we still here? Because we're archangels?'

Naturally, that thought came first, but Shirone slowly shook his head.

'No, that doesn't matter.'

Exploring the Pyramid of Truth, Shirone recognized the identity of the characters engraved on the wall.

'Each one is an angel. If that's the case, even archangels can't avoid it. Their commonality lies elsewhere.'

Heart.

With nothing else to think about, Satiel clenched her fist and shouted.

"This is absurd!"

The sight of the angels who ruled the world lying helplessly on the ground was the epitome of misery.

"How can this be? The angels…"

At that moment, a flash of light flew in front of her, and the archangel of light, Raiel, landed.

"Satiel, the laws are changing." When the system changes, the definitions of specific concepts also change.

"The concept of angels has lost its absoluteness. They have been demoted to the status of humans. Given the cycle of the time waves, we too will eventually be unable to avoid it."

Satiel looked at the ordinary angels.

They were still breathing, but the holy light, the symbol of their nobility, was nowhere to be seen.

Her gaze turned to the pyramid.

'What on earth is that?'

The massive structure, charged with electricity, seemed poised to do something soon.

"What are you going to do?"

"I can match the timing. Let's go together. We need to resolve this before our status falls."

Satiel immediately understood.

'Damn it.'

She wanted to deal with Ikael right away, but now even her existence was in danger.

"Hmph, it's rather convenient. Think of it as the punishment given to you. Becoming the human you so adore."

Ikael, whose holy light was much fainter than before, just kept her mouth shut.

"Let's go, Raiel."

As soon as Satiel finished speaking, Raiel activated the Judacial Halo Accelerator.

Uriel muttered as he watched their traces disappear at the speed of light.

"Humans."

The idea of their status falling was a dreadful thought, but strangely, his heart fluttered.

'Not bad.'

He looked back at Ikael.

"What will you do? Satiel doesn't seem to like this situation."

Amy asked.

"Where did Raiel go?"

"The sun."

"The sun? So… that?"

When Amy pointed to the sun in the sky, Ikael nodded.

"Yes. Humans are born from planets, but angels are born from the sun. We are conceptual beings. The signals of light condense and materialize."

Shirone spoke.

"Raiel will arrive in ten minutes. Following him isn't a problem, but…

Can a human body withstand it?

"Hmm, I see."

As if to dispel Shirone's worries, Amy's body once again burst into flames.
