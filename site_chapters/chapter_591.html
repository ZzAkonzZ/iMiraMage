Chapter 591:

As the Stop magic was cast, an event emerged from the specific structure of Istas.

Event Coordinates

Time: 00:00

Location: Istas Warehouse 72

Inside Istas, recreated as it was 19 years ago, a woman entered through the door. It was Miro, looking much the same as she did now, holding a newborn baby.

With a serious expression, as if bearing the weight of the world, she stared down the corridor and then turned to ask, "How much time do I have?"

"……"

Someone outside the door spoke, but the sound didn't carry, indicating they were outside the event.

"Don't worry. I will succeed."

Miro promised the unseen person.

"If I don't return in time, just close the time. I won't blame you."

The person outside seemed to speak at length, and Miro stood for a long time before nodding and turning away.

"Yolga."

Three minutes into the event, as she left Warehouse 72, this was the last word she uttered.

Event Coordinates

Time: 2 hours 42 minutes

Location: Istas Warehouse 72

Opening the door of Warehouse 72, Shirone and Anchal stepped into Istas.

Though the scene was familiar, today it made Shirone's heart race with excitement.

"Phew."

Taking a deep breath, Shirone turned to Anchal and asked, "Who cast the Stop magic? If Armin didn't come here, I mean."

"It must be Fermi's regulation-external magic. The upper echelon's event is hidden at a space-time coordinate. When time stops, it separates into time and space."

To them, the event played out like reality, but to outsiders, everything here happened in an instant.

"What if I cast Four Symbols Explosion here…?"

Before Shirone could finish, Anchal grabbed his wrist and pulled him aside.

"Hide."

Using Jincheon Sorcery, they blended into the wall with an illusion.

Moments later, Miro, covered in blood and with limp arms, entered Warehouse 72.

Though her expression was dazed and her steps heavy, her eyes were sharp.

'Miro? What happened?'

Miro suddenly stopped, but Anchal watched her calmly.

'It's okay. She won't notice us.'

The Majingan's ability to create illusions as real as reality made it impossible for even Miro to see through.

"Shirone."

Miro's call made Shirone flinch.

"Don't come. Whatever you're thinking, don't come here."

Thinking he needed to talk to Miro, Shirone moved towards the wall, but Anchal held his shoulder.

'Not yet. It's not good to get involved in the event from the start.'

Rumble!

Istas began to shake, and the structure started to change.

Miro turned and shouted, "You fool…!"

Before she could finish, Miro's form faded and vanished like a lie.

As the shaking stopped, Anchal dispelled the illusion and spoke.

"The structure of Istas has changed. Warehouse 72 is no longer the entrance."

"Does that mean the event changes if the space changes?"

Anchal nodded.

"The upper echelon's event is a record imprinted on a specific time and space. If either changes, the event doesn't occur. But from now on, it will be different. The warehouse moving means it's already on a different path."

"Who moved the warehouse?"

"It could be Fermi… or maybe you."

Shirone didn't understand.

"But I just got here. How could I move the warehouse?"

"The upper echelon's time is an infinite loop. It's a circle, not a straight line, with no distinction between past, present, and future. We just arrived, but your future self might have moved Istas for some reason. Otherwise, how would Miro know your name?"

"Oh…"

Shirone's mouth fell open.

"The upper echelon's event is a record from 19 years ago. She shouldn't know your name. So the Miro we saw has already met you."

"She knew I was there because she heard from my future self?"

"We can't be sure yet. The event taking a different path means it will continue to do so. Especially the space moving is serious."

"In what way?"

Anchal cast an illusion to create a cube.

"Simply put, Istas is like a cube. Goffin placed a specific event here. For example…"

A picture depicting a war familiar to anyone from Tormia appeared on the cube's six faces.

"Let's say this picture is the upper echelon's event. It's a simple picture, but it contains many stories depending on the observer's perspective."

Shirone realized how Goffin hid the event in space-time.

"He compressed a 3D event into 2D."

"Exactly. He imprinted the event like this picture. But we're inside the picture now, experiencing the 2D event in 3D. And now, for some reason, Istas moved."

As the cube spun randomly, the picture became a jumbled puzzle.

"Ugh."

"Now do you understand? The events recorded at specific times and spaces are all scattered."

Anchal pointed to the passage to the next warehouse.

"It takes less than a minute to get to Warehouse 14 from here. But it might be an hour in the future or an hour in the past."

If space twisted and the timeline broke, another hypothesis could be made.

"What if I twist time? What if I cast Four Symbols Explosion here?"

"How far can you perceive time?"

"About one second before and after."

"That would cover all the upper echelon's time. This is a 2D event with no time. If you cast Four Symbols Explosion now, you'll experience all the events in this space."

"But I can't change the space. So I might have moved Istas."

"Exactly. Fermi is suspicious, but if anyone needs to change the space, it's you."

"What necessity would that be?"

It had to be something serious to risk twisting the event and moving Istas.

"We have to go to the engine room. If the event changed, we need to know to fix it."

As Anchal turned towards the engine room, Shirone asked in surprise, "Did you know Istas's equations?"

"Don't underestimate the Jincheon Space Agency's intelligence. We can get blueprints even if they're top secret."

Shirone's eyes widened.

'Top secret…'

He felt lucky to be alive, even more so than when he went to heaven.

Event Coordinates

Time: 1 hour 17 minutes

Location: Istas Warehouse 14

As soon as they stepped out of the warehouse, a powerful explosion erupted.

"What?"

Turning his head, Shirone saw the warehouse covered in blood.

"Aaaargh!"

"Chase them! Don't let them escape!"

Shirone's eyes widened.

'That voice!'

Recognizing the voice he heard when he first cast Four Symbols Explosion at Istas, he saw a man with clawed gauntlets chasing Miro.

"Don't let your guard down! The opponent is the strongest Banaya!"

Though Miro ran forward, the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara's vision saw everything.

"Aaaargh!"

As Avalokiteshvara's hand pinned another soldier to the wall, his body burst like a fly.

"Reporting! Team 1 is annihilated! Team 1 is annihilated!"

"Damn it!"

Team 2 leader Lask gritted his teeth and charged.

"Miro! Surrender!"

The gap closed quickly, and Miro finally turned.

"Damn!"

As Avalokiteshvara's hand split to target Lask, his claws created afterimages as he swung.

With a thunderous roar, Avalokiteshvara's hand was deflected, and Lask landed, crossing his claws in an X.

"Annoying…"

Miro glared at the baby in her arms and turned again.

"Chase her! Target the baby!"

Mars's soldiers rushed past Shirone.

Just watching the battle left Shirone breathless. As he sighed in relief, Anchal dispelled the illusion and spoke.

"Team 1 of Mars was annihilated at this coordinate. Judging by the baby, not much time has passed."

"She seems desperate to protect the baby."

Otherwise, someone as powerful as Miro wouldn't flee from enemies.

'That baby…'

Shirone recalled the night before he descended the mountain after training, when Miro asked about his parents.

Shirone, maybe I…

What was Miro trying to say then?

"I need to find out who that baby is."

"Then let's move to the next room."

They reached Room 13, where Miro had fled, but found no signs of battle.

"The space is right, but the time is different."

At Anchal's words, Shirone took out Armand.

Grasping the sword and activating Diamond Armor, an organic robe enveloped him, and an artificial brain floated above his head.

"Is that Armand?"

Though Valhalla Action was repaying its debt, its other functions would greatly increase his survival chances.

"I need to meet Miro now. I'll cast Four Symbols Explosion here."

Perceiving time differently would separate Anchal and Shirone's coordinates, so there was no reason to stay.

"Alright. I'll head to the engine room. Find me when you're done."

After confirming Anchal left the warehouse, Shirone cast Four Symbols Explosion, and the surroundings began to shimmer.

"Huuuuh!"

The perception of time was entirely different from before, as the 2 hours and 48 minutes of the record flooded in.

The sensation of past, present, and future compressing into 2D was grotesque, like looking through a kaleidoscope.

"Ugh!"

Amid the overwhelming influx of information, Shirone desperately pinpointed the time Miro had been there.

"Aaaargh!"

As Four Symbols Explosion faded, his head throbbed with pain.

Event Coordinates

Time: 1 hour 32 minutes

Location: Istas Warehouse 13

"Who are you?"

Miro, clutching a baby, leaned away from the wall.

"Miro?"

Quickly regaining his senses, Shirone stopped in his tracks.

The Mars Unit 2 that had been chasing her lay brutally slaughtered on the warehouse floor.

'The battle is over.'

"I asked who you are. If you're with Mars, I'll kill you."

Shirone hastily pulled back his hood.

"No, I'm a student from the magic school!"

It was the best answer to reassure Miro.

"A student? You didn't get out?"

If he was a student of the Paranormal Psychic Research Society, it was possible he had entered before Goffin cut the timeline.

"That's unfortunate. I'm sorry, but you need to accept it. You can't leave here."

Relaxing, Miro leaned back against the wall and sat down.

Shirone approached, looking at the baby in her arms, still peacefully asleep.

"No, I came to see you, Miro."

"Me?"

Miro, her face weary, turned to look at him.
