Chapter 1136: Major Adjustments (4)

Shirone's expression grew serious.

"Heukgangsi."

Aside from being a shaman from the Mun Kingdom, much about him was shrouded in mystery.

'Those talismans are tricky.'

The method of enforcing laws through the power contained in specific objects was difficult to counter with Hexa.

"Do you really intend to stop me?" Unlike with the residents, Shirone had no need to show mercy to the stars of the Ivory Tower.

"We won't know until we try."

As Heukgangsi finished speaking, flashes of teleportation surrounded Shirone.

'Tanjura, Daiho.'

A 1-star from the Law Department and his satellite.

'Borbor.'

Then Borbor, a 2-star from the Balance Department, landed behind Shirone, securing his rear.

'A total of six stars.'

Even though they outnumbered him in stars, it didn't feel like a balanced fight.

"You might die." The corners of Heukgangsi's mouth twitched under the talismans covering his face.

"Such a human thing to say. Who do you think we are?"

In the Ivory Tower, where even residents risked their lives, life was trivial compared to beliefs.

As the residents who had regained consciousness retreated to the tower's outer walls, the battle began.

Miracle Stream surged to the ceiling, and the stars and satellites simultaneously shifted positions.

Chief Justice Tanjura shouted.

"For inciting discord in the Ivory Tower during humanity's crisis, 300 years in prison! For making misguided judgments due to stubbornness, 273 years in prison!"

As his ability 'Judgment' activated, a binding force began to envelop Shirone.

Borbor shook her head.

"It's useless."

All the stars of the Law Department dealt with laws, but even Xing couldn't stop Yahweh.

'Of course, I know that.'

Nevertheless, Tanjura continued to pass judgments.

"For ignoring the Chief Justice's judgment and fleeing, 600 years in prison! For appealing without remorse, 987 years in prison!"

The more Shirone resisted the judgment, the heavier the weight of the sins in Tanjura's judgment became.

'Eventually, he'll be caught.'

The hundreds of talismans Heukgangsi had thrown were gradually catching up to Shirone.

"For not repenting, an additional 800 years! Another 800 years! Yet another 800 years!"

As the sentence exceeded 20,000 years, Shirone's movements were finally restrained.

"Death penalty."

As Tanjura delivered the final judgment, his satellite Daiho drew his twin swords.

The ability 'Execution' activated in the 'Imprisoned' state had power comparable to Rian's divine transcendence.

'It's over.'

As talismans stuck to Shirone's body and the cutting power of the executioner's blade bit into his neck.

'Hand of God!'

Shirone vanished.

"Damn it!"

If everyone was 100% certain, Shirone could deceive their minds.

'A double-edged sword. But it never fails.'

Matters of the mind couldn't be corrected immediately just because one understood them.

"Watch out!"

Borbor shouted, and a giant fist of light flew towards Heukgangsi.

'Can't block it.'

Heukgangsi, crouched in the air, burned all the talismans attached to his body.

As the light exploded, Shirone felt a massive shockwave through the Hand of God.

'Did I block it?'

A long, thin breath was heard.

"Whew."

Heukgangsi's body, now devoid of talismans, shone brightly like the sun.

"It's been 20 years since I revealed this form."

"Baekgangsi."

Even without the talismans, his body was inscribed with the Mun Kingdom's shamanism.

Tanjura stroked his beard.

'Heukgangsi has no satellite. If the seal is broken, he becomes a different person entirely.'

According to Great Star, Baekgangsi was a 4-star.

'Like Yin and Yang, Holy Brain, and Luber, he's a being somewhat different from humans.'

As if fuel was evaporating, a mirage shimmered around Baekgangsi's long, solid body.

"Yahweh, I am a being born as a law weapon of the Mun Kingdom. My body contains 88 spells."

Each one was an incredibly powerful spell.

'What a tumultuous life he's had.'

His life, treated as a weapon rather than a human, until he became a star of the Ivory Tower.

'Everyone has their own burden.'

The stars of the Ivory Tower were impossible to persuade because they had chosen to bear their burdens themselves.

Tanjura waved his fan and spoke.

"We are not ordinary residents. We can be killed, but we cannot be saved."

Even so, it was unpleasant that Shirone had received a not-guilty verdict.

'Hand of God. Truly terrifying.'

From the victim's perspective, it felt like being deceived, but in reality, it was the truth.

'In the current state, judgments are useless. To stop Yahweh, we must break his spirit.'

Borbor, who had reached the same conclusion, spoke to Mika, the electromagnetic organism within her body.

'Scan Five Stars.' If Mika scanned Yahweh's information, she would mimic it.

-This is a dangerous tactic. Yahweh's emotions are predicted to be of a type not in the database.

'It's the only way.'

Once the mind was mimicked, Borbor could change the opponent's emotions as she wished.

'Lead him to self-doubt and destroy his spirit.'

As the satellite Mika generated electricity, Miracle Stream twisted like a snake.

'Did it react?'

-He seems faster than me. Knowing Shirone and facing him as an enemy were entirely different challenges.

"Five Stars."

Baekgangsi's body burned like steam.

"I will consider it an honor."

His body, imbued with countless spells, became a single talisman and rushed at Shirone.

'Power of Strength.'

As the fist with a vortex pattern struck the ground, an explosion shook the floor.

Shirone's skin tingled.

'It's not easy.'

As Tanjura's judgment bound his body, he felt Borbor's gaze.

'I don't want to kill them.'

Aren't they also fighting for humanity?

"…Please stop now."

"Impressive. To remain composed before the stars of the Ivory Tower."

Baekgangsi did not stop.

"But we cannot."

The hieroglyphs engraved on his abdomen began to twist with his muscles.

'Three Thousand Worlds.'

His abdomen split open, revealing kaleidoscopic fragments spinning like a rainbow.

'Buddha?'

Shirone, recalling the teachings from each color, defended with the Hand of God.

"Release."

The release of a massive energy wave scraped the surface of Hexa and pierced the walls of the Ivory Tower.

"Ugh!"

It was likely the essence of Baekgangsi, a human talisman, that could not be used twice.

'To use something like that… on a human instead of the world.'

"You fools!"

As Tanjura's judgment surpassed 20,000 years again, Borbor hugged Shirone.

'Mika.'

In the moment when sparks flew.

-Scan complete.

With the dry voice, Borbor's mind fully absorbed Yahweh's emotions.

'This is the end.'

In the mental space where the distinction between friend and foe disappeared, Shirone and Borbor faced each other.

A tear rolled down her left eye.

"…Huh?"

The emotion, too vast to feel at first, enveloped her entire body.

Ahhhhh!

In the mental world, the tears became a flood, filling the space she occupied.

-Shock! It's a shock! Cutting off consciousness…

In the emotional storm that even buried Mika's voice, Borbor realized.

'I can't even mimic it.'

She couldn't handle even a fraction of the despair Shirone felt.

'How can he endure this?'

No, why does he have to go this far?

'It's too unfair. For one human… to have to do this much in this world.'

That injustice broke through the limits and invaded her mind, finally snapping her consciousness.

"Borbor!"

As the other stars watched, Shirone hugged Borbor and shouted.

"Wake up!"

Electricity flowed through her body, and her closed eyelids slowly lifted.

'Mika??????'

-Defibrillation complete. But she will die soon. Your mind is already destroyed.

Like a rubber band that had lost its elasticity, her mind could no longer dwell in reality.

"…Five Stars."

Borbor spoke.

"I'm sorry."

She couldn't persuade Xing.

No matter what Borbor said, it would be considered false information from Xing's perspective.

'I'm no different. Knowing humans best, yet unable to trust them.'

But Shirone was different.

'It doesn't matter if it's real or fake.'

Even if it's an illusion, a world where such feelings can exist would be beautiful.

"Please let Five Stars go."

Of course, the stars of the Ivory Tower wouldn't change their minds with just a few words.

"Please."

For the first time, Borbor showed an expression.

It felt like that.

"Hmph."

As Borbor died, talismans flew from all directions to seal Baekgangsi's body.

"Five Stars, it's not that we've changed our minds. It's the sorrow of losing a great star."

All the stars of the Ivory Tower liked her.

"Borbor was…"

Shirone knew her life through Omega only up to the age of four, but that was enough.

"She was a person of great courage." -Her heart has stopped. As blood flow was cut off and brain death progressed, Mika conveyed Borbor's words.

'Yes. Biologically, she's already dead. You should leave my body now. Thank you for everything.'

Mika seemed to ponder.

-I will stay here.

'I can't think anymore. If you stay here, it's the same as dying.'

-Yes. I suppose so.

Borbor felt puzzled by the satellite's words, as Mika had no emotions.

-It's more constructive to parasitize your silence than someone else's mind.

'Haha, I see.'

With Mika, even death seemed like just a long sleep.

'When I open my eyes again…'

Her life flashed before her eyes.

Born in Morai Market, Borbor was abused by her stepfather from the age of three.

Her father changed three times by the time she left home at seventeen, and they were all terrible.

While her mother worked, all she did was sit on the market floor.

"Hey, there's the tall girl. Hey, tall girl!"

At twelve, she grew tall without gaining weight, becoming a target for teasing.

"Hey, you! If you play with us, we'll give you money. Want to come?"

The security was so poor that human traffickers approached her in broad daylight.

"We'll treat you well. Come with us."

Each time, Borbor just observed the street with her thin body curled up.

'I'm not scared.'

The scariest place was home.

She became a notorious figure in the market, reduced to a spectacle.

"Ah."

One day, she suddenly realized.

'They're not looking at me.'

There was no difference between people watching her and her watching people.

"…Borbor."

Looking at the cold, lifeless Borbor with sad eyes, Shirone held her hand.

'Rest in peace.'

At that moment, a tingling current was felt at her fingertips, and a voice spread in her mind.

-I have something important to tell you.

'Mika?'
