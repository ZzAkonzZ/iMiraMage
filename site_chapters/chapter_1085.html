Chapter 1085: Solar War (3)

Aegis's wide-area communication spread.

Urgent! Yahweh2 and the Operator are in a standoff! Golden Wheel's first team, should we provide support?

Golden Wheel members checked the satellite video.

Wild West, with smoke rising from his .44 Magnum, gaped like a fool.

"What? It's real?"

Around them, West Country users lay sprawled with holes in their foreheads.

Should we go? If we end it here, it's over. Since they've taken the bait, we can drop the Solar War.

Yolga's son communicated.

Just wait. The outcome won't be decided early on.

Fermi, who gave the order, thought.

'The Solar War win rates for the East and West Countries are almost 50-50. But their victory patterns differ.'

Excluding the invincible user, the Operator, the East Country's power is rated higher.

Ascension's guild members are also highly skilled, but Golden Wheel is a company of professionals.

Yet, the win rate is still 50-50.

This shows what the Operator means to West Country users.

Aegis opened a private communication channel.

Boss, what do you think? If we've really drawn out the Operator, this is an opportunity.

There's no need for the East Country to sacrifice.

Even so, the agreement is a duel. Both the winner and loser must accept it.

But a direct confrontation has no chance of winning. Yahweh2 admitted it himself.

An undefeated record.

In a Solar War with thousands of users clashing, never dying is practically impossible.

'Therefore, the record must have been managed.'

If he wanted, Fermi could also have an undefeated record.

'But there's no reason for that.'

In a system where you revive after dying, how inefficient is it to care about honor?

'In High Gear, dying is also a tactic. But the Operator can't do that anymore.'

It's a matter that affects not just personal honor but the morale of the entire West Country.

Let's watch. As long as Yahweh2 focuses on the Solar War, the Operator won't hit him hard.

Shirone is right.

Understood.

Ending the private communication, Aegis relayed Fermi's instructions through wide-area communication.

Meanwhile, Yahweh2 and the Operator were engaged in a fierce probing battle.

Broadcast satellites, live-streaming to all continents of High Gear, zoomed in on the two.

"Wow…"

Users gathered in front of large screens were mesmerized by their battle.

"The four legendary grades."

Not only was it the first time someone had collected them all, but their skill in handling the parts was superhuman.

A light sphere vibrated above Yahweh2's palm.

"Michelangelo."

Dodging the rapid flashes, the Operator braked by extending one foot.

Simultaneously, her back opened, and a mechanical frame with gun barrels swung over her shoulders.

"Black Gun."

Every time ion cannons fired from the two barrels, the frame shook back and forth.

Matching Michelangelo's speed, Shirone's time-sense slowed the flow of time.

'What…'

Seeing the Operator between the ion cannons scraping the ground in two rows was chilling.

'How is she so fast?'

Her delicate fist, sharp as an awl, grazed Yahweh2's cheek.

"Phew!"

Immediately, her other fist flew in and struck Shirone's side hard.

Boom!

Though Raphael's strong armor activated, the machine was sent flying.

'The machine itself is a weapon.'

Top rankers are all strong, but the Operator seemed to focus more on the machine's performance than firepower.

As Yahweh2 landed, dragging along the ground, East and West Country forces crossed, blocking the view.

"Kahaha! I'll take Yahweh2!"

Above the crowd, a machine over 8 meters tall fired a rotary cannon.

Code name Bishamonten.

As large bullets scraped the sides, East Country users were shattered.

'A monstrous form.'

In reality, a person with two arms would struggle to control a machine with four arms.

'Yet he handles it like his own body. It means the avatar itself is huge. Is he a demon?'

Since any dreaming creature can enter Under Coder, it's uncertain.

Bishamonten activated his booster and charged.

"Is this all?"

From lenses on both shoulders, plasma beams spread out in a fan shape.

As the beams swept forward with a roar, dozens of East Country users' armor melted.

"Aaah!"

As Bishamonten smiled at the pleasing screams, his back felt a chill.

Turning his head 180 degrees, he saw Shirone with a massive light sphere.

'Damn! Is he a bat?'

As Bishamonten crossed his arms to block, his forearms opened, transforming into thick armor.

The flash struck the armor plate directly.

"Ugh…"

The machine shook with a thud, and the 80-ton machine slid across the ground.

"Aaah!"

Finally, the armor plate of the fallen Bishamonten was horribly dented, and East Country rankers rushed in.

"You bastard! Just wait!"

As his dying words echoed, the Operator slid in with her legs spread wide.

A black sword was in her hand.

"Checkmate."

As the bat opened, Shirone jumped through codes to escape behind the Operator.

What he saw then was…

'Oh no.'

The black sword's blade coming at his neck.

'Foolish, Yahweh2. I know all your positions.'

Even if he jumped through codes, he couldn't escape the designer's intent.

As High Gear roared at full output, the blade drew a terrifying arc.

Sparks flew from the black sword.

Clang!

Just the fact that the Operator's arm was raised indicated the bullet's power.

"Gehgehgeh! Don't forget about me."

Turning to the side, Shirone saw Wild West spinning his .44 Magnum.

Though called a .44 Magnum, it was the most powerful single-shot weapon in High Gear.

The Operator lowered her raised arm.

'It's the Solar War.'

Watching the East Country users charging from afar, she bent her legs.

With her arm over her head, aiming her black sword, she said,

"…Bring it on."

Wild West loaded bullets into his .44 Magnum with his bony hands and said,

"If you push too hard, your undefeated record might get tarnished. How about staying quietly in the palace, lady?"

The Operator checked the satellite video.

'We're being pushed back.'

It's just the beginning, but East Country users were concentrating here.

If the undefeated Operator was the morale of the West Country, defeating her was the morale of the East Country.

'Not stepping in might help.'

Though she thought so, the Operator's resolve dug deeper into the ground.

"Then try to defeat me."

She considered it a reward for Yahweh2, who took the Solar War seriously.

"This might be your only chance. And maybe… Yahweh2 is undefeated too?"

People watching the broadcast murmured.

"What does she mean? Yahweh2 isn't even a ranker, why does the Operator care so much?"

"Yeah, he's just famous."

Meanwhile, rankers actually fighting the Solar War felt it in their bones.

'This Solar War is different from usual.'

Golden Wheel's entire guild was moving to support Yahweh2.

Wild West, mouth agape, fell into thought.

'Is she saying Yahweh2's death isn't free from morale? Well, the risk is higher for us at level 307.'

Aegis communicated.

The boss's order is to proceed as planned.

"Okay."

Wild West raised his gun.

"Let's go."

As powerful bullets fired, the Operator moved her black sword, leaving afterimages.

The sparks from the blade signaled the clash between East and West Country forces.

The scene spread through the broadcast screen.

Even users fighting the Solar War kept the screen open, showing intense interest.

Rankers gathered, and even second-team members joined, turning the battlefield with Yahweh2 into the Solar War's core.

Destroyer707, firing wildly, shouted through the friend channel.

Hey! Look at the broadcast screen! Is that me on the screen? That's me!

Death Princess's voice trembled.

It is. We're on the main screen of the Solar War. Are we becoming famous? Hohoho!

Strongest Code Name spoke.

We're already famous. Users across the continent are watching us.

"Alright! I'm the Destroyer!"

As Destroyer707 increased output and charged, augmented reality flickered off.

'Huh?'

Moments later, his machine exploded, and Death Princess and Strongest Code Name turned like mirrors.

"Destroyer!"

West Country ranker Little Witch stood on the broken machine.

"Hello?"

Though just a simulation, hearing her voice made their souls freeze.

Moments later, two explosions followed.

'What?'

Shirone confirmed his friends' deaths but had no time to worry about it.

Aegis joined the battlefield.

Yahweh2. I'll cover you.

Even if they fought evenly, the level difference meant a lack of power.

'Backup charger…'

Just before Shirone retreated, Little Witch unleashed all her grenades.

"Get Yahweh2!"

Realizing her words, West Country rankers focused their firepower, causing an explosion.

"Phew!"

Stumbling out as a bat, Shirone saw a shadow in the smoke.

It was Wild West.

"Gehgehgeh."

Isolated by the bombardment, he kept pulling the trigger, spinning the empty magazine.

"Unlucky."

The Operator, appearing like a ghost, slashed his High Gear with her black sword.

The sound of the machine being pierced was heard.

"…Good judgment."

Jumping through codes as a bat, Shirone took the sword hit in front of Wild West.

'Better I die.'

Wild West met Yahweh2's eyes.

"A debt."

Even as electricity flowed through his machine, his hand loaded the magazine.

The black sword withdrew.

As Yahweh2 fell, the .44 Magnum's barrel fired forward.

Bang! Bang! Bang!

The Operator dodged sideways.

'That's dangerous.'

As the grenade smoke cleared, the broadcast screen showed all Solar War users flinching.

'What?'

Yahweh2 was down.

'One win for now.'

Coldly counting, the Operator leaped to find another prey.

'Considering the power, the sacrifice was good. But what about East Country's morale?'

Aegis communicated with Fermi.

Yahweh2 is dead! He's back at the starting point! Should we retreat from the battle zone?

Just hold on. He'll come back.

But East Country's morale is…

It will rise.

What?

As Aegis squinted and asked again, Fermi couldn't hold back his laughter.

Hehehe. Hehehehe.

If he truly becomes a High Gear user…

Boss?

Hold on.

Fermi's fierce speed transmitted through the communication frequency.

That will be the most exciting place.
