Chapter 736:

'Could it really be…'

Was such a state possible?

Goffin was already erased, existing only as a hypothesis among many magicians.

The Infinite Magician.

No one could guess what it truly was, but the head of the Jincheon Space Agency had evidence of its existence.

"Did you encompass the entire universe?"

Nane's question was indifferent, but there was a hint of expectation in his eyes.

"What did you realize?"

Shirone spread his arms and said,

"Life."

All life that exists in the universe.

"Existence is not a sin, Nane."

Even though they spoke the same language, Shirone's words carried a different depth, and Nane understood.

"If the only reason we suffer is because we were born, if our fate is to eternally reincarnate in the cycle of emptiness…"

Shirone wept.

"That is too sad."

Disappointment flickered in Nane's eyes.

"Did you gain only a heart from encompassing the universe? Isn't that heart the very source of suffering?"

"It's not something we can control. No matter how vast the universe is, it is not greater than a mother's love for her child."

Can you deny it?

"The heart transcends the universe."

Nane, who had almost realized all truths, pondered Shirone's words deeply.

"If the universe disappears, the heart disappears too."

The heart exists because there is existence.

"Though it may seem right, it is almost wrong, Shirone."

As Nane's sermon, the sword containing the concept of ending the universe, still pointed towards the ground, another sword was born.

"A god is heartless."

A steel-colored sword that removes all emotions in this world, Merciless, soared above and was absorbed into the sword of the end.

As Nane's will grew stronger, the ground trembled, and the feeling of the world's end approached.

Mun Kyung gritted his teeth.

"Damn it! How are we supposed to stop that?"

Nane's sword was pressing the world from the realm of concepts that transcended phenomena.

"My refusal to back down is not stubbornness. Do you know how much suffering that love you realized will cause in the future? It is suffering without recompense. Aren't they pitiful?"

The distance to the annihilation of the universe was 0.00001 nanometers.

"With no beginning and no end, it is just reincarnation. Is it unreasonable to end the eternally repeating suffering?"

Nane's precise Kar score, racing towards 99.9999…%, extended to 184,000 decimal places.

Shirone spoke.

"Because it is not our sin."

Even if it is human-made good and evil, human-made suffering, there is no original sin in existence.

Therefore, Shirone, who has no cause…

"I love."

By absolving the sins of all who exist, he denied Nane's emptiness.

"That too is hollow."

Dozens of swords unfolded behind Nane and rushed towards Shirone.

Destruction, annihilation, disintegration… All concepts necessary to erase an existence were being fired.

At the same time, the entrance to World No. 1 exploded.

"Gasp!"

As the quantum information tunnel transcending time and space opened, the realization of philanthropy surged in.

Then Shirone's Kar score, which had been well over 90%, began to plummet rapidly.

Kar Score: 86.24%.

'Love.'

Towards absolute philanthropy, where there is no distinction between future lives, good and evil, trivial and grand.

The entrance to World No. 847 exploded.

"Aaah!"

Kar Score: 49.24%.

Shirone's mind was reduced to the level of an animal, but what we must hold onto until the end became clear.

Kar Score: 24.11%.

To achieve perfect love, what is needed is a pure and innocent emotion that does not discriminate against any existence.

'It is not hollow, Nane!'

Nane was almost right, but even if the world was full of suffering, the reason we must exist is…

'The heart is greater than the universe.'

Because we are so precious to ourselves.

Kar Score: 1.4%.

As they watched Shirone, whose eyes were bloodshot and shedding tears of blood, everyone was terrified.

Just as they couldn't understand the Infinite Magician, no one knew what was happening to Shirone now, but one thing was clear.

"He is bearing something… immense."

Kar Score: 0.24%.

"Arrgh!"

From animal to plant, from plant to tiny creature, to the lowest phase with nowhere else to descend.

But because of that, he can love.

'Everything.'

Kar Score: 0.0000017%.

"Don't you know that the hope you give is the seed of suffering?"

Kar Score: 0.0000000000000001%.

There was no trace of subjectivity.

For him, who distributed equal love to all things, senses were no longer a standard of judgment.

The eighth sense that a living being can have, Superqualia.

No one could predict what would happen if they were swept away by the storm of time and space.

"Can you withstand it?"

Nane, whose Kar score was close to perfection, guessed why Shirone was not being swept away by the storm of time and space.

Shirone, enveloped in light, spoke.

"Is this the answer you wanted?"

As the light blocking time and space expanded into a giant sphere, the storm of time and space was torn apart.

"Your Highness! It is disappearing!"

"It blew everything away."

The light that had spread towards Shirone, standing in the middle of the endless desert, began to be absorbed.

The light surrounded the brain, the source of the mind, and pale but not cold droplets of light endlessly flickered.

"Yahweh?"

The name of the special light that arises from the sense transcending cognition, omnipotence that only the Infinite Magician, who contains all the information of the world, can wield.

No one in the world has a higher Kar score than Nane, so no one can deny him.

The only truth has not yet been established in this world.

Goffin did not leave a question.

"Arian Shirone."

No, the existence of Hexa itself was the final barrier blocking perfect truth.

Shirone and Nane, Nane and Shirone.

"I think it is emptiness."

The universe is full of void.

"It is love."

The heart transcends the universe.

Both knew it was just a dream, but the two perspectives on the same truth were so extreme.

"Contradiction. No one can obtain the answer."

Shirone's Kar, free of prejudice, was very weak, but it was also the only point Nane could not reach.

"I can never be right."

Nane's fingertip pointed at Shirone.

"And you can never be wrong?"

Thus, living beings must endlessly ponder the value of existence at the lowest phase of the universe.

"But two standards cannot coexist. If you and I coexist, countless lives will suffer."

Mahagaruta said that Nane's enlightenment was the ultimate synthesis of thesis, antithesis, and synthesis after conquering his own enlightenment.
