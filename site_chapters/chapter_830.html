Chapter 830: Breaking the System (2)

'Idea.'

The ultimate level Ogent reached.

Swinging a concept from outside the world was indeed worthy of being called breaking the System.

It wasn't about cutting with a sword but forcibly injecting the concept of cutting into the world.

'But it's that dangerous.'

It's neither beautiful nor cool.

It's only remarkable because a human achieved it, but ultimately, Ogent ended his life as a living being.

'The body can't withstand it.'

As Rian's Idea replaced Ogent's, he no longer heard Smile's hallucinations.

'Do I have such willpower?'

The terrifying obsession made Ogent immortal, but Rian only inherited his genes.

'There's no reason to live like that.'

Rian is not Ogent.

'Honestly, I don't even understand.'

The main reason he could come this far was that he resembled Smile's disposition.

Didn't swing the sword to become the best?

'I will become the best swordsman.'

If his ambition wasn't greater than Ogent's obsession, his body would completely disappear with this strike.

'I'm grateful for what you passed on, but... does it matter?'

"I won't stop."

For a moment, Rian felt as if Ogent was smiling.

Faust approached.

"It's a creature's fate to want to confirm the end."

Even when he was just a nameless bat.

"I saw the clash of the two strongest creatures. It was just a primal feeling, not even a memory."

Faust's body, exuding intense killing intent, began to dissolve into black smoke from his feet.

"Where did we come from? What is this world we inhabit? There are many ways to find the answer, but the simplest is..."

Like a mountain on fire, the massive smoke rose, completely covering the sky in darkness.

"To become strong enough to destroy that world."

This was why Anke Ra forbade breaking the System.

The smoke filling the sky twisted sharply and began to rush toward Rian.

Even in its half-spirit, half-demon state, its power was incomparable to that of a Lord.

"Rian!"

At the sight of all the darkness in the world converging, Xenia shouted.

'Cut!'

Rian took a step forward and swung his great sword.

At the level of divine transcendence, the speed of Idea was enough to burn the body.

The skin burned first, the muscles evaporated, and the skeleton turned red-hot.

Movements that surpassed human limits would melt even steel.

"Yaaah!"

is indestructible.

As Rian’s shout abruptly stopped, the massive smoke split left and right.

"Kraaa!"

In its half-spirit, half-demon state, the black smoke split in two, revealing a clear night sky.

Xenia's eyes filled with astonishment.

"This, this is..."

The sky split open, revealing another world with a terrifying glow.

'Where is that place?'

But the glow disappeared before she could ponder the question, and a realistic shock covered the world.

Boom!

A shockwave, powerful enough to split the world, endlessly advanced forward.

As the sound of collapsing buildings faintly echoed, Xenia knocked on the glass sphere.

"Rian! Rian!"

At the same time, the great sword fell from Rian's skeletal hands with a clatter.

"No!"

The sword, which had stood firm even without arms, seemed to fall as if Rian's life had ended.

Soon, the charred skeleton scattered in the wind, turning to ash.

"Krrr!"

Faust, split in two and returned to a human body, fell to the ground.

'I saw it.'

There was light.

It was an intense light that couldn't confirm anything, but it certainly existed.

'It's a pity.'

He wanted to stay in the world a little longer to see the end, but now he had only a few minutes left.

"Rian! Rian!"

Hearing Xenia's cry, Faust slowly lifted his left head.

'Has he disappeared?'

If the light he saw was real, existing in this world was impossible.

'Now that it's come to this... it doesn't feel good.'

'It's futile. In the end, nothing remains.'

Somewhere, the sound of the wind could be heard.

'I am Rian.'

His voice echoed throughout the universe, but no one could hear it.

'Yes, I am Rian.'

It had spread so perfectly that it couldn't be defined as sound.

But the faint wave continuously forced something upon itself.

'I know. I am Rian.' But what does that mean?

He was so satisfied with his current state that he didn't want to think any further.

'The best strike...'

Even recalling such memories was bothersome.

'I am Rian.'

Endlessly repeating himself was all he could do.

Like Arius using Apoptosis before, Rian was just in a state of concept.

Therefore, he had no will.

He just had to exist, endlessly repeating himself, permeating this world.

'What is this?'

Thus, it was a miracle that the current Rian had a question about a specific subject.

'A sword?'

A concept forcibly intertwined with him.

'I am Rian with .' Idea?

As information began to gain individuality, a bothersome memory surfaced.

'Yes, I used this to deliver the best strike.'

For what reason?

'To become the best swordsman.'

It was such a wonderful feeling that he thought, 'Should I go back now?' but it was far from reality.

Like lying in bed and thinking, 'Should I just die now?' it was a weak will.

'Why?'

The most unnecessary yet essential question among the six principles.

'Why should I go back?'

At that point, Rian's concept changed direction, looking at the opposite of reality.

And he realized that a beam of light, possibly from somewhere far away or right in front of him, was connected to him.

A signal coming from outside the world.

Thus, the concept Rian was thinking of now was also due to that signal.

What is outside?

Rian tried to go where the light was coming from but couldn't feel himself moving forward.

'I am Rian. I am Rian. I am Rian...'

The more he didn't give up, the stronger the feedback became, to the point where he couldn't think of anything else.

'Rian! Rian! Rian! Rian! Rian! Rian!'

He was afraid of losing even the part of himself that thought, but he kept moving forward.

Rian Ogent.

In the end, a single concept endlessly flickered in his thoughts, forming his entirety.

As everything became clear, his perspective turned to the opposite side, regardless of his will.

The shadow of a man holding a sword shimmered at the end of the dark world.

'Do I look like that?'

There was no way to confirm, but the first will to act emerged.

'Yes, I am Rian Ogent.'

The shadow is indestructible.

"It is the sword of Shirone."

As the wave of sound, defined as a voice, occurred, the world he was in exploded into paleness.

"Rian... If you die like this..."

Xenia sat helplessly in the glass sphere, crying.

The hunters, who had defeated the true demon Faust but lost a precious comrade, felt the same.

"Xe-Xenia."

At that moment, Katain pointed forward.

The air where Rian had disappeared shimmered transparently, forming a human body.

Like filling an empty mold, colorful hues began to dot it.

It was a human body.

Bones formed, nerves and muscles covered them, and finally, the skin rapidly regenerated, revealing Rian's face.

"How, how?"

The shock of disbelief, rather than joy, gripped the hunters' minds.

"Krrr!"

As the terrible pain from the realm of reality surged in, Rian gritted his teeth and knelt.

'This feels really bad.'

Was this how Shirone felt when he returned from the limits of the Immortal Function?

"Rian?"

Hearing Xenia's voice, Rian grabbed the great sword and slowly stood up.

His stride was dignified, but the problem was that he was naked.

"No, no..."

As Xenia hurriedly turned her head, a flash passed before her eyes.

The glass sphere, which had withstood any impact, was smoothly cut in half, and she turned her gaze back.

What she saw was Rian, standing unconscious.

"Rian! Wake up! Rian!"

Xenia ran over and shook him, but it was impossible to topple him, as if he were fixed in place.

"Release us!"

As the hunters unlocked the physics machine's lock, they checked Rian's condition.

"How is this possible..."

Even in an unconscious state, the law that activated weighed as much as moving a ton.

Katain, who barely laid Rian down, checked his condition.

"What happened?"

"Fortunately, he's breathing. But his pulse is weak. We should move him to the base quickly."

"Even if we move quickly..."

The hunters present could barely handle Rian's weight.

"There! Over there!"

Hundreds of security forces ran from afar.

Only after confirming the face of Security Chief Benoff did Xenia sigh and sit down.

'But such power...'

Every time she checked the landscape destroyed by Rian's sword, she couldn't believe he was human.

'The world's strongest swordsman.'

No one knew what would happen next, but when Rian woke up again, everything would be different.

Niflheim. Deep in the world of the frozen dead, Ymir's eyelids snapped open.

Even that caused the massive ice sheet to tremble.

'Is it time?'

Through the symbiotic creature Suoi, he knew that Anke Ra had disappeared.

Yet he hadn't moved because there was no reason to.

'Ogent.'

As promised 10,000 years ago, his bloodline has once again opened the world of breaking the System.

"Rian!"

As his lips moved, the Suoi quickly passed through the solid molecules, and cracks began to form on the surface of the unbreakable ice sea.

"Let's settle this!"

After Ogent left the world, Ymir was also trapped here for breaking the System.

"Talking about the world outside, protecting this world, all scared out of their wits!"

As Ymir clenched his fist and stood up, the surrounding ice evaporated into steam.

"Whatever it is, just break it!"

As his immense presence twisted gravity, the ice sea began to churn chaotically.

"I am the strongest!"

Otherwise, there was no reason to exist.

"I am..."

Rumble!

Hundreds of tons of ice chunks floated into the air, shaking uncontrollably.

"I am Ymir!"

As the shockwave spread, it pushed the ice wall with the form of energy, and the roar shook all of Heaven.

Ymir, the king of giants. The revival of his true form.
