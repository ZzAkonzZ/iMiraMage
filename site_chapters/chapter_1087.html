Chapter 1087: Reload (1)

It was a shock that made all the users fighting in the Solar War stop.

"The Operator…"

Shirone's hand, which had pierced her chest, was sticking out of her back.

An unmistakable fatal wound.

"Ugh."

As he pulled his arm out, electricity flowed through the Operator's body as she staggered forward.

Gritting her teeth and lifting her head, her machine exploded with a pop.

"She's dead."

The effect was no different from any other user, but somehow it seemed more brilliant.

In the unprecedented event in High Gear history, users even felt fear.

What should they do now?

As no one could provide direction, Shirone turned to the East Country and shouted.

"Let's go!"

As if drawn by a magnet, East Country users cheered.

"We defeated the Operator! She's not invincible! Now begins the era of the East Country!"

As East Country soldiers activated their turbos, the previously balanced front line pushed back 300 meters in an instant.

Yolga's son smiled bitterly.

'It's all about momentum.'

Their excitement was at its peak, and they were eager to fight immediately.

Aegis communicated.

Should we control them? If they push too hard, there might be delays later.

Leave them. We can't control this anyway.

They had marked a loss on the strongest machine in High Gear.

Conversely, creating this level of momentum is something only Yahweh2 could do. Draw East Country forces to key strategic points. Just holding those will be beneficial.

Understood.

Fermi looked at Little Witch.

"Are you going to continue?"

She, who had been pushing with terrifying killing intent just moments ago, now seemed to lack the will to fight.

"Defeating the Operator in a duel, the condition is cleared. Does Yahweh2 have anything left?"

"He does."

Shirone wouldn't stop.

"You know what kind of guy he is."

If it was just about achieving results, he wouldn't have left any room for the Operator.

"He won't give up until he's satisfied. So we have to keep going."

"Excellent after-service. Truly a big company."

"…The customer is picky."

From Golden Wheel's perspective, Shirone was the most troublesome customer.

"Haha, but you still want to catch him."

Without answering, Fermi activated his booster and flew up.

"Let's make it interesting. The East Country will win."

As he headed to the East Country's base, Little Witch also turned towards the Solar Palace.

"Hmm."

She was curious about the Operator's face.

Operator's Meeting Room

The Operator's death was shocking not only to users but also to the operators.

"…She lost?"

Messages arriving individually to operators exploded on the shared channel.

"Ugh! That scared me. What's going on?"

Smile Mark said.

"The forum is going wild. Users are posting videos of the Operator's death."

Checking the forum, they saw videos pouring in like a waterfall.

"Title: Operator Dead! The first post already has over 7,000 views."

"That shows how shocking it is. Honestly, I didn't think the Operator would lose either."

Candy Cane Mark said.

"About that, Yahweh2's double jump through codes at the end is strange. It's like knowing the location of the second door before even opening the first."

"Hmm, that's not something a guest can do."

"Right. Only the homeowner knows all the door locations. This is clearly a host's authority."

Gear Mark said.

"The Operator is also a host. Even in a surprise situation, it shouldn't have been unpredictable."

"Looking at the logs, there's a moment of stiffness. After all, she's human. The problem is Yahweh2. His senses have risen to a level that can no longer be called personal."

The meeting room fell silent.

"Look at this."

Black Circle Mark opened a window.

"This is Yahweh2's current movement."

What they saw was not an image but a rush of countless codes, making it even more shocking.

"The code…"

As Yahweh2's code changed, the entire code was changing in real-time.

It had a strange texture, like a convex lens moving on the screen.

Smile Mark asked.

"How is this possible? Can one user change external codes at will?"

"He's not changing them."

Gear Mark said.

"It's not artificial manipulation. The code is automatically redefined to match Yahweh2's changes."

Demon Mark muttered.

"Because it moves with the system, the balance doesn't break?"

Candy Cane Mark agreed.

"Yes. Yahweh2's status isn't changing drastically. It's likely the system's limit to handle Yahweh2's vibrations."

"Hmm, if it's not something you can understand with your head, then it's right to see it as perfectly assimilated. Like resonance, moving together."

Black Circle Mark said.

"In the end, High Gear is safe. It's not an error. But the host's authority has already been surpassed, and the Operator lost in the inflation. So, what do we do now?"

As silence continued, someone shrieked.

"Where on earth is Number Seven?"

Solar Palace

Ascension Guild, holding the primary defense authority, resurrects at the Solar Palace upon death.

After 20 minutes of rapid charging, Mid Gear opened, and the Operator slowly opened her eyes.

Everything looked unfamiliar.

Even as she reached the solar furnace where Low Gear was, she didn't open the communication channel.

'I died.'

Checking the satellite video, she saw East Country forces charging towards the Solar Palace.

'It's still okay.'

With the ebb and flow, it was normal to gradually approach the Solar Palace.

Kneeling quietly before Low Gear, the Operator surveyed the battlefield.

Nothing registered in her mind.

"Hah!"

She stretched out her legs and lay flat on her back, having straightened her bent knees.

"What should I do?"

She had truly died.

Leaving aside her promise with Yahweh2, she now had no world to escape to.

"This was everything."

She had believed that dying even once would be the end, making High Gear her reality.

But on the other hand…

'What's changed?'

She saw herself, having revived perfectly, thinking about various things.

"Haha! Hahaha!"

It was ridiculous.

'Yeah, I know. I know too. There's no such thing as a real world anywhere.'

She was just…

Afraid to throw her heart into it.

'Yahweh2.'

She watched Yahweh2 fighting the West Country's strong users through the satellite screen.

'Huh? It's Tiger?'

The third-ranked duelist in the West Country.

A skilled user with a cobalt-blue machine and powerful claws.

'He's pretty good. I once invited him to our guild…'

Recalling that time, a massive flash from Michelangelo struck Tiger directly.

'It was a bit lacking. Michelangelo's power seems to have dropped. Has he been fighting continuously?'

As expected, Tiger, with one arm blown off, charged at Yahweh2's back.

'Oh, Air God.'

The air bullet from Air God, equipped on Shirone's left arm, struck Tiger.

'Ignoring durability. A great choice. But the base damage is fixed at 10.'

Even if hit by Michelangelo, it would take hundreds of shots to destroy one piece of armor.

'Reload. He'll never run out of bullets. Wasn't the motif someone named Gaold?'

It was a vivid dream.

Yahweh2 continued to accumulate damage on Tiger's armor while jumping through codes.

'That was annoying. Of course, it won't happen twice. I was just scared then.'

Tiger's machine exploded.

"He's really good."

Though an enemy, Yahweh2's skill had improved so much that she couldn't help but praise him aloud.

"No one can catch him. Except maybe me…"

Her voice trailed off.

'I want to do it again.'

She wanted to smell the gunpowder with countless users in the world of High Gear.

"One win, one loss."

Ah, I see.

Sitting up abruptly, the Operator smiled and opened the communication channel.

"One win, one loss."

Hey! What are you doing with the communication off? Can't you see we're losing the Solar War? What should we do?

Oh! The boss is back! Don't get discouraged just because you died once! We just need to win the Solar War!

As she opened all the doors of the Solar Palace, all sorts of loud noises rushed in.

The Operator drew her black sword.

West Country first team, gather at the Solar Palace! We'll lure the East Country in and then take them down! That's our comeback!

Okay! Sounds good!

Quickly grasping the West Country's change, Aegis communicated with Yolga's son.

They're luring us. What should we do?

They plan to pick off the rankers. It's a good strategy, but not as easy as it sounds.

Golden Wheel is full of professionals. In terms of ranker skill, we surpass the West Country.

That's why the West Country was taking a gamble.

They're coming out strong. They must be confident they can take Yahweh2. Let's match them. Let's go in.

But just going along with it…

It's after-service.

Aegis immediately nodded.

Understood.

Watching the East Country's first team head to the Solar Palace, Fermi chuckled.

'Is it the burning heart of a teenage girl?'

Anyway.

"There are too many troublemakers."

Six hours into the Solar War. Leaving the battlefield behind, the top rankers clashed inside the Solar Palace.

The East Country had the upper hand in skill, but the West Country had the home ground advantage.

We can't go for a long battle. The West Country's reinforcement speed is twice that of the East Country. 20 minutes or 40 minutes. If it goes beyond that, we'll fall behind in utilizing delays.

As Yolga's son and Little Witch fought fiercely, the Operator charged in.

"Yahweh2!"

Having already cut down 13 rankers, the Operator raised her black sword towards Shirone.

'This is the end.'

From the moment they lured the enemy into the Solar Palace, the West Country had also committed fully.

"Hey, it's happening."

With all eyes in High Gear focused, Yahweh2 and the Operator collided.

"Ugh!"

With a head-on physical attack, Yahweh2's machine was pushed back at a terrifying speed.

The shadows that broke through the wall split into two and then collided again at incredible speed.

"Wow."

Host level.

Golden flashes shone, and the black magnetic field sphere deflected and spun them.

Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang!

Fist against fist, firepower against firepower, output against output, everything collided.

'She's really strong.'

They both thought the same.

'I can't win.'

After testing everything, they reached the final conclusion.

"I…"

"You…"

Activating Da Vinci, Shirone thrust his right arm, covered in strong armor, at terrifying speed.

"Lost."

The Operator.

Her bangs were blown back by the wind pressure, and his fist pierced her chest with a thud.

"How is it, Yahweh2?"

Users in front of the big screen stared intently at her revealed face for the first time.

"High Gear, isn't it fun?"

Shirone nodded sincerely.

"Yes. It's really fun."

"Hehe."

Her smiling face looked much younger than expected…

"I knew it."

She looked like an ordinary girl.
