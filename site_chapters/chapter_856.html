Chapter 856: Ugly Woman (3)

The elite 200,000-strong army of Jincheon glared at the Gustav Empire across the massive boundary line.

"I can't believe this is actually possible."

The two empires connected through the spatial distortion of Gwangcheonseong were the pinnacle of magic.

"Princess..."

The rumor spread throughout the Jincheon army.

Everyone knew that Seongeum had sacrificed herself to create this opportunity.

"Even death is not to be feared."

The reason the demons could fight without holding back was because they could be reborn in the fires of hell.

But now, they were cut off by Seongeum.

And her sacrifice beyond death had raised the morale of the Jincheon army to the level of the demon's domain.

Rian was not affiliated with any unit and stayed by Shirone's side.

"This will be a battle that will mark a new chapter in history."

From now on, the number of demons killed would be fixed as an objective force.

"But Shirone..."

Rian observed Shirone, who was glaring at Gustav with murderous intent.

"Are you really okay?"

The person Rian was looking at now was not Yahweh, who saved the world, but a magician burning with vengeance.

The anger was cold, as befits a magician, but it couldn't be said to be 100% rational.

"Let's go."

Before the commander of Jincheon, Oryongjang, could give the order, Shirone crossed the boundary line alone.

A single step that leaped over the vast sea.

Rian followed Shirone, who was casting teleportation and stretching out coolly.

"Huff! Huff!"

The sight of rushing with the law of Maha was as fast as flying in the sky.

Oryongjang, who was watching the two disappear in an instant, gave orders to the troops.

"All troops, advance!"

With a huge roar, the 200,000-strong army charged into the Gustav Empire.

"Shirone! Too fast!"

Rian, who caught up with Shirone at a speed comparable to teleportation, shouted as he looked back at Shirone.

"Damn it!"

Although he didn't expect an answer, the moment he saw Shirone's expression, Rian was convinced.

"That's not Shirone."

To be precise, it seemed like two Shirones with two different natures were overlapping.

Yahweh, who practiced love for all things.

What a human had to accept to contain that impossible state was limitless anger.

"Schizophrenia."

It might not be a pathological symptom, but without splitting one mind into two, Yahweh could not be lost.

"I see it."

From the point 100 kilometers away from Gustav's capital, Marsak, the demon army was spread out.

A demon scout who discovered Shirone and Rian fell to the unit in a parabolic arc.

And shortly after, the demon army began to scatter as if a giant ice block had boiled away in a second.

"I..."

Another voice of himself was heard in Shirone's head, which was filled with enemies.

"I wanted to be a magician."

I thought I worked hard.

"It was something I liked, and I was happy."

Of course, I also thought about succeeding as a magician, making money, and not letting my family suffer anymore.

"I'm human too."

But I knew what was really important.

"Purity."

Magic itself.

Other than wanting to do what I liked best, I had no other desires.

"But when I came out into the world... I found that just breathing and living was the hardest thing."

Things to be responsible for, things to handle.

So many cards to choose from, but none that could be discarded.

"The only thing I could discard."

Was myself.

"No matter how hard it was."

Really, the only card I could discard was myself.

"I just had to endure."

Responsibilities piled endlessly on my shoulders, and the things to handle grew like mountains...

"At some point, when I came to my senses."

I found myself shouldering all of humanity.

"Shirone! Get ready!"

At the point when the demons reached 100 meters ahead, Rian shouted, but Shirone did not respond.

"Shirone!"

He just kept moving forward, casting teleportation.

"I hope no one suffers."

Of course, there are people I dislike in the world, but I hope they are happy too.

I mean it.

"Where did this game of hot potato start?"

Why couldn't I beat Nane?

To be honest, deep down, I might have desperately wanted it.

"If this is how it's going to be, let's just end it all."

Let's blow it up.

"There's nothing to regret. If the world closes, that's it. If everything disappears, there's no hardship. Yes, let's end it here."

End it.

"Shirone!"

At the same time as Rian shouted, Shirone's face twisted fiercely.

"Ahhh!"

The sphere of light that rose in his right hand vibrated violently, emitting a white light.

"Die! Human!"

The face of the demon flying with a spear aimed was not visible to Shirone.

Just emotions, some feeling.

"Ahhh!"

A feeling of wanting to destroy immediately, terrible and hateful.

"Purity?"

The initial compassion had disappeared somewhere.

"It feels like I'm in the middle of the universe."

The planet I left behind looked as small as a star, making me doubt if it was really my starting point.

"Even losing my sense of direction."

All I had left was the faint inertia of the first step I took from the starting point.

"I can't do anything."

All I could do was hope that inertia would safely carry me to my destination.

The moment a flash of light struck the demons, an explosion blew away a radius of tens of meters.

"Damn it..."

Rian, who was hit by the shockwave, stopped and hurriedly raised his arms to cover his face.

"An explosion?"

Photon cannons are not bombs.

"It's like falling in the middle of the sea."

If you can't push the water away, you'll drown, but there's no one to help you.

"Do you know what's really scary?"

Being alone.

The fact that no one is there is so clear that you can't even scream for help.

"When you know no one is there..."

Your expression doesn't change.

A terrified face, a scream, they mean nothing.

You just struggle quietly in the silence.

Your pupils dilate, your face freezes, but strangely, your mind remains clear.

"Struggling to do something..."

It was Yahweh.

As if the space was melting, spheres of fire rose from all directions.

"Ahhh!"

Hundreds of photon cannons per second.

The only difference from before was that the photon cannon's flash exploded before reaching the target.

"Light?"

There's no way to distinguish the four types of light speed in the magical world with the human eye.

But Rian felt that Shirone's photon cannon was closer to light than before.

"Ahhh!"

Every time a scream burst from the frail magician's body, the demons burned.

Another photon cannon rose in Shirone's hand.

"Die!"

The speed of the mass-bearing flash exceeded sub-light speed and reached near-light speed.

"Die!"

Compared to the speed of the photon cannon, the particles in the atmosphere were practically stationary.

Boom!

There were no demons standing in the area melted by the high heat.

Rian, who sensed how dangerous the near-light speed photon cannon was, gritted his teeth.

"It's not a spell aimed at a target."

If it's a method that literally strikes the entire space, even civilians in the capital wouldn't be exceptions.

"Damn it!"

Rian left Shirone, who was slaughtering the demons, and flew towards Gustav's capital.

"Shirone, won't you really regret it?"

If you're not going to live forever insane, shouldn't you avoid the worst massacre?

"I won't regret it."

Even as he ruthlessly trampled the endlessly advancing demon army, his heart did not waver.

"I will end this war."

Everything was a mess, so now I couldn't even guess my position with my senses.

"But I feel it."

The emotion of the moment I took the first step still sparkled deep in my heart.

"I will return."

When this terrible war ends, I will be able to return to that pure time.

"Ataraxia."

Physical amplification.

Shirone's mental power, which passed through the colorful magic circle, expanded explosively.

Thinking of the number of demons per unit area being annihilated, Shirone felt a thrilling joy.

"The demons are decreasing."

If you kill them, they die.

The fact that they are no longer replenished was the core hope Seongeum brought to this world.

"Hmm..."

Shirone's face turned into a frown as he thought of Seongeum.

"What is this?"

Will Seongeum come back because of this?

"What is this!"

The moment the blast spread with a roar of anger, the diameter of the blast exceeded 200 meters.

Boom!

An explosion occurred with a radius of several kilometers.

Capital Marsak.

"Commander! The 200,000 troops of Jincheon are rushing towards the capital!"

"What?"

Gustav's first commander and Rian's older brother, Ozent Guy, frowned.

"What nonsense is this? How can they... with the army of hell occupying the Jincheon sea..."

Guy, who stopped talking, fell into thought.

"There's no point in figuring out the cause of what's already happened."

He immediately changed his expression, snatched the report brought by the messenger, and read it.

"Spatial manipulation?"

But how?

Although he wasn't deeply versed in magic, he had never heard of a magician who could manipulate space to the extent of folding the sea.

"Jin Seongeum."

If there was even a slight possibility, it would be her.

"How long will it take to reach the capital?"

"If we mobilize the entire army of hell, we can hold them off for at least three days."

The army of hell in the capital of Gustav made up half of the total forces.

Moreover, the presence of numerous legion commanders who could open the demon realm was a significant strength.

"Good. I will send a telegram to His Majesty. Until the general's orders arrive, launch an all-out attack."

"Urgent report! Urgent report!"

The second messenger rushed in.

"A magician, presumed to be from the Jincheon army, is approaching while annihilating the demon troops."

"A magician? How many?"

"According to the report... just one."

Guy's eyes lit up.

'It's him.'

Yahweh.

If it were the Jincheon Empire's army, it would be one thing, but with Yahweh coming, they would need to prepare with the corps commanders.

"Alright, now..."

Just as Guy was about to turn, the third messenger entered.

"Reporting! A swordsman has leaped over the demon troops and is approaching the capital."

"A swordsman this time?"

"He hasn't inflicted significant damage yet, but his approach speed is the fastest. At this rate, he will arrive within an hour."

The fact that he was leaping over the army of hell indicated he was no ordinary warrior.

"Ho? Who is it?"

"That is..."

The messenger hesitated for a moment as he looked at the report.

"The Knight of Maha."

Guy's eyebrows twitched.

"It's Ozent Rian."

Even after the report was delivered, Guy's expression did not change.

"Hmm."

After pondering for a moment, he stopped walking and looked up.

"You've come, my brother."

A moment of tender emotion flickered in Guy's eyes, which had been filled with madness.
