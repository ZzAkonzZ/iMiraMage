Chapter 1076: Raid Control (2)

West Country Free Pass?

DexGamsung's surprise was understandable.

Only the Ascension Guild had unrestricted access to all the hunting grounds occupied by West Country.

"Where did you get this?"

"I found it in Atogram. That's why I took the train to West Country. If you help me, I'll let you use it. It's not a bad deal for you either."

Shirone spoke according to the plan.

Little Witch had given him a physical item instead of an electronic card to conceal its origin.

DexGamsung examined the card again.

'If it's a physical item, finding it isn't impossible. But Yahweh2?'

It seemed too coincidental.

'There's a hidden agenda. An internal deal between Golden Wheel and Ascension? Betrayal by Ascension? Or maybe Yahweh2 is from West Country and infiltrated Golden Wheel…'

Without knowing the full context, he couldn't reach a conclusion.

'Well, it's not my concern. The issue is the West Country Free Pass. With it, we can level up in the most efficient hunting grounds. The items we get are a bonus…'

Exchanging glances with Mafia, DexGamsung turned back to Shirone.

"What are the conditions? If you give us this Free Pass, what do we have to do for you?"

"I want to get two items: Michelangelo and Donatello. If I find these, I'll give you the Free Pass."

Mafia, who had been looking incredulous, laughed.

"Haha! What nonsense is this? Michelangelo and Donatello? Do you know the odds of getting legendary items? Plus, it's a myth-level difficulty. It adjusts automatically, regardless of level. You'd be better off buying them for a fortune."

According to Fermi, there were an estimated 12 of the four legendary items in High Gear.

'Certain guilds must be monopolizing them.'

If Golden Wheel had Raphael and Da Vinci, Ascension likely had Michelangelo and Donatello.

Knowing the internal situation, they would never hand over items to East Country.

DexGamsung added, "I get what you're thinking. You want to equip all four legendary items. But that's a dream. No one in High Gear has achieved that."

"Let me hunt Ergos. If it's impossible, I'll give up."

Shirone pinned his hopes on his senses.

'As my sense of time sharpened, space opened up. In reality, the next step after time dilation was the bat.'

This place was different from reality.

But the irony was that he could open new worlds through his senses.

'Maybe…'

Even the real world might be a simulation from a distant perspective.

Focusing on the immediate goal, Shirone stopped thinking and made a practical calculation.

'Combining time dilation and the bat opened the path. In High Gear terms, it's like having programmer-level access.'

He might be able to control the raid.

"Let me do it. I won't cause any harm. Lower levels are advantageous, right?"

Myth-level hunting grounds adjusted difficulty based on average level, so being strong wasn't everything.

"…Let me discuss it."

Mafia and DexGamsung returned to their group and held a ten-minute meeting.

DexGamsung approached Shirone.

"Alright, let's try. But if there's no hope in the first round, we're quitting. We don't want to waste time."

"That's enough."

With Yahweh2 included, the eight-member party left Democracy for the hunting ground.

Mafia asked, "What's your level again?"

"Level 100."

"Then you have ten selective options unlocked. What options are you using now?"

"Exo Barrier, Hawk Eye, Booster."

Exo Barrier created a shield for one second but consumed a lot of power.

Hawk Eye provided a wide field of view with increased speed, and Booster was a rocket engine.

"Hmm. Options for levels 70, 80, and 100. Efficient for single play, but different for a party."

DexGamsung instructed, "Remove Exo Barrier and switch to Constant Speed Mode. You might run out of power while hunting."

Constant Speed Mode, activated at level 30, saved power during long-distance travel.

"Okay, I'll switch now."

"When we reach the hunting ground, remove Constant Speed Mode and add Drone Communication. If we don't know where the creatures are, the party will be wiped out."

Shirone nodded.

"For the other two slots, use Infrared Vision and the noise-reducing mode, what's it called? Ah, Silent Mode. That's best for level 100."

"Hmm, all support functions. But shouldn't I keep Turbo, just in case?"

"Turbo at level 100 isn't much. It's mainly for duels. Rankers sometimes use it in raids, but we're all above level 230. We don't expect you to deal damage."

Codename Sona interjected, "Focus on survival. If you die, the difficulty increases. Drone Communication helps us change one of our options."

"Got it. I'm the guide."

"Exactly. Honestly, it's risky. Letting a first-timer guide Ergos."

They had little to lose, so the plan proceeded.

They arrived at a massive cave, 80 meters high, carved into a natural cliff.

'Sanctuary of Ergos.'

DexGamsung said, "All High Gear creatures change patterns through genetic algorithms."

Shirone knew this from hunting Metal Rats in the Iron Homeland.

"The core is learning and evolution, but there's a mutation rate. Ordinary creatures have less than a 10% mutation rate."

Creatures didn't actually reproduce, but data was passed to the next generation.

"But Ergos has a 50% mutation rate. Each generation evolves unpredictably."

Shirone understood.

"So we don't know what's inside until we enter."

"Exactly. That's why myth-level creatures are impregnable. No fixed patterns, so no strategies. You fight purely on instinct."

Estimating the difficulty, Shirone entered the cave with the group.

The raid begins.

As the event message appeared, the scenery changed, and information from the outside world was cut off.

"Drone."

Shirone activated the drone communication, which unlocks at level 40, and launched a small device.

DexGamsung said, "Mafia will give instructions using wide-area communication. You and the other members don't have communication options, so focus whenever instructions are given."

Including communication in the selective options, which are limited to three slots, was indeed a luxury.

However, at higher levels, options that integrate various functions become available, with the most notable being Magnan, which activates at level 260 and includes most communication functions.

'That's why everyone talks about Magnan. The real game starts at level 260.'

Without Magnan, which covers drones, hounds, satellites, and both short and long-range communication, large-scale guild wars and the Solar War would be impossible.

"Let's go. Time isn't important. We can retry as many times as needed. But if you die, you'll respawn outside the cave, so minimizing mistakes is the priority."

In that sense, the guide's role was crucial.

Shirone flew the drone into the cave, guiding the party along paths with the fewest creatures.

'Avoiding combat is best.'

The average level of creatures was set to be 50 levels higher than the party's average level.

Thanks to Yahweh2, the difficulty was lowered, but it would be a disadvantage when facing Ergos.

'The boss creature is 100 levels higher.'

If even one member died, the chance of clearing the raid would approach zero, so the party was extremely cautious.

"Now."

When Mafia designated a target and gave the command, six members simultaneously attacked and destroyed the machine.

Screech!

In the nerve-wracking progress, Mafia's group realized the rumors about Yahweh2 were true.

'The tactical completeness is understandable for Golden Wheel, but how does he know the way?'

For a first-timer in Ergos, memorizing the maze-like paths was difficult.

'This way.'

Shirone was sensing the codes.

'Every code has an intention. Even if the creature's patterns evolve like living beings, from a broader perspective, it's just one network. And the shape of that network is…'

The designer's intention.

'It's not cold.'

He felt the Operator's heart.

'No matter how high the difficulty, it never reaches a zero percent chance.'

A fact the designer might not even be aware of.

'She really loves this world, High Gear. She loves the users enjoying High Gear.'

Feeling the designer's intention meant Shirone had reached a level equivalent to enlightenment in reality.

'I can do it.'

At that moment, the cave shook.

"It's here! Everyone, get ready!"

In the pitch-black darkness, undetectable even with infrared vision, a mechanical voice echoed.

Who dares to invade the sanctuary of the gods!

Mafia gave instructions via wide-area communication, and Sona launched a barrage of flashbangs.

A bright light exploded.

Revealed was a massive eyeball.

Insignificant creatures!

And four steel legs extending from the eye, gripping the cave.

"It's close to Type C! Focus on mobility and evasion!"

As soon as the command was given, steel-clawed tentacles flew from behind Ergos.

Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang!

The eight-member party scattered, and small creatures emerged from the cave's crevices.

A fierce battle began.

The key was to chase Ergos, which retreated by striking the cave walls with its four legs, and land hits.

"Damn! It's too fast!"

Creatures interfered, and the skeleton, except for the eyeball, took no damage.

Then Shirone charged in alone.

"Idiot! Follow the instructions…!"

The words stopped as Shirone dodged dozens of tentacles and rushed towards Ergos.

"How…?"

Information about Yahweh2's special abilities regarding time and space was abundant on the bulletin board.

But the path Yahweh2 took now was entirely different from jumping through space.

'How does he know the pattern?'

Moreover, his movements reminded them of a specific user they had watched with awe on broadcast screens.

"Operator…"

Graaah!

As Shirone closed the distance, Ergos fired a beam from its eye.

DexGamsung gaped.

'The pattern has already changed.'

Normally, it took 20 minutes just to chase Ergos to the end of the cave.

'We can do this.'

With newfound confidence, he shouted, "Let's go! Attack!"

As the seven followed, destroying creatures, Shirone pondered.

'Why the eye?'

The evolutionary form created within a 50% mutation rate was an eye.

'Because it's the most suitable form for receiving photon signals. Then…'

Why is it called a god?

Shirone had an intuition.

The secret of the outside world he longed for might be here.

"Yahweh2! Now! Destroy it!"

After a 7-kilometer chase, Shirone reached Ergos.

'Not yet.' Destroying Ergos's eye here wouldn't yield Michelangelo.

A near-certain feeling.

'Probability is mathematics, but…'

What determines that probability is the mind, an extremely uncertain quantum signal.

Shirone's eyes widened.

'Now!'

This spot was Michelangelo.

As Suicide's melee weapon pierced the eye, Ergos convulsed as if electrocuted.

Screeeech!

Amid the deafening roar shaking the cave, Mafia's group checked the augmented reality information.

Unidentified item drop: Legendary grade.

"…Unbelievable."

It was a 0.001 percent chance.
