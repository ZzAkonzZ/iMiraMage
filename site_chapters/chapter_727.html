Chapter 727:

As the seconds ticked by, the lucky slot began to transform into a new card.

"I need to make a quick decision."

Time spent thinking meant that the roulette couldn't spin forever.

"This is harder than the Scramble Royal."

Back then, it was just a matter of picking one from black or white, but WhiteBlack had many more types of cards.

"I only have one chance."

Even if CPR appeared, everything would be in vain if the inertia triggered the explosion.

"This is it!"

As the light peeled off the card, the faint letters "CPR" appeared next to the S-rank card.

"Stop! Stop! Stop!"

As the thoughts accelerated, a signal shot up the spine and struck the brain.

"Phew!"

That was one second.

"This can't be…"

In that brief moment, the participants from White saw Shirone's card flashing wildly.

"How did you do that?"

A warm CPR card, like freshly baked bread, was in Shirone's hand.

Breeze swallowed hard.

"It really came out."

It was clear that some kind of magic was manipulating the odds.

"Take back the remaining cards."

When Shirone extended the card, the four participants flinched and looked at each other.

Even Ballad, who would normally act immediately, couldn't move in front of Shirone.

"He's not just a magician."

Like the true meaning of the word "magic," nothing seemed impossible for him.

"Distribute them as you see fit."

Shirone handed the cards to Breeze.

"Me?"

She was the only one who had shown gratitude when the law of harmony was invoked on behalf of the White zone.

"I don't need the cards anyway. By the way, can this CPR be used here?"

Atria shook her head.

"I don't know. I've only heard about it, never seen it. We weren't trained like the Black zone…"

Breeze interrupted.

"Weapon cards have a defined activation area. It's probably a circle with a diameter of about 2 meters centered on the card."

Without an activation area, there would be bizarre battles fought with cards from one end of Anarchy Mountain to the other.

"Then we need to go back to Kai."

As Shirone turned, Breeze spoke.

"Can I come with you?"

As the other three participants looked puzzled, Shirone said,

"I'm Black. As long as Kai is in Black, I have no intention of changing the law. You'll have to fight me eventually."

"It's not about the survival test. I need another CPR card. If you delay saving Kai until the last moment, I'll find a way. I'll get a card that can be duplicated somehow…"

Marco frowned.

"Hey, what are you talking about? CPR? And that guy is Black. Do you think he'll do that?"

"How do you know that?"

"Ugh!"

As Marco shrank under the sharp gaze, Shirone asked Breeze again.

"Why do you need CPR?"

For a seeker who had already realized the contradiction of WhiteBlack, distinguishing people by law was meaningless.

"I can't reveal that."

Despite the card's life-related effect, Breeze bit her lip and shook her head.

"Is it a secret worth risking your life for?"

In any case, if she couldn't reveal the reason, Shirone couldn't help her.

"I can't accept that proposal. Sorry."

Ignoring Breeze's sad expression, Shirone firmly cast a teleportation spell.

"It can't be helped. Saving Kai comes first."

Arriving at the remembered location, Shirone immediately looked for the tree where Kai's body was placed.

"What?"

The body had disappeared without a trace.

"It was definitely here."

If the teleportation had been successful, there was no chance of mistaking the location.

"Are you looking for Kai?"

Hearing a voice from the bushes, Shirone turned with a photon cannon ready.

"Come out."

"Wait! Calm down! I'm not here to fight!"

The man who appeared was short, with large glasses, in his mid-thirties.

"A weapon card ability?"

Otherwise, there was no explanation for why he hadn't been detected by the Spirit Zone until now.

"I know what you're thinking."

The man said.

"My job is a detective. My special ability is stealth. When I'm not moving, no one can sense me."

"Where is Kai?"

"I want to make a deal."

The man took out a weapon card.

"Windbag (Function)."

Description: The better the performance, the longer the afterglow lasts.

Effect: Can replay sounds that occurred within a specific area within 2 hours.

"A perfect card for a detective. I found Kai's body and used Windbag. I heard what happened here. I know you met with the participants from the White zone. You came here, so you got CPR, right?"

"What do you want?"

"So far, we're on the same side. We're both Black. But you're a seeker, and you might join them at any time."

It was now a given in WhiteBlack that Shirone's side would win.

"So I want to propose. Bring me all the cards from the White participants. Then I'll give you Kai."

He wanted to say to kill them all, but pushing too hard would backfire.

"As you know, cards have activation areas. If I don't reveal where Kai is hidden… Ugh!"

Shirone's gaze froze.

"Why should I accept that proposal? I can just ask you here and now."

No weapon card could subdue Shirone.

"You should think carefully. Kai is…"

As Shirone's eyes widened, the man pulled out a weapon card.

"Fool! You'll regret this!"

"Old Position (Consumable)."

Description: Everyone has moments when they think, "Why did I do that?"

Effect: Returns to the position from 1 hour ago.

"Activate! Old Position!"

The man's voice echoed through the mountains.

"…"

The man's eyes shook with shock as he looked at his empty hand.

"How…?"

He had only seen a flash of light.

"Not just anyone can do it."

Turning around in a daze, he saw Shirone waving the Old Position card she had snatched from him.

Snatching a card while moving with teleportation was a skill only high-level magicians could perform.

"You have to concentrate until your head feels like it's going to explode. So I'm in a very bad mood right now."

At Shirone's most terrifying threat, the man backed off with an awkward smile.

"Ahaha, well…"

"Where is Kai?"

"Damn it!"

A being standing outside the law of WhiteBlack.

That was the definition of a magician the man realized.

Labyrinth Andre.

In the landscape of the inner world, where structures like the hearts of creatures were connected like fruits…

"Phew."

Shirone was sitting cross-legged.

"Is it still far?"

Shirone's mind, which had to maintain the Wheel of Emptiness, never lost focus for a moment.

"It feels like it's been about six days."

The moment the entrance to Andre, guarded by Kido, was breached, Shirone activated the bat and hid in the inner world.

"Is the battle over?"

Currently, he was about 10 meters underground in Andre, based on the real world.

It was like being submerged in deep water, with outside sounds being indirect, but now even that was inaudible.

"I feel like I'm going to die of frustration."

Shirone in this place couldn't know the emotions felt by the 19,000 Shirones in each world.

But it was clear that some intuition beyond logic kept flooding through the circuit of insight.

"If I stay like this, I'll go crazy."

Every time complex emotions, not clearly defined by language, accumulated, the frustration grew worse.

"This is the first time for something like this."

Even when developing God's Punishment, Shirone hadn't felt this stifled and had eventually broken through.

"Is this Andre? Surprisingly, it's nothing special."

Hearing a voice from above, Shirone quickly looked up.

"Who is it? Kido?"

Getting up and following the slope of the inner world to the surface, Shirone saw unfamiliar figures.

"Orientals?"

Judging by their appearance and attire, they were people from the East, with dozens of soldiers following the woman.

"Amazing. That gait…"

From a magician's perspective, the distance from the leading woman was precise to the inch.

"Your Highness, I don't sense any human presence. Could Shirone have already finished exploring the labyrinth?"

"They know me."

At the words of a man who looked like a master at a glance, Shirone climbed the slope of the inner world.

Finally, as the view of the real world's surface aligned, he could see them clearly.

"Foolish, Mun Kyung."

Sung Eum's group had crossed from Jessica to Andre in one leap by pulling space to the extreme.

Sung Eum's large eyes moved slowly, clearly visible to Shirone 20 meters away.

"Just because it doesn't register on your senses doesn't mean it doesn't exist. Assassins also use the blind spots of intuition, don't they?"

Mun Kyung knew that assassins used the Equalizing ability to blend with objects and hide their presence.

But it was an absurd statement for a warrior protecting the princess of the Jincheon Empire.

"Your Highness, I'm not that dull."

How many assassins in the world could deceive the senses of someone granted three steps by the strongest sorcerer, Jin Sung Eum?

Sung Eum glared and said,

"Then tell me. Can you guess the color of my heart right now? If you get it right, I'll reward you."

It was a reward he truly wanted, but Mun Kyung could only tilt his head in confusion.

"I don't understand what you mean."

"Really? I'll show you myself."

Turning forward, Sung Eum slowly raised her hand.

"Reveal yourself, one who dwells in my heart."

It was as if a new landscape had appeared in the space where reality had been sliced away, like looking at a cross-section of a fruit.

"Who are you? Reveal yourself!"

The members of Sambo, who had discovered Shirone, simultaneously drew their swords and took a fighting stance.

But they still couldn't comprehend what they were seeing.

'No way. How could there be a person there…?'

If the blond boy had been an assassin, anyone other than Sambo would have been helpless.

"Choeni Bardo, one who walks the boundary between life and death."

Jin Sung Eum instantly grasped the esoteric truth contained in Shirone's technique.

"Are you Arian Shirone?"

Sung Eum's voice, more excited than usual, was a shock to Mun Kyung.

'Is Her Highness… nervous?'

Shirone's lips trembled slightly as he smiled.

'I see. This is the level of an Ivory Tower candidate.'

Even without any explanation, the mere act of separating reality from the inner world was enough to confirm it.

"Jin Sung Eum."

It was the first encounter between the candidates for the Ivory Tower.
