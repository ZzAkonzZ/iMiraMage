Chapter 1070: East Country and West Country (5)

"The closer Yahweh2 got, the more Chaos5MinutesBefore's expression twisted.

'What is this? What's going on?'

He was used to bullying beginners in the neutral zone and had his own manual for it.

Most beginners had strong pride, so after a few provocations, they would charge in like Yahweh2.

Even if they attacked first, the moment they retaliated, both parties would become hostile according to High Gear's system.

In that state, if he eliminated the opponent, he could loot them, even if they were from a different faction.

The loot was random, either 10% of their galaxies or a specific part.

For a high-level player with trillions, 10% of galaxies would be a jackpot, but Chaos5MinutesBefore enjoyed getting parts from beginners.

And now, he thought he could easily prey on these newbies who had just equipped their beginner packages.

'But…'

Even with High Gear's output at maximum, Yahweh2 didn't get hit once.

'How is this happening?'

Chaos5MinutesBefore was bewildered by the unexpected situation, and his evasion probability finally dropped below 20%.

"You bastard!"

Shirone shouted, raising his arm, and energy bullets pounded Chaos5MinutesBefore's body.

"Ugh!"

Of course, due to his high durability, a few hits wouldn't kill him immediately.

'This is strange. It's a bug. A system error.'

Chaos5MinutesBefore felt a chill.

It was like a cuckoo clock popping out at the wrong time, something too familiar to notice until it went wrong.

'As the distance closes, evasion probability drops. But the same goes for him.'

The battle continued.

Shirone dodged through the barrage, landing several hits on Chaos5MinutesBefore.

Sparks flew from his body, and the durability of the damaged parts appeared in augmented reality.

'Heh. Heh heh.'

Durability 482/500.

Though momentarily flustered by the abnormal situation, the numerical difference in power was clear.

'His weapon lacks power. In close combat, I'm much more advantageous.'

And one more thing.

'I have this.'

Activating the level 20 selective option, 'Turbo,' his output surged.

"Die!"

Using energy bullets, which were affected by output, his firepower more than doubled.

"Ugh!"

As the barrage speed increased, Shirone wrinkled his nose and heightened his concentration.

Time began to slow…

'A specific pattern.'

The cluster of round flashes, like dandelions, became clear.

'There.'

It was geometry unfolding in space-time, and Shirone quickly found and exploited the gap.

Chaos5MinutesBefore's heart sank.

"Ahhh!"

Unable to endure the anxiety, he sprayed bullets wildly, but it only made things easier for Shirone.

Finally, as Shirone closed in, Chaos5MinutesBefore shouted.

"Alright! Let's see who wins!"

Bang!

Chaos5MinutesBefore's eyes shook, and his companions' expressions turned blank.

Instead of continuing the firefight, Yahweh2 had punched Chaos5MinutesBefore in the face with his other hand.

"Ugh!"

The unexpected blow knocked Chaos5MinutesBefore down, and Shirone pinned him to the ground.

With the gun barrel aimed at his head, Shirone saw the fear in the man's eyes.

Silence fell.

Destroyer707 muttered, "Why isn't he shooting?"

"Maybe it's his first time killing someone? He seems sensitive about that," someone suggested.

Shirone asked, "Why did you attack us?"

"What nonsense? Ugh!"

Chaos5MinutesBefore tried to sit up, but Shirone pressed him down with his foot.

"Apologize to Death Princess. Hurting others for no reason is wrong."

'What's this now?'

Chaos5MinutesBefore was baffled.

'Is this a concept? No, I can still win. My durability is much higher.'

Feigning surrender, he spread his arms.

"Alright, I'll apologize. Just let me go. I won't bother you again."

Death Princess shouted, "Don't believe him! He's lying!"

As Shirone remained still, Chaos5MinutesBefore aimed his gun upwards.

"Of course, you fool."

As he tried to fire, he saw the cold look in Shirone's eyes.

'What?'

Killing intent.

A thought flashed quickly, and Shirone's voice sounded surreal.

"Penetrate."

He was already a dead man.

"Wait, wait…!"

Bang!

Shirone's shoulder twitched, and the energy bullet shattered Chaos5MinutesBefore's head.

"Gah… gah!"

The headshot made the machine convulse and then stop with a mechanical sound.

"Hey, Yahweh2."

Destroyer707 and his group, who had been running over, stopped.

'What's with this atmosphere?'

Shirone's serious demeanor as he looked down at the machine was too intense to interrupt.

Strongest Codename said, "Chaos5MinutesBefore's durability was around 500. It would take a long time for Shirone's weapon to penetrate. He must have calculated that. He didn't seem to care about the person, just the calculation."

Though they hadn't known him long, they had a rough idea of Yahweh2's nature.

"Probably. But…" Death Princess said, "He looks so sad."

Who was he?

What kind of life did he live in reality to feel sadness even in a simulation?

Shirone smiled and turned his head.

"We won. Is everyone okay?"

As the heavy atmosphere lifted, Destroyer707 and his group finally moved.

"That was amazing. Dodging even energy bullets. Are you a combat genius?"

"I was lucky. If Chaos5MinutesBefore had been smarter, it would have been a tough fight."

"What are you talking about? It was a perfect victory."

Shirone reviewed the battle.

"If it were me, I would have retreated while fighting. With the old joints, you can keep an eye on the back while sprinting forward. It takes advantage of high output. He probably didn't think that far in the heat of the moment. That's why I managed to take him down."

It was a difference in judgment.

"Underestimating the enemy, thinking there's no way I could lose—these things greatly affect the outcome of a battle. In the end, he was just a high-level opponent, not a particularly strong one."

Destroyer707 and his group thought of the rankers.

"The AI system allows even combat novices to enjoy all the content. That's its big advantage, but…"

That alone doesn't make a ranker. Rankers are those who understand and thoroughly exploit the system, not just rely on it.

Dodging energy bullets was a secondary issue compared to that.

"He's learning incredibly fast. Even under the same conditions, we'd lose now."

They didn't feel particularly bad about it.

"Well, he is a friend of Yolga's Son."

At that moment, Shirone pointed to the ground and asked, "There's something here?"

"Loot!"

Death Princess clapped her hands.

"If you die while hostile, you can get loot. No one else can see it until you pick it up. What is it? What dropped?"

Shirone picked up the part on the ground.

"This? It's the right arm that guy was using. Maron, Bolt Gun-Z204 model."

Destroyer707 burst into laughter.

"Haha! He dropped his most expensive part. Serves him right. He's probably fuming right now."

"Can't it be restored? What about Death Princess's left arm?"

"No. Loot or parts with zero durability are permanently lost upon death. It's okay. I can buy my left arm as a single part from the shop."

"Hmm, so what should we do with this? Death Princess can't use it since it's a right arm. Should we sell it for galaxies?"

Strongest Codename said, "That's possible, but not recommended. The shop charges high fees. And it's not a popular model, so selling it to users isn't ideal. Mass-produced parts usable at level 20 have their limits."

Destroyer707 said, "Just use it yourself. That's the best option. Let's find an engineer in Atogram. We need to replenish ammo anyway."

"Okay. But an engineer?"

"Shop items can be replaced immediately, but user-developed parts or loot need an engineer to install. Large guilds even hire dedicated engineers."

"So that's what you meant by 'you'll experience it eventually.'"

"Exactly. Shop parts are good, but legendary or artisan items are obtained through hunting or duels. Don't worry too much. A right arm isn't that weird."

Strongest Codename added, "Personally, eye replacements are the most annoying."

"Agreed."

Back in Atogram, they bought Death Princess's left arm and energy bullets from the Maron shop.

Next, they looked for an engineer.

"Shops have engineers, but they're expensive. It's better to hire a user."

"Yeah, let's save money."

Even if they weren't short on galaxies, Shirone preferred to learn the basics.

Entering the shop of 'Hermes,' a famous engineer in Atogram, they saw a familiar face.

"Huh?"

Chaos5MinutesBefore was there with his companions, equipping a newly bought bolt gun.

Shirone tensed up, but Chaos5MinutesBefore didn't act aggressively like in Destroy.

Destroyer707 smirked.

"Got yourself a new part? Still went with a bolt gun, huh? Looks good."

"You… think this is over?"

Shirone checked the beginner tips.

Combat wasn't possible in the city unless a special event was happening.

"Hmm, I see."

Chaos5MinutesBefore's companions gestured.

"Hey, are those the guys?"

"Yeah. Remember their faces. Pick East Country, so we can have a proper fight."

Shirone noted their codenames.

'Chaos1MinuteBefore. Chaos2MinutesBefore…'

A machine with a round body and thick, long arms like a gorilla approached Shirone.

It was Chaos1MinuteBefore.

"Yahweh2, don't meet me on the battlefield. I'll crush you every time."

'Amara integrated type. Over level 70.'

As his group tensed, Chaos1MinuteBefore walked out the door.

"Let's go."

After finishing his repairs, Chaos5MinutesBefore left, and Shirone replaced his right arm.

The engineer said it should be fine with his output, at least for one part.

When they stepped outside, it was already getting dark.

"Shall we head to Freedom Square? It's annoying when those Chaos guys gang up."

Following Destroyer707, they arrived at Freedom Square, which was bustling with people.

Many users were selling items, and some were scouting for guild members.

"When is he coming?"

As they checked the time in augmented reality, the square's entrance became noisy.

"Wow! A ranker!"

Shirone's group turned their heads.

Leading a crowd was a petite woman, about 160 centimeters tall.

Pale face, light purple hair covering half her back, and bangs cut horizontally at the eyebrow line.

There was no need to check her codename.

"It's Aegis."

She was ranked 8th in the duel rankings, highly regarded by users.

"Why is Aegis in Atogram? Golden Wheel members usually hang out in Nascashi."

"Yeah. Is there an event I don't know about?"

Without glancing around, Aegis walked straight towards Shirone.

Destroyer707 swallowed nervously.

'Her machine is amazing. How much does that cost? We'll never afford it.'

Finally, Aegis stopped and carefully checked Shirone's codename.

"Yahweh2…"

"Um, who are you?"

Aegis, with her arms at her sides, bowed.

"Nice to meet you. I've come on the boss's orders. Please follow me. I'll escort you."

Freedom Square fell into a huge silence.
