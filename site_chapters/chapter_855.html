Chapter 855: Ugly Woman (2)

Anchal couldn’t hide her emotions.

"I know it's unreasonable. But what other choice do we have? Maybe… we can turn this around."

It was the first glimmer of hope.

"There's definitely a chance if we close the spiritual realm… The army of Hell is just numerous, that’s all."

Shirone was lost in thought.

"It's unforgivable. This will only make the demons stronger. But this time..."

He would be forever trapped in Seongeum's mind.

"Shirone, this is different from the choice you made in the capital of Kashan. With this, the demons will be sealed away forever. Send the princess away. You have your own battles to fight." If they couldn’t conquer the Gustav Empire, they wouldn’t be able to push back the demons in the central continent.

"Amy."

His family in Tormia would die.

"I’ll go to Seongeum."

How could this be acceptable?

"You all have lost your reason. It's not just death; it's being trapped in Hell forever. Could you do it?"

"Yes! I’ve lost my reason!"

Anchal shouted.

"Being calm in this situation is the real madness. Not everyone is as resolute as you. You’re the crazy one! We are the sane ones!"

Really?

Facing the crisis of a loved one, ignoring the death of his family to save Seongeum—is that madness?

Anchal grabbed Shirone by the collar.

"Just stay put. Just… don't think about anything. It will all be over soon. You can think after it's over."

"Let go of me."

Shirone's eyes turned icy.

"If you don't want to see what real madness is."

Chilled by the murderous intent that seemed to slice through the air, Anchal released her grip.

"… Do you really have to go?" Even Anchal felt a surge of murderous intent.

"I’m going."

"It's because of you that everything will be destroyed. Because of you, all of humanity will suffer. It’s all because of you!"

There was no rebuttal.

"Seongeum's choice is the strongest move. Certainly, I couldn't produce a better outcome than her."

He was practicing ultimate love.

"Such beautiful words."

Everyone dreams of such a world, but what comes back to Shirone is the blame of all humanity.

"Nane."

At the crossroads of a decision without a single ally, ironically, Nane came to mind.

"What would you have done?"

It seemed he could hear Nane’s answer.

"Yes, I’m right. But…"

Tears streamed down Shirone’s face as he looked up.

"You can never be wrong."

It's not that it's right.

It’s because it’s not wrong that he protects this world until the end.

"Come to your senses, Anchal."

Shirone gritted his teeth.

"What more explanation do you want? What philosophy, what belief, what emotions can justify…"

The murderous intent disappeared.

"Saving Seongeum is the right thing."

"I'll kill you."

Anchal removed her eyepatch and unleashed her demonic vision.

"For the sake of the Jincheon Empire, I'll…" His voice choked off.

A sinister glow emitted from her demonic eyes as blood trickled down.

"Save her… please, our poor lady…"

At the crossroads of decision, everyone knew what had to be done.

Anchal knelt down and wept.

"Save the lady, please…" As she finished speaking, Shirone’s body transformed into a streak of light and flew towards the grand hall.

"Activating Amplifier No. 12! Spatial intercept system Guangchunseong is ready!"

In the grand hall, a dark cylindrical device was set up.

Wires connected in all directions, and blue electricity sparked from the walls.

As the doors of the device named Guangchunseong opened, there was just enough space for one person to enter.

All officials of Jincheon were present, but none could meet Seongeum’s eyes.

"Father."

Only Emperor Jinkang of Jincheon, having chosen eternal hell for his daughter, looked down at her.

"Ensure our victory."

Sitting askew, resting his head, Jinkang remained silent.

"Don’t be afraid."

Knowing the terrible outcome, Seongeum no longer left any warmth behind and stepped coldly forward.

As she entered Guangchunseong, the door clunked shut.

Once Seongeum fully opens the Immortal Function, Guangchunseong will precisely control the space of the entire planet.

This state would close the spiritual realm and reduce the distance between Gustav and the Jincheon Empire to zero through the sea, enabling a surprise attack on Gustav’s capital via land.

"The princess has activated the Immortal Function."

Officials of Jincheon Space Agency received the signal from Guangchunseong and reported.

"Not a second of hesitation."

Her father was proud of her, and for the first time, emotion appeared on Jinkang’s face.

"Your Majesty…"

That emotion, his monstrously twisted face, belonged to the most enraged man in the world.

"Spare none of their bones."

Kill them, kill them, and kill again.

To his daughter, wandering forever in hell, he would keep sending the terrible corpses of demons.

"Mental level rising! 60%! 70%!"

As reports continued, the doors of the grand hall burst open, and Shirone entered.

"Seongeum??????!"

Glancing around swiftly, Shirone saw no sign of Seongeum, only the dark cylinder.

"Damn!"

It wasn’t too late yet.

As Shirone dashed towards Guangchunseong and was about to exert force on the cylinder.

"What are you doing?!"

Jinkang shouted.

At the same time, the commanders of Jincheon’s army, the Five Dragons, surrounded Shirone with their weapons drawn.

"Stand back. Even if it’s you, we’ll have to kill you."

Shirone glared at Jinkang.

"Stop this. Please. Don’t create a regret that will last a lifetime."

Time was of the essence, so his words were brief, but the emotion behind them was immense.

"You will regret this."

Jinkang stood up.

"I will, and so will Seongeum, until the moment of death. No, Seongeum can’t even die."

"So what?"

Jinkang’s eyes emitted an abnormal frenzy.

"What does regret matter? Accept it. It’s what I and my daughter are enduring!"

"You’re insane."

"That doesn’t matter either."

Jinkang extended his hand.

"Drag him out."

The commander of the Five Dragons, Yodal, swung his weapon, the Great Pole, and charged at Shirone.

'No time to waste.'

Using time vibration to take down Yodal in the shortest distance possible.

Bang!

At that moment, the ceiling crumbled, and Rian landed beside Shirone, twisting his waist.

"Huh!"

The great sword swung horizontally, and the four greatest masters of Jincheon retreated simultaneously.

Rumbling!

At a height of three meters, lines carved into the walls on all sides as dust rose.

The entire grand hall was sliced through, but due to perfect horizontality, it didn’t collapse.

The ceiling was supported, and the power and precision rapidly broke the fighting spirit of the Five Dragons.

"The Knight of Maha."

Shirone shouted.

"Rian!"

"I don’t know what’s going on, but just do it. I’ll handle it."

Without knowing the details, but if Shirone had chosen it, there was no doubt.

"The Immortal Function is fully open!" An employee of Jincheon Space Agency shouted the moment Shirone approached Guangchunseong.

"Seongeum! Wait! No!"

A rumbling noise decreased as Guangchunseong’s operation slowed.

Officials turned their heads in shock, and for a moment, rationality returned to Jinkang’s face.

"Seongeum…"

As Shirone spoke, a blue light burst forth from the completely sealed Guangchunseong.

"No!"

An official of Jincheon Space Agency shouted.

"Starting Guangchunseong! We will seal the psychic barrier and connect the territories of Gustav and Jincheon!"

"My lady…"

The belatedly arrived Anchal knelt down, and the Five Dragons also bowed their heads in sorrow.

"We will not forget the princess’s sacrifice."

Rian shouted.

"Shirone! Is it over?"

Of course not.

"I’ll be back!"

Once the Immortal Function is fully open, returning to reality is impossible, but…

'I can’t let it end this way!'

Shirone was an infinite mage.

As the light of Yahweh spread fiercely, Shirone’s mind expanded into the infinite realm.

Information intertwined massively, but Shirone did not lose sight of Seongeum.

'There!'

In the state of a spiritual body, there was no standard to identify friend or foe.

But if expressed in Shirone’s feelings, he was certain he had grabbed Seongeum’s hand heading towards the underworld.

"Don’t go."

At the fiery gateway to hell, Seongeum slowly turned around.

"You came, Shirone. That’s like you."

"There’s no need for this. No matter how much the world resembles hell, it’s wrong for someone to end up like this."

Seongeum shook her head.

"It’s too late. I’m not an infinite mage. And… I have no intention of returning."

"I won’t let go."

Shirone gripped Seongeum’s hand even tighter.

"I will definitely take you with me."

It felt like just a sensation occurring at the mental level, but Seongeum seemed to smile.

"You, Nane, and I."

The three competitors with the highest Kars.

"Each had a belief worthy of respect. But, Shirone, I believe that perseverance isn't everything."

Shirone's mind trembled.

"What do you mean by that? Don't do this. Let's go back and talk about it."

Although he felt like she was holding Seong-eum's hand, in reality, he wasn't holding anything.

"I hope you don't hate Nane too much. You and him understand the world better than anyone."

"Okay. I'll do that if you come back. If I let you go like this, the people left behind won't be able to survive."

"That's not true."

As long as hell cannot be eradicated, Buddha and Yahweh will be in eternal conflict.

"If the three of us could drink tea and chat..."

Seong-eum's presence began to fade.

"...I used to think it would be nice." Shirone desperately held on.

"Wait. Please."

Seong-eum, who had disintegrated to the point of being unrecognizable, slipped away from Shirone's mind.

"No!"

Seong-eum's last words were heard.

‘I respect you, Shirone.’

Her purity, unable to convey her true feelings until the end, made Shirone even angrier.

Feeling Seong-eum fall into hell, Shirone focused all his reason on one point.

"Ahhh!"

Shirone, who regained consciousness in the infinite realm, grimaced and bent over.

"Ahhh! Ahhh!"

Anger.

As uncontrollable anger exploded, the light of Yahweh surrounding Shirone began to burn black.

"That, that..."

Anchal's face turned pale.

'The incarnation of Yahweh is disappearing.'

Clearly, the only thing left in Shirone was immense anger, a distinctly human emotion.

"...The spiritual realm?" Although the voice was low, the official of the Jincheon Space Agency was so terrified that his vision blurred.

"It's closing."

"Let's go, Rian."

Rian, with his great sword slung over his shoulder, asked.

"Where to?"

"Gustav."

Shirone's eyes glared fiercely ahead.

"I'll kill them all."

The cold words of the ruthless magician who had lost all compassion froze the air in the hall.

At the moment everyone felt the cold aura, one image came to mind.

'Monster.'

Contrary to expectations, it wasn't violent.

'It's waiting.'

It was just smiling coldly at the world, as if pleased that the prison door had opened.
