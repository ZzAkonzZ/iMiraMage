Chapter 685: Offering Flowers (5)

The power of the high-speed armor-piercing rounds was immense, capable of piercing ancient armor with sheer kinetic energy, but Lupist felt uneasy.

Seonghwa needed to be handed over to the kingdom intact.

‘No reaction.’

Behind the iron barrier, now riddled with holes, nothing could be sensed.

‘He opened the floor and escaped.’

As Lupist expected, Florino reappeared through an iron plate in a completely different location.

“Let me show you exactly what your arrogance has wrought.”

The floor beneath Lupist rippled like water, forming a massive steel sphere that trapped him.

“Are you kidding me?”

A steel prison for the Steel Mage?

Just as he was about to smash it, he felt the inertia of a fall stronger than gravity.

‘So this is his strategy.’

It was the power of Small World Creation, controlling not just Seonghwa’s systems but its very matter.

The steel sphere fell endlessly through the holes it had created, finally crashing to the ground with a thud.

“Dodge!”

As the sphere suddenly fell from the ceiling, Shirone grabbed Maylay and leaped away.

Kido, already far away, blinked in surprise as the sphere exploded, revealing Lupist.

‘Blocked.’

Checking the ceiling, Lupist clicked his tongue.

“This is getting annoying.”
