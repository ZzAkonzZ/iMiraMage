Chapter 970: Simultaneous Events (5)

Bashuka Palace.

As the royals clutched their heads in terror, the palace guards stirred.

"Ugh...!"

The Wrath of Yahweh.

A red light of Miracle Stream was dyeing the spacious room blood-red.

"It, it wasn't my order."

When Adolf 13th made eye contact with Shirone, his legs gave out and he slumped to the floor.

Shirone's face was unflinched, yet it felt like seeing a ghost.

"Then who ordered it?"

"My younger brother, Robins. Please believe me. I didn’t know anything until he mentioned it."

Adolf 13th, having been severely punished for lying in the past, chose his words carefully, speaking only the truth.

"My brother."

Shirone knew that Robins, like Ténar, had a cruel and cold nature.

'Even worse, he's trying to kill his own kin. And Gunjo Bedium.'

Shirone had known since school that Lycon's father was the strongest hitman in the Black Line.

But the Bedium Shirone had assessed through Omega was far beyond his expectations.

'The reputation as the strongest hitman is just the tip of the iceberg. He’s roamed the world, assassinating two kings alone, and about 47 high-ranking officials.'

Such numbers were merely part of the history Bedium had achieved before Shirone was even born.

'The world hasn't turned upside down because his backers have never been exposed.'

It reminded him of the saying that the best assassins are those unknown.

"Where is the prince now?" If Robins was connected to Bedium, it was crucial to find him immediately.

"I don't know. Truly."

This time, Adolf 13th didn't close his eyes, and Shirone attempted quantum transmission.

'It failed.'

Quantum transmission, despite its powerful feature called simultaneous events, had its drawbacks.

'If I know the coordinates of time and space, spatial teleportation can always be successful.'

Because photon signals are cold.

'But the heart is different. To exist simultaneously in another space, the observer's heart must intervene. And the heart is unstable and uncertain.'

For quantum transmission to succeed, Shirone's presence needs to be recognized 100 percent.

It's not a difficult condition.

With family, even with less than 30 percent information, Shirone could be confidently perceived.

'But those like Robins, with a priori information, can't materialize me.'

That was why most of the Twelve Apostles were previously dispatched to strategic points.

The royals glanced around.

'What's he thinking?'

Despite hearing the plan to assassinate Pony, it was unclear why he still remained in place.

Shirone muttered with pity in his eyes.

"... Stupid kid." Adolf 13th twitched, but Shirone's gaze was directed beyond the ceiling.

"Huff, huff!"

Suppressing the terror of death, Pony watched the light rising over Lycon's shoulder.

'Shirone. Shirone...'

As electricity coursed through her throat, her nerves spasmed, causing her limbs to twitch uncontrollably.

"What are you looking at? It’s been a while since we met, shouldn't you at least make eye contact before dying?"

Contrary to his words, Lycon's eyes were observing Pony as if seeing her for the first time.

"I thought you were a decent woman," said Lycon as he forcefully pressed down on Pony’s neck and drew a sickle.

"Well, that's life."

Following the cold metallic friction, the sickle plunged towards Pony’s brainstem.

"Choke!"

Just before feeling the handle, a powerful impact hit Lycon’s back, throwing him against the wall.

"Who’s there?"

Crouching like a beast and pulling on his sickle, he saw a familiar face.

"Oh, shit..."

It was the second most hated face in the world for him, and with added disgust, it could be the first.

"Pony, are you okay?"

Shirone, ignoring Lycon’s gaze, approached Pony and helped her sit up.

"How, how did this happen?"

Though her body was still in shock, the confusion was evident on her face, now regaining her senses.

"Let’s get out of here. Can you stand?" As sparks flew ahead, and Lycon swung his arm behind Shirone.

Just before the sickle could pierce his neck, Miracle Stream completely enveloped his arm.

"Ugh...!"

As if forgetting how to exert force, no matter how hard he tried, his muscles wouldn't move.

Shirone stood up.

"Lycon."

Lycon’s arm moved on its own, placing the blade deep against his own throat.

"Kiiii! Kiiii!"

Lycon shrieked like a snake threatening Shirone, but his expression remained unchanged.

"It’s been a while."

The snake-like noise abruptly stopped.

"You're still annoying. Still caught up in your righteousness, wandering around?"

"Yeah. Sorry."

The days of arguing over whose philosophy was right were long past, so he went straight to the point.

"Where’s your father, Bedium, now?"

Lycon struggled to break the Miracle Stream, but no effort was effective.

"Damn it. Did it really come to this?"

Despite being trained by the world's strongest hitman, his father, the gap in their abilities was too great.

"This is a technique of the heart. It’s just interaction between you and me. If your heart were stronger, you might push the Miracle Stream away and move." He couldn’t see that happening.

"Kill me."

Shirone pondered the true intent behind those words.

"Hehe, you would save me if I begged, I know. But I don’t want that, so kill me."

"Why? Out of pride?"

"No. If I'm saved by you, I’d have to give up something worse than death."

Lycon cracked a smile.

"You want to know where Gunjo Bedium is? Fine, I’ll tell you. If that’s what you want."

Suddenly, Shirone's eyes widened.

"Stop!"

As Miracle Stream broke, the sickle slashed halfway through Lycon’s neck.

Blood sprayed horizontally, and Pony, shocked, stared at him.

"You..."

Staggering, Lycon spoke with blood-filled lips.

"Hehe. Look. I won, didn’t I?"

Shirone had won his heart, but at the cost of his own life.

"... Stupid kid."

As sadness welled up in Shirone’s eyes, Lycon shook his head as if he was wrong.

"I’m an assassin."

His lifeless body thudded to the ground.

Silence followed, and as Shirone nodded to Lycon’s last words, he said,

"Bedium will come."

To avenge his son's death.

"Breaking Miracle Stream, using his own life to try to kill me. Yes, you're definitely an assassin."

A villain of the world, yet he lived every moment driven by his convictions.

"Evil is strong."

That's why more people's help was needed.

"Pony, the one who hired the assassin was Prince Robins. But the other royals are complicit too."

"I thought so. I didn’t expect them to act this quickly."

"It’s because I came. They're keeping us in check. And I, Shirone, have made a decision.

"I wish you would lead Tormia. To fight against evil in the future, we need your help."

"My help?"

Shirone explained the principles of quantum transmission and the Ultima System project.

"We’re in a time crunch. Having a capable leader could greatly shorten it. Someone who can unite people and focus them on a single goal."

"I understand."

Pony said.

"But I don't have the qualities of a king. You know, Shirone. People who are ashamed of lying can't lead everyone. I can only be responsible for myself."

"I know. That's why I came to you."

As Pony blinked, lost in thought, an idea suddenly passed through her mind.

"If I become king, I'll end up denying the king."

A magician, called a child of intellect, criticizes even the privileges given to him.

"That's enough."

Shirone smiled.

In the city of Bashka, citizens engaged in relief activities simultaneously pointed to the sky and shouted.

"Wow! What's that?"

A dragon covered in long, green scales like grass was flying overhead.

The appearance of a small, cute girl had disappeared, replaced by a size that dominated an entire city block.

"Is this a good spot?"

Eitra, having chosen a suitable location, took a deep breath and prepared to unleash her breath.

'Healing Breath.'

The dragon's breath boasted a powerful ability comparable to the angel's judicial halo.

It charged the breath into a special organ within its body, with the charging time varying for each dragon.

Eitra's breath had an exceptionally high charging cycle among the 12 apostles, taking a full 100 hours to charge.

"Roaaar!"

As Eitra opened her massive mouth and roared, a golden gale swept through Bashka.

"Ah, ahhh..."

The wounds of those injured by the demons healed, and one by one, they stood up.

Miro and her companions were shocked.

'Incredible healing power.'

Eitra, who had breathed over the entire Bashka, folded her wings and descended to the ground.

People cheered.

"Oh, great dragon! Save us! Glory to Tormia!"

After experiencing several miracles in one day, it felt as if Tormia had been chosen by the gods.

A maid announced.

"Miss Pony requests an audience."

The eyes of the royals were filled with shock, and Adolf XIII's face turned pale.

'How, how?'

Shirone was clearly present here, and there had been no leak of the assassination plan.

When Pony entered, all hypotheses disappeared, leaving only the harsh reality.

"Damn it..."

Forgetting his royal dignity, the king cursed, but there was little time left to wear the crown anyway.

Pony's gaze passed over the kneeling royals and the fainted guards, landing on Shirone.

'It's true.'

Meeting Shirone again so soon after parting felt like a memory glitch.

Shirone looked at the royals and spoke.

"From this moment on, Pony will succeed the throne of Tormia. As a star of the Ivory Tower and one of the five great stars of the Integrated Universe Management Department, I have determined that we cannot crown someone who has assassinated their kin and mocked the people by taking credit for others' achievements."

The royals realized that it was Shirone who had resolved the disaster in Bashka.

"How dare you..."

Adolf XIII, seeing his 44 years of waiting dissolve like bubbles, was consumed by anger.

"Pony! You finally...! Ugh!"

At that moment, the Miracle Stream struck his heart, turning the king's face as white as a sheet.

He collapsed with a thud, clutching his chest, his eyes filled with immense fear.

"Gasp! Gasp!"

Shirone spoke coldly.

"I don't want to waste time. Confess your wrongdoings to the people and relinquish your power. Then I will treat you as a human being."

"Ugh..."

Adolf XIII's face crumpled into a sob.

"I'm sorry."

Even if someone had held a knife to his throat and threatened him, he wouldn't have been this scared.

'My heart, my heart is stopping...'

A strange sensation, difficult to experience even once in a lifetime, lingered in his mind.

"Then from now on..."

Just as Shirone began to speak,

"Your Majesty! Something terrible has happened!"

The door burst open, and all the noble officials in the palace rushed in.

"A giant, a giant...!"

Shirone and Pony turned to look at each other simultaneously.
