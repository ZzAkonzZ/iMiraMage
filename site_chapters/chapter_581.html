Chapter 581:

"Is this…?"

Surrounded by light, Eden felt a tangible holiness for the first time in her life.

Only with extreme defensive magic could anyone withstand such a powerful mass wave without feeling pain or shock. The impossible light, seemingly not of human origin, transmitted its vibrations through a thin barrier.

At the peak of this transcendent vibration, the output suddenly surged, turning her mind blank.

'I need to escape.'

She didn't know if Scramble Royal had an out-of-bounds height limit, but staying like this felt like she might be launched into space.

'Oh God…'

Eden fixed herself precisely in the space her body occupied.

'Why are you angry?'

As the friction increased, the vibrations outside the barrier intensified.

It sounded like a wail.

Not a beast's or a human's scream, but something else entirely.

"Huuuuh!"

As long as God existed, Eden's barrier would not break.

'It's getting stronger.'

But holding her body in place was a different matter.

'I was careless. Damn…!'

In the endlessly increasing light, Shirone's plan became clear.

'He intended to blow me away from the start!'

As the chains of law holding her broke, her body seemed to vanish, leaving its coordinates.

"Ah…"

When she regained her senses, the wailing vibration was gone.

Below her, a vast sea of clouds stretched out, and a giant moon hung in the night sky.

"Beautiful."

Everything created by God is beautiful.

Even as gravity pulled her body back to earth, accelerating through the sea of clouds, her eyes remained steady.

'Don't worry.'

God would protect her.

"…"

In the quiet mountains where the flash had disappeared, Shirone looked up at the sky.

His gaze followed the white body falling in the most natural posture.

As she crashed headfirst, the ground shook, and dust rose from the deep crater.

Moments later, Eden emerged, her hair untouched.

'This woman is…'

A monster.

Hearing about it from Iruki hadn't felt real, but experiencing it firsthand made his hair stand on end.

"The plan was good. But you underestimated me."

In Scramble Royal, the out-of-bounds height limit is beyond the gravitational field.

If he had pushed her straight from the start, who knows what might have happened. But from now on, Eden wouldn't be so easily caught off guard.

'Should I be satisfied with getting the scramble?'

Since Eden wouldn't attack, there was no reason to stay.

'I need a new plan.'

As he turned to leave, Eden called out.

"Wait a moment."

Shirone turned back, puzzled.

"The amplification magic just now. How does it compare to when you met Gaold? Is it stronger?"

It was incomparable to its power before reaching heaven.

"Probably. Why do you ask?"

Eden fell silent, lost in thought.

"You seem concerned about Gaold. Why? Because you're both from the Yor Church?"

Eden's expression twisted.

"Don't associate me with heresy."

"Heresy?"

"He abandoned God. Driven mad by mere human lust."

Now Shirone's brow furrowed.

It could be interpreted that way, but Gaold's love for Miro was genuine.

"Why speak of it like that? If God truly exists, then human emotions are also His creation."

"Of course. But only under God's care. Gaold killed many and threatened all humanity for one woman. Just to satisfy his own desires. He's a disgrace, erased from the Yor Church's records. That's Gaold."

"Is that your omnipotence?"

Eden stood proudly.

"Everything is under God's guidance. That's why I'm invincible without relying on anything."

"You're not invincible."

Shirone thought so.

He hadn't found a logical way to break her Absolute Barrier, but if Eden truly believed she was invincible, then it wasn't absolute.

"Do you have no sympathy for Gaold's suffering?"

"Sympathy?"

Eden's lips curled.

"There's no room for sympathy for one who abandoned God. I'm stronger than Gaold. I'm proving it now."

Shirone recalled Gaold blocking Ataraxia with a vacuum press in the secret underground bunker of the Magic Association.

Unlike Eden's calm expression, Gaold's face had shown extreme pain.

The sum of all the negative emotions a human, a living being, must endure.

'Gaold…'

Maybe that's why he felt compelled to support him.

Perhaps Gaold's suffering seemed greater than the existence of an uncertain God.

"It's not over yet. No matter how great your defensive magic is, nothing is absolute unless you're God."

"I am God."

Eden placed her hand on her chest.

"That's why I'm absolute."

Realizing they couldn't find common ground, Shirone fell silent.

"Come anytime. God neither runs nor hides."

Eden turned and disappeared into the forest.

Returning to her hideout, she would call upon God's name until the next scramble was summoned.

'Eden. And Gaold.'

Between the peace given by God and human suffering, Shirone didn't know which was more important.

The second scramble of the sixth day was summoned, and the participants, each assigned their tasks, moved busily.

Dorothy also increased her speed to collect as many scrambles as possible.

'Two, no, I need to find three.'

Otherwise, she couldn't keep up with Pisho's pace.

Bzzzz!

Jona's keen senses detected a vibration in the air.

'Ultrasound?'

As Hickory lifted her into the sky, a tree behind them exploded with a bang.

'Hershey.'

The sound-based attack magic, Kaiser Gun.

Amplifying the natural frequency to explode objects took time but had the advantage of being invisible.

'Why now?'

Hershey's hand was ⓡⓡⓡⓡ.

With the total number of scrambles decreasing, it was unlikely she had opened it.

'She's trying to exhaust my hand.'

To block Hershey's Kang, Dorothy had to use the scrambles Hickory had stored.

'In that case!'

Prepared for Pao, Dorothy entered battle mode.

'Canon Kick (←→→ + attack)!'

As Hickory charged with a back kick, breaking trees, Hershey's hidden form was revealed.

'She's not calling Kang. What's she aiming for?'

At that moment, Hershey dodged Hickory's attack and took a stance with dual pistols.

'Double Kaiser Gun.'

As the two waves resonated, Hickory's body trembled.

'Damn!'

From the start, she had targeted the scrambles Hickory had collected.

The wave-based attack, ignoring durability, was iron's nemesis.

'I have to subdue her quickly!'

Double Super Rocket Punch (→→→ + attack, defense)!

The two rocket punches, devastating the forest, had lethal power, but Hershey's detection abilities were formidable.

"Targeting the enemy's weakness is basic."

As Hickory's frame shook violently, Sona's magic was forcibly canceled.

'Cancellation?'

"Targeting the enemy's weakness is basic."

As Iruki appeared from the sky, Hershey quickly threw herself aside.

"Ugh!"

The rocket punches were tracking her.

'The battery has been analyzed. Sona is no longer possible.'

Losing her detection ability, Hershey felt isolated in the dark.

'Air Suit!'

All she could do was wrap herself in the basics of defensive magic.

'Control Mode (attack + move), maximum output (→→→→→→).'

As Dorothy's rocket punch hit her abdomen directly, Hershey's expression crumpled like paper.

Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang!

Watching Hershey fly through the trees, Iruki shouted.

"Master card!"

If they eliminated Hershey, the allied team would lose its brain.

'Even with Eden, we can win.'

As Iruki and Dorothy rushed into the forest, the scenery suddenly changed bizarrely.

Jincheon Sorcery - Lust Demon.

"Huh!"

What they saw wasn't a human body.

It was the primordial desire that arose when a single cell decided to split into male and female.

"Huff! Huff!"

As the illusion disappeared, Iruki breathed heavily.

'Incredible. This isn't an ordinary illusion.'

Turning his head, he saw Dorothy swallowing nervously as she looked at him.

"Ah…"

Regaining his composure, Iruki spoke.

"We succeeded. Keep collecting scrambles. If we reach our goal, we'll have the advantage."

"Oh? Oh, okay."

As Iruki was about to leave, Dorothy called out.

"Iruki."

"Huh? Hmph!"

Suddenly kissing him, Iruki's eyes wavered.

Without any prior agreement, their lips parted simultaneously, and Dorothy, her face flushed, spoke quickly.

"I'll definitely succeed."

Watching Dorothy teleport away, Iruki momentarily lost his sense of direction and looked around.

"Ah, I need to go to Sabina…"

A flash rose in the opposite direction of Dorothy.

"Damn cancellation."

Hershey clutched her aching stomach, grimacing.

She had blocked it, but it felt like her abdominal muscles were torn.

"Are you okay?"

Hershey glared at Anchal.

Once again, her magic had saved her, but she wasn't satisfied.

'She's definitely strong. Only Fermi or Lycan could subdue her.'

That meant she was stronger than most of the teachers at the school.

"Do you even plan to win? Don't you know there are consequences if we lose?"

"I think I'm doing as much as anyone else."

"That's not what I mean."

"I used mind projection on Nade yesterday."

Hershey paused.

Now that she thought about it, Nade hadn't moved since yesterday.

"So what? Do you want praise for that?"

Anchal smirked.

"You'll understand someday. Just that means I've done my part in this match. So don't blame me."

"Wait! What are you talking about…!"

Before Hershey could finish speaking, Anchal teleported away.

The third scramble was summoned.

'I can't stop Eden right now. Instead of gambling, I'll collect one more scramble.'

With no guarantee that Eden would yield as easily as in the morning, Shirone decided to bypass the coordinates where he expected Eden to be.

"Huh?"

Suddenly, holy mist spread all around, and an Eastern-style tomb split open.

Jincheon Sorcery - Middle Heaven Realm.

The sound of a coffin opening was heard, and Anchal walked out of the tomb.

"I've been waiting, Shirone."

Having already experienced Anchal's illusions, Shirone was on high alert.

'Anchal's hand is…'

As if reading his thoughts, Anchal took out her master card and threw it on the ground.

"What are you doing?"

"I don't intend to attack you. I want to talk."

Shirone recalled the secret agreement Amy had mentioned.

'If Anchal withdraws, it would definitely be advantageous. But…'

Shirone made the same decision as Amy.

"Pick up your master card."

"I haven't said anything yet."

"I don't want to hear it. I don't know why you joined, but let's fight fairly."

"You'll need my help. If you investigate Istas alone, you'll die."

"If you're talking about upper-level rumors, I'm not interested."

"The Great Purification. If you led the third reset, you can't be completely unrelated."

Shirone's eyebrow twitched.

'Who is this woman?'

Anchal, cautiously approaching, whispered into Shirone's ear.

Even with magic blocking the surroundings, confidentiality was a basic skill for a spy.

"Istas is…"

Hearing Anchal's words, Shirone's eyes widened, and his heart began to race.

After gathering his thoughts, he slowly spoke.

"…What do you want?"

"Companionship. Come with me to Istas."

Anchal, having dissolved the Middle Heaven Realm, tore her master card in half and disappeared into the forest.

"Come find me when the match is over. I'll be waiting."

Anchal.

Scramble Royal withdrawal.
