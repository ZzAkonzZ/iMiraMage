Chapter 1090: Reload (4)

The average level of the creatures bombing all the cities in High Gear was 700.

Though weaker than Dr. Martin, they surpassed the existing boss creatures in High Gear.

"Exterminate. All. Humanity."

Explosions erupted.

All the beautiful or technologically advanced structures were collapsing.

"Damn it! This is disgusting! I'm done with this simulation. Hey, let's all log out."

Users tried to disconnect, but none could.

"What? What's going on?"

Seeing the system message that disconnection was impossible, they finally felt fear.

"Screeech!"

Bat-like creatures dive-bombed and bit the users.

"Ugh! What is this?"

Unidentified program detected.

As the AI was infected with a virus, users began attacking each other.

"You idiot! Why are you shooting us?"

"Exterminate. All. Humanity."

The infected users repeated the same phrase, and the cities quickly turned into chaos.

Number Seven, watching the end of High Gear from a dark space, felt conflicted.

"Are you satisfied now?"

It was the world the Operator loved.

"I knew it from the start. I let myself be used, knowing the heart of a teenage girl."

She would now live in reality.

'Good for her.'

She would forget the adventures in the virtual world and exchange genuine feelings with real humans.

"Tch! Live well."

He had hoped for that, but why did he feel so empty?

"Number Seven!"

A voice came from beyond the darkness, followed by an electromagnetic field spreading as if hitting an invisible wall.

The operators were infiltrating.

"Hey! What are you doing? Stop the event now! High Gear will shut down at this rate."

"…Such fuss."

Number Seven looked at the virtual wall where the electromagnetic waves were rippling.

"It's just one simulation ending. It's not a big deal if this one disappears."

"Are you crazy? This is the world we've built. You can't just destroy it as you please."

"We built it? Only the Operator can create this world. We can be replaced, but not the Operator. And she wants to quit."

"So we stay and hold on…"

"She doesn't want that!"

Number Seven shouted.

"Stay for what? To be a dog guarding an empty house? I don't want that."

At that moment, a child's machine holding a lollipop tore through the darkness.

"Number Seven."

"Lollipop?"

Though blocked by the firewall, just infiltrating this far was impressive.

"I need to talk to you. Block the signal going outside for a moment."

"No need to talk. And when did you start calling me brother? How do you know if I'm a man or a woman?"

"I met the Operator."

The signal was blocked.

"What? Where?"

"In reality. She was busy with in-play, so I visited her workshop."

To Number Seven, this was more shocking than the Operator kissing Yahweh2.

"Why?"

"Just curious. I wanted to see her in person, and she invited me."

Number Seven was curious too.

"How was it?"

"Haha, the same. Prickly, not very talkative. But not a bad person. She even gave me snacks."

A clear image formed, but his curiosity wasn't satisfied.

"Meet me outside, brother."

Number Seven was silent.

"Tell her to meet. She hates reality but doesn't hate the connections here. That's probably why she agreed."

"Connections?"

Wasn't this the world she fled from?

"Forget it. Why would I meet the Operator? We were enemies here too."

"She talked about you."

Number Seven turned his head.

"Me? What did she say?"

"Nothing special. She said your tone is rough and you always grumble, which is annoying."

Sensing Number Seven's disappointment, Lollipop laughed and continued.

"But she said your skills are the best. No one understands their world like you."

Of course.

"Stop this, brother. There's no need to destroy it. Leave it here and go to reality."

That was also a terrifying thought.

'I want to meet her.'

He wanted to see the only programmer who surpassed him in Under Coder.

"If you destroy High Gear, there's no chance. Don't hurt her anymore."

"Damn it!"

Number Seven turned and said.

"Help me. I made this with the end of service in mind, so the code is all tangled."

"Hehe, that's my specialty. But first, this firewall…"

At that moment, Number Seven suddenly screamed and collapsed.

"Brother!"

Seeing his machine disappear transparently, Lollipop swallowed hard.

'Forced disconnection.'

High Gear was still heading towards destruction.

He's awake.

Little Witch received a communication.

Just hold him for now. He might be useful later. Don't break anything.

It was necessary to act before Number Seven changed his mind.

Understood.

Ending the communication, Uorin watched Shirone ram into Dr. Martin's core.

'The world's secrets?'

She never cared about that.

'Shirone, I only need you.'

If High Gear ended abnormally, the Operator would feel despair.

If the excavation of the apocalypse was delayed, Shirone would eventually come to her.

'Fermi. He's no ordinary guy.'

A cunning snake wouldn't miss her obsession with Shirone.

'But he agreed to the deal because he sees this situation like I do.'

The difference was…

Fermi wanted Shirone to win.

Your Highness.

Another communication came.

"Hehe."

Having listened to all of Number Seven's confessions, she flew into the sky.

"I won."

Simultaneously, a loud noise erupted as Dr. Martin's core wobbled.

"This is the end!"

Flying with his booster, Shirone fired Michelangelo's flash into the core's center.

The 750 million durability was completely worn down, and the core collapsed in a chain explosion.

"We did it."

Rankers fighting the creatures turned to look.

"We… caught that?"

The Operator, who hadn't dared to join the battle, fell to her knees.

"We protected it."

At that moment, the flattened core shook, and the central part exploded.

"Kiaaaah!"

A phoenix-like machine emerged, spreading its massive wings and gliding.

Seeing the information through augmented reality, the Operator cursed.

Boss Creature: Real Martin

Soon, other users saw the durability of the machine, which was 3.2 billion.

"Kiaaaah!"

Real Martin's monstrous roar seemed like a signal flare, drawing users from all directions.

They were infected by the virus.

"Exterminate. All. Humanity."

The Operator couldn't contain her rising anger.

"Raaah!"

Real Martin's flash cannon swept the world, turning many rankers to ashes.

"It's not over yet!"

Only Shirone didn't give up, flying into the sky and firing Michelangelo.

The rankers could only watch as the overwhelming firepower clashed.

"Exterminate. All. Humanity."

The infected users no longer attacked but formed a barrier with their machines.

Beyond that, creatures filled the sky, attacking the remaining users.

The Operator gritted her teeth and stood up.

"Fight! Don't retreat!"

A massive battle erupted between Real Martin and Shirone, creatures and rankers.

'Damn it! Let me out! Let me out!'

Meanwhile, users trapped in their machines could only watch the war as a wall.

In the battle that seemed to split the world, Shirone brought down Real Martin.

"Kieee!"

Just before the 3.2 billion durability dropped, the machine quickly transformed into a new form.

'Real Martin Stage 2?'

Stunned by the 13 billion durability, the rankers were ambushed by creatures.

"Aaah!"

As more users were infected, Shirone activated Michelangelo.

Insufficient power

"Damn it!"

From Real Martin's grotesque machine, tens of thousands of guided missiles fired simultaneously.

It was the entire landscape, and Aegis flew up, activating the ballistic decoy system.

This is the last.

Aegis's machine, drawing the missiles, was engulfed in an extravagant explosion.

"Damn it!"

As Real Martin flew, Shirone retreated, responding with Air God.

Durability decreased by 10 each time.

"…It's over."

Seeing the speed at which the 13 billion number decreased, the rankers finally realized reality.

"It's all over."

Shirone reloaded Air God.

"Reload!"

He could easily intercept the creatures following Real Martin.

Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang!

As many creatures fell, Yahweh2's experience points rapidly increased.

The experience required for levels in the 300s was much lower than for rankers…

'Level up.'

As he gained a level, High Gear and Michelangelo's power were restored.

"Yaaah!"

As the light sphere condensed rapidly in Yahweh2's palm, the Operator shouted.

"It's not over yet!"

Yahweh2 stopped retreating and charged.

"Maximum output."

Michelangelo's massive flash cut through the air and struck Real Martin directly.

A thunderous noise erupted, and the creatures around Real Martin exploded.

'Level up!'

He was recharged again.

'Level up!'

As Real Martin's durability hit rock bottom, a roar shook the world.

"Kieee!"

The roar alone caused ground users to explode, and Yahweh2's machine crashed to the ground.

"Damn it! What now…"

Shirone's expression went blank.

"What?"

Real Martin's grotesque machine expanded even more, filling the sky.

Real Martin Stage 3.

Durability: 140,000,000,000/140,000,000,000

Thud, thud, the rankers fell to their knees.

Yolga's son stopped fighting, and Little Witch watched the Operator's trembling shoulders.

"Hic. Hic."

She was crying.

Though no tears flowed from her mechanical body, it was the saddest sound she had ever made.

"What is this, you bastard?"

Everything she had designed was denied.

'It's over.'

Little Witch laughed.

'Completely broken. There's no recovery now.'

Users trapped in infected machines also heard the Operator's voice.

'This is so pointless.'

Had they dedicated all that time just to be mocked by these numbers?

'What did I even do here? Just end it. Let's quit and leave.'

Feeling as if she could hear their voices, Uorin shrugged at Fermi.

'I won…'

At that moment.

"Re…!"

Someone shouted.

"…load!"

Everyone turned to see Yahweh2, reloading Air God, flying into the sky.

"…Huh?"

As she looked up at him, the Operator's heart began to beat again.

Thump, thump.

It was the sound of everyone's hearts.
