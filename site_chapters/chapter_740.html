Chapter 740:

Despite the grand name of the Great Sanctuary, a long silence followed Shirone's question.

"Shirone," Great Star began heavily, "Humans have the ability to name things that exist. This is intelligence."

Naming is the essence of intelligence.

"But just because we have the ability to know something doesn't mean we can know everything."

Many scholars explore the ends of the universe.

"If you think you will someday know everything, it is merely the limitation of a human who has not yet grasped the essence of intelligence."

The answer lies beyond infinity.

"Therefore, even though I know everything in this world, I cannot assert what lies outside the universe."

"But I am here."

It wasn't about the outside.

"Right here, you can tell me what you see beyond the infinite veil."

"I truly don't know. I have no reason to lie to you."

"Can't you at least guess?"

"Guessing is not something I should do."

Great Star was firm.

"When you stand on the ground and look up at the sky, you can make many guesses. It's free to let your imagination soar. But at the end of the world, you must not do that. Shirone, you know how dangerous that is."

You cannot believe the essence of the world based on guesses.

"Beyond the veil, there may be only nothingness. This could be the beginning. Why are you denying yourself?"

"It's not denial; I want to acknowledge it. I heard that Mr. Mahagaruta attained enlightenment. And he did not return."

Had Mahagaruta awakened from the dream by now, or had he achieved annihilation in eternal nothingness?

"I was able to return to this world because I let go of everything in the realm of infinity…"

This was why Shirone was sad.

"If I had crossed the boundary, I wouldn't have returned either."

You cannot return to a dream from outside the dream, and thus Shirone knew Nane was right.

"To protect this world against Nane, or to surpass him, I think I need to define Hexa first."

Great Star sighed deeply.

"Infinity…"

Then she turned to Shirone and asked,

"What is it? What is emptiness? What is a human? What is a star? Everything is just a name given to things that exist without reason. They just are, and we define them according to our needs. Therefore, infinity and emptiness are just names we have given."

Great Star pointed her finger at Shirone's forehead.

"Even gods."

"……"

"To humans, the concept of a god doesn't need to be complicated. It's a matter of faith, and it was named for that purpose. But when you enter the realm of gods, as Shirone, the Infinite Magician, has…"

Great Star lowered her arm.

"You encounter all sorts of contradictions. You can handle all the information in the world. You are a god. But the problem is, that information is from a human perspective."

Shirone had indeed become a god, but only a god as defined by humans.

"I understand why you asked the question. The idea you realized with the eighth sense just exists; it doesn't need to be or not be…"

Thus, you can control all phenomena freely.

"But that too is just the infinity named by humans, so you wonder if the true meaning lies outside."

Great Star continued immediately.

"Also, because Hexa is made of light, in other words… it is just an abnormal signal flowing in the system, not a specific entity."

Hearing this from Great Star felt like his heart stopped.

"So, I am…"

Great Star's expression softened.

"This is my hypothesis."

"Hypothesis…?"

"Yes. Let's try it. What you want to know. Let's guess beyond infinity at the end of the world."

Great Star's kind heart was evident.

"You know, Shirone, I am the reincarnation of a star. My name, Gaia, governs all stars. Let's say… I am actually a program. The Gaia Program."

Great Star emphasized that this was a hypothesis.

"The universe is born, and within it, the natural world forms. There are two giant programs that make up the natural world. One is me, Gaia, governing planets. And the other…"

Great Star looked up at the ceiling.

"The Stellar Program, Ra."

Ra was another name for the sun.

"The Gaia Program builds new systems through the energy transmitted by the Stellar Program. The lower system of the natural world, the biological world, is born."

"And from that biological world…"

"It's too early to talk about humans."

Great Star interrupted.

"When Gaia creates the environment using Ra's energy, the biological program Argones spreads cells. The possibilities of evolution are infinite, but the essence is the simplest information transmission substance."

Shirone's eyes widened.

"Argones?"

It was the name of the alien organism that Pisho, who specialized in insect magic, had become a host for during the graduation exam.

"Ra's energy, Gaia's environment, Argones' cells. Combining these three elements infinitely creates specific spaces in this vast universe where life can exist. Then the basic preparation is complete."

"Preparation for what?"

"For the user to use this world."

Shirone fell silent.

"Assuming Shirone's guess is true. Even if life exists, building a mind is another matter. It must seem real, and yet offset the fear of death."

"A god."

"Yes. The mind program, God. Since it is derived from the stellar source of life, humans call it this."

Great Star raised her finger.

"The eternal Ra."

Anke Ra.

"Even so, Anke Ra wouldn't know it is a program. It would only know itself as a being that suddenly awakened in the realm of nothingness. When it defines all concepts subjectively and stores them in the Akashic Records, they are transmitted to all life in the universe through quantum transmission. That's why Nane called this world Anke Ra's dream."

Everything Shirone had sensed with super cognition became vividly clear.

"But an unexpected problem arose."

Great Star's eyes darkened.

"The mind program activated, and all users were to achieve eternal life through reincarnation. But the first humans from Gaia, the Gaians, began to reject the god program."

Shirone recalled the records of the Gaians he had seen in Babel.

"They tried to escape from Anke Ra."

"Yes. It was a fierce war. Every time Anke Ra tried to reset to fix errors, Gaia desperately resisted. They believed their enlightenment was more important than the universe. They had to pass it on to future generations."

Shirone swallowed hard.

"Anke Ra used all the concepts it had defined to attack them. The tide turned, and it seemed they couldn't prevent the reset. Then, one of the Gaians found a way to preserve their enlightenment."

"That method was…"

"Go outside and directly inject the code. Insert a special signal that can withstand the reset."

Shirone shook his head.

"How is that possible? Mr. Mahagaruta, having attained enlightenment, can no longer affect this world."

"There is one way."

Shirone's eyes widened in sudden realization.

"Could it be…"

"Yes. Goffin's erasure."

Great Star waved his finger.

"Escape the photon system at the moment of reset and inject a new signal. This is the truth contained in the first reset."

Shirone clenched his teeth.

"Then Hexa is…"

"The mind program transmits information through quantum, the biological program through electricity, but ultimately, the network is based on photons. Time, matter, energy, all are light. And the Gaians called that information unit…"

Great Star spoke cautiously.

"Hexa."

At the same time, tears streamed down Shirone's cheeks.

"The concepts of cause and effect are just definitions interpreted by Anke Ra's dream. Hexa, coming from outside this world, has no cause. Therefore, it is free from Anke Ra's reset and is the only being living outside his dream."

"Sob. Sob."

Shirone's sobs broke Great Star's heart, but they had to reach a conclusion.

"The light that makes up Hexa mimics human information. So Shirone is human. But from the outside, as Shirone guessed…"

Great Starbit her lip.

"It may just be a signal."

Shirone's shoulders shook as he lowered his head.

"I… I…"

His parents who raised him, his beloved Amy, his friends from the magic school, the countless people he met over 20 years.

'Not a single one…'

None of them were connected.

They were just part of a program created by some visionaries to escape Anke Ra's control.

"But it may not be."

Great Star's words made Shirone look up again.

"I told you, Shirone. This is just a guess. I don't know the truth either. I just applied what you realized in emptiness to the perspective of infinity. If you want, you can create any other story. The biggest problem is that Goffin has already been erased."

"Why is that a problem?"

"I know the history of the universe, but I can't remember Goffin. The most curious part is why the Gaians composed Hexa with human information."

Shirone blinked.

"Even if this world is a point in emptiness, there is plenty of evidence that humans are not the only users. The Theraphos species and the Gaians too. It's clear that the model is much more complex than Shirone imagines. But surprisingly, the Gaians chose humans."

"Hmm."

Finally regaining his composure, Shirone stroked his chin.

"You have opened the eighth sense and become an Infinite Magician, but the Gaians are a species that have opened the eleventh sense, the ultimate sense. They operate on a different level of omnipotence compared to you. Therefore, their analysis of the universe and their guesses about what lies beyond the veil might be entirely different from yours."

"Beyond infinity."

This was the phrase Goffin had left Shirone after the Gaian records ended and the screen reset.

"…Come to me."

Great Star grasped Shirone's trembling shoulders.

"It might not be just a signal, Shirone."

Goffin was erased, but…

"You might not be alone."

"I'm… not alone…"

Much hotter tears streamed down his face.

"Surpass Nane and reach the ultimate. Then, at the end of this world, meet your true parents with confidence."

Shirone nodded repeatedly.

"Yes. I will…"

At least, it wasn't time to give up everything yet.

"Good. Then."

Great Star patted Shirone's shoulders and smiled warmly.

"Let's begin the star conferment ceremony."
