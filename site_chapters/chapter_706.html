Chapter 706:

According to the esoteric teachings, the "Bardo" is a state where one experiences the essence of existence, similar to near-death experiences in medical terms.

Patients in a comatose state often report encountering landscapes different from reality, and highly trained monks can glimpse the world of the soul through extreme discipline.

However, Shirone's current state was not a fleeting realization but a vivid sense that allowed him to perceive both the real and the reverse worlds simultaneously.

"Confirming again."

As Paknyeo approached, destroying everything in sight, the color drained from Shirone's face.

'Boundary of Life and Death!'

Just as the "Simultaneous Perception of Past, Present, and Future" (SPPPF) merges past and future, the Boundary of Life and Death merges two spaces, revealing numerous previously unseen routes like the circuits of a precise magic circle.

Shirone dodged the attack and cast a Homing Photon Cannon, sending a spinning flash towards Paknyeo.

Her monstrous senses activated, causing her upper body to vibrate like a plucked string.

The mere shaking of her upper body caused the air to explode, and the Photon Cannons swirling around her collided and dissipated.

'Is she human?'

Paknyeo was equally astonished.

'He has indeed opened the seventh sense.'

The seventh sense corresponds to the sensory hierarchy of a mid-level angel in heaven.

Not only does it allow for a broader perspective, but it also encompasses the results of exploring this world with seven different senses.

Lowering her stance, Paknyeo said,

"From now on, I will evaluate you in my own way."

Extremely intuitive, yet never missing the mark.

'What is that?'

Behind Paknyeo, a massive mechanical device resembling a steel heart flickered like a hallucination.

Axing-Nirvana E-Engine (V-12 Cylinder).

As the hallucination faded and the vivid V-shaped incarnation rose like wings, Shirone's face twisted.

"Ugh!"

It felt as if the world was bending around her, similar to encountering Rian's Mach.

'Since that day…'

Facing Rian in the Apocalypse, he had experienced the highest acceleration but was not satisfied.

If it was the same incarnation, a straight line was a waste; bending it into a V allowed for more cylinders of the law to be installed.

'Now I can run even faster.'

Few knew that before gaining immortality, Paknyeo was a genius engineer and a speed fanatic among the Mecha tribe.

'Amita would oppose it.'

The Ten Demons did not favor pre-immortality habits.

"Shirone."

Paknyeo's lips stretched into a long smile.

"Will you go all the way with me?"

Feeling a chilling aura for a moment, the V-shaped incarnation flared up like flames, and Paknyeo vanished.

'Damn!'

Using all his abilities, Shirone dodged the attacks but doubted he could hold out.

'How is she so fast? Can she control it?'

Watching Vanguard turn into a battlefield, Momodo clutched her head.

"No! How can I protect Vanguard like this!"

Suddenly, a hole burst through an intact wall, followed by crashing sounds from inside.

Soon, a cloud of dust pushed out as the shops began to collapse in sequence.

'She's catching up!'

At this speed, a hundred spells were useless.

Finally, Paknyeo, who had circled seven times farther than Shirone, charged at him.

"Shirone!"

Rian blocked her path, swinging his greatsword, and the blade deflected with a sharp sound.

"I'll handle this! Get Latusa!"

Shirone bit his lip.

"Are you sure? That woman…"

Shirone always trusted Rian, but this time he couldn't leave him alone.

"I know what you mean. I've already fought her."

The battle in the Apocalypse still sent chills down his spine.

"Don't worry. I won't die. But honestly… I can't win here."

Rian's words clarified Shirone's judgment, and he used the Boundary of Life and Death to escape.

"Second time seeing you."

Paknyeo approached, lowering her sword.

"Shall we run?"

Rian could only smile wryly.

Boom!

The sound of Rian and Paknyeo's clash destroying Vanguard reached Kido's ears.

"Damn! Are we just goblins?"

When the Maga Bandits arrived, Kido stood on a spear held by his ten toes.

"Who are you? Why are you attacking us?"

"You don't need to worry about your death."

As the deputy leader drew his sword and approached, a nerve-wracking killing intent enveloped Kido.

'They're incredible.'

Few organizations could wreak havoc on Vanguard, a powerful presence in the central desert.

Each one was a Drakker, and the woman fighting Rian had transcended human limits.

"Hehe, hehe."

Kido's laughter made the deputy leader's eyes narrow.

"Have you gone mad? What's so funny?"

"Now I feel clear-headed."

Facing imminent death reveals certain truths.

'Fate that may come at any time…'

Is utterly pointless.

"Right, Shirone?"

As Kido bounced off the spear he held with his feet, the spear spun and whistled through the air.

When it fell back in front of him, Kido spun with the same speed and rolled sideways.

"Running is the best option!"

Kido rolled the spear like a wheel down the apartment wall, and the Maga Bandits jumped after him.

"Chase them! We must catch them in Vanguard!"

Meanwhile, Paknyeo, who was pressuring Rian, spotted Shirone climbing over a wall in the distance.

'Did he choose to meet Ra?'

Unable to let Shirone go, Paknyeo ran to a nearby sand removal device and grabbed the tank.

"Wait! Don't touch that!"

Momodo's shout was futile against the wind, and the V-12 cylinder powered Paknyeo's body.

"Ugh!"

As the RPM soared wildly, the tank began to shake violently.

"Oh no…"

With increasingly intense vibrations, the metal snapped with a loud crack.

"Yaaah!"

Paknyeo let out a beastly roar, twisting her body, and the tank, propelled by centrifugal force, flew towards Shirone.

"It's 100 tons…"

Momodo, snapping out of her daze, shouted.

"This is bad! If the outer wall collapses…"

"Shirone! Move!"

Hearing Rian's voice, Shirone turned to see the massive tank and his eyes widened.

"What!"

The 100-ton tank crashed through the wall with a thunderous noise.

"Ugh! What is this?"

Shirone, who had rolled outside Vanguard, grabbed the desert sand in a prone position.

The first thing he noticed was the sound of wind like burning fire.

'It's coming.'

Something massive was approaching from the deep darkness of the desert.

"Activate the second tank and repair the windbreak! If we don't stop it early, everything will be destroyed!"

Momodo quickly issued orders, but powerful winds rushed into Vanguard like a breached dam.

Sand scattered, double windows rattled, and debris from buildings injured many.

A subordinate who had jumped from the watchtower shouted.

"Manager! Noskarta… no…"

Realizing that the name of the equatorial wind couldn't fully express his emotions, he changed his words.

"The desert god is coming!"

Regaining his composure, Shirone cast a shining spell, and Kaidra flew in from where the moon was.

'Good. It was nearby.'

Kido and Rian ran towards the exit, and a Photon Cannon blasted the iron door at the perfect moment.

"Rian! Kido! Hurry!"

Shirone waved and urged them on, while the deputy leader shouted to the subordinate guarding the exit.

"Stop them! Don't let them board the beast!"

As the bandits on Omeks charged with raised swords, Kido halted.

"Damn! Go ahead!"

"Catch the goblin first!"

The Omeks leaped into the air, their sharp claws striking where Kido had been.

"Kido!"

Rian, who had reached Shirone, turned and shouted.

Roar!

As the dust settled, the Omeks with severed legs rolled on the ground in agony.

Kido, who had rolled out like a ball, bit into a beast's leg and ran towards the exit.

"Hehe! How was that? Did I do well?"

"Stop eating!"

Kido, scolded by Shirone, threw the leg aside and climbed onto Kaidra, grabbing the reins.

"I'll drive! We've seen enough here!"

The Maga Bandits, all mounted on Omeks, whipped their mounts and charged towards Kaidra.

"Leader! Shall we pursue?"

The wind was so fierce that voices could barely be heard.

"All the way."

As Paknyeo, who had turned the reins, took the lead, the deputy leader looked back and gave orders.

"Beat the drums! We'll locate them by sound!"

Boom! Boom! Boom! Boom! Boom!

With the sound of drums, Kaidra took off.

"Damn! This wind is insane!"

But the wind resistance was so strong that they couldn't easily gain altitude.

The wind grew louder and louder, and even the fearless bandits swallowed nervously.

'Are they really going through this?'

Sensing the wind's intensity, Kido shouted.

"It's coming! It's coming! It's coming!"

As the deafening roar reached Shirone's ears, Rian pointed ahead.

"Shirone! There!"

Noskarta.

A desert tsunami, nearly 2 kilometers high, was swallowing the horizon.

Shirone burst into laughter with a tearful expression.

"Hahaha!"

The bandits on the ground also saw the massive sand wall and felt dizzy.

"Brace yourselves! If we get swallowed, it's over!"

Boom! Boom! Boom! Boom! Boom!

Paknyeo took a deep breath, feeling the fierce wind that seemed to tear her skin.

'This is the wind of nature.'

She stripped off her only garment and spread her upper body wide, lifting her head.

"Haaa."

The whites of her eyes showed as her eyelids trembled, and her waist moved like a wave in rhythm with the Omek.

'She's literally an animal.'

The deputy leader clenched his lips as he watched Paknyeo's smoothly rising back muscles.

'It doesn't matter if she becomes a beast. If it's her…'

Even if she fell into the realm of animals, he would willingly serve her as his queen and give his life for her.

Kido grabbed the reins and shouted,

"Hold on tight! We're going to crash!"

Shirone stared at the massive sandstorm, understanding the law that governed the desert.

'It's not just wind.'

The essence of Noskarta, conveyed through his heightened senses, was the emptiness of countless lives that had perished over eons.

Graaah!

Was it a coincidence created by the flow of air?

The sand particles flowing down the desert wall formed a giant skull, letting out a terrifying scream.

'I won't be swallowed by emptiness!'

Even if fate left only a miserable end, he would fight against the world to the very end.

"Rian! Kido!"

A skull emerged from the sand wall, reaching out its bony hands to engulf Kaidra.

"Let's go!"

At the same time, Rian swung his greatsword vertically.

"Uwaaah!"

Divine Transcendence - Mach.

Boom!

Rian's will split the world, and the skull's face carved into the sand wall exploded into two.

Finally, the desert storm swallowed Kaidra.

"Yaho!"

Kido's voice spread on the wind.

"Don't let them escape! We are the Maga Bandits!"

Boom. Boom. Boom. Boom. Boom.

The desert god comes, swallowing emptiness.
