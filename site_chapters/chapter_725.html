Chapter 725:

Seong-eum and the Three Treasures gazed at the massive crater that looked as if a meteor had struck.

"That place…"

It was the very center of Labyrinth Jessica.

"It looks like something crashed there."

Mun-gyeong's voice was serious because the scale of the crater was beyond imagination.

'But there's no sign of the object that fell. What could have caused this?'

Volcano's leader, Jix, muttered.

"Ymir's Tooth."

Seong-eum, who had annihilated Volcano, had forcibly made Jix their guide, but it was also his first time reaching the center.

However, there was a legend.

"According to rumors, Jessica is the area where Ymir's Tooth fell a long time ago."

"A tooth? Are you saying a single tooth caused this massive crater?"

The crater was a staggering 2 kilometers in diameter.

"Your Highness, according to ancient texts, Ymir was a giant who absorbed over ten thousand bodies. If a tooth was extracted from such a massive being, its mass would be considerable."

Jix laughed.

"You're mistaken."

Mun-gyeong turned with a displeased expression.

"Mistaken?"

"You know about Heaven, right? The Elixir of Life is a technique that melts humans to create giants. Ymir was an unprecedented giant who reached the 10th stage of the Elixir of Life. Do you know what that means?"

Seong-eum asked indifferently.

"What does it mean?"

"The number of humans required for the Elixir of Life is roughly 8 to 10 per giant. These giants then gather in groups of ten to form the next stage of giants. According to this calculation, the number of humans needed to reach the 10th stage of the Elixir of Life is…"

Jix raised one finger.

"Ten billion."

"Ten billion…"

Mun-gyeong couldn't even imagine Ymir in her mind.

"That's the king of giants, Ymir. Never mind how they integrated ten billion minds; the number itself is unbelievable. Even if not in a giant state, if such a being's tooth fell, it could easily create a crater of this size."

Seong-eum rested her chin in thought.

"If that's the case… what event could have extracted such a being's tooth?"

"Who knows. Maybe someone from prehistory had a proper fight with Ymir."

Jix looked around the vast crater.

"As you know, giants are granted the right to leave for a new world once they surpass the 6th stage. This is because they can manipulate their telomere levels from that point."

Mun-gyeong recalled the secrets of the Jincheon Space Agency.

"Fragmentation and gigantification."

"Yes. A 6th stage giant can create new entities from parts of their body. They can also transform into a giant form by exhausting their telomeres. The average height of a 6th stage giant is 80 meters, but when they activate gigantification, they can reach up to 2 kilometers."

Having dedicated his life to studying giants, his information was on par with the Jincheon Space Agency.

"Interestingly, from the 6th stage onwards, the average height actually decreases. We call this condensation. As the size decreases, the power increases. Volcano estimates that Ymir's condensed form was around 3 meters."

"A 3-meter giant made from ten billion humans?"

"Perhaps the Ymir who fought here long ago wasn't as large as we think. In fact, I don't even want to think about it. It makes you realize how small humans are."

As Mun-gyeong remained silent, Seong-eum looked up.

'From that high place…'

Before the artificial sky existed, Ymir must have fought some entity in the real sky.

'The power of a single tooth…'

During the battle, a tooth was extracted and fell here, and judging by the shockwave, everything was swept away.

In the place where civilization disappeared, giants built pyramids to honor Ymir.

"So, it's the ruins of giants."

The only thing left in the ruins was Ymir's Tooth.

"Alright, we've confirmed it."

Jix's eyes turned wistful.

"Even though I didn't achieve it myself, my comrades can now rest in peace."

It was a sight he had dedicated his life to seeing.

"Kill me. My role ends here."

The only reason he had followed Seong-eum, who had killed his comrades, was to complete Volcano's mission.

"The journey isn't over yet."

Seong-eum moved towards the crater.

"Ymir's Tooth. We need to see what it is with our own eyes."

"Your Highness, but…"

As Mun-gyeong hurriedly approached, she sensed the distance of the Three Treasures and stopped abruptly.

"It's dangerous."

That was the essence of her feelings.

It was a place where a part of Ymir, not just a fragment, remained.

"I'm worried something might happen."

"There's always a reason not to do something."

Seong-eum clicked her tongue and looked at Mun-gyeong.

"If you're afraid of a single tooth, how will you pass the Ivory Tower test? I heard one of the candidates has already been to Heaven."

Before leaving the Imperial City, Anchal from the Jincheon Space Agency had visited and said,

"Your Highness, I have some advice regarding one of the Ivory Tower candidates. His name is Shirone."

"I don't need advice. This isn't a children's talent show. It's about fighting for your life and getting results."

"Of course. Everyone knows Your Highness's strong character. But that's why I'm telling you this."

At this point, Seong-eum became curious.

"Is Shirone strong?"

"Yes."

The answer came without hesitation.

"Shirone is undoubtedly strong. But it's not just about winning or losing. Your Highness is a heroine, so the outcome of the battle will be decided by the heavens. What I'm concerned about is…"

Anchal turned her head slightly, recalling Shirone from Alpheas Magic School.

"He is a human with a completely different temperament from anyone Your Highness has fought before."

"A completely different temperament?"

"Your Highness's nature is unyielding, never bending. That's what makes you great. Like a steel pine tree tempered a thousand times. But Shirone…"

Anchal's concern arose here.

"He will never try to break you."

"…"

"No matter how strong the wind, Your Highness will not budge. But a gentle breeze can melt steel."

Seong-eum slapped her knee.

"What are you saying… Do you see me as just a woman? No matter who Shirone is, I will never give my heart to him!"

"It's not because you're a woman."

Anchal spoke seriously.

"It's because you're human."

Seong-eum's brow furrowed.

"No, perhaps it's because you're a living being."

"What are you talking about…"

"Maybe!"

Anchal cut her off firmly.

"It's simply because you exist."

Seong-eum adjusted her expression and asked,

"Is he that great?"

Anchal brought her forehead to her clasped hands.

"I cannot measure his greatness."

"Hmm, Shirone, huh?"

"Your Highness, the strongest candidate in this test is Nane. But his strength is clearly visible. You will never back down. But…"

Anchal slowly raised her head.

"You cannot clash with something that doesn't exist."

Seong-eum's eyelids fluttered rapidly.

"Your Highness, be wary of Shirone. Never let him enter your space."

"I understand."

Seong-eum raised her chin.

"But don't worry. No one in this world can enter my space."

Returning from her recollection, Seong-eum resumed walking.

"The candidates competing with me are not to be underestimated. If they risk their lives in the competition, I won't avoid any danger."

Unable to change Seong-eum's stubbornness, Mun-gyeong sighed and followed, with Jix and the Three Treasures entering the crater.

The 2-kilometer-wide crater was also hundreds of meters deep, narrowing like a funnel.

'Small. Only something incredibly small with immense power could create such a crater.'

Jix's shoulders trembled as he witnessed the scene proving Volcano's hypothesis.

"Where is Ymir's Tooth…"

As Seong-eum murmured, the crater shook violently, and the soil from all directions began to be sucked down.

"Don't come closer."

As Seong-eum halted the group, four massive statues rose from the sinking ground.

"Those are ancient weapons! Your Highness, it's dangerous!"

Jix stopped Mun-gyeong.

"Wait! Those are Yols! They're the gates of the test! If we get too close, we'll be caught by the law!"

Just as there is a name Hera for the angel's law, in Heaven, the giant's law is called Yol.

You who have reached this place.

The four statues that had risen slowly finally detached from the ground and floated 10 meters high.

Their faces, resembling beasts with long horns embedded in their foreheads, looked down at Seong-eum, curled up like fetuses.

Are you a being who crosses the universe?

The sound from their iron bodies trembling seemed to directly strike the heart.

"If you're asking if I'm a giant, the answer is no. But if you're asking if I can cross the universe…"

Seong-eum covered her mouth with her sleeve and sneered.

"Why wouldn't I be able to do what you can?"

…Prove Yol.

Powerful beams shot from the long horns of the four statues, striking Seong-eum.

"Your Highness!"

Unable to avoid the activation of the law, Seong-eum's body was enveloped in light and floated, emitting a tremendous beam.

"Ugh!"

The immense impact sent Seong-eum's consciousness far into the depths of space.

'This place…'

She could only see her home planet for a fleeting moment before the vast expanse of space, filled with countless stars, unfolded before her.

'I'm flying endlessly.'

As her consciousness sped away at the speed of light, Seong-eum realized the immense scale of fear.

'Compared to the vast universe, humans are nothing but dust.'

The distance was so vast that she felt she could never return to her original world.

'And this is…'

Seong-eum's body, enveloped in Yol's beam, stood upright, and her lips parted slightly like petals.

"This is my step."

Ether Wave - Infinite Path.

From the far reaches of space where Seong-eum had been flung, the space crumpled and rushed back towards her body.

"Your Highness…"

Finally witnessing her step, Mun-gyeong trembled with tears of emotion.

'Jin Seong-eum's step crosses the universe.'

No one could ever approach her.

"Is it done now?"

As Seong-eum landed on the ground, the four silent statues sank back into the earth.

At the same time, something white shot up from the center and fell in front of Seong-eum.

Jix's eyes sparkled as he shouted,

"Ymir's Tooth!"

Disliking physical contact, Seong-eum fixed the space and observed from a distance.

A small tooth, small enough to be mistaken for a human's, glittered in the artificial sunlight.

'This is serious! The legend was true!'

If Ymir was such a giant, even a single tooth could create a new entity, so Mun-gyeong hurriedly drew her sword.

"What is it?"

When there was no reaction for a while, Jix offered his opinion.

"Ymir is the only being in Heaven's army who can choose the battlefield. Perhaps his will is preventing rebirth."

"Hmph, how dull."

Feeling insulted, Seong-eum tossed the tooth, and Jix hastily caught it with both hands.

"La Enemy is not here. It's unfortunate, but we must move on."

From the beginning, only Jin Seong-eum could explore all three labyrinths.

"Then, where will you go?"

Andre was closer in distance, but for Seong-eum, who could leap across stars, it didn't matter.

Seong-eum looked beyond the horizon and said,

"I will go to meet Shirone."
