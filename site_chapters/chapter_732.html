Chapter 732:

Ivory Tower Artificial City

The seven stars summoned by Great Star were discussing the world, having lost track of time.

"Yes, I will do that."

Many issues were addressed.

"But…"

From ancient history to the present, it seemed Great Star knew everything, and the stars' questions finally approached the essence.

"What does Ra truly want?"

At Tzuoi's question, the six stars fell into thought.

"He wants to become a god."

Heukgangsi's answer was close to incorrect, as it did not resolve the question.

Mini asked again,

"What is the definition of a god? A creator? If so, Ra cannot become a god."

Borbor, still lying like a spider, looked at Mini sitting on his head.

"Our existence implies that there is already a creator. A god is a god; we cannot become gods."

A god is a god.

Ghost Goblin Arte fanned himself.

"If we can create something, we are no different from gods. It's just a cosmic thought. A true god would be something that transcends the cosmos."

Dreamer Luber looked at Great Star.

"I'd like to hear your thoughts. What do you think Ra wants?"

"Righteousness."

Great Star raised her index finger.

"If a god created the universe, it means that god is absolutely right in this universe. Whatever he does is right. Therefore, if Ra realizes absolute righteousness, he can also be called a god."

"A god is absolutely right."

Luber raised his head with a dreamy look.

"When we dream, everything is right. Perhaps a god is a being who dreams an eternal, unbroken dream."

"Yes, our nobility lies in that."

For the first time, Great Star stood up and looked down at the planet shining below the glass floor.

"From the universe to nature, from nature to the biological world. In the cosmic hierarchy, we are infinitely weak. But we are beings who dream."

Great Star looked at the stars waiting for her words with kind eyes.

"We can dream our own universe. Even if this place is a god's dream, we must continue to dream."

In the solemn atmosphere, Great Star, who had closed her eyes for a moment, slowly opened her lips.

"The time when that dream breaks is approaching."

In the world of nothingness outside the Gate of Extinction, Nane had come to organize many thoughts.

Ra Enemy's true form still lay motionless as if dead, and at some point, his events were transmitted to Nane's mind.

"Have you organized your thoughts?"

Nane answered the past Ra Enemy.

"Already."

"Then why do you struggle? What attachment remains for you who have realized emptiness?"

The world Nane realized was ultimately a series of sufferings, and at the end of that suffering was eternal nothingness.

"Annihilation is always right. Except for one thing…"

Nane approached Ra Enemy's true form.

"That it is irreversible."

There was no hesitation in accepting Ra Enemy's proposal, but it was irreversible.

"Because it is irreversible, it is absolutely right."

That was also true.

"To save sentient beings who groan in pain. If that is my last mission…"

Ra Enemy continued.

"Swallow my dream. Become the irreversible righteousness and save the sentient beings you desire."

Because it never existed, it is empty.

'Why do you suffer?'

Pleasure, pain, unhappiness, happiness, all are illusions in a dream that leave no substance once passed.

"Awaken."

Nane decided to become a Buddha himself and enlighten the minds of sentient beings who suffer in the secular world.

"Now it is your dream."

As Ra Enemy's true form dissolved into particles of light and merged with Nane, his Kar score began to rise endlessly.

Kar Score: 99.9999999999…%

From Nane's body, which had taken the seal of preaching, blades close to infinity spread out in a circle.

"Only those who realize can escape the cosmic suffering."

Nane was almost right.

Therefore, his words became a powerful truth that influenced this world…

"Open your eyes."

As his tongue moved, the world of nothingness shattered with cracks of light.

"W-what is that?"

As Kanis's eyes widened, Harvest grabbed him and Arin and moved away at tremendous speed.

Even so, Kanis couldn't take his eyes off it.

"What on earth is happening…"

A field of energy mixed with red and black light was expanding endlessly in a spherical shape.

As the explorers stationed near Paitaros scattered in all directions, Arin conveyed a shocking truth.

"Kanis, the boundary… is disappearing."

"What boundary?"

"The boundary of the superview. I can see something peeling away. Probably what you see is the same as what I see."

Arin's superview penetrates the veil of the inner world by grasping the nature inherent in matter.

'That means…'

Reality and unreality were merging.

"Kanis, something is coming out of there."

The energy field Harvest pointed to was so large that it exceeded 100 meters in diameter.

"Damn! Demons! Everyone, prepare for battle!"

From that vast space, hideous inhabitants of the inner world appeared, shouting incomprehensible words.

"Impress our terror! Conquer this world!"

It was the cannon fire announcing the beginning of the Great Purification.

'Finally, it's here!'

Buddha Mahagaruta, who guarded the room of Great Star in the Ivory Tower, opened his eyes wide.

As the brilliant light he emitted calmed down, his pupils, which were unlike those of humans, appeared.

"Has the time come?"

As he unfolded his legs from the lotus position and placed his feet on the floor, the surrounding scenery poured down like a waterfall.

"Great Star?"

The seven stars gathered in the room turned their heads to see Mahagaruta, who had entered the artificial city.

'Mahagaruta? No, he's not human.'

Mahagaruta's eyes, which had lost their luster, had two pupils in one eyeball.

"You have come, Mahagaruta."

Great Star greeted him with a sad smile, and Mahagaruta bowed his head with his hands together.

"Just now, Nane became a Buddha."

"What? What did you say?"

Tzuoi jumped up, and Borbor and Mini opened their mouths in disbelief.

Great Star replied calmly.

"I see. So… you are leaving?"

"There cannot be two righteousnesses in one universe. That is the dialectic of thesis, antithesis, and synthesis."

Nane had conquered the entire world, and it undoubtedly included Mahagaruta's righteousness.

Arte asked,

"What will happen to the altar?"

"Nane has swallowed Ra Enemy's dream. Soon, altars around the world will open, and reality and unreality will no longer be separate."

Dreamer Luber said,

"People will wake from their individual dreams and dream only Nane's dream. It will be a nightmare. Many will suffer."

"A nightmare is still just a dream."

Mahagaruta understood Nane.

"If I am one righteousness, Nane is another. He chose to liberate those who dream from the suffering of this world."

Tzuoi said,

"I don't want that. I just want to live and suffer."

"That is also an illusion created by suffering. Once the suffering is gone, you will finally understand."

At the irreversible moment, it becomes the truth.

"Nane saw from the farthest distance. Life endlessly reincarnates and endlessly suffers. From a distance, this universe is also just one life form. That life form is wandering in eternal suffering. Without any meaning."

Mahagaruta looked around the universe.

"It is not wrong for someone to end it. Annihilation is always right. Except for the fact that it is irreversible."

Because it is irreversible, Mahagaruta could not choose it.

"Now my righteousness will disappear, and Nane's righteousness will guide the world. I can only hope that his 'righteousness' is right."

Great Star said,

"If he is wrong…"

There was only one case where Nane could be wrong.

"What if all living beings establish an integrated mental system and reject him with one will?"

Seeing Great Star still not giving up hope, Mahagaruta looked troubled.

To have life means to be selfish, and therefore we can never become one.

"…Find a new star. Surely there will be someone to take my place."

Great Star’s eyes became moist.

"I will miss you."

A kind smile appeared on Mahagaruta's lips, and finally, his eyes flashed with light.

"…"

As if he had never existed, the place where Buddha had vanished left the stars' minds in turmoil.

'Where did he go?'

To the world of nothingness, or perhaps beyond even that.

Arte asked,

"Great Star, please give us guidance. What should the Ivory Tower do?"

"I will observe the surface."

Taking a deep breath to calm herself, Great Star looked down at the planet below the glass floor with sharp eyes.

'What is happening?'

Great Star’s body, which had been gradually becoming blurry, finally turned transparent like air and disappeared.

The two-star residents stood with their mouths agape.

'This is Great Star's ability.'

She could feel all the events happening on this planet as if they were touching her skin.

'Gaia.'

Because she was this planet itself.

Ymir's presence was overwhelming.

"Kahahaha! This is thrilling!"

Despite the combined attack of Shirone, Jin Sung Eum, Rian, and Kido, his punches shattered the cave walls.

'This is chaos. The wind pressure alone could kill me.'

Dinai, the earthbound spirit, was crushing the ground like a swamp, but Ymir didn't even pretend to try to escape.

"Pitiful creatures…!”

Ymir, pushing his body further into the ground up to his waist, was struck in the face by Rian’s fist.

'Yes, this is it.'

The sparks exploding in his mind sent shivers down Ymir's spine.

'This is what it feels like to be alive.'

What if he had to live in a world where there was nothing heavier than himself?

What if the strongest stimulus was just a gentle breeze tickling his skin?

'I would have gone mad.'

For Ymir, Rian’s punches were a pleasure, even in pain.

"Here I come!"

Without any chance for Mind Strike to activate, the monsters' exchange of blows made Mun Kyung tremble.

'Damn it! I can't even intervene.'

Even the insignificant goblin was contributing to controlling Ymir's actions, hurting his pride.

"More! Hit me more!"

Ymir, fully excited, suddenly found his fist disappearing and striking his own face.

"…"

The impact was tremendous, but his body had durability that surpassed the limits of living beings.

"Such a pathetic…"

Ymir's gaze turned to Sung Eum, who was deploying an ether wave and panting.

'It doesn't work. What kind of body is this?'

After striking Rian’s solar plexus, Ymir turned and strode towards Sung Eum.

"That's why I should deal with you first."

This kind of battle was not fun.

"Shirone, I'll bind Ymir's feet. Attack him. If we combine our strength, we have a chance."

Instead of a reply, there was the sound of someone collapsing to their knees.

"Hnnng!"

When Sung Eum turned around, she saw Shirone gritting his teeth, his hands trembling.

"What's wrong? You weren't even hit…"

Sung Eum's heart sank.

'He's going to explode.'

As Shirone's face turned from pale to transparent, the entrances to the 19,000 worlds began to tremble simultaneously.

'It's coming. Something so immense…'

Insight.

The Wheel of Emptiness was coming to an end.
