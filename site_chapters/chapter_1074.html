Chapter 1074: The Only Chance (4)

West Country Guild Rank 1, Ascension

At the top of a 23-story building, seven guild members were preparing for a raid.

They were all rankers.

"Is everyone here?"

As the thick door, like a hangar, opened, the Operator walked down the expansive runway.

Yagwi said, "Eight members in total. It's our first time taking on Arachne with this setup, but we need to push hard. The Solar War is coming up."

He had chosen a monstrous form, with a flat, diamond-shaped face like a shield and a cloak.

"Yes."

As the Operator walked like a cat, the long sword on her back swayed side to side.

'Yahweh2, huh.'

She had checked the hit news on the global bulletin board.

'I will never lose!'

As she clenched her fists, the High Gear cycle rapidly surged, and her machine vibrated.

All members, prepare for deployment.

High Gear, level 487.

With immense output, her machine shot forward like a cannon.

Leaping at supersonic speed, she passed the entire city and landed in the wasteland.

The sprint resumed, and the sonic boom made West Country users turn their heads.

"What? How is that machine so fast?"

"Idiot, it's the Ascension Guild. Operator, Little Witch, Yagwi, Taedaecheon… they're all rankers."

The Operator's AI showed a blue sea.

Switching to high-speed mode.

Rocket engines emerged from her deeply indented back, spraying immense pressure.

Four minutes later, the raid point, Skull Island, came into view for the guild members.

'Trap zone.'

The control tower on the island lit up, and a massive red laser net surged forward.

Trap level - 467.

She could evade with AI, but she disabled the function and relied on her senses.

Zing! Zing! Zing!

As the laser net narrowly missed the Operator, a guild member whistled.

"Her combat sense is the best."

After the trap zone, Little Witch sped up to catch up with the Operator.

"What's going on? You usually trust the system. AI would save power."

"…I don't like the feeling of dulled senses."

"Hmm."

She answered, but it wasn't the truth.

"Yahweh."

The Operator spoke again.

"What kind of person is he? In reality, I mean."

"Surprising. You mentioned reality. Does it bother you that much? You'll win anyway."

"A video was posted on the bulletin board. He bypassed space using the designer's code. Is that possible?"

Little Witch seemed to smile.

"I don't know. I'm just a user. What I can tell you is that in reality, he's someone who can do even more easily. He's the greatest mage in human history."

The Operator replayed the video. 'He took down an enemy over 50 levels higher. Of course, he was equipped with Grim Reaper…'

With similar durability, penetration was possible.

'In other words, it was a situation where energy beams couldn't win. Beyond special abilities, he's a human with overwhelming combat experience.'

The Operator muttered, "Even so, this is my world."

At that moment, the island shook, and a colossal machine emerged from the forest.

"Graaaah!"

Boss creature Arachne.

A colossal weapon at level 588 appeared, and the AI transmitted the creature's information.

Durability: 78,000,000/78,000,000.

The spider-like machine, weighing hundreds of thousands of tons, opened 8,000 gun ports to greet her.

All members, standby. I'll signal.

Flying solo, she saw 20,000 bullets darkening the scenery.

Bullet barrage level, 499.

It was beyond what AI could handle.

'I won't lose.'

Dodging the barrage with acrobatic flight, she landed on the island, where small creatures swarmed.

Though small, their speed at an average level of 500 couldn't be ignored.

"Haaa!"

The Operator twisted her body.

Every time she changed direction, it felt like the machine was screaming in agony.

'Closer, closer!'

As she made a sudden stop, 27 tons of pressure hit her right knee, triggering a warning message.

Unstable durability detected.

Sliding on the ground, she twisted her body at the limit to disperse the force.

Little Witch smirked.

'A monster is a monster.'

With extreme combat nerves, the Operator analyzed all the creatures' attack patterns.

'Shirone senses, but the Operator calculates.'

Who is superior?

Sliding backward, the Operator braked with her left hand gripping the ground.

Sparks flew from her fingers.

Slowing down near Arachne, she grabbed the handle of the black long sword on her back.

'Physical attack here.'

As Arachne raised its leg, a massive shadow loomed over her.

Collapse (Magnetic Force).

A level 480 selective option, currently only activated with the black long sword.

'This timing is the only chance.'

As the blade struck the ground, a black magnetic field swelled into a massive sphere.

"Graaaah!"

Caught in the magnetic field, Arachne twisted its hundreds of thousands of joints, struggling.

Rumble!

Finally, four legs were forcibly bent, and the massive body crashed to the ground.

All seven guild members attacked simultaneously.

"Let's make it hot!"

Sun-like flashes erupted over the island, and the seawater surged like a wall.

That Evening

Atogram was bustling with users from all over the continent.

Their main interest was whether Yahweh2's group would switch from the Grim Reaper.

Reporters were gathered in front of the Amara shop, where it was expected they would upgrade at level 70.

"This is RubyRuby from East Country. Many of you have seen the videos, but the burning question is whether Yahweh2's group has reached level 70. As a personal reporter, I'll try to get an interview."

The voices of users saying the same thing mixed, making it noisier than a marketplace.

A man quieted them down.

"Look, there they are!"

Yahweh2's group, equipped with the Grim Reaper, was walking quickly into the shopping district.

Death Princess complained, "I'm tired. This is exhausting."

"We're not done yet. We need to reach level 80 by dawn to meet the deadline."

Reporters swarmed them.

"Hello! This is RubyRuby! What's your current level? Why are you doing this? Is it a secret project of Golden Wheel?"

"Move! We're busy!"

Destroyer707 pushed RubyRuby aside, but the reporters followed closely.

"Just a few words! Is this for the record?"

Ignoring the barrage of questions, they entered a shop, but it wasn't Amara; it was Suicide Corp.

Death Princess asked, "Aren't we switching to Amara?"

"Amara is the best for hunting level 70 creatures at level 70. But we're raising the difficulty. We're heading to a level 100 hunting ground, so speed is crucial. More importantly…"

Destroyer707 turned his head.

"Yahweh2 has different abilities. As long as the output keeps up, he can quickly take down level 100 creatures."

"Ah."

After ordering from the manager, the items were quickly delivered, and they immediately replaced their parts.

With a clear sound, the Grim Reaper parts rolled around them.

A moment of silence followed.

'Will they discard those too? They probably will.'

Unlike the Hundred General set, these parts were coveted even by intermediate users.

True to the company's focus on speed, Suicide's parts were sharp and cold.

They were also specialized in melee weapons, with performance depending on the user's skill.

'What a waste.'

Destroyer707 felt a pang of regret.

"Should we keep the Grim Reaper? It's a real masterpiece. If we leave it here…"

Users would swarm like bees.

"Let's just go. We'd have to visit a storage service. We can buy it again later with the credit card."

"True, but…"

The problem was the users eagerly waiting for Yahweh2's group to leave.

"Alright! Let's go!"

As soon as they stepped out, the shop became noisy.

"Tch! They're having a blast."

Shirone consoled Destroyer707.

"I regret it too. But we can't solve problems without giving up something. Every goal has risks, so let's stay focused on our objective."

"Hmph! Easy for you to say. You sound just like Bart. You really resemble him."

"Bart?"

"My personal magic tutor. He doesn't teach much but always says boring things."

Death Princess showed interest.

"Oh? You learn magic?"

"Learn? Not really. Spirit Zone or whatever, I can't grasp it no matter what."

"Haha! Same here. My dad made me try, but I just got sleepy when I concentrated."

Shirone looked weary.

'I thought they weren't ordinary kids if they got into the undercoder, but having Bart as a personal tutor…'

If it was Brian Bart, he was a top-tier archmage.

"Well, it can't be helped now. Let's level up quickly. Suicide's main weapons are melee. Aim for the gaps in the armor, and be careful; the durability is low."

"Hmm, got it."

Entering the level 100 hunting ground, they saw medium-sized creatures unlike before.

Screech!

As the quadrupedal enemies charged, Shirone smirked.

'I can see it.'

Flowing smoothly like water among the vicious creatures, Shirone's machine cut them down.

Two days passed as promised.

"Huff! Huff!"

Arriving in Atogram, they were greeted with applause from users.

"Level 100 in 48 hours! It's a record!"

Destroyer707 collapsed to the ground.

"We did it. We really did it."

Feeling a thrill from the unbelievable achievement, Aegis approached.

"Congratulations. We've been waiting."

"It's Aegis! Take pictures!"

Reporters rushed in, but as they got close, they all grimaced.

"Ugh!"

It was a selective option, one of the few implemented in the city, causing radio interference.

"Sorry, but we'll exercise our right to refuse unauthorized filming."

Aegis politely announced and bowed to Shirone.

"Let's go."

Arriving in the northern slums, she entered a much shabbier bar than before.

Golden Wheel's first team was seated, and the bartender opened a door to a deep passage.

Sensing the serious atmosphere, Shirone asked, "Is this the real start?"

"Yes. The boss will explain the details. Oh, and there's another guest."

"Guest?"

"Please, go in."

Opening the iron door at the end of the passage, they saw Yolga's Son sitting on the bed, raising his hand.

"Congratulations. Level 100 in two days. Impressive."

Not in the mood for a handshake, Shirone raised an eyebrow and entered the room.

"Huh?"

There was an unfamiliar user at the tea table.

'Is this the guest?'

Short legs dangled from the chair, with hands resting between them.

The face was unreadable.

"Hello? It's been a while."

"Do you… know me?"

Shirone used the search function but found limited information, indicating a West Country user.

User codename, Little Witch.
