Chapter 341: Trinity (2)

As his breathing became labored, Shirone’s oxygen consumption skyrocketed.

Remaining oxygen consumption time: 1 minute 17 seconds. 1 minute 3 seconds. 42 seconds.

Light flew from all directions and pierced Shirone’s body.

With the approach of the law blocked, it tried to penetrate his mind directly through physical means.

Armand reacted immediately.

Observing nerve junction points. Docking and copying.

Armand's tendrils connected with the needles of light. Then even more light surged and pierced Shirone’s body.

Dozens, hundreds.

The endless light demanded one thing.

Disintegrate.

"Ugh!"

Shirone’s face twisted in fear.

The feeling of his existence being absorbed by another was as humiliating as being eaten alive by a beast.

Remaining oxygen consumption time: 4 seconds. 3 seconds. 2 seconds.

At this rate, he would be absorbed.

"How dare you...!"

Shirone focused his mind on a single point.

As the Immortal Function opened, his infinite mind spread throughout the world of law.

Detecting immeasurable focal point. Penetrating.

Armand's organic matter multiplied infinitely. The tendrils extended and docked with all the thorns piercing Shirone.

Armand copied the information trying to hack its master's brain and began constructing a suitable firewall based on it.

Finally, Shirone and the Ice Queen shared a single world as individual subjects of the law.

Firewall complete. Activating.

Light burst from Shirone’s retina.

Small World Creation.

"Where is this?"

Shirone’s looked around.

It was a dark space, like the world of law. The difference was that there were no twinkling stars.

But Shirone could see.

'How can I see?'

Is it possible to see in a place without light?

'Maybe...'

Shirone found an intriguing answer.

Memory. There is no light in memory.

Light does not linger in the past.

But he was in reality.

Therefore, this place was a space where all time unfolded. It was the entirety of time, where past, present, and future could not be distinguished.

As he took a step, countless Shirones emerged from the curtain of darkness.

"What is this?"

He turned around. It was as if infinite versions of himself were revolving, like a space expanded with mirrors.

Shirones pointed to one and approached. As the distance shortened, the perspective narrowed, and countless Shirones lined up in parallel.

There stood a version of himself with black pupils and an evil face.

Shirone reached out. The other Shirone also reached out.

He felt the cold texture of a glass wall.

'Is this me?'

No, this is not me.

Where is this place? Outside the mirror, or inside? Am I the one looking at the mirror, or the one reflected in it?

"Ah..."

Shirone finally realized.

"This is..."

Both Shirones spoke simultaneously.

"You."

Crash!

All the glass walls that had been expanding in infinite parallel lines shattered at the speed of light.

With the sound of breaking glass, the boundaries of the mirrors began to destroy at the speed of light.

Shirone and the other Shirone’s hands touched. But before he could feel the sensation, the other Shirone disappeared.

Within the Ice Queen's law, Shirone and the other Shirone referred to each other, achieving a perfect trinity.

The evil Shirone with black pupils was no more. Only the absolute, unshakable Shirone remained.

In heaven, this technique was called Incarnation. But mages considered it a state of mind and called it this:

Mental Transcendence.

The protagonist of life (1)

Having completed the trinity, Shirone’s eyes snapped open. Simultaneously, through the multi-law network Small World Creation, Armand began to counterattack the Ice Queen's law.

The world shook, and starlight flared. Unable to withstand Shirone, the Ice Queen extended her neck and retched.

"Ugh! Ugh! Ugh!"

Amy's face turned pale as she watched from the ground. The sight of the giant face straining as if its eyes would pop out was beyond horrifying.

"Ugh!"

With one final effort, the Ice Queen vomited out Shirone, who was covered in tendrils. He spread his Light Wings and hovered, the first thing he did was inhale deeply.

"Ha! Ha!"

For the first time, he realized that even air had a taste.

"Shirone! Shirone is back alive!"

Amy's face, which had been contorted in pain, finally brightened. The grotesqueness of the Ice Queen couldn't overshadow the joy of Shirone’s safe return.

But Dante's expression remained serious.

Above all, the Ice Queen's condition was strange.

The Ice Queen was making a grotesque expression that human facial muscles couldn't create. But it was the most accurate representation of her current emotions.

'Infinite? Infinite? Infinite? Infinite? Infinite?'

The Ice Queen was confused.

'What is that?'

As Armand constructed the firewall, the Ice Queen also copied Shirone’s information.

But there was a problem.

Even with Optros' vast information, it was difficult to fully analyze the Immortal Function.

As the immense euphoria, surpassing the scale of the law, surged in, the Ice Queen couldn't control her gaping mouth.

She was heading towards infinity.

That was the only fact the Ice Queen could grasp.

Her endlessly widening jaw exceeded the range of her joints and began to reverse, swallowing herself.

Like a grape peeling its skin and revealing the flesh, a new entity emerged from her throat.

'I am infinite.'

With human faces on three sides and dozens of arms sprouting from her shoulders, spreading like a peacock's feathers.

Three-faced Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara.

It was Optros' second dedication, having absorbed the Immortal Function.

"Grrrr!"

The sound of thousands of beetles crying erupted from the laughing mouth of the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara.

"Ugh!"

Hit directly by the sudden sound wave, Shirone adjusted his hearing through Armand and widened the distance. The Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara's neck turned, and a face with raised eyebrows and a downturned mouth took the front. As the law of anger erupted, countless fireballs shot out in all directions.

"Watch out!"

As Liria pointed to the falling fire rain, Dante spread a massive defensive magic circle above their heads. As the fireballs struck, a loud crack echoed.

"Ugh! Damn it!"

Dante's expression was grim. In an isolated space, a mental entity's growth is limited, but the entity that emerged after swallowing Shirone had a law far stronger than the Ice Queen's. It was likely the Immortal Function. Indeed, there was no better stimulant for a law-absorbing entity than Shirone.

"Deploy Pakyojin immediately!"

Liria watched the battle in the sky with confused eyes. Shirone was firing photon cannons, but they didn't even scratch the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara's body.

"Not yet. The range of Pakyojin is much narrower than the sealing formation. If we can't lure it here, it's better to die here."

Amy shouted towards the sky.

"Shirone! Push the monster this way!"

Armand copied Amy's voice. The noisy voice was transmitted to Shirone’s brain.

"Push it?"

It meant they had found a way on the ground. But how could he push a monster that didn't even flinch from the photon cannons?

'I have no choice.'

Shirone focused on increasing the power of the photon cannons. Then the mental transcendence expanded greatly.

The Immortal Function could borrow infinite mental power but couldn't increase its power beyond the Spirit Zone.

But mental transcendence forcibly strengthened the Spirit Zone.

Of course, like divine transcendence, the mental backlash that followed was a separate issue.

Just as Rian’s arm exploded when he swung his sword faster than his body could handle, attempting more than his capacity could lead to mental destruction.

Expanding focal point. Activating overdrive.

But Shirone didn't hesitate. Feeling an immense output he had never experienced before, he extended his hand, and dozens of flashes struck the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara's torso directly.

"Grrrr!"

The body of the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara, which had been immobile, began to be pushed back slightly. As it fired sound waves in all directions, Shirone didn't stop his assault. In fact, the power was increasing over time.

250 meters to the Pakyojin installation point.

Those watching the battle from the ground were mesmerized by the sight of dozens of photon cannons flashing.

"How can he use such magic...?"

Liria, the mage, was the most shocked. She classified mental entities as demigods because they could freely manipulate the law. Law entities must be sealed with law. But Shirone was pushing back a demigod with sheer physical force.

"...I thought he was just an innocent kid."

Dante gave a bitter smile at Liria's misconception. Judging Shirone by his appearance led to this. He had no interest in fashion, and he was a rustic boy who calmly sewed in a café. But he was the only boy who had defeated him.

"Well, he's the coolest when he's a mage."

Liria was conflicted. The distance the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara was being pushed was visibly shortening. If the photon cannons didn't get stronger, it would be impossible to push it to the Pakyojin installation point.

"Liria. Deploy Pakyojin. Whether Shirone succeeds or fails, if we have to do something, now is the last chance."

'What should I do...?'

If Pakyojin fails, the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara will escape into the world. Even if the kingdom prepares, given the scale of the law, a small city could be devastated in a matter of hours.

Liria turned to Dante and said,

"I'll prepare Pakyojin."

They had to rely on Shirone. The people here were just as precious as those in the outside world. This was different from not killing the monsters.

"Alright. Here's the plan. If Shirone can push it 50 meters closer, I'll set up a defensive magic circle to trap the monster. It won't be as strong as the sealing formation, but it will provide some safety. Meanwhile, Amy, prepare a spell to support Shirone with firepower."

Amy nodded.

"Got it. I'll cast the strongest spell I have."

Jocre watched them desperately preparing to fight. The contrast with his own helplessness made his anger rise.

Why were they risking their lives?

They could just leave. He would bear all the consequences, and the kingdom would handle the aftermath.

"Why are you fighting?"

Dante, who was protecting Liria, turned his head.

"What are you talking about? If you're thinking of interfering, I'll take you down first."

"Why are you fighting? You haven't done anything wrong, so you could just run away! Don't you want to live? You're so strong, with such natural talent! You have a bright future ahead of you, and you want to die here for nothing?"

Dante looked at Jocre in disbelief, then sighed as if he understood.

"Did you learn magic because you wanted to succeed?"

"What?"

"Isn't it the other way around? You learned magic because you liked it, and you became good at it, so success followed. Wouldn't you feel sick if you ran away now? I'm just fighting with the magic I learned. So don't interfere and just watch quietly."

Amy, having finished her calculations, opened her eyes.

"It's done. I'll start now."

Her red eyes emitted a crimson glow, and two pillars of fire rose from the ground, merging into a massive fireball. It was an Inferno several times larger than the one she had used in the Radius of the Spirit.

Jocre stared blankly at the blazing fire, then lowered his head as he realized something.

'These people are crazy about magic...'
