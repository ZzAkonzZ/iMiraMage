Chapter 609:

At 4 AM sharp, Shirone threw off his blanket and got out of bed.

It was still dark outside, but he could hear the bustling sounds of his peers moving around in the hallway.

Those sounds of life broke the night's silence and seemed to invigorate him.

Shirone went to the bathroom, washed himself with warm water, and stood in front of the mirror in his underwear.

'I should have cut my hair.'

When you spend all day focused on magic, eating and sleeping become the only things that matter.

'This is just the beginning.'

As he buttoned his white shirt, he felt a renewed sense of determination with each button.

Arian Shirone (Final Rank: 27th in the graduating class).

Major: Photon Magic, Divine Particle Series.

Special Notes: Immortal Function, Prajna, Ataraxia, Elysion, Temporal Destruction, Perception through Temporal Reversal.

'Should I tighten it one more notch?'

Amy pulled the skirt button at her waist as tight as she could.

She had lost more weight since last year, and even with her blouse tucked in, her waist looked as slim as an ant's.

'Second graduation exam.'

When she failed last year, she couldn't see how she would endure another year.

'I did my best.'

Time eventually comes.

When that day finally arrived, she didn't want to have any regrets, so she had trained desperately every day, and now those days were paying off with a sense of satisfaction.

'I couldn't have prepared more perfectly than this.'

Even so, the pass rate was one in three.

Still aiming for the top of the daunting pyramid, Amy bit her hairband and put up her hair.

Carmis Amy (Final Rank: 4th in the graduating class).

Major: Fire Magic, Sniper Series.

Special Notes: Jack O' Lantern, Fire Spirit using Spirit's Battery, Magnetic Backup Ability with Red Eyes, Magic Enhancement through Samadhi.

With a click, Fermi buttoned the sleeve of his suit and looked at himself in the mirror.

He was used to wearing suits, so it didn't feel awkward.

'This year, I'll have to stop dealing with students.'

With the dissolution of the Golden Wheel, it was impossible to expect significant profits from the school.

'It's time to leave, Mother.'

The large sum he received in exchange for extracting Valhalla Action would be transformed into numerous high-level spells to be showcased in the graduation exam.

Fermi winked at his reflection in the mirror.

"Looking sharp."

It was time for the big fish that had been lurking beneath the surface of the school to swim out into the open waters.

Ardino Fermi (Final Rank: 1st in the graduating class).

Major: Non-standard Magic.

Special Notes: Magic Collection Ability through Depreciation Transactions.

"That's it."

Arin pinned the Alpheas Magic School badge onto Kanis's suit.

As if it had been absorbed into the clothes, the magical creature Harvest revealed its shadow and stood up.

"Heh heh, I thought I'd die waiting. Are we finally leaving this wretched school?"

"You don't feel boredom."

"That's just a figure of speech. I can't believe I've been at this school for over a year. If Arcane heard, he'd be rolling in his grave."

'Master.'

Kanis thought of the face of the Archmage Arcane, an authority on dark magic.

The end wasn't good, but thanks to him, Kanis had a chance at a new life.

Kanis (Final Rank: 12th in the graduating class).

Major: Dark Magic, Physical Series.

Special Notes: Disciple of the Archmage Arcane, Decipherer of the Book of Light and Darkness, Subjugator of the Magical Creature Harvest.

"Yes, we'll definitely pass. Both of us."

Arin smiled at Kanis's words.

She had always worn cheap clothes, so she hadn't realized it, but in her neat uniform, she exuded a surprisingly mature beauty.

"You look great, Kanis."

"You look pretty too. But… what do you mean by 'great'?"

Arin tilted her head and smiled.

"That's a secret."

Arin (Final Rank: 8th in the graduating class).

Major: Dark Magic, Mental Series.

Special Notes: Disciple of the Archmage Arcane, Decipherer of the Book of Light and Darkness, Extreme Mental Assimilation through Insight.

"Shirone, are you ready?"

Following a knock, Iruki's voice came through the door.

"Yeah, I'm coming out now."

When he opened the door, Iruki and Nade, both neatly dressed, were waiting.

"Oh, looking good."

It was the first time Shirone, Iruki, and Nade had worn uniforms that complied with the association's regulations.

"You too. You look different."

Iruki's face, which others might find strange, now looked like a unique feature.

"The current time is 4:50:32. Let's go. The association only opens the long-distance spatial transfer magic circle for one hour."

Mercodine Iruki (Final Rank: 5th in the graduating class).

Major: Explosion Magic, Detonation Series.

Special Notes: Savant Syndrome, High-Speed Detonation Equation Calculation, Cancellation, Nuclear Fusion through Double Spirit Zone Integration.

"Alright."

As Shirone stepped out, he looked back at Nade.

"But… are you okay?"

Oscar worked at the Creas branch of the Magic Association.

Meeting him while his emotional wounds were still fresh would be tough for Nade.

"I'm fine. It's already over."

Nade was smiling, but he couldn't hide his bitter expression.

West Nade (Final Rank: 21st in the graduating class).

Major: Electric Magic, Lightning Series.

Special Notes: Application of Alchemical Magic Engineering, Controllable Magic Assimilation in Specific Situations.

'Still, I made it to 21st place.'

It was possible because five of the Scramble Royal participants, except for Anchal, had all dropped below Nade in rank.

This also resulted in a significant rise in the rankings of the lower-ranked students.

'It's all mixed up. Honestly, rankings might not mean much anymore.'

When they arrived at the assembly point, it was still dark, but the students arriving one by one were using light magic, making it easy to find the location.

"You're early."

Amy waved as she approached.

Shirone and his friends, who were about to greet her, were momentarily speechless at her mature demeanor.

"What? Why are you staring at me like that? You're the same."

Amy was just as awkward.

"A uniform assembly. This new head of the Magic Association is quite the eccentric."

Kaiden approached.

Since the dissolution of the Golden Wheel, Amy was one of the few people he still talked to.

"Oh? But you…"

Amy noticed the cross sword at Kaiden's waist and pointed at it.

"It's a family symbol. I always carry it."

Shirone asked.

"But the notice said no equipment of any kind. I even left my Cubrick behind."

Kaiden lifted the cross sword and cast a spell, causing the blade to glow brightly and then disappear.

"Alchemy?"

Nade immediately recognized it.

"I didn't use it last year because it was a hassle, but now I have a reason to leave the school."

Kaiden reformed the cross sword from metal, and Amy looked at it curiously.

"You're good. When did you learn that?"

"This morning. It was easy."

"Annoying. Should I kill you?"

Amy spoke her thoughts out loud, but Kaiden just chuckled.

Cross Kaiden (Final Rank: 26th in the graduating class).

Major: Magic Swordsman.

Special Notes: Law of the Red Cross Star, Ability to Fully Utilize Potential in All Fields through Sacrifice.

"Good morning, senior!"

Mark's voice broke the dawn's silence.

"How do you feel?"

"Great. I'm not nervous at all."

Mark's combative nature, which he had shown on the bridge, was acknowledged, and that's why he had challenged the graduation exam.

"Let's do our best. And you don't need to call me senior in the graduating class."

"I know. Just until today. I'll beat you in the graduation exam."

Slider Mark (Final Rank: 29th in the graduating class).

Major: Earth Magic, Strike Series.

Special Notes: Thaad's Disciple, Gambler, Unrestricted Combativeness.

'Of course, he wouldn't have decided without a plan.'

Having nothing to lose was a significant psychological weapon.

Mark also had Maria, the only student in the graduating class specializing in healing magic.

"Maria, you too, hang in there."

"Thanks. I'm not very confident, but…"

Her gentle nature was unchanged, but she was different from the girl who had trembled in fear while operating the bridge's mechanism.

"I've grown too."

Erlang Maria (Final Rank: 30th in the graduating class).

Major: Healing Magic, High-Speed Activation Series.

Special Notes: Thaad's Disciple, Longest-Serving Student in the Advanced Class, Capable of Using Advanced Magic in All Fields.

"Dante's coming."

At someone's words, everyone turned their heads.

Although they referred to Dante, it included Sabina and Closer, who had transferred from the Royal Magic School.

Closer, who had confirmed the assembly point, turned his head.

"…How was Alpheas Magic School?"

Dante looked up at the dawn sky, recalling his year and a half journey.

"At first, I underestimated it."

Then he lost to Shirone in the 2000th duel and waited for the day to redeem himself.

"The competition was fiercer than expected."

The average ability was 3.8 times higher than other prestigious schools.

"Still, it wasn't particularly different. It was just as boring."

Airhine Dante (Final Rank: 2nd in the graduating class).

Major: Photon Magic, Information Series.

Special Notes: Olivia's disciple. Genius-level information entropy analysis (capable of calculating the information entropy of each individual in an ant colony). Operator of the integrated information processing system Pascal.

When Dante arrived, Boyle glared at him.

"What's this? The fashion-conscious Dante is strictly following the rules. Even the great Dante is afraid of going to the association?"

Dante looked Boyle up and down.

Boyle had neatly combed his hair with pomade and was wearing a suit that fit his plump body perfectly.

"True fashion is not about dressing up. It's about the face-off."

Boyle's face turned red from losing the face-off.

"Hmph, let's see if you're still confident after the exam. If you think the summoning beasts I’ve shown so far are all I've got, you're mistaken."

Chris Boyle (Final Rank: 17th in the graduating class).

Major: Summoning Magic, Beast Series.

Special Notes: Special affinity with beast species. Possesses a total of 18 summoning beasts.

'Of course.'

As a top student in the advanced class before Shirone arrived, Boyle likely had some hidden summoning beasts.

Meanwhile, Mark couldn't take his eyes off Closer.

'How dare he mess with me?'

Mark still remembered how Closer's dirty trick had beaten him when Dante's group first transferred, and it kept him awake at night.

'And in front of Maria, no less.'

Closer noticed Mark's gaze and bared his teeth.

"Hey, how do you feel? Finally made it to the graduating class? Though it won't last long."

"We're on the same level now."

"Heh, even if we're both in the earth series, we're on different levels."

"Want to know a secret? I chose the earth series just to beat you."

Closer's eyes widened.

"Then let me make a prediction. You'll be the worst failure in the graduation exam. I'll make sure of it."

Bonifar Closer (Final Rank: 15th in the graduating class).

Major: Earth Magic, Soil Series.

Special Notes: Olivia's disciple. Capable of soil transmutation, a type of alchemy.

'Ugh, macho smell.'

Ignoring the macho posturing, Sabina approached Nade as soon as she arrived at the assembly point.

"Hi."

No one else seemed to notice, but she could tell that Nade's demeanor had subtly changed.

Victor Sabina (Final Rank: 14th in the graduating class).

Major: Air Magic, Blow Series.

Special Notes: Olivia's disciple. Capable of aerodynamics control using the press series.
