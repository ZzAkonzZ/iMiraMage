Chapter 465:

The Dawn of Morning.

The large Tagis engines roared to life.

Powerful electromagnetic waves struck Heaven directly, completely paralyzing Heaven's Aegis air defense system.

"Charge!"

Under the command of the unified rebel headquarters, an army of 150,000 soldiers advanced towards the gates from the First Heaven to the Sixth Heaven, excluding Araboth.

Thus, the war with Heaven began.

"Senpai, this way."

Shirone and Plu successfully infiltrated the Third Heaven, Shehakim, where the immortal citizens resided.

Currently, the rebels were unable to break through Shehakim's gates, but that was part of the strategy.

The most important task was to transport Shirone, the Terminator, to Araboth.

It was an espionage operation to divert the enemy's attention to the outskirts and infiltrate a small number of personnel into the enemy's territory.

Shehakim, which they visited for the first time, was different from Shamain, where the citizens lived.

Although the races of Nor, Mecca, and Kergos were mixed, the place where they stayed was a standardized building called Paradise.

The white brick houses varied in size but were all beautiful.

The people walking around had abandoned their racial characteristics and wore the airy clothes of the immortals.

Immortality. Those who had escaped the terror of the elixir of myths.

Despite the ongoing war on the outskirts, their lives had not yet seen significant changes.

"Truly impressive. The confidence of the immortals?"

Plu observed them with a critical eye.

There was no sign of fear in the way they smiled kindly and chatted with the passersby.

"First, let's get some clothes. We can't walk around like this."

With only the two of them as combatants, they couldn't fight their way to Araboth.

The best strategy was to blend in with the crowd.

Shirone and Plu infiltrated the nearest Paradise.

Inside, a family was enjoying tea time by the indoor pool and the glass walls.

Shirone went around the back of the building and jumped to the second floor.

From the high vantage point, he could see many fairies moving about the city.

Given that the war had already begun, security was likely much tighter than before.

Meeting Plu's eyes, Shirone opened the window and entered.

They found Shehakim's clothing in the wardrobe and quickly changed.

After changing clothes and putting their original clothes in the Kubrick, the door opened.

"Huh? Who are you?"

Shirone and Plu froze, watching the girl.

Even with the Spirit Zone deployed, they hadn't sensed her presence.

"Could it be… are you spies?"

Shirone slowly stepped back.

She wasn't human.

It was clear they had walked into a trap from the start.

Plu summoned Phoenix to block the girl's approach.

They already knew she was incorporeal.

But the problem was that they couldn't find a way to escape.

The sense of reality was so strong that it was hard to believe she was fake.

'Is it a space-bound ability?'

Plu shouted.

"Get out of here!"

"Kyah!"

At the same time, the girl screamed and transformed.

Her innocent appearance vanished, replaced by the grotesquely twisted face of an evil witch, who lunged at them. Plu activated Phoenix Formation.

A bird of flame soared up, spewing massive fireballs at the girl.

As the fire spread, the scenery wavered like a mirage.

Shirone activated Vajra Armor in response to the distortion of the landscape beyond the limits of matter.

Regulation Exception - Paradise.

The ability of Orga, the head of citizen management in Shehakim.

A mental magic that materialized Paradise in the mind, causing horrific illusions for those who entered the building.

Shirone and Plu headed for the window they had originally entered through.

But outside the window was nothing but a void of darkness.

"We're completely trapped!"

Moments later, the door opened, and monsters with patchy hair, like lepers, rushed in.

"Graaaah!"

Shirone swung Arman's tendrils, cutting them down in one sweep.

The severed monsters were absorbed by the ability of the devouring ghost Kuzen, which converted them into energy, granting Shirone powerful physical abilities.

As they exited the room, monsters flooded the stairs.

When Shirone unleashed the Photon Cannon, the monsters' bodies caved in as if struck by a hammer, tumbling down the stairs.

Shirone jumped over the railing and descended to the hall.

Plu flew across the air and landed on the opposite railing.

The paintings on the ceiling materialized and attacked them.

Snakes, bats, gargoyles, and unknown creatures swarmed, turning the inside of Paradise into a battlefield.

No monster could inflict even a small wound on Shirone.

Arman's tendrils grew faster with Kuzen's absorption, eventually moving so fast they were invisible.

Only the sound of a strong wind echoed in the hall as countless monsters exploded like fireworks.

'This won't be enough to stop us. But…'

They couldn't find a way out.

If entering the building triggered the Regulation Exception, finding the door was the closest solution to deactivating it.

"Senpai! The door…!"

Plu somersaulted in the air, striking the monsters with Phoenix.

Landing next to Shirone, she looked around with a pale face.

The doors lining the corridor rattled open, and even more ferocious and gigantic monsters poured in.

"There's only one place where no monsters are coming from."

Shirone had guessed it too.

Without further words, the two of them headed for the basement.

"Graaaah!"

The monsters blocking their view were torn to pieces by the tendrils.

Even Plu, who had fought alongside Shirone at the Second Rebel Command, was overwhelmed by his immense power.

But there was nothing more reassuring for carrying out the mission.

'We can do it! We just need to get to Araboth!'

As they entered the basement, the door closed automatically, and the torches on the walls lit up.

No more monsters appeared, but they knew they could never return to the surface.

The two headed down the dark corridor to the end.

Entering a brightly lit dressing room, they were greeted by a large mirror.

But the reflection was not of the two of them; it was a woman with long black hair and a seductive appearance.

Regulation Exception - Paradise.

Mirroring: Equal Rights.

The woman, smiling from within the mirror, stepped forward.

Shirone and Plu heightened their vigilance and slowly backed away.

"Be careful, Shirone. The complexity of the Regulation Exception surpasses human understanding. She's an incredibly powerful fairy."

Shirone had no objections.

No matter how specialized in mental abilities a fairy was, it was difficult to materialize a building in such detail.

'How did she do it?'

"Welcome. I am Orga, the manager of Paradise. I've been waiting for you."

Shirone didn't respond.

He knew they were caught in the Regulation Exception.

As expected, she spoke immediately.

"To leave this place, you must win a bet with me."

Plu asked.

"What kind of bet?"

"Equal rights."

Orga pointed her index finger with cold eyes.

"From now on, we will make 'performable proposals' to each other. And we must follow those proposals. However, when making a proposal, you must also perform it yourself. The one who fails to perform the proposal loses."

Shirone's face grew serious.

He didn't fully understand the intent yet, but he felt the danger.

Plu asked.

"Who performs the proposal? There are two of us."

"Only one of you needs to perform it. That's equal rights."

With the Regulation Exception activated, they had no choice but to comply.

As Shirone and Plu nodded in understanding, Orga made the first proposal.

"Take off your top."

Orga removed her top and threw it on the floor.

Having fulfilled the condition, it was now Shirone or Plu's turn to take off their top.

'She's trying to deactivate the Vajra Armor.'

She couldn't directly propose to deactivate Arman.

In equal rights, the proposal must be performable by both parties, and Orga, without Arman, couldn't perform it.

Plu was glad she was there to support Shirone.

If Shirone had faced Mirroring alone, he would have been disarmed without a chance.

"I'll do it."

If they didn't comply, they would lose.

The result would likely be death.

Plu removed Shehakim's clothing and took off her top. Noticing that Orga wasn't wearing underwear, she matched the state.

It was the correct answer.

"Good. Now it's your turn to make a proposal."

"I'll do it."

Shirone stepped forward.

The standard approach when dealing with a Regulation Exception user was to understand the rules and find a solution, as Marsha had said.

There was something he needed to confirm.

When physical damage was inflicted, did the real Orga take the damage?

If the woman in front of him was just an illusion, self-harm would only damage Shirone.

'No, that's unlikely.'

The abnormal rules of equal rights were based on the principle of equivalent exchange.

If the ability didn't allow damage to be inflicted on oneself, it wouldn't allow damage to the opponent either.

'She's an incredibly clever fairy. With the principle of equivalent exchange alone, she can strike the opponent and respond to numerous variables.'

Therefore, there was something worth trying.

'If this works…'

Shirone took a step forward and made his proposal.

"Break your left index finger."

Using Kenser's healing ability, it would heal in a few hours.

The key was her condition.

Orga continued to watch Shirone without any change in her expression.

There was no reason for her to follow a proposal that the opponent couldn't perform.

Shirone also didn't avert his gaze and grabbed his left index finger.

Gritting his teeth, he applied pressure until the joint bent outward with a crack.

It hurt, but he had to endure it.

This was war.

Orga slowly lowered her hand and grabbed her index finger.

When the sound of the bone breaking was heard, Shirone's eyes sparkled.

He noticed the mirror behind Orga shake for a moment.

'Physical damage works. That means…'

Without giving her time to think, Orga made the second proposal.

"Take off your pants."

Shirone clicked his tongue at her persistent attempt to deactivate the Vajra Armor.

It was only natural, given that it was a powerful weapon capable of sweeping away countless monsters in Paradise Hall.

'Ah, I see!'

Shirone noticed the beautiful ornaments on Orga's body as she stood naked and realized something.

If this continued, they would eventually have to deactivate the Vajra Armor.

Plu, now fully undressed, had only the Kubrick left.

On the other hand, Orga still had two pairs of earrings, a necklace, two rings, and three bracelets.

If Plu discarded even her ring, she would be in a state where she couldn't perform the proposal, according to the conditions.

No matter how abnormal the situation, if it followed the rules, it was a Regulation Exception, and they would be cornered.

'There's no time. I have to settle this in the next proposal.'

If the Vajra Armor was deactivated, they wouldn't be able to handle the monsters in Paradise, and that was what Orga was aiming for.

As Plu began to take off her pants, she muttered.

"Why is it always me…"

Despite the seriousness of the situation, Shirone couldn't help but let out a small laugh.

'I'm sorry, Senpai.'

In any case, Plu had come to the same conclusion and complied with the proposal.

And now it was time to end it.

'I can do this. No, it's the only way.'

Shirone glared at Orga with a determined expression.

"My proposal is…"
