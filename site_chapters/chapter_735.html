Chapter 735:

Labyrinth Andre - World No. 1

Shirone's body began to emit a radiant cross-shaped light, spreading throughout the world.

It was a sacrifice to revive the countless beings erased by the twisted history.

'Past lives, future lives.'

All are precious.

As Shirone gradually disappeared into the light, the dragon Karatorsa lowered his body in reverence.

"Because of you, we exist."

Numerous dragons that had been in the sky descended behind Karatorsa and mimicked his actions.

"Because of you, we exist."

Mitochondrial Eve shed tears.

"Shirone…"

With this, history would be restored, and she could eventually become the empress of the empire.

"Remember this light, Blitz."

As Karatorsa conveyed to Blitz, gazing at the particles of light filling the sky,

"This is life."

Blitz did not respond, but the memory would remain unaltered in his mind for ages to come.

Labyrinth Andre - World No. 283

As divine punishment struck, the bodies of the army ants invading the Garnet Kingdom shattered into pieces.

"M-Magician."

Merot trembled in fear, and Shirone gently patted her.

"It's okay. There's nothing to be afraid of."

This world is someone's dream.

Thus, Shirone could close his eyes and ignore all of this at any moment.

'But…'

If even the desire for happiness is an illusion, what reason is there to be born into this world?

"Everything will be alright."

With Merot, Shirone headed to the room of the queen, the ruler of the Garnet Kingdom.

"The 13th Night…!"

As they reached the entrance, Merot shouted,

"No one can approach!"

The 13th Night, rolling on the floor while activating transformation magic, had already lost half of its six legs.

"Yes, I know."

Shirone lovingly watched the 13th Night's desperate struggle against the powerful army ants.

To him, Garnet was just one of many entities with biologically similar information, but…

"That's why it exists."

We are destined to love what resembles us, and thus we will forever reach out to a broader world.

"Roar!"

The scream of the 13th Night, surrounded by army ants, echoed through the cave.

Unable to leave the queen's chamber, Garnet trembled at the death of her offspring.

'Pain. Terrible pain.'

Yet we will endlessly continue our journey of feeding and reproducing for happiness.

'Yes, this is how it should be.'

"Mother! Mother!"

As Merot, releasing pheromones of anger, charged, the 13th Night, crushed by the army ants, shouted,

"Don't come! Get out of here!"

Merot had the mission to safely escape the colony and create a new one.

Shirone understood all their hearts.

'Even the life of a tiny creature is no different.'

Immortal Function.

As Shirone's body emitted a powerful light, the ants filling the cave simultaneously stopped fighting.

"This is…"

The light of life.

"What… what is this?"

As 19,000 crosses filled the labyrinth of Andre, Kido looked around in fear.

"What happened? Why suddenly?"

The truly serious issue was that Shirone's body in the labyrinth was also glowing in the same form.

Sung Eum finally realized.

'Could it be that he explored all the worlds of Andre?'

For Shirone, who could control time as Sung Eum controlled space, it wasn't impossible.

But the thought of surpassing his own limits while wandering through 19,000 worlds was astonishing.

"Damn…! It's going to collapse!"

If the entrances to numerous worlds exploded simultaneously, the cave's ground would collapse in no time.

'No, it's not just that.'

Recalling why it was called the prison of time and space, time and space would be mixed up.

"We need to escape!"

As Sung Eum widened her eyes and prepared to cast an ether wave,

"The prison of time and space."

Nane muttered indifferently.

'The 19,000 worlds defined by McClain Goffin.'

Though Nane had swallowed Anke Ra's dream, he could only vaguely guess about Goffin.

'It's being reintegrated.'

Without fully understanding Goffin's intention, Nane took a step forward.

"What dream are you dreaming?"

He couldn't help but ask.

"In this nightmare where only terrible annihilation awaits, what hope are you holding onto?"

Nane slowly extended his hand.

"You must be wrong."

Sermon - End.

A red sword containing the concept of annihilating the universe pierced the ceiling and shot down to the ground at the speed of light.

The vertical flash was so fast it seemed to connect the ground and sky, making it impossible to react.

"Ugh!"

Yet Kido's groan was heard because Nane's truth did not penetrate the ground.

"How?"

Nane's sermon, End, trembled as if blocked by something just above the ground.

Sung Eum slowly turned her head.

"Shirone."

As she looked at Shirone's body, becoming as clear as pure water, tears welled up in her eyes.

"Until the end…"

If Nane's Kar had been perfect, no living being in this universe could deny his truth.

But the fact that End did not strike meant there was still another opinion denying Nane's enlightenment.

"I want to live."

The life force Shirone was spreading was preventing Nane from reaching perfection.

Tears streamed down Sung Eum's cheeks.

"I was foolish."

Strong or weak, all are just dice in the endless cycle of life's reincarnation.

Thus, Shirone was willingly sacrificing himself to prevent the annihilation of the universe for the sake of life itself.

"No."

Sung Eum shook her head, sobbing.

"You can't leave like this, Shirone."

Though he was blocking Nane's truth with brilliant will, Shirone would eventually spread into infinity.

"You are wrong, Shirone."

As Nane pressed his palm down, the sermon End began to wriggle fiercely towards the ground.

"Ugh!"

At the same time, the cross-shaped light emitted by Shirone intensified, but this only hastened his annihilation.

"Momentary hope cannot save the world."

If loving something could erase the world's suffering, Nane would have sacrificed his body thousands of times.

"Human emotions are just mirages once they pass. Who will remember you?"

To living beings, being alive is so natural that no one would commemorate Shirone.

"From a distance, we are just passing burdens and blocking pain from each other."

Endlessly reincarnating, thus everyone suffers.

"I will end it."

As Shirone's consciousness faded, Nane's sermon End burrowed into the ground with terrifying momentum.

"Is it the end…"

As Mun Kyung muttered with a pale face, Shirone's voice, now dissipated like smoke, was heard.

"Even if life has no meaning, I will protect life until the end in this nightmare."

"Why? In a world that ends if you close your eyes, why endure pain to exist?"

"There is no reason."

Isn't it enough just to love?

"We are…"

As Shirone's body, perfectly dispersed into light, vanished, his voice echoed like a hallucination.

Because we exist without reason.

"No! Shirone!"

Sung Eum ran to the space where the light had dispersed, but there was nothing to grasp.

"No reason?"

The being blocking Nane was gone, but he did not fully strike down the sermon End.

"Shirone, that is more… more wrong than any truth I know."

It was an incorrect conclusion.

"Is that enlightenment? Is that really Goffin's intention? To live without reason, to die without reason, to reproduce without reason, to eat without reason?"

There was no answer.

For Nane, it was frustrating, but for all who knew Shirone, it was a profound sorrow.

Kido lowered his head.

"Is it over now?"

"No."

In contrast, Rian still burned with determination, staring at the empty space where Shirone had vanished.

"He wouldn't disappear like this."

The will of a Yaksha is strong enough to deny reality, but even Dinai couldn't revive Shirone.

"Give up. Shirone has become infinite."

Sung Eum looked around at the transparent air surrounding them with a sorrowful gaze.

Everywhere and nowhere, he had become a vast spirit perfectly permeating the world.

Nane spoke.

"Do not grieve. This is also just a dream. Why bear the sorrow of existence?"

As Nane placed his hand on the handle of the sermon End, he pressed down with all his weight.

"This is the end."

Anyone witnessing the end of the universe would be dizzy, and Mun Kyung tightly closed his eyes.

"Why…"

Nane's voice was heard.

When everyone looked, the sword of the end was slowly being pushed back by Nane's palm.

"Why can't I enforce it?"

Nane's Kar was still not perfect.

"Is there still something denying my truth?"

"Rian! Look there…!"

In front of them, where Kido pointed, faint particles of light were gathering one by one.

"It's not over."

As World No. 2940 exploded, more particles of light began to gather.

Then World No. 8765 exploded, followed by World No. 32, World No. 10837, World No. 8546, World No. 4109…

Boom, boom, boom, boom, boom, boom, boom!

As 19,000 worlds simultaneously liberated, the mass of light began to take human form.

Sung Eum spoke in a trembling voice.

"It's not just one."

The Immortal Function, which began in the Samadhi of Emptiness, was breaking through each world and rising back up.

Kido cried out with tears streaming down his face.

"Shirone!"

Light has no will.

Therefore, the phenomenon of the photons assembling into Shirone's exact form was because…

"Hexa."

There was no cause.

"Ugh!"

As the mind that had spread into infinity reformed into a body, Shirone's face twisted in pain.

"He's back! Shirone is back!"

It was welcome news for those who had been waiting, but the process of descending from a supreme spirit to a living being was horrifying for Shirone.

'Feeding and reproducing.'

However, having experienced it before, the process of reattaching the biological system was smoother than before.

"Nane, your compassion for the world isn't wrong. But we will exist until the end."

Sung Eum trembled as she confirmed Shirone's clear gaze, unable to comprehend what she was seeing.

"Impossible. That is…"

It was an impossible feat.

Sung Eum, being an unlocker herself, knew better than anyone that a mind expanded into infinity couldn't return.

'No, there is one way…'

Recalling the only method, Sung Eum stared at Shirone with a shocked expression.

The Infinite Magician.
