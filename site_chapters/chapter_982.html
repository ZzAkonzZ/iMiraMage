Chapter 982: The Grand Premise (4)

Even in her dazed state, Miro could clearly see Gaold's gaunt face.

Despite living in far more excruciating pain than she was, his eyes were still full of life.

'How...'

All her assumptions were shattered, and for the first time, Miro realized.

'How have you lived like this?'

She couldn't believe that he had endured such pain for 20 years while she was terrified of just breathing.

"I don't know what's going on, but..."

Miro realized that Gaold was unaffected by Emotion Sickness.

This meant that the pain Gaold usually felt was beyond imagination.

"Just hold on a little longer. Don't fight the pain, don't try to avoid it, just accept it."

Miro let herself go.

Forgetting the concept of breathing, she entrusted her basic survival to the laws of nature.

The intensity of the pain didn't change, but her attitude towards it became much more relaxed.

From the platform, Adolf XIII's voice could be heard.

"Ugh! I am trash! I am a worthless being. I am the scum of the earth."

It seemed he had finally relinquished his royal authority.

This also meant that Adolf XIII's personality had completely changed.

"Please save me. I am a pitiful wretch. Please..."

Crawling on the ground, crying and sniffling, there was no longer any trace of royal dignity in him.

The people in the square were still writhing in pain.

The pain induced baseless anger, leading to acts of violence in certain areas.

Some had their noses broken, others were trampled and lay bleeding from their heads.

Surprisingly, those who were beaten regained their senses, free from the pain of Emotion Sickness.

'The body.'

Humans cherish their bodies, so destroying it can alleviate the symptoms.

Miro bit her lip.

'This is insane...'

She finally understood why Paimon had boasted so much and why Havitz coveted Meirei's ability.

'This disease is hell.'

The terror of Emotion Sickness lay in its ability to sicken not just the body, but also the mind and spirit.

Someone shouted,

"Hit me! It won't hurt!"

Experience was the fastest teacher, and another method was proposed in the square.

'What they gave up was morality.'

Destroying the body was effective, but abandoning the idea of being a law-abiding citizen also worked.

The scene in the square quickly turned into a brawl.

"Die! Just die!"

Pain paralyzed their thoughts, and they would do anything to stop the immediate suffering.

The citizens were like beasts, and Shirone watched the scene with sad eyes.

'This is Havitz's world.'

The current situation wasn't even a tragedy.

For now, they could survive by giving up a few things, but how long would that last?

'To survive, they must keep killing. First others, then friends, family, and even themselves...'

As time passed, the atrocities committed would far surpass merely hitting someone.

"It's done."

Finally, Pony succeeded.

As her friends turned to look, she was biting her lip with her index finger broken.

Kangnan spoke to Miro.

"You should break something too."

Miro shook her head.

"It's useless."

The first thing she had abandoned to reach the state of absolute purity was herself.

"I'll do it."

Thinking it was time, Shirone raised his arms and activated the Miracle Stream.

Brilliant light gathered above his head and spread out as countless particles.

"Argh! I'm so annoyed! I'll kill everyone!"

The citizens in the square couldn't see the light.

All they saw were the detestable faces of others and a blood-red world.

As Lupist appeared on the platform and cast a spell, a giant iron pillar struck the center of the square.

Boom!

With a loud noise, the ground shook, and the citizens finally stopped in fear.

"Hah. Hah."

With a bit of sanity returning, they realized it was no longer difficult to breathe.

"How...?"

Most had unknowingly killed something, but even those who hadn't felt the same.

Gaold spoke.

"You did something dangerous."

Controlling the citizens' minds through Hexa could temporarily alleviate Emotion Sickness.

"Pain is a sign of life. You shouldn't numb it so easily."

"There was no other choice."

If the structure required continuously killing something to maintain the status quo, forcibly removing the pain would only raise the threshold.

That's why Shirone had waited for people to remove the pain themselves.

"The second wave will bring greater pain. But Miro has no other way."

Miro stuck out her tongue with hollow eyes.

"Thanks. Honestly, I feel a bit better."

Pony came down from the platform, showing her broken index finger.

"The threshold of actions is also a problem. If breaking my index finger convinced my brain, then I've already killed it. So next time, even if I break the other index finger, it won't go back to the dormant state."

Nade asked,

"Can't you break your middle finger?"

"That's ambiguous. Even though it's a different type, the concept of a single finger is the same. Breaking two at once might work, but we need accurate information to last as long as possible."

Shirone nodded.

"We'll need a lot of data. I'll go to the World Health Organization and relay this. Seriel will help. Blitz, go ahead and set up the foundation for quantum transmission."

"Yes."

Blitz bowed and immediately flew into the sky, disappearing beyond the horizon.

Amy spoke.

"Pony, you don't have to think so physically. You can't give up being queen, but there are many conceptual things. If the next wave comes, give up on me. The concept of being friends."

"I can't do that."

Pony shook her head firmly.

"I'd rather my body be broken. I'll never do that."

"But how will you endure..."

Iruki spoke.

"Pony is right. The danger of Emotion Sickness is that the threshold keeps rising. You might be able to break ties with Amy. But what about after that? The same method won't work. Eventually, you'll have to harm her, and if it continues..."

You'll have to kill.

Just imagining the future that hadn't yet arrived sent chills down everyone's spine.

Nade looked up at the sky and asked,

"What will happen next? To us, and to this world."

No one could answer.

"Oh, Yahweh."

As the short strategy meeting ended, all the citizens approached Shirone.

"Your light has saved us again. Please protect us. We don't want to go through this again."

All the citizens knelt and shouted.

"Lord Shirone! Stay at our house! Let us bask in your light!"

"What are you talking about? You want to monopolize that light! I have three children!"

Seeing the citizens trembling and approaching from all sides, Shirone felt suffocated.

"Then let's take turns basking in the light! That's the fairest way!"

"Nonsense! How can everyone take turns? Let's create a specific place and gather there! Yes, let's build a church here!"

Shirone couldn't respond to their voices.

"This is how it is."

Gaold spoke.

"They don't think about the people outside Bashka. Everyone's own pain comes first. Whether they eat an apple or a strawberry. If you free all humanity from pain, they'll accept it. Because you're giving them both. But if you can't, they'll endlessly covet you."

The Great Star spoke.

"The first wave was at a level where giving up one of many things was enough. So Agape, the strength of the heart, wasn't that great. But as people's thresholds rise, even Hexa's power will have its limits."

Shirone's Ultima System alone couldn't save all of humanity.

"There is a positive side."

Miro didn't miss even the smallest detail.

"Seeing them, the opening of the Demon Realm makes integrating the Ultima System relatively easier. The more people suffer, the more they trust you."

"It's ironic. Evil strengthens love."

Miro nodded at Sein's words.

"The cycle of good and evil. This might be humanity's last turn. We don't know which spokes will break and which will remain."

"Let's go back."

As soldiers blocked the citizens' approach on Pony's orders, Shirone turned around.

"We need to close the Demon Realm as soon as possible."

Shirone's group arrived at the castle through mass teleportation.

Pony's first order was to promote all castle staff.

'Most senior officials have relinquished their positions. There's a severe shortage of people to handle the work.'

They could try rehiring, but no one was applying.

"As of now, I declare martial law. All citizens except castle staff are prohibited from going out. Minimizing the chaos from the second wave is crucial."

After handling the urgent matters, Pony asked Shirone,

"How is it? Have you reached the World Health Organization?"

"Yes, I just arrived. I'm talking to Seriel. I'll relay the information as it comes in."

Even those who had experienced it firsthand still couldn't grasp the exact nature of Emotion Sickness.

The Great Star spoke.

"This isn't the only problem. Five Demon Realms have opened worldwide, including in the central continent. The Demon Realm installs a new system into reality. To solve this, we need to remove the fundamental part."

Shirone already knew the answer.

"The Underworld."

"Yes. The Demon Realm's system brings the laws of the Underworld into reality. If we can completely purify hell, the Demon Realm will naturally disappear."

Sein spoke.

"First Ymir's mind, now the Underworld? Who will go there?"

Once again, everyone raised their hands, and Nade pushed the hardest.

"Me! I won't back down this time! Shirone, I'm going!"

The Great Star shook her head.

"No, you can't. The Underworld isn't a place just anyone can go. Shirone can because he opened the sanctuary, but for a being with only five senses to enter hell, a special method is required."

"What is it? I can do it too."

"You have to die."

"The Underworld is a place where extreme emotions from reality gather. To go there in a living state, you need to either make a pact with Satan or have emotions so intense that the system of reality can't accept them."

"Emotions that reality can't accept?"

"Yes. Just like Shirone's philanthropy, you need to reach the extreme of some emotion. Death is the most common and easiest way, but there are special cases. This is called sin. Those who are consumed by evil. The Underworld might be such a case."

Iruki rested his chin on his hand.

"That's a problem. We can't accumulate sin, nor can we die."

"Yes. That's why I'm asking."

The Great Star turned her head.

"Rian, only you can fight in hell alongside Shirone. Please stop the Demon Realm."

"Ah..."

Everyone realized.

Only Rian, with his ability to restore Idea, could experience hell while still alive.
