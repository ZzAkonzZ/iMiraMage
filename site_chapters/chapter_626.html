Chapter 626:

The white light engulfed the Colosseum, leaving the audience unable to see anything.

Only the analysts connected to the main system could monitor the battlefield through the code.

"That's Ataraxia."

It was Shirone's signature wide-area emission magic, even featured in Spirit magazine.

"What's the impact?"

"Approximately 18 million crash."

Seven times higher than the Photon Cannon of the Ataraxia physical system, but numbers no longer surprised anyone.

As the flash faded, the audience's eyes scanned the vast Colosseum.

"It's ambiguous."

Most participants were gathered at the edge of the Colosseum, with only Eden in the center, deploying Absolute Barrier.

"Hnnng!"

She had blocked Ataraxia's Photon Cannon in the Scramble Royale, but the 2000 system had no pushback judgment.

"It hit directly."

She had managed to split the high-output mass wave with her body, but the impact was too much to withstand.

"Check the parameters."

"Her mental strength has dropped to 2 percent. It was close. Unfortunate for Shirone."

"No, I think he knew."

As Baikal finished speaking, Shirone advanced, casting Photon Cannon.

'No one is weak.'

He didn't obsess over fighting everyone, focusing all his energy on defeating the enemy in front of him.

'Take Eden out first!'

Climbing step by step was the only way to reach the top of the pyramid.

"Hup!"

As Eden tilted her localized shield, the Photon Cannon's flash deflected diagonally.

"Good choice. Lowering the angle increases the shield's thickness."

'In that case…!'

Seeing the participants charging from behind Eden, Shirone compressed the Photon Cannon in his right hand.

"Yaaah!"

The vibrating sphere of light extended through Magical Action.

"Projectile speed: 400 meters per second!"

The Photon Cannon, flying at supersonic speed, struck the shield again, making Eden's head ring.

"Ugh!"

At such speed, the impact concentrated on a point, regardless of the angle.

"Eden! Attack!"

As Shirone teleported and cast Photon Cannon at close range, Eden spread her arms.

"I've lost, Shirone."

The powerful impact struck her brain, and the red light on her bracelet indicated her Spirit Zone had vanished.

-Participant number 25 has exited the 2000 system.

As Eden collapsed, leaning on Shirone's shoulder, she heard his voice.

"Good job."

Her eyes closed gently.

"Thank you."

As a devout follower of the Yor Church, Eden would be remembered for not using violence until the end.

"Finish him now! There won't be a second chance!"

If Shirone unleashed Ataraxia again, many participants would be eliminated.

As the participants charged from the edge of the Colosseum, Shirone pushed off the ground and advanced.

Eden, unconscious, fell to the ground, and a flash of light reached the center of the 26 participants.

"So you want to see this through to the end!"

As Boyle summoned his familiar, the blue-skinned Gaos, crackling with electricity, appeared.

"Oh?"

Spines lined its spine, and instead of a tongue, a tube-like barrel protruded from its jaw.

"There's a functional change. It's Dream Thought."

Dream Thought, which altered a creature's form and function, was one of the most challenging summoning techniques.

"It's the first time seeing it in the graduation exam."

Boyle had trained in Dream Thought instead of raising his tier, solely to pass the graduation exam.

"Go, Gaos!"

Roar!

As Gaos, emitting blue electricity, swung its claws, Shirone teleported to create distance.

"Now!"

Closer's Eruption exploded, and Sabina's Wind Cutter followed.

Then Fire Strike, Ice Wave, and 36 types of regulated magic enhanced by Suabi's buff rained down.

'Time Explosion!'

As he distorted space-time and moved out of range, Screamer's back kick struck his abdomen.

"Ugh!"

With a burst of air, Shirone's body flew.

"It hit perfectly!"

Screamer's specialty was physical attacks, capable of killing any mage if they connected.

"Detected increased air pressure. It's an Air Shield."

Compressing air in his abdomen saved him, but he was on the defensive, using non-specialty magic.

"Damn! So close!"

As Screamer fumed, Conger charged, pulling dozens of steel wires from his palms.

'Shirone, you're quite cute.'

Despite his appearance, Conger was feminine but ruthless in magic, befitting a steel mage.

"Death Fence."

As he spread his arms, the wires shot towards Shirone like streams.

Caught in the steel wires with sharp barbs every 10 centimeters, he would be shredded instantly.

"Hup!"

As Shirone pulled back, the wires grazed his abdomen, bending away.

"Dodged…"

'You can't escape my love that easily.'

The wires, like burning hair, twisted and cut Shirone's arms and legs.

As the mental shock struck his brain, Arin cast Shadow of Capture, binding Shirone's body.

"Canis! Now!"

'Power of Darkness.'

Saw-like shadows extended from Canis's shadow, aiming for Shirone's neck.

"Grrr!"

Using Diamond Body's mental strength to break Arin's magic, Shirone cast Radiance without time to dodge.

Pop pop pop pop pop!

The Power of Darkness scattered like burnt paper, but Canis smirked.

"I knew it."

Light and Darkness - Incarnation Reversal.

The shadow outside the light barrier swelled like a balloon and pierced through Radiance.

'It broke through the light?'

Struck by the Power of Darkness, Shirone's body was buried in the shadow and pushed to the edge of the Colosseum.

'Don't be surprised. This technique is solely to defeat you.'

"Is it still not enough…?"

As Shirone continued to be pushed back, sighs of disappointment began to emerge from the audience.

"Who can we blame? It's a self-inflicted situation."

Though Baikal shared the audience's emotions, he maintained his objective judgment as a scout.

"It was the only choice. I acknowledge that. But now, we must admit it's impossible."

Elizabeth reported, "The percentage has dropped to 7%. If there's no time for recovery, they'll be eliminated."

"Let's go!"

Cayden, who had formed a cross sword, took the lead, and the other participants followed, equipping their magic.

"What will you do now, Number 27? If you can't show something more here, your graduation exam is over."

"Whooo!"

As Shirone exhaled and expanded the Spirit Zone, the eyes of the approaching participants widened simultaneously.

"What is this?"

Elizabeth read the changes in the main system. "It's slow magic."

The magic that slows time using the acceleration of light was indeed marvelous, but for a mage, photonization was a basic skill.

"Hmph! So, all that's left are tricks!"

As soon as they equipped the photonization batteries, the slowed time instantly returned to normal.

'Shirone, slow magic isn't used like this.'

Unless it was to exploit a gap with an instant magic circle like Dante, it was a tactic only effective against a group of ignorant bandits.

"Huh?"

Suddenly, the slow magic was lifted.

As the participants felt puzzled, the analysts in the VIP seats grimaced with headaches.

"Ugh!"

Hearing Elizabeth's groan, Baikal spoke, "It seems you're not adapting well."

The error caused by the Time Explosion was directly impacting the analysts' brains.

"That's part of it, but this time it's different."

"Different?"

"It's not time."

Elizabeth struggled to find the words to explain for a while. "Space is existing simultaneously."

Time Explosion - Force Dimension (4D combat space).

"What is this?"

The participants charging towards Shirone were stunned by the phenomenon unfolding before their eyes and hastily stopped.

Twelve Shirones stood in different poses, each with a photon cannon floating above their hands.

"Illusion magic?"

As the first Shirone fired a flash, photon cannons flew from all directions.

"Damn it!"

As each participant dodged, Cayden deflected the light with his cross sword, and Dante's instant magic circle collided with immense power.

"Ugh!"

Just by looking at the expressions of those who defended, the participants could guess what was happening here.

"They're not illusions."

All twelve Shirones were real.

"Is this possible?"

Elizabeth couldn't answer Baikal's question.

"It's not something an ordinary analyst can comment on. But based on the data, it seems Number 27's time distortion ability is being implemented through spatial coordinates."

The only person who could provide a clear answer was Miro, who first detected the Time Explosion’s ability.

'Time and space are originally one system.'

When the Time Explosion is activated, numerous possibilities, including past and future, are realized as golden threads.

'If you add another dimension using the time nature of slow magic…'

Possibilities that existed only in theory appear superimposed on the coordinates of reality.

In other words, it means dominating a 4D space, which is the same mechanism used by Goffin to create the upper layers of Istas.

While the time energy of slow magic was consumed to maintain the Force Dimension, time flowed normally, but now the problem was space.

"Damn it! How is this even possible!"

The intimidation felt when 26 people cornered one Shirone disappeared completely in the face of the reality of having to deal with twelve Shirones.

'This way, I can fight more.'

The efficiency of the Force Dimension depended on the level of slow magic, and the current 4D combat space controlled by Shirone had a diameter of 20 meters.

It wasn't a large or small number, but everyone knew that fighting within this area was suicidal.

"Everyone, get out of here!"

As all the participants retreated towards the outskirts of the Force Dimension, the twelve Shirones glared at their targets.

"The situation has reversed!"

In the face of retreating from the brink of victory, even Baikal was at a loss for words this time.

"Transformation magic is a measure of realm, not strength. So, it won't have an immediate effect, but…"

Miro followed Shirone's movements and spoke.

"It has the advantage of being applicable to all magic."

'Now!'

Shirone gritted his teeth and cast the photon cannon.

4D combat style - Quantum Superposition.

Then, the twelve Shirones, superimposed in reality, simultaneously fired flashes at Boyle.

"Ugh!"

As the concentrated impact from all directions struck his back, a massive shock overwhelmed Boyle, and his body collapsed to the ground.

-Participant Number 17. Exiting the system.

The participants who barely escaped the Force Dimension stared blankly at the fallen Boyle.

Even in such a situation, the twelve Shirones were still targeting all the participants within the 4D combat space.

'Damn it! How are we supposed to beat someone like that?'

In terms of headaches, it was worse than Infercus, who had simply bombarded them with attacks during the first evaluation.

"All the skills mastered in countless situations are integrated into one incarnation called Time Explosion."

Miro rested her chin on her hand and smiled contentedly.

"The magic of Banya begins from there."
