Chapter 1042: The Limits of Discipline (1)

Oris, who had been staring at Shirone's face for a long time, finally came to her senses and took his hand.

"Oh, right. Thanks. But…”

Even with her excellent vision, she couldn't recall what had happened.

"How did you do it?"

There was no way to explain the enlightenment of quantum movement followed by the destruction of the incarnation.

'And I shouldn't explain it.'

For now, he was Yupi, not Yahweh.

"It's not over yet. This opponent won't go down that easily, so let's move to a safer place."

Oris pouted.

'What? Now he's just ignoring me? That's too much.'

But she couldn't argue because she had witnessed Son Yu-jung's prowess firsthand.

"I lost in a direct confrontation, so I can't say much, but how about we fight together? I could at least be a decoy…"

At that moment, the side wall exploded with a bang, and Moonom, who had evolved into a grotesque form, arrived.

"Hoo!"

One arm was as large as a lobster claw, and his head elongated to the point where the back of his head touched his back.

Shirone realized.

'Super evolution.'

It wasn't just a mutation applied to the body; even the brain functions had evolved.

Proving this, Moonom took one look around and grasped the situation.

"Did she fly over there?"

His voice was incredibly deep.

"Moonom, the enemy is stronger than we imagined. All seven generals need to work together, especially with Yupi's power."

Moonom's single eye focused on Shirone.

"…Is that so?"

The violent temperament he had shown in the tent was now controlled by an intelligent gaze.

"If you say so, then you must be of royal blood. But I'm a warrior who has trained for a hundred million years. I don't back down without a fight."

Moonom's evolution was based on mutations.

"I overcame a 2.7% probability to reach the fourth-generation mutation. I can kill her."

If someone other than the Seven Generals defeated the enemy, it would be embarrassing for Croseide.

As Moonom turned forward, Son Yu-jung was walking towards them, exhaling smoke.

"You."

Her expression was serious.

"What was that technique you used on me just now?"

The reason this combative woman initiated a conversation wasn't just because of the power.

'Her incarnation cracked.'

Feeling the weight of Buddha in an attack from an ordinary demon was not something that could be mistaken.

'It was an enlightened strike. And…'

If it felt like being hit by Nane, it meant the depth of the state was comparable to Buddha.

Even Son Yu-jung, who feared nothing in the world, felt uneasy in the face of enlightenment.

While Shirone remained silent, Moonom approached her.

"Did you kill Croseide?"

His appearance was something one would only see in hell, but Son Yu-jung didn't give him more than a second glance.

The red weapon was aimed at Shirone.

"Answer me. What's your identity? How did you grasp the essence of destruction…"

Bang!

Moonom's massive fist struck Son Yu-jung's side, slamming her into the wall.

"Tch. Nothing special."

As he withdrew his fist, Son Yu-jung, imprinted like a stamp on the organic wall, scowled.

"Do you want to die?"

Satisfied that he had finally gotten her attention, Moonom rotated his shoulders.

"You're certainly tough. But…"

As the air vibrated with a hum, Moonom launched his massive body and unleashed a barrage of attacks.

"If I keep hitting, you'll break eventually."

As Son Yu-jung deftly twisted her waist to approach, her eyes widened.

"Huh?"

As if he had anticipated it, Moonom's fist abruptly changed course and struck her hard.

Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang!

As dozens of attacks landed almost simultaneously, her mind raced.

'He doesn't seem that fast, so how is he predicting my movements?'

With super evolution reaching the fourth-generation mutation, Moonom's IQ was a staggering 1,700.

'I can see all the variables. No matter how you move, I won't miss.'

The speed of his barrage increased.

"Hahaha! Die! I'm the strongest demon!"

Oris muttered.

"Fourth-generation mutation. He evolved repeatedly, ready to die. But with that ability…"

He might be able to defeat Son Yu-jung.

"No."

Shirone shook his head.

"That's not enough."

In reality, the one whose face was contorted was Moonom, who was repeatedly attacking.

"Ugh!"

Tough?

No, he could feel the impact of his hits.

'That means she's a living being made of flesh and blood. But why, why…'

Reaching the limit of breathlessness, Moonom screamed.

"Why won't you break!"

Realizing Yupi's words were correct, Oris immediately kicked off the ground and launched herself.

Even with the combined attacks of two of the Seven Generals, Son Yu-jung was the one dominating.

"This won't do. You two are unqualified."

As she skillfully swung her two clubs, Son Yu-jung suddenly twisted her waist.

"Die."

As immense kinetic energy was generated, time slowed, and the surrounding buildings bent outward.

'Huh?'

Oris pursed her lips.

'This time, I can see it?'

The red club seemed to split into multiple parts, approaching through space.

In the seesaw of time, she couldn't move a finger, and neither could Moonom.

'Can't dodge.'

With an IQ of 1,700, he analyzed the current situation with brutal clarity.

'It's over.'

They were going to die, and Oris, Moonom, and even Son Yu-jung didn't doubt it.

A clear future.

And Shirone cast Miracle Stream on the frozen event like a law.

Hand of God - a technique of the hand. As the hand of light swept up Oris and Moonom, the seesaw of time was released.

Boom!

Buildings within a radius of dozens of meters were blown away, but the Seven Generals were not included.

Exhaling smoke from her mouth, Son Yu-jung glared forward over her shoulder.

"What is this now?"

It felt like a specific event had disappeared.

But since that was impossible, it must have made it seem like it disappeared, which was the correct answer.

'Is that possible? At the level of law?'

A shiver ran through her, but the bizarreness beyond normalcy made her thoughts cold.

'Buddha wouldn't do this.'

Because it wasn't the truth.

'But I don't think it's a lower state than Buddha. Then…'

A god?

Moments later, the hand of light that had placed Oris and Moonom down turned white and evaporated.

"Huh? What?"

Oris looked around, and Moonom, with a dazed expression, opened his mouth.

Though he didn't know how his IQ of 1,700 had analyzed it, he didn't speak in the end.

Shirone stood in front of them.

'This is the limit.'

If Son Yu-jung activated her golden eyes, Yahweh's identity would be revealed.

"I'll handle the intruder. You two go to Itaka and get new strategies."

Moonom's single eye glared.

"Don't talk nonsense. We're demons. Do you not know what it means to leave an enemy behind?"

Shirone wasn't a demon.

"Hmph, royals are always like this. Itaka is the same. Always calculating efficiency like humans…"

"Moonom."

Shirone spoke coldly.

"Go."

Feeling like he was being pierced by 90% of Yahweh's gaze, Oris shivered.

'I'm a Seven General, and I'm scared.'

At the same time, a sweet and strange emotion she had never experienced before enveloped her body.

'I… I like this demon…'

Of course, if this feeling amplified to 100%, affection would turn into hatred.

Oris grabbed Moonom's arm.

"Let's go. It's best to leave it to Yupi now. Itaka would think the same."

"Ugh!"

In the end, Moonom relented and left, and Oris looked back at Shirone.

"Be careful, Yupi."

Facing Oris's cat-like expression, hoping for a gentle touch, Shirone felt heavy-hearted.

'I shouldn't have chosen the name Yupi.'

He hadn't meant to mock them.

"I'll be quick."

Oris's face brightened at Shirone's smile, and she nodded before leaping away.

Son Yu-jung asked.

"Will you tell me now?"

The reason she had just watched until now was evident in Shirone's slowly turning gaze.

"Hehe, finally showing your true colors."

Just looking into his eyes felt like her heart was being cleansed.

"…Did Nane send you?"

"Hmm."

Feeling like she was conversing with Buddha, Son Yu-jung stroked her chin, lost in thought.

'I really don't know. Since he doesn't recite the incantation, he doesn't seem to be on par with Buddha.'

Shirone knew the incantation, but to be effective, he had to be 100% Yahweh.

"Go back and tell Nane. I'm busy, so handle your own burdens."

Son Yu-jung's eyebrow twitched.

"Well, fine. I'll just ask my weapon from now on. Are you ready to fight?"

"Let's change the location." Preferably somewhere without Oris. "Location?"

Lowering her center of gravity, Son Yu-jung shot forward like molten metal.

"Haha! Why should I care about that?"

'Like a thunderbolt.'

Wrinkling his nose, Shirone activated 93% of Yahweh and charged at her.

Rumble!

The city shook, and a massive cloud of dust rose into the sky.

Lethe slammed the table.

"Regardless of Yahweh's nature, the stance of the Fire Management Company remains unchanged. Duke, you are making a grave mistake."

"What do you mean…"

Tired of the repetitive situation, Lethe stood up coldly.

"If the Duke doesn't know shame, I'll show you. I'll wait here, whether it's for a year or a hundred years. Your lies will be exposed."

"I am a demon who prefers quiet research."

Lethe gave a cold smile.

"Are you scared now? The fact that the informant hasn't detected anything means Rian is still in the laboratory."

As Lethe inspected the laboratory equipment, Marguerite spoke softly.

"These are sensitive machines."

"Then talk! Don't make me disgrace you!"

"I am not deceiving you. I understand your concern, but this is a waste of time."

"Hmph!"

Lethe approached Mercede.

"Tell me honestly, Mercede. Have you seen Yahweh or Rian in this laboratory?"

"N-No, I haven't."

Marguerite's eyes narrowed.

While the Duke's mind could resist Lethe, Mercede couldn't withstand it.

"Don't worry. I won't let any harm come to you. Just say one word."

"No, I haven't seen them."

Mercede looked almost on the verge of tears.

"You know, don't you? Now, don't look away. Look at me. Look into my eyes."

The succubus, struck by Lethe's gaze, felt a fear that seemed to shatter her mind.

'Huh? Oh no?'

No matter how much a child denies it, they can't deceive their mother's eyes.

'I'm going to get caught.'

Realizing the signal of a lie was appearing in her eyes, Mercede made a decision.

"Lady Lethe."

She faintly smiled.

"It's true."

As Lethe read her eyes and was about to shout, Mercede's head exploded.

"Ah…!"

The mental shock made Lethe's body sway back and forth as she stared blankly at the headless corpse.

"Me-Mercede."

Memories of laughing and talking with her flashed through her mind without missing a single scene.

Marguerite approached.

"Please leave. I need to clean the laboratory. I'll send word separately."

Lethe turned her head, her eyes filled with determination.

"Duke, this won't make me…"

"Will you ask me too?"

Though the Duke wouldn't make the same choice, his words endlessly amplified Lethe's guilt.

'I'm sorry, Mercede.'

Just like Gaia in the real world, all demons held equal value to Lethe.

"This is the last time. When I come back, I won't back down."

To not make Mercede's resolve meaningless, Lethe promised to return.

As the laboratory door closed, Marguerite took the hand of the headless corpse and kissed the back of it.

"Rest in peace."
