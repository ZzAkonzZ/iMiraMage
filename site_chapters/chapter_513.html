Chapter 513: Questions Left Behind (3)

Gando, having learned the full story, fell into deep thought.

It was understandable that Anke Ra, upon realizing Satan's existence, used the reset to give humanity another chance.

However, one question still remained.

"Why… did he try to erase himself?"

Anke Ra Erasure.

If Satan were annihilated, Ra could once again conquer the human world through the final war.

There was no reason for him to erase himself.

Uorin, who had always answered questions clearly, was silent for a long time this time.

Gando realized something through this silence.

There was no déjà vu about the Goffin Erasure, but it was a story that could be sufficiently inferred.

Terraje met Ra.

Otherwise, such a compromise wouldn't make sense.

"Yes. Terraje Mistra had a one-on-one meeting with Ra at the end of the final war. But the person he truly wanted to meet was not Terraje. The reason Mistra could predict Ra's erasure and the reason the Empress was replaced earlier than expected was because of that child."

"That child?"

"Arian Shirone."

Gando recalled Shirone's face.

The bold boy who had proposed a deal to Gaold at the Magic Association.

Gaold probably intended to use him for the Heaven Project, but it was surprising that the name Shirone was mentioned even by Terraje.

"Why did Ra seek Shirone?"

"To get an answer to the remaining question."

When Gando tilted his head, Uorin added an explanation.

"Satan, that is, absolute chaos, was a concept that Anke Ra could never understand. He is the whole, but chaos is outside the whole. The fear of the unknown awakened Anke Ra's long memory."

Uorin's expression twisted slightly as he pulled out information close to primordial memory.

"Perhaps Goffin was a person who existed until recently. It's just that we have forgotten."

Maclaine Goffin erased his existence from the Akashic Records.

Although still mentioned by a few people, what kind of person he was could only be inferred from other information associated with his name.

"The cause of the Goffin Erasure is unclear. Currently, it is only thought to be somehow related to Shirone. In any case, it seems clear that Goffin had some sort of agreement with Ra before his erasure. And during that agreement, Goffin posed one question to Ra."

"What was the question?"

"Ra is the avatar of the Akashic Records. The entirety or the whole of this world. When Mistra faced Ra, the question he wanted to hear answered from Shirone was this."

Uorin, with his long neck held high, turned his head slightly towards Gando and said,

"Why can't I…"

"Become a god?"

Ra asked, but Shirone still did not answer.

His avatar, bankrupt due to the debt of the Valhalla Action, was isolated in the prison of space-time.

Knowing this, Ra asked once more.

That was the promise made with Shirone during the Goffin Erasure, and the one problem Anke Ra could not understand.

"I am the whole. The beginning and the end of this world. So why can't I become a god? Why is chaos outside of me?"

"……"

Even a moment of silence was frustrating for Anke Ra.

A flash of powerful energy was striking the spire of Aravot, precisely at him.

If he couldn't get an answer from Shirone, he had only one choice left.

"If I can't get an answer from humans…"

Anke Ra's eyes widened.

"I will become human myself."

"Ooooooh!"

Uriel continued to push Miro to the end.

Time left until the destruction of heaven: 23 seconds.

But before the reset was attempted, before God's punishment destroyed heaven, he had to kill Miro.

"Grrr!"

Even with his jaw broken, Sain clung to Uriel and cast the Sun and Moon Halo.

Ikael amplified the power of the Sun and Moon Halo, but the fully ignited Uriel still ran towards Miro and swung the Ultimate Staff.

Boom!

Miro's eyes, forming a hand seal, shook with the impact.

An archangel with such powerful force that he could create cracks in Miro's space-time.

Though she entered a state of deep concentration to repair the cracks, the root of all this trouble was Uriel.

"What is it, Miro? Your mouth isn't moving anymore, is it?"

"Ugh!"

Miro focused all her strength and mind.

Crack.

Even so, small cracks were forming in the small-scale barrier she had deployed.

19 seconds.

"Victory is in sight!"

"Everyone, run towards death! Life is beyond death!"

The rebels desperately advanced towards the spire.

The moment they entered Aravot, all thoughts burned away, leaving only an instinctive obsession with victory.

17 seconds.

"Kieek! Kiek!"

The Garas swept through the ruins of the temple like the wind, hunting angels.

The natural enemy of the noble spiritual beings, the angels, was the simplest form specialized only for reproduction.

Even at the moment the world ended, the Garas would be driven solely by the animalistic urge to reproduce their species.

15 seconds.

"No! You can't leave us!"

Kanya hugged the broken Babel and wept.

The transparent droplets rolling down the cold metal surface looked like Babel's tears.

13 seconds.

"Mister! Mister!"

"……"

Gaold was like a living corpse.

Where was his soul, dragged into hell, wandering now?

Kangnan buried her head in Gaold's chest, thinking she would like to walk through that hell with him if she could.

A flower blooming in hell.

That is why love is beautiful.

11 seconds.

Rumble!

Buildings were collapsing everywhere.

A large rock crashed down next to Kuan's face as he looked up at the sky indifferently.

Perhaps the next rock would crush his face, but he just kept looking at the sky.

'It's ending.'

It was time for the clown to exit.

9 seconds.

Arius, knocked away by Uriel's blow, knelt like a defeated soldier and looked up at the sky.

Perhaps he discovered it earlier than others because he had lost his eyes.

"Eeh. Eeh."

A great light was descending from the sky.

8 seconds.

Plu staggered to his feet in front of the unconscious Shirone.

Without distinguishing between friend and foe, everyone fought with everything they believed was right.

"What is this…"

Plu shed tears.

Desires always shattered like mirages before reality.

"What is this!"

In the unknown twists of the future, where was humanity flowing?

7 seconds.

"Shirone…"

Exhausted from the Roar of Demon Destruction, Etella knelt and arched her back, looking up at the sky.

"Well done, Shirone."

She just gave her whole heart.

"Myths are just myths."

Animalistic desires, supreme spirit, good and evil, everything one could give their heart to.

All the myths created by humanity were rushing towards destruction, driven by a cold thought that flashed through a boy's mind.

6 seconds.

'Farka Kuan.'

Armin felt a sense of defeat and gave a bitter smile.

'If it's him…'

He could protect the woman he loved most for the rest of his life.

"Shina, be happy."

"Brother?"

Armin gave a beautiful smile and cast a flicker spell towards the sky.

Realizing too late, Shina clutched her chest and screamed.

"Nooo!"

The speed of God's punishment was a staggering Mach 41.

That is, 41 times the speed of sound.

Even a master of schema had an extremely low chance of stopping at such a speed.

5 seconds.

'I'm sorry, Keira. I don't think I can keep my promise.'

She had seen him leave the ivory tower but hadn't activated the pact.

Perhaps because she saw Kuan's sincerity.

Today, he missed Keira's face in his memory a little more.

4 seconds.

'Amazing.'

Armin's keen eyes, with a much larger range of vision than others, finally caught sight of God's punishment.

A speed beyond description by his experience.

The distance was still far, but it felt as if it had already arrived before his eyes.

3 seconds.

"Damn…"

Even with only 3 seconds left, God's punishment was still at an immense height.

'I can't go any higher!'

As the thought ended, a light flashed in the black sky, and a massive flash pierced through Armin.

"Gasp!"

In a moment that seemed to split a second into a hundred, Armin clearly saw it.

A golden bird enveloping him.

'Post-Metery?'

God's punishment pierced through the space-time bird and rushed to the ground.

2 seconds.

"Zulu?"

Plu turned to see Zulu arriving at the spire of Aravot.

According to the mission, she was supposed to find the Metagate in the underground of the temple.

'Why is she here?'

Zulu, having deactivated the Post-Metery, raised her hand to the sky.

Erga, the 10-tier monster she had first succeeded in taming, grabbed the Metagate and began to fly into the sky.

"For a new beginning."

Plu's gaze followed Erga.

1 second.

The Metagate opened, and finally, from the distant sky, God's punishment appeared, shaking the atmosphere.

It was the last sight humans could capture with their eyes.

A long flash, like drawing a line from the sky to the ground, was sucked into the black sphere of the Metagate.

Beep.

And then, twisting space-time, God's punishment struck Zion in the underground of the temple, where another sphere with the same coordinates was located.

"I will understand!"

Anke Ra's eyes moved at the speed of light.

Now that Satan had disappeared, it was the perfect time for a reset.

Reset.

The world went dark.

A perfect nothingness, a concept that cannot even be expressed as "nothing."

In that state without time or space, suddenly something amplified, and the world opened up.

As the concepts of birth and destruction intertwined, numerous events occurred, but all probabilities were already contained in the initial moment of amplification.

The universe and all its contents were once again drifting through billions of years, repeating the events before the reset.

The only difference was one thing.

23 seconds before the destruction of heaven, Anke Ra did not summon Shirone but erased himself immediately.

This was the true Anke Ra Erasure.

Thus, the time of the universe passed the zero-second mark of the destruction of heaven when Anke Ra implemented the reset.

"Ugh!"

Uorin bowed her head as if experiencing a shock to her heart.

"Your Majesty!"

As Gando jumped up, she raised her hand to stop him.

When a powerful déjà vu surged in, it felt like all the cells in her body stopped functioning, causing a shock like this.

'This was the moment.'

Uorin instinctively knew.

'The point when the third reset began. So, Anke Ra is currently erased.'

Uorin, having calmed her breathing, stood up.

"Follow me. There is something you must do from now on."

When Gando stepped outside, she was already standing in front of a room at the end of the hallway.

When she knocked on the door, the 30 guards who had been protecting the room saluted and left in a rush.

Although invisible, her subordinate Crouch was likely hiding somewhere, guarding against intruders.

"Only you and I will remain in this room."

The room Gando entered was spacious and devoid of furniture.

A carpet with golden patterns on a red background was laid out, and Uorin sat cross-legged in the center.

"Listen carefully from now on, Gando. The reset has already begun. And from now on, I will…"

Uorin's eyes sharpened as she spoke.

"I will find Anke Ra."
