Chapter 490:

Metiel remained silent.

From the beginning, Satiel was not always this cruel and cold-hearted.

In fact, in the early days of his creation, she was the most gentle and pure among the eight archangels.

McClain Goffin.

Although he had already left this world, his presence still had deep roots and influence in it.

Even in the mind of the archangel Satiel.

"They are approaching quickly."

Metiel turned her words as she looked at the battlefield.

It wasn't that she feared Satiel's madness, but it was better to be cautious with his words as Ra's will might be at play.

As Metiel turned her words, Satiel also deactivated her judicial halo and looked back at the battlefield.

The rebels were pouring in like a tide, and although the heavenly army was following, control was still lacking.

Even in one heaven, there were different skies.

Taking advantage of the Aegis interception system being paralyzed, the rebels broke through the walls and used unfamiliar strategies, which worked in their favor due to the lack of angelic command.

"Shouldn't we step in? If they reach Aravot at this rate…"

"I've already sent all the Mara. Now we must restrain our actions…"

Grrrrrr!

Before Metiel could finish her words, a tremendous roar shook the world from the east of Zebul.

A massive black sphere, about 200 meters in diameter, expanded, pushing away all objects and sucking in everything around it.

If the landscape of Zebul was a painting, it was an alien scene as if pressed by a brush, and the speed at which tons of rocks rotated around the sphere seemed slow in the face of its massive scale.

"That's Metatron's gravity field. What's going on?"

It was hard to believe that an archangel would directly intervene in battle, especially Metatron, who had lost his mouth for defying Ra's will.

Metiel and Satiel simultaneously unfolded their halos to look inside the gravity field.

Nothing could be seen due to the gravity that absorbed even light, but as Metatron's power gradually faded, a scene was revealed.

"That is…"

The eyes of the two archangels shook with shock.

"The angel's power… doesn't work."

Metatron muttered with the mouth of his mind.

If Ra hadn't destroyed his mouth, he would have been staring blankly at the person standing before him.

"Kekeke, is this the power of an archangel?"

Frankwine, caught in the gravity field, flexed his muscular body, naked.

His skin was red and blotchy, and the wounds inflicted by Etella were hideously scarred, but his expression was still smiling.

"Nothing special."

As Frankwine kicked off the ground, Metatron floated hundreds of gravity spheres, trapping him in an inescapable maze of attraction.

But surprisingly, Frankwine ignored all the gravity and approached Metatron directly, landing a punch.

Metatron, rolling heavily on the ground, looked at the floor with an expression of disbelief.

"……"

Without a mouth, he couldn't even groan.

"The fundamental concept is not being applied. That means…"

Reaching a conclusion, Metatron turned his head with eyes wide in disbelief.

Frankwine, with four triangles above his head, was arrogantly walking towards him, swinging his shoulders.

"Four-sided Mara. It's Satan!"

If the angel's halo symbolizes eternity, Mara's triangle symbolizes absoluteness.

And all these concepts are integrated to form the Akashic Records.

But Satan has four horns.

This was a concept unattainable by law, and thus Satan was unaffected by any law.

"The reason for prohibiting the angels' activities wasn't because of the Nephilim. Ra was…"

Ra had evacuated the angels fearing Satan's descent.

It was the right choice in the end.

Satan, being an entity outside the law, was the only concept that couldn't be erased by the Akashic Records.

Therefore, the most threatening variable in heaven was not the rebels or the Nephilim, but Satan.

"He must be eliminated now. If the angels fall, no one will be able to stop him."

The Akashic Records are perfect, so the number of laws is always one.

But using a special reason, it is possible to downgrade this immutable status to a general concept.

That is, assuming there is "something" outside the concept of the whole that refers to the whole.

No one knows what "something" is, but the moment it is assumed to exist, the concept is born.

From that point, the Akashic Records are downgraded to the number of laws being two, referring to each other, and eventually entering the zero-sum game that mages call the seesaw of law.

The entity born from the subjunctive, without substance, is Satan.

Perfect imperfection is the only concept that can erase the Akashic Records by law.

"Angels are said to have high self-esteem, but why not just give up? Before you end up in a more hideous state."

Metatron stood up, trembling all over.

Although his characteristic majestic shout could no longer be heard, his momentum was conveyed through the trembling air.

"This ends now!"

As the judicial halo activated, the area around Frankwine bent towards a point as if transparent water was being sucked into a funnel.

Frankwine's face twisted as the ability to compress gravity and pierce through space-time took effect.

"Krrrr!"

Then his body twisted around the point and quickly tightened towards the hole.

"Kekeke, this is… child's play."

Suddenly, Frankwine's body disappeared from space as if evaporating.

"……!"

Metatron's eyes were filled with shock.

As Frankwine's fist cleanly penetrated his thick chest, the halo above his head weakened like a candle wick burning out.

"It seems it can't be done…"

The reason angels are powerful is that they handle the deep principles of this world that no creature can approach.

But Satan, who ignores all these concepts, was the natural enemy of all angels, including the archangels.

The halo, which had been shining like a last struggle, exploded like glass, and Metatron's heavy body fell over.

"Foolish creature."

Frankwine looked down indifferently at the archangel, who was disintegrating into light, and unclenched his fist.

"So, what will happen now?"

When Satan proposed the deal, the information that most intrigued Frankwine was this.

The seesaw of law.

Every time he annihilated an angel, Frankwine's law grew stronger.

And now, one of the eight fundamental concepts had disappeared.

In other words, one-eighth of the principles governing the world had been removed.

As Metatron's corpse, turning into light dust, completely disintegrated into the air, Frankwine's eyes flashed open.

As the fly in his pupil spun madly, his body began to convulse.

"Ooooh! Ooooooh!"

A tremendous power surged in.

His entire body swelled like a balloon, his height rising to two meters, and his skin turned blood-red.

His eyes were wickedly slanted upwards, his teeth jutted out, and his thick neck flowed down like a mountain to his shoulders.

"Hahaha! Hahahahaha!"

Frankwine's voice, laughing wildly at the sky, was hoarse as if squeezed out by muscles.

Looking up at the building of Zebul, cut by the gravity field, he kicked off the ground and flew up.

Entering the 23rd floor of the 43-story cross-section, the subordinates of Yameng were lined up in a row, waiting.

"Welcome, boss!"

Frankwine smiled with a hideous face that had lost its original form.

Every step he took made the pale walls, symbolizing angels, flicker with ominous red shadows.

"Now, shall we start playing in earnest?"

Satan's angel hunt had begun.

Zebul 13th floor.

Armin, Siena, and Kuan formed a team and were heading to Ingris, where the Akashic Records were located.

Although it was not difficult to infiltrate Zebul due to the lack of angelic activity, the problem was that there was no special plan even if they arrived.

"I hope Etella brings the disk safely. I felt it during the deal, but those Yameng guys are very suspicious."

Siena shared the same thoughts as Armin.

Especially the leaders of Yameng, Frankwine and Meatgun, exuded a unique temperament different from the other members.

"Etella-sensei will be fine. I know her skills better than anyone."

Having lived together as teachers of the same age at Alpheus Magic School, the reason he couldn't close the distance with her was partly due to his reverence for her.

Beyond the title of bishop of the Karsis Monastery, she had a genuine heart for the world.

"Yes. Mr. Zulu is also a strong person."

Currently, Zulu is on a solo mission to find the Metagate.

It is presumed to be in the military factory underground in Zebul, but the outcome is uncertain due to the wartime situation.

Despite the precarious situation, Armin steeled his resolve.

As the angel's power released the stop, Armin's timeline was also refilled.

The only concern was Miro.

Is she safe?

Or did she fail to match the time and die when Kariel's magic circle exploded?

"Well, she's not a magician I should worry about. Just focus on protecting Siena."

Armin stopped and looked back at Siena.

"If an uncontrollable situation arises, come behind me unconditionally. I can block it once."

"Got it."

Armin's stop would certainly help, but for Siena, receiving help was only a part of the strategy.

"But make this clear. If you cast stop just to save me, I will never respect you again."

"Okay. I promise."

Armin answered without even thinking.

If he had been afraid of earning her resentment, he wouldn't have left the Ivory Tower and flown to heaven in the first place.

Whether she knew this or not, Siena snorted and quickened her pace.

Kuan, watching the two converse from behind, didn't feel very good.

What had he expected?

Did he think that sharing life and death together would bring about something new between them?

Because Siena had come to him directly with the proposal, did he think she might have some expectations as well?

'Foolish guy.'

Siena had Armin.

The war was reaching its climax, and no matter who won, this place would be his grave.

"Only death will save me."

"Huh?"

Siena turned around at Kuan's low muttering.

Even though he was not one to show his emotions, he hadn't even offered tactical opinions since entering Zebul.

It was as if he couldn't bear the thought of not cutting someone down, his eyes filled with a murderous intent.

'In this way, he's like a child…'

When she became a certified 5th rank, he was the first to send a congratulatory letter, and when she sent urgent funds to propose going to heaven, he came running.

It doesn't make sense to say she didn't know Kuan's true feelings.

Although he still didn't show his emotions, she could feel the disappointment hidden in his cold demeanor.

Siena smiled and made a mischievous joke.

"Kuan, it's the same for you. No matter what happens, the mission comes first, right?"

It was a statement that subtly revealed she understood his feelings, and it took great courage for her to say it.

But Kuan, without any change in expression, passed by her and replied firmly.

"Don't worry. I don't have the ability to protect anyone."

The mage from the Ivory Tower who had mastered time magic was protecting her.

Siena appreciated his concern, but she hated being pitied in any situation.

'Did I make a mistake?'

A joke she would never have made under normal circumstances. Perhaps she had the courage because this might be the last time.

Pouting her lips, Siena watched Kuan walk away.

His fatigue was evident as he limped more severely than usual, making his back look even more forlorn.
