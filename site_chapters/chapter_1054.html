Chapter 1054: Free Gene (1)

In the fire, Rian thought.

'Damn it.'

Purifying Ymir's Demon was on a different level from any emotion he had felt before.

'Is this Ymir?'

It was an endless dissatisfaction.

As if he hadn't been able to breathe for over a hundred million years, as if he hadn't been able to excrete for over a billion years.

'No living creature can endure this.'

But what would Ymir think in that cosmic contradiction, born to endure?

"Ahhh! I will eat it! I will eat it all!"

Monoras endlessly craved.

The more he did, the larger Gehenna's flames grew, and the land turned into lava, surging like the sea.

'The pinnacle of emotion.'

The reason Shirone couldn't act was the same reason Lete was watching.

'They are pushing each other beyond their limits. Not providing variables is actually helping.'

As Gehenna's chain transcended the limits of purification, Rian's body began to melt.

"Ugh!"

Seeing the burning flesh fall off, Monoras's eyes filled with blood.

"I, I…

The land of lava shook as large as a mountain.

"I will eat it!"

With a pop, Rian's body evaporated, and a cold smile appeared on Lete's lips.

"No one can stop Ymir's Demon. It's the same in reality…

She looked back at Shirone.

"It's unfortunate. Your and the Grand Duke's efforts have been in vain. Just give up."

Annihilation in the underworld literally meant the nothingness of existence.

"It can't be helped."

As Lete's eyes narrowed, Shirone spoke.

"…That's what you feel. When it happens, when such a result comes out."

The lava rippled unnaturally, and the entire land began to swell as if it were rising.

"But Rian is different. If he wants to do it, he will definitely do it. If Ymir's power comes from contradiction, Rian's belief is also a contradiction."

Rian's sword was a sword that denied everything.

"And denying everything means transcending the world."

It could be said like this.

"Divine Transcendence."

As the slowly swelling lava suddenly shot up to the sky, Lete turned her head.

Shock was evident in her eyes.

"That is…

It was Rian, so huge that he could probably be seen beyond the horizon of hell.

"Ahhh!"

The lava in the form of a Yaksha had no vocal cords, but the vacuum of the cavity alone was enough to shake the world.

At that moment, Lete realized.

"Monoras! Run away!"

But it was already too late; Monoras was in agony, showing the whites of his eyes.

"I will eat it! I will… I will…

Lete, who had been covering her face and twisting her body, opened her eyes as the heat disappeared.

An endless, massive pit had formed, black as the universe.

'Everything is purified.'

The land, Laviette, and the many cities around it had become nothingness.

Shirone entered the pit. "Rian!"

As he descended tens of kilometers, he heard a sound like tens of thousands of people screaming simultaneously.

'Resonance of darkness. This is bad.'

Rian had created an abyss by purifying the entire land.

'The sense of time is fading.'

Time doesn't flow in the abyss.

It's the same principle as time disappearing when all matter in the universe is removed.

"Rian!"

Feeling all eleven senses paralyzing, Shirone exploded the light of Agape.

If he went deeper, he wouldn't even be able to sense his own existence.

'Where is it? Where are you?'

The sharp light like a blade shattered the darkness but caught nothing.

'Damn it!'

It was no longer space.

"Hey, hey. Are you okay?"

In the darkness of the abyss, Son Yu-jung groped Rian's seemingly dead body.

Richela's voice came from her waist.

"Let's get out of here quickly. This place is strange. If we stay here, we'll go crazy."

Son Yu-jung felt the same.

'The abyss.'

A powerful nature that swallowed countless monsters.

"Son Yu-jung."

Hurriedly coming to her senses, Son Yu-jung refocused on the hand touching Rian.

'I can't let my guard down. If my attention wavers even a little, I'll lose my senses.'

Honestly, she doubted whether what she was touching was real.

"Ah, it's warm. Son Yu-jung. The sea is so beautiful. My mother, she drowned…"

"Shut up. Don't make a racket."

Son Yu-jung shook Rian.

"Hey, hey! Get a grip!"

"Hehehe! You idiot, why did you jump in to save this guy? You should have run away."

She wanted to smack Richela's head, but she couldn't tell where her hand was.

'Do you think I saved him because I wanted to?'

If she could act just once while injured, she had to grab Rian.

'In the abyss, you lose yourself. He's the only one who can escape this place.'

Idea was the only standard.

"Hey! Get up! Get up!"

Desperate, she tried anything, but it was probably difficult to regain consciousness.

'I've never heard of humans creating something like this. What's this guy's limit?'

Now she had to gamble.

'My hair. My hair.'

Using her last bit of concentration, she pulled out her hair and caused a string phenomenon.

"Huuu…"

A faint light seemed to illuminate the surroundings, but the darkness soon swallowed her hair.

She screamed.

'No! Get a grip!'

But it was only happening in her mind; no actual voice came out.

'Ah! Ah!'

No matter how much she screamed, her senses didn't return, and eventually, her consciousness was swallowed by darkness.

…

The moment even her ego disappeared, a drop of light from the string fell below.

"Huh?"

Feeling the refreshing energy cleanse his mind, Rian slowly opened his eyes.

'What is this?'

He couldn't see anything, and he quickly realized it was different from the darkness humans usually imagine.

'I can't feel anything.'

As he reached forward, Gehenna's chain was sucked in, and he grabbed .

"Stronger." The Ymir's Demon he had absorbed was filling his entire body with power as if it would burst.

"Idea."

The great sword pierced the darkness.

"Divine Transcendence."

A single coordinate that Rian had established redefined the void of the abyss into space.

"Rian!"

As the light of Agape surged in, it brightly illuminated Rian's surroundings.

As Shirone smiled, making eye contact with Rian, he saw Son Yu-jung with her joints twisted.

"Anyway…

It was commendable that she had tried to save Rian, and the Hand of God grabbed them.

"Let's go."

As they teleported, a flash pierced through the abyss in an instant.

"Whew."

"It's incredible."

"Yes. But this is just a part of hell. Purifying Jin Seong Eum will be much more difficult." Rian gripped the great sword and looked around.

"Where's Lete?"

"She left. We'll meet again soon. She'll be waiting where Jin Seong Eum is."

It was unfortunate they couldn't settle it immediately, but Rian decided to bury it in the darkness of the abyss.

'Marguerite.'

It would have been good to raise a glass.

'Rest in peace.'

Stang National Magic School.

When you wake up in the morning, make coffee, and go to work, the students are already training intensely.

"Yes. Good morning."

"Rampa."

"I don't know much about romance, but it's better to draw a clear line in such cases."

"It's because he can't say no."

Although she didn't show it, Amy couldn't help but feel a bit relieved by Rampa's words.

But she couldn't go to him first and lose face.

"Brother!"

As Shirone walked to the center of the training ground, the teachers and students stopped training and gathered around.

Wizard understood.

"It's a very cold technique. It's also cruel because you have to forget your loved one."

Shirone suddenly became serious.

"If you can't do it, you'll die."

"…Yes."

A curtain of light spread above Shirone's head, drawing a cross over 10 meters long.

"It's faster to see it than to explain. This vision magic will show my Force in real-time."

Wizard swallowed and nodded.

"Force."

Ka-ka-ka-ka-ka-ka!

"Unbelievable."

As the buff magic of the Five Great Stars came in, even the teachers, as well as the students, looked at their bodies.
