Chapter 501: Despair (4)

Yin-Yang Wave Fist: Heavenly Collapse Kick.

Etella's spinning kick struck Satan's abdomen.

Hundreds of waves that had already entered interfered, creating a massive shockwave that surged inside.

"Argh!"

It felt like his entire muscles were rippling like waves.

Satan flew to the end of Zebul, slumping against the wall.

'This can't be the end.'

Etella took a neutral combat stance, her left hand behind her back.

The second confrontation.

Just a few hours ago, she had faced death at the hands of Frankwine.

'And yet, he isn't moving…'

Because he has a plan.

As she expected, Satan's gaze went beyond Etella to Metiel and Satiel.

'Hmm, a follower of good. This is dangerous.'

There were two choices.

Annihilate the archangels to rapidly tip the seesaw of law, or use all his power to eliminate Etella.

Feeling Satan's gaze, Metiel spoke.

"Run, Satiel."

Satiel frowned.

"What? Run? We, archangels, run?"

"No, you go alone. Even if one escapes, we need to gather more angels. Otherwise, we can't win."

Though Satan was the nemesis of angels outside the law, Satiel couldn't easily accept it.

If he could be knocked back by a human's attack, it meant Satan's power wasn't that strong.

"Let's end it here. If we both attack without using our abilities, we can annihilate him."

"Not a chance. Satan is already strong enough and keeps getting stronger. The ordinary angels are falling to Garas."

"But that human's attack worked. Our attacks should work too."

"It's not about power. That human's law negates Satan's law, which is why it worked."

Satiel glared at Etella's back.

"Negates Satan's law? How is that possible? Humans, like angels, are bound by the law of the Akashic Records."

"Yes. Maybe that's why Anke Ra fears humans."

Metiel finally understood the true meaning of this war, but it was too late to explain.

"What do you mean? Ra fears humans?"

"Satiel, do as I say."

As Metiel spread her wings of light and flew, Satan pushed off the wall and charged.

His target was Etella.

'This is the most bothersome.'

The will of human goodness destroys Satan's will.

Even Satan didn't know how a being included in the Akashic Records could do that.

'But she's weak.'

That's the limit of living beings.

Meanwhile, Satan had grown more than twice as strong as when Etella had hit him, just by stalling for a moment.

'I can end it now.'

Satan charged at Etella with terrifying speed, raising his massive clawed hand.

At the same time, Metiel flew in from the side and struck Satan's ribs with her slender arm.

"Ugh!"

It was Metiel who grimaced.

Without the power of law, Satan was so solid that even considerable force couldn't budge him.

"Keke, sacrificing one to save another?"

A strategy to sacrifice one archangel to save another.

Even so, it wasn't a loss for Satan.

Twisting his upper body, Satan changed targets and pierced Metiel's chest with immense strength.

"Metieeeel!"

Seeing Satan's red arm burst through Metiel's back, Satiel screamed in rage.

Her wings spread wide, and her upper body leaned forward.

Opposite natures born at the same time.

Though they had been rivals all their lives, at this moment, it felt like losing half of herself.

"I'll kill you!"

As she prepared to strike with her golden wings, Satan's cold eyes targeted Satiel.

Annihilation.

Feeling a chilling fear, Satiel instinctively changed her mind and flew away with all her might.

'Keke, too late.'

The time Metiel bought by dying was only one second.

But that one second was the only time Satiel had to escape.

As she compressed her muscles to spring away, Etella's fist struck Satan's face.

Bang!

The impact sent Satan flying sideways, crashing into the corridor, while Satiel broke through the ceiling and escaped Satan's sight.

Satan clicked his tongue in disappointment and turned his reptilian eyes to Etella.

Despite growing so strong, his avatar shook with a single blow.

A strike with soul was no exaggeration at this moment.

Seeing Satiel escape, Etella widened the distance and took a combat stance.

"Deceitful being, don't try any tricks. There's only me left now."

"Ha ha ha ha ha!"

Satan laughed heartily, straightening his body and spreading his arms wide.

"I acknowledge that you are on the side of good. But…"

Satan's eyes turned to Metiel, who lay with a hole in her chest.

Her body turned to light and disappeared into fine particles.

Two archangels annihilated.

Two out of the eight parts of the Akashic Records were gone.

Rumble!

As Satan visibly grew larger, Etella stepped back with a serious expression.

"In the end, evil wins."

A 10-meter-tall red demon bared his fangs, looking down at Etella.

"Aaaah!"

Gaold's scream pierced the sky.

The power of pain amplified a hundred million times was strong enough to crush the fallen angels and Maras surrounding him, but it was only possible as long as he could maintain the concentration to use magic.

In extreme pain, Gaold was being sucked back into hell.

Just like that unforgettable time ten years ago in Kangnan's life.

"Argh! Argh!"

With his eyes completely rolled back, Gaold flailed his hands aimlessly.

Though the initial power had intimidated them, it was only a matter of time before they realized the Air Press had disappeared.

"Aah! Aaaah!"

Kangnan pulled with all her might on , but her body couldn't move forward even a step.

She couldn't let Gaold go like this.

But her desperate desire was shattered by the harsh reality.

Even in that tearful struggle, Kariel remained indifferent.

In the end, Miro didn't come.

As long as she was alive, humanity hadn't lost.

'Despicable Miro.'

He quickly scanned heaven with his vision, but couldn't sense her presence.

'Where are you? Where are you?'

At that moment, Kariel saw a shocking scene.

"Ikael…"

Kariel trembled, unaware that Uriel was looking back at him.

Ikael was dying.

No, it might have been satisfying.

What humiliated him was that she was being butchered by Faiel to protect Shirone, the second most despicable after Miro.

'Human! Dirty archangel who lay with humans! How far will you fall with that filthy body!'

Seeing Ikael risking annihilation to protect Shirone, Kariel remembered the source of his forgotten anger.

'Why does she…'

Not look at me?

A powerful flash of light soared into the sky and curved into Aravot with a roar.

'Ikael! Ikael!'

Shirone wept hot tears from bloodshot eyes.

Watching Ikael grow weaker under Faiel's aura, he hated his own helplessness.

Valhalla Action borrowed the principles of the Akashic Records, so even the caster couldn't know the boundaries of actions needed to reverse causality.

He could only feel powerless and resent everything in the world.

Understanding Shirone's feelings, Ikael never lost her smile, but her face had long lost its radiant light.

Every time Faiel broke through the barrier of amplification and pushed in the aura of annihilation, Shirone's heart grew more desolate.

'Hurry! Please hurry!'

Less than a minute remained until the price of Valhalla Action ended.

Each second felt agonizingly slow.

He would do anything.

If he could move, if he could return to a state where she could do something, he would willingly fall into hell.

"Shirone, when Valhalla Action ends…"

Ikael smiled kindly.

"Leave heaven. Never come back."

'No, Ikael!'

There were still so many things he hadn't heard.

Ten seconds remained.

Could Ikael survive until then?

As fear overwhelmed him, something began to condense in Shirone's heart.

'I won't forgive you!'

Imagining Faiel behind Ikael, Shirone endured the hellish ten seconds.

Nine seconds. Eight seconds.

'If only I could move…'

Four seconds. Three seconds.

'No matter the cost…'

Two seconds. One second.

He wouldn't forgive Faiel.

Valhalla Action reset.

Shirone's mouth opened.

"Icha…!"

"Ichaeeeeel!"

At the same time, a woman's voice, as if tearing apart, drowned out Shirone's voice.

Bang!

Satiel, who had flown here from Zebul to escape Satan, kicked Ikael and landed.

"Because of you…! It's all because of you!"

She brutally stomped on Ikael.

Even as Metiel, whom she had known the longest, was annihilated by Satan, she wasted her strength protecting a mere human.

'Why did he…'

Love such an unprincipled angel?

Ignoring the one who had always supported him despite all the angels' hatred.

"It's all because of you! Everything!"

, already weakened, couldn't even take a defensive stance as she was beaten.

Seeing this, Shirone's eyes blazed with fury.

Valhalla Action.

As the black-and-white magic circle spun above Shirone's head, Faiel moved to stop it.

The combination of Valhalla Action and Ataraxia was an unknown realm for angels.

Even if a human wielded it, he couldn't leave it alone.

"This ends now, Nephilim."

Just before Faiel's dark hand could pierce Shirone's neck, a flash of teleportation struck Faiel.

"Ugh!"

Plu, her face twisted in pain, tackled Faiel to the ground.

Thanks to her zoner’s excellent sense of distance, she had pinpointed the exact spot where the teleportation would end. If she had gone a bit further, her body would have been shattered.

"Shirone! Fire!"

Plu shouted, holding Faiel tightly.

As Valhalla Action activated and the sound of Ataraxia unfolding reached Shirone's ears, he closed his eyes tightly.

Whatever magic was cast, her life would end here.

Shirone's low voice was heard.

"Goodbye, everyone…"

Contrary to expectations, no magic was cast.

Also, the fact that he could speak during Valhalla Action meant the computation had already begun.

Feeling puzzled, Plu turned around to see Shirone moving towards Ataraxia.

Ikael, who was being attacked by Satiel, shouted in horror.

"No, Shirone! That is…!"

Before the shout ended, Shirone's body crossed the boundary of Ataraxia.

Flash!

At the same time, the world shone brightly for a moment, and Shirone's mental power amplified to fill and overflow Aravot.

Ataraxia - Physical Manifestation.
