Chapter 1040: Another Belief (3)

"Ugh! Ugh!"

Shirone emerged from the intestines into a secluded alley in Laviette city.

"Ugh, this feels really weird."

After experiencing intense intestinal movements for 20 minutes, he felt like he had become excrement.

"Huh?"

Smoke was rising from various parts of the city visible through the alley.

"What's going on?"

At the same time, a group of demons came running from the back of the alley.

"The intruder isn't alone! Find and slaughter them all! Show them the power of the Stealth Squad!"

Demons with animal-like features quickly passed by Shirone.

"Hey! You!"

A demon with a lion's face jumped down from a building and landed in front of Shirone.

"Who are you? You look like a demon, but don't you know what's happening in the city?"

"Uh, well…"

The lion-faced demon's expression turned fierce.

"Don't tell me you're running away?"

Since Croseide, one of the Seven Generals, was a demon, it was naturally shocking.

"You pathetic coward! How can a demon run away from the battlefield!"

The demon extended his sharp claws, ready to slash Shirone's throat mercilessly.

"What's the commotion?"

A woman with a cat's face approached, her fur standing on end.

"Ah, Captain!"

It was Oris, the Stealth Captain of the Seven Generals of Laviette.

"The city is in chaos, and instead of working together, you're fighting among yourselves? What a disgrace!"

"No, Captain. This guy is a deserter. He ran away in fear."

Oris examined Shirone and had the same thought as her subordinate.

'A demon. And an intelligent one.'

He didn't look like a combatant, and judging by his appearance, he seemed quite young.

"The death of a captain is sad. But it's not like Buddha has come, so why should demons be terrified?"

"I-I'm sorry."

If it could prevent suspicion of coming down from the mountains, being a deserter was acceptable.

Oris patted Shirone's head.

"Don't worry. Even if Croseide was the strongest in terms of power, war isn't won by strength alone. Your intelligence will surely be helpful. Come, I'll take you back to the unit."

As soon as she finished speaking, Oris grabbed Shirone and shot forward like an arrow.

"Wow."

Shirone could match the speed, but what surprised him was the incredible sense of balance.

'Definitely different from humans.'

"Hehe, don't be so surprised. If you train hard, you can run as fast as me someday. Though demons rarely put in the effort."

Wanting to leave Shirone with an honorable memory, Oris continued.

"You're an intelligent type. Running away from instinct means you're quite smart. But demons shouldn't run from enemies."

"Yes. I won't run away anymore." He meant it.

If running away made him more noticeable, it was better to fight.

"Haha! But don't worry too much. I'll take care of all the intruders."

Arriving in the city center where the combined forces of the Seven Generals were, Shirone joined the Special Task Force.

"…Deserted?"

After hearing the explanation from the Stealth Squad member, the 7th Squad Leader, Ishak, looked at Shirone with contempt.

'Tch, a weak intelligent type.'

He was probably someone who spent his time playing with numbers at a desk unless it was an emergency.

"Though you deserve to be killed immediately, I'll let it slide this time for Captain Oris's sake. Get in!"

"Y-Yes."

As Shirone joined the demons of the 7th Squad, it was like seeing a giant and a dwarf.

"Who's this kid? Can you even fight with that body? No matter how strong demons are…"

"Leave him. His mind is already rotten. Growing up won't change anything."

Ishak shouted.

"Quiet! There's a report of an enemy 200 meters west. Are you all ready?"

"Yes!"

The demons' responses were loud, and Shirone's voice added to the sound waves.

"Let's finish them off!"

With the 7th Squad's battle cry, 200 demons charged at incredible speed.

'Who is this intruder causing such chaos?'

Even while matching their speed, Shirone couldn't shake his curiosity.

At first, he heard it was a low-level demon.

But defeating one of the Seven Generals meant breaking the limits of their species.

"There!"

Amidst the barricades set up by Kimara's subordinates, a woman was rampaging.

"Screech! Screech!"

Shirone's expression turned incredulous.

'Son Yu-jung?'

The intruder in Laviette wasn't a low-level demon but an actual monkey.

"Screech! Too weak!"

Every swing of her two clubs shattered the armor of the security forces.

"Attack! Take her down before she escapes the encirclement!"

Following Ishak, 200 Special Task Force members charged at her simultaneously.

'This isn't right.'

Even as he was swept along by the army, Shirone sensed the futility of the battle.

'The great-granddaughter of the Monkey King.'

According to Omega, Sun Wukong was a martial god who had the longest record of fierce battles against Ymir.

'But why is she in hell?'

More importantly, she wasn't real.

Through Ultima, he sensed that she was just a special signal, not yet defined as a living being.

'A clone. So the real body is elsewhere…'

Her string ability was similar to Hexa's but differed in that it couldn't exist in reality.

'Though that reality is everything.'

In the end, it was half-baked power.

Yet Son Yu-jung's prowess overwhelmed the entire Special Task Force and security forces.

"Haha! Over here! Over here!"

As she roamed the city, Son Yu-jung changed direction and approached Ishak.

Ishak also generated electricity from his horns and threw a punch, but his arm was severed instead.

"Ugh!"

Though he was prepared to die, Son Yu-jung seemed to follow a principle of one strike per person and moved on.

"That bastard…!"

Enraged by the monkey's antics, Ishak turned his head but was soon dismayed.

His 200 subordinates were being crushed by the clubs without even a single clash.

"What the hell is that?"

As his subordinates fell one by one, Shirone's figure stood out.

Ishak shouted with bloodshot eyes.

"You idiot! Fight!"

From his belligerent perspective, Shirone seemed more hateful than the intruder.

"You brainless fool! I should kill you myself…!"

As if to echo his sentiments, Son Yu-jung swung her club at Shirone.

'It's over.'

It really seemed that way.

But as the illusion cleared, an unbelievable situation registered in his mind.

"W-What?"

Shirone had dodged the blow with just a twist of his waist, something even Ishak hadn't seen.

'Quantum movement.'

For the first time, Son Yu-jung's clone, finding nothing to hit, looked intrigued.

"Ook?"

Shirone recalled Marguerite's suppression rate of 7%.

'If I get more than 93% close to Yahweh, the hidden code will be released. I need to stay in the city until Lethe leaves.'

Son Yu-jung's clone grew irritated.

"Screech!"

No matter how many attacks she launched, she couldn't even graze Shirone.

'As expected, clones have significantly lower intelligence. They just mechanically repeat a few phrases.'

Without the Nimbus Cloud, a clone was less than half a being.

'What's the attribute of demons again?'

Fire and electricity.

'Then I…'

As Shirone moved with quantum precision, Son Yu-jung's clone made a startled face.

Probably an animal instinct.

Experiencing a probability that couldn't exist in nature, she became wary.

Though he was just approaching from behind, Son Yu-jung's clone screamed.

"Screeeech!"

Then, using all her strength, she twisted her body and swung her club.

The red afterimage passed harmlessly over Shirone's bowed head…

'Electric Photon Cannon.'

A blue electric sphere crackled menacingly in Shirone's hand.

'Take this.'

As he slammed it into her abdomen, Son Yu-jung's hair stood on end.

…

Unable to even scream, she stood with her mouth wide open before collapsing forward.

"Ugh, this is tricky."

To find out how she got here, he needed to meet the real body, but that would bring another set of problems.

"You, you…"

Meanwhile, Ishak stared at Shirone with a dazed expression.

'Controlling fire and electricity is a demon trait. But that movement…'

It wasn't an ability allowed to an ordinary demon.

"Name, no."

Holding his severed shoulder, Ishak knelt before Shirone.

"May I ask your name, my lord?"

"What?"

"I apologize for not realizing sooner. You possess a different kind of magic than us demons. I was rude to someone who inherits not Satan's flesh or blood, but his spirit."

If it wasn't a hidden code inherited by a few bloodlines, it couldn't be explained.

Ishak looked up again.

"Please, may I have the honor of knowing your name?"

Shirone searched Omega's records for a suitable name.

"Yupi."

"Oh, how splendid. A name that fits your intelligent and royal appearance perfectly."

In hell, there are over 4,000 words for lies, and Yupi's nuance was 'a small lie.' When someone asks how you're doing in passing, and you just say you're fine, that's Yupi.

"Lord Yupi, please become the captain of the Special Task Force. I will serve you with all my heart."

He didn't want to stand out, but he realized that demons' nature was the opposite of reality.

'Refusing here would be even stranger.'

It was like an employee shouting they wanted to work more even after the boss told them to go home.

'The strong must rule.'

It was the iron rule of hell.

"Fine. If there's no one stronger than me in the Special Task Force, I'll lead you."

"I can assure you. Though I'm not weak in the unit, I can't compare to you, Lord Yupi."

Ishak discarded his severed arm without hesitation and led Shirone back to the city center.

Where Son Yu-jung's clone had rampaged, demon corpses were scattered everywhere.

'What a mess. At this rate, Laviette will be destroyed in a few days.'

"Wait here."

Ishak said, lifting the tent flap of the temporary command post and entering.

"As of now, sightings at blocks 13, 27, and 66… Who are you?"

Execution Commander Itaka stopped explaining, and three generals turned simultaneously.

"Ah, I have urgent news, so I came despite the rudeness. I apologize."

A large demon with skin like burns blocked Ishak.

It was Defense Commander Moonom.

"If you know it's rude, don't do it. What's the matter? And what happened to your arm? Were you attacked?"

His face had melted, leaving him with one eye, and his lips were burnt, exposing all his teeth.

"Yes. But I took one down."

Itaka's eyebrows twitched momentarily as he listened with his fingers interlaced.

"So, to report a few things…"

Oris interrupted.

"Took one down? Did you really?"

If the reports were accurate, they were the only unit to subdue an intruder by force.

"No, it wasn't me. By Satan's grace, there's an exceptional demon in the Special Task Force."

Kimara asked.

"Where is he now?"

"Waiting outside the tent…"

Before he finished speaking, Itaka ordered.

"Bring him in."

Moments later, Ishak entered with a demon child, and silence fell over the tent.

'Small? Demons are known for their physical strength. How did he take down an intruder?'

Then Oris's eyes widened.

"Oh? You?"

As everyone turned to her, Shirone awkwardly smiled.

"Ha ha, hello?"
