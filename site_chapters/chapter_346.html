Chapter 346: Divine Transcendance (3)

“Wow…”

As exclamations of admiration erupted from all around, Bawio turned his head with a triumphant look. However, the object of admiration was not Bawio, but Rian. Everyone couldn't take their eyes off him.

Rian’s body was developed without missing a single small muscle. It was astonishing that such a variety of muscles could fit inside human skin. It felt perfectly compact, like it had been squeezed into a mold.

Even the male cadets, who prided themselves on their physical prowess, were mesmerized, so it was no wonder that Tess had hearts in her eyes.

"Rian…"

But her gaze soon turned wistful. She knew how much pain Rian had endured to perfect his current body.

After consuming Ymir's arm, Rian’s right arm had developed abnormally. While increased strength was good, the imbalance was not. From that point on, Rian undertook extreme training, swinging a club heavier than a greatsword 3,000 times a day.

The feedback from his body was truly remarkable, and over time, his entire body began to develop in a way that balanced with his right arm.

Thus, Rian’s current body was born.

His perfectly balanced physique had become four times stronger than it was six months ago.

He might be the strongest person among those who couldn't use Schema.

"He has matched his body to the sword."

While the cadets marveled at Rian’s appearance, Kuan focused on the functional aspects.

It wasn't a body built for simple goals like strength or endurance.

It was optimized for the task of wielding a sword.

"But it's quite peculiar. It's not the first time I've seen a trained body, yet it feels like I'm seeing one for the first time."

Kuan's sense of unease was accurate.

Rian’s body had surpassed the limits of what a normal human could achieve through the feedback from his abnormal right arm.

"Anyway, we'll see what that body can do among the Schema users."

Kuan put his thoughts aside and raised his head.

"Everyone, attention. I will briefly explain today's evaluation."

The cadets, who had regained their composure, focused on Kuan. Even Bawio, who had been glaring at Rian in annoyance, turned his head.

"Wrestling is a major subject for heavy infantry positions. Therefore, we won't require any special techniques from rookies like you. You will pair up and compete, and strikes are allowed as long as you remain in close contact. Since weight classes differ, wins and losses won't be part of the evaluation criteria, and you may use 'folding'."

The cadets' eyes changed at Kuan's last words.

Schema is a virtual human diagram that exerts its effects by layering multiple diagrams of a specific build.

'Folding' is an advanced Schema technique that folds a specific human diagram in half, creating an effect as if two diagrams were layered.

For example, if a swordsman who bases their technique on strength enhancement uses folding, their strength increases by more than double.

At higher levels, double folding is possible, known as 'double'. This progresses to triple, quadra, and penta, with the current human limit known to be seven folds.

Of course, even a single fold is a difficult technique for cadets. However, Kaizen Swordsmanship School, where they were, is a prestigious institution that gathers the most talented individuals in Tormia.

Even though they were still considered rookies by the instructors, more than half of them could perform basic folding.

Tess looked at Rian. As expected, his expression was dark. Rian’s skills were not inferior to other cadets. However, as long as Schema was part of the evaluation criteria, he would always be at the bottom.

Schema techniques include more than just folding.

There are numerous advanced techniques, such as 'penetration', which simultaneously manifests the properties of two human diagrams, 'reversal', which inverts the technique build, and 'cross', which folds the human diagram diagonally.
