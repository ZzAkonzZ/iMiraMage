Chapter 629:

Within the Force Dimension, Shirone dominated space and time, making it seem impossible for anyone to break the absoluteness of that dimension.

Shirone and Fermi were the two massive pillars controlling the battlefield, and the participants' goals couldn't be separated from these two.

"It's a tight match. Neither Shirone nor Fermi are wavering," Siena remarked.

Etella responded, "Fermi is facing more opponents. Conversely, it could mean the participants are more wary of Shirone."

When Shirone first said that, no teacher could believe their ears.

"He's already surpassed the limits of tension. He probably thinks of nothing but passing," Olivia commented.

"No," Alpheas disagreed. "Even that isn't in his mind anymore."

'It feels like being hit by a metal chunk.'

'I will win! In my master's name!'

As the massive Dark Worm, conjured by the power of darkness, arched and attacked, Shirone's movements faltered.

"Oh no!"

Poltar flinched, but Miro remained calm.

"His rhythm won't break from this."

Cracks appeared in the shadow of the Dark Worm, and the beams exploded, completely dispelling the darkness.

"A victory of strength."

Arin's mind, casting Mind Control, was counterattacked and destroyed by Geumgangtae's mind.

"A victory of mental power."

Shining Chains destroyed Conger's beams and struck his solar plexus.

"A victory of precision."

Homing Photon Cannons bypassed the ice and bombarded Prings mercilessly.

"A victory of sensitivity."

Elizabeth shivered at Baikal's assessment.

"He's overwhelming in every domain."

The situation of Shirone defeating his competitors with their own specialties seemed surreal to the scouts.

"Why wouldn't he feel resentment?"

Once called a genius, Alpheas could understand Shirone's feelings.

"Why wouldn't he want to give up? He's human too."

Shirone, single-handedly pushing back his enemies, gritted his teeth and fired Photon Cannons in rapid succession.

'They said I couldn't become a mage!'

There were twenty years of thinking that way.

-Participant Number 6 has exited the 2000 system.

Elizabeth's voice grew louder.

"Participant Number 18! 20! Eliminated!"

Binder and Suavi, engulfed by the Force Dimension, lost consciousness and fell from Shirone's strike.

'They're all just dropping like flies.'

Luman fled the Force Dimension, and Prings, the only one surviving within Shirone's rhythm, didn't dare approach a second time.

"The situation is getting interesting."

Miro's gaze turned to Fermi.

'What will you do? At this rate, you'll really get devoured.'

If Shirone enforced his system, all participants would likely launch a full-scale attack on Fermi.

'Hmm, is this the graduation exam?'

Even so, Fermi remained calm, and Hershey, who was pushing him, grimaced as if fed up.

"What a monster."

Even with former members of the Golden Wheel, Lycan and Richard, joining, Fermi still controlled the situation.

At that moment, Fermi's cold gaze targeted Hershey.

"Child's play. The 2000 system is nothing."

Disaster Magic - Black Death.

Black smoke rose from Fermi's body and spread in all directions.

"What…!"

The teachers jumped up, and the scouts' eyes widened.

"That's black magic, isn't it?"

"I thought it was lost."

It was the magic of the Black Line mage, Witch Carnes, who once roamed the world and massacred three million people.

Fermi's mouth twisted wickedly.

"This is the power of money."

The smoke spread as if an explosion had occurred at the center, and black spirits emerged from the edges of the cloud.

"Dodge! If it touches you, it's over!"

All participants escaped the smoke, but few avoided the spirits.

"Number 15 (Closer) eliminated! Number 23 (Hershey) eliminated! Number 13 (Dorothy) eliminated! Number 19 (Pandora) eliminated!"

With four participants eliminated simultaneously, the scouts began reviewing the rules.

In reality, participants affected by the spirits would have died in excruciating pain as their flesh rotted.

"As long as the 2000 system can implement it, there's no problem with the rules. However, since it's a banned spell by the Red Line, there might be some issues later," Lara said.

"Black Death is evil because it can't arbitrarily target. That's why it's strong. The eliminated participants must be furious."

"You can't say they were just unlucky."

Baikal alternated his gaze between Shirone and Fermi.

"After all, they chose Number 1."

As the effect of Black Death faded, the battle entered a lull.

With nine participants eliminated by Shirone and Fermi's attacks, the remaining participants were deep in thought.

'Now there are 16 survivors left.'

If six more were eliminated, graduation would be guaranteed.

'I can't believe I've made it this far.'

Maya's heart pounded as she thought about how she had always been the first to be eliminated in the graduation exams every year.

Although she felt she had benefited from the clash between Fermi and Shirone's systems, that wasn't her main concern.

"Chief, I think my sister might actually pass."

The mysterious tribe members sitting in a corner of the audience clasped their hands together with hopeful eyes.

"Maya…"

Though the chief knew little about magic, he could see that Maya's skills were lacking.

'Poor girl.'

Maya was a genius, but the battlefield, ruled by cold logic, was not where she belonged.

'I must pass.'

She was willing to accept her fate for the sake of her tribe, but reality was harsh.

'It's Maya.'

'We need to eliminate Maya first.'

In a situation where eliminating just one person would significantly increase the chances of passing, targeting the weakest, Maya, was the logical choice.

As Pisho, Luman, and Sabina attacked from three directions, Maya felt a wave of darkness wash over her.

'I can't fall here!'

Just as she gritted her teeth with determination, Cayden stepped in front of her and began attacking the three.

"Why?"

The most baffling thing in this graduation exam was Cayden's actions.

Rumors had it that the faction led by Fermi had disbanded, but the main reason Maya had made it this far was that Cayden always appeared at critical moments to secure her position.

'It must be a coincidence.'

In a graduation exam where personal feelings were not allowed, there was no reason for Cayden to fight for someone he wasn't particularly close to.

"The dynamics are getting complicated."

This was the biggest turning point in the graduation exam, the moment when the true distinction between friend and foe disappeared.

'Rapid Stream!'

A deep blue whirlpool of water swirled around Pony and violently swept in all directions.

'Mother, are you watching? Your daughter is carving out her own future.'

"Total weight: 4.2 tons. Rapid coefficient: 3.3. Kinetic energy per unit area: 983 water per cubic meter."

"Please evaluate. Only Number 11's grade hasn't been recorded yet."

Pony was royalty.

"Royal authority surpasses that of the association. Record it as S."

"It's the reality of royal rule. From now on, don't analyze Number 11. No matter what grade is given, all magical institutions under the kingdom will bow and take her in."

"…Understood."

As Lara recorded it in her notebook, Baikal added, "Of course, she still has to pass first."

With the top 10 within reach, attacking Shirone and Fermi was a foolish choice, and no one had been eliminated in the intense standoff.

'Graduation is assured.'

Having assessed the situation, Fermi leisurely crossed the chaotic battlefield, waiting for others to self-destruct.

"Hmm?"

At that moment, a flash from a Photon Cannon surged towards him.

'Ghost Movement!'

His automatic response algorithm made him lean back, and he saw Shirone flying towards him.

'Fermi. I won't let you pass.'

Reading Shirone's thoughts, Fermi's eyes gleamed with malice.

'What a brilliant fool!'

As the two S-rated mages clashed head-on, the audience's attention was riveted.

There was no doubt about Fermi's skill in handling Quantum Superposition with numerous high-level spells as if performing magic tricks.

"Definitely Number 1."

The more this happened, the more Shirone's anger grew.

'Unforgivable!'

All the magic Fermi used had been bought with the defeats of students who had given their all for six years.

Disaster Magic - Black Death.

'I'll pass with a smile, Shirone!'

It was the moment when the true incarnation technique, Shirone's omniscient divine particle, merged with the Light Angel's incarnation.

"Damn it…!"

Incarnation Technique - Angel's Punishment.
