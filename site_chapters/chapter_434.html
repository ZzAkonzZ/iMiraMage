Chapter 434:

One of the eight slots in the Fuzix Machine was filled with Isermon's plasm.

Seven slots remained.

Raycis planned to combine the traits of other creatures—Voltum, Grabber, and Liston—to enhance Isermon's Antithesis ability.

Voltum, which looked like a mosquito magnified a thousand times, had a unique trait called "blood adhesion," which created a bond with the target whose blood it sucked. This was presumed to be a psychological mechanism evolved to suppress hemolytic reactions, and in the lab, it was used as a tool to subjugate organisms.

"I will pour Voltum's plasm."

With the addition of the plasm of Grabber, which had levitation abilities, and Liston, which increased the activity of organisms, the four types of plasm were ready to be mixed in the Fuzix Machine.

Unlike the Elixir of Legends, to draw out unique traits, a main type had to be set. Currently, Isermon was the main type, and the three sub-types would enhance the Antithesis.

"Let's begin."

When the button was pressed, the four types of plasm, which had filled the glass spheres halfway, were all sucked into the central glass tube.

As electricity flowed through the vacuum, the liquid began to bubble and boil.

"Ohhh!"

It was always a marvelous sight.

Researchers who once felt repulsed by inhumane biological experiments couldn't help but feel the joy of creation at this moment.

As electricity discharged in the glass tube, a blinding white light spread out.

While everyone turned their heads, only Raycis watched the changes inside the glass tube with ecstatic eyes.

As the new species was born, the glass tube split into four parts and fell to the floor.

"It's a success!"

The researchers were amazed by the completely different form of the creature, which deviated from their predictions.

It was a giant eyeball with a diameter of one meter, covered by a thin epidermis that acted as an eyelid. From the area where the optic nerve would connect, long, dark red tentacles extended and fluttered like a tail.

Biological Weapon - Akamai (Prototype)

A cold shiver ran through the laboratory.

Could this even be called a living creature?

Looking at something organic that had no relation to environmental factors and only served human purposes, it felt like there was nothing that shouldn't happen in the world.

"Haaa."

Raycis's red lips parted slightly.

What a beautiful form!

God had given creatures the potential for infinite change.

"Ugh!"

Raycis's face, which had reached ecstasy, twisted in pain.

Her red cloak fluttered as something inside it moved roughly.

A biologist approached with a frightened expression.

"Commander, you need to make the contract now…"

Raycis quickly corrected her expression.

The sensitive stimulus felt like her intestines were spilling out, but fortunately, it soon reabsorbed into her body.

"Right, I almost forgot."

Raycis cut her wrist with her nail and offered her blood to Akamai.

Thud. Thud.

As the blood was absorbed into the epidermis, Akamai's large iris contracted tightly.

Then, as if showing affection, it blinked its eyelid a few times while looking at Raycis.

The blood adhesion of Voltum was complete.

"Is it a success?"

"Of course. It has become my loyal subordinate. Now, let's get serious. Bring Shirone's clone."

When the researcher contacted the underground, the floor split open, and Shirone's clone came up on an elevator.

Currently, Shirone's brain was empty.

He was like an animal with only basic desires such as eating and reproducing.

When the researchers shone a light through a device disguised as a brainwave scanner, the pattern of memories was transmitted through the retina.

It was similar to the method jointly researched by Arcane and Alpheas.

However, the failure factor was the same, and the clone with the implanted memories would soon suffer a mental collapse and die.

Of course, this was not a problem for Raycis.

Once Shirone absorbed all the traits of the creature, she only needed to swallow him.

Boom! Boom! Boom!

A rampage was unleashed from the bed where Shirone lay.

Researchers were swept away by the shockwave, and Shirone's body sprang out like a wild beast, seeking a safe zone.

'What is this? Where am I?'

Shirone moved his eyes left and right to gather information.

Laboratory. Raycis. Strange devices he had never seen before.

He still didn't understand the situation.

He had been lying next to Plu, undergoing a brainwave test, and suddenly he was here.

"What have you done to me?"

"Hmm, who knows?"

Raycis tapped her lips.

"I'd like to explain, but I won't. I have a feeling I'll have to say the same thing quite a lot in the future."

When Raycis snapped her fingers, Akamai, floating in the air, activated Antithesis.

"Ugh!"

As if the air had turned into solid iron, Shirone couldn't move a finger.

"This, this is…?"

"Sorry. To use the Nephilim's abilities, you need to have intelligence. It will be over soon."

The Immortal Function began with insight, something a clone with only animalistic instincts could never achieve.

Shirone ground his teeth.

For someone who said sorry, her eyes were far too gleeful.

Shirone, captured by Akamai, was moved into the glass sphere of the Fuzix Machine.

Unlike corpse experiments, it was necessary to confirm mental changes, so it was effective to put him in whole and dissolve him directly.

"Commander, what should we combine him with?"

A Nephilim with 100% affinity could easily embrace the traits of any creature.

And to verify this, it was best to use a creature with advanced functions.

"Combine him with Akamai."

"What? But…"

"It doesn't matter. This is just a prototype. It's the perfect opportunity to experiment with Shirone's clone."

The biologist obediently followed the order.

When the preparations were complete, plasm filled the glass sphere containing Akamai and Shirone.

The black liquid passed Shirone's neck and covered his face.

After a while, the level dropped, flowing into the central glass tube, and Shirone's form disappeared without a trace.

The researchers watched the glass tube emitting a powerful light with tense faces.

Main type Shirone, sub-type Akamai.

The two creatures melted perfectly and were reborn as a single organism.

"What, what is this?"

The glass tube separated, and Shirone stood there with his eyes closed.

Even though the focus was on function combination, mixing two creatures with different forms should have resulted in some changes.

In the unexpected situation, the biologist adjusted his glasses and examined closely.

"This is just a human. Could the experiment have failed…?"

At that moment, Shirone's eyes snapped open, and a light burst from his differently colored left pupil.

"Aaagh!"

The researchers screamed in twisted postures as their bones contorted.

Raycis, who had quickly distanced herself, wore a dazed expression.

'He only absorbed the functions?'

The changes that occurred in the plasm state were known only to those who had experienced it firsthand.

And Raycis was one of the few humans who had experienced the plasm state the most in the world.

When immersed in plasm, thought ceased. But within the liquid, integrated as a single substance, countless signals flowed.

'It's the Ultima System.'

Therefore, if one could control the unified signals in the plasm state, it was entirely possible to selectively acquire functions and forms.

'This is dangerous.'

No, it was beautiful.

The ability to possess the traits of any creature in existence.

It was literally reaching the realm of gods.

"What have you done to me?"

Raycis's lips trembled.

Shirone had even controlled his consciousness. Akamai had completely submitted.

'I want that ability even more.'

Raycis spread her arms and approached.

"Shirone, let's become one."

Shirone activated Antithesis. The overwhelming binding force stopped Raycis in her tracks.

But she instead savored the thrilling pleasure.

It was a power on a different level from Isermon's Antithesis.

Shirone mercilessly fired a Photon Cannon.

Raycis was slammed into the wall, and the ceiling shook. With each flash of light, the dust cloud grew larger where she was.

"Haa. Haa."

Shirone's eyes, filled with shock, wavered as he panted and glared ahead.

Emerging through the smoke, half of Raycis's face had transformed into a grotesque monster.

Her eyes slanted upward as if to tear her forehead apart, her pupils were as thin as a snake's, and her lips, which stretched across her lower face, were filled with three times as many teeth as a human's.

"Kii, Shi…rone…"

Shirone, horrified by the sound like nails on a chalkboard, opened his palm to summon Armand.

'Diamond Armor… huh?'

It was then that Shirone realized he didn't have Cubrick.

No one could have taken it. He hadn't lost consciousness from the moment he underwent the brainwave test until now.

'Could it be, I'm not myself?'

Shirone quickly retraced the steps.

He hadn't lost consciousness, but the process from the brainwave test to here was completely cut off.

The liquid he saw in the Elixir of Legends. The changes from being dissolved in it to being reconstituted by the Ultima System.

'I've been cloned!'

Not being the original, he didn't have Cubrick. And Raycis was using this for some experiment.

'A biological combination similar to the Elixir of Legends.'

A strong insight flashed in Shirone's mind.

'Armand, if it's Armand…!'

Something swung from behind Raycis's cloak, and Shirone's neck was severed from his body.

"Whooo."

Raycis quickly retracted the part of her body that had extended.

It was a grotesque form, but the researchers didn't show any reaction.

The moment they assigned meaning to the physical form, they would end up like Shirone, with their heads flying off.

"From now on, double the production of clones. We must find out what changes have occurred."

The biologist spoke with concern.

"It's too dangerous. Shirone controlled the biological combination. If, by any chance, the commander is devoured in reverse, no creature will be able to stop Shirone."

Raycis didn't like that possibility either.

"For now, only experiment with Type A. The clones before Babel. As long as the Ultima System isn't involved, it should be fine."

"But the original is Type B. The danger remains unchanged."

"It doesn't matter. If I set myself as the main type, even the Ultima System won't be able to exert its power."

According to mythology, the Elixir of Legends was created to destroy the Ultima System, but since it was unclear how much of that was true, it couldn't be guaranteed.

Raycis gave a bitter smile.

"Even if I get devoured."

"What?"

"If Shirone and I become one, it will be something that is neither me nor Shirone. That will suffice. As long as I can become one with Shirone, I won't stop the experiment. Proceed as planned."

Even if the main type was set, the resulting creature would be entirely new.

Therefore, defining herself with specific characteristics wasn't important to her.

But the biologist still felt uneasy.

Raycis thought it was enough to just mix, but the Ultima System perfectly controlled biological combinations.

And the researchers didn't know how that was possible.

'Yes, Shirone can't overturn the situation. At best, he can only become one with the commander.'

Despite this, the biologist's vast experience in the lab made him uneasy.

Being optimistic that nothing would happen meant that the information they had was limited to a narrow scope.

Variables always lie in the unknown beyond perception. And when something starts moving in that darkness…

'Is it really okay to proceed like this?'

A bio-shock will occur.
