Chapter 1088: Reload (2)

The Operator's pure smile on the big screen felt like a twist to the users.

It was far from the divine beauty they had imagined…

"She's pretty."

But she could smile just like them.

"Yeah, she's pretty."

As Yahweh2 pulled his arm out, the Operator staggered back and slowly lifted her head.

"I lost. And the Solar War too. By the time I revive, everything will probably be over…"

High Gear overloaded.

"Enjoy it to the end."

As her engine exploded, her machine shattered into pieces and disappeared.

"Wow!"

East Country users cheered.

"Charge! Charge the Solar Palace!"

Since the system allowed for revival, the end of the Solar War was always one side's annihilation.

As the balance of power rapidly collapsed, West Country users dwindled by the hundreds.

East Country users surged like a massive wave, completely taking over the Solar Palace.

"We've arrived! We've arrived!"

Though part of the third team, Destroyer707 could enter because he belonged to Golden Wheel.

"Come on, you bastards!"

Shouting boldly as he looked inside, he saw no West Country rankers.

The only survivor, Little Witch, raised her hands in surrender.

"I surrender. Don't kill me. The rankers won't revive for a while anyway."

Though death was common, Uorin wanted to stay until the end to watch Shirone.

"Sorry."

Yolga's son raised his gun.

"We can't leave any variables. Golden Wheel's motto is to handle things thoroughly."

As he aimed at her forehead, waiting for the critical hit, Little Witch muttered.

"…You bastard."

Fermi laughed cheerfully.

"Come back next time."

Fermi found dealing with Uorin much easier than with Shirone or the Operator.

'She's not a troublemaker.'

A single gunshot rang out, and Little Witch fell backward.

Finally, with all West Country rankers gone from the Solar Palace, only the capture remained.

Seeing Yahweh2 approaching on the screen, Fermi spoke to Destroyer707 and his team.

"Hey, you guys."

"Yes? Yes!"

As the guild leader called, they ran over nervously.

"Want to give it a try?"

"Try what?"

Fermi pointed to Low Gear in the solar furnace.

"Capturing it."

Entering the user code into Low Gear from the siege side would determine the Solar War's outcome.

Conversely, the defending side would win if they held out for 24 hours.

"Me?"

Usually, rankers captured Low Gear unless the situation was urgent.

"Yes. Since you're part of Golden Wheel, you can earn the primary defense guild qualification."

"But there are rankers…"

"Haha! It's okay. They're all my employees."

Golden Wheel was a professional group focused on making money, so the honor of the Solar War didn't matter.

"Besides…"

Fermi thought of Shirone.

'He'll like it.'

A customer who complained if they weren't 100% satisfied, even if they liked 99%.

Death Princess said,

"Let's do it. When will we get another chance like this? All High Gear users will be watching us."

"Right?"

Even now, the video was rolling, so Destroyer707 straightened up.

"Then I'll…"

With Death Princess and Strongest Code Name by his side, he headed to Low Gear.

'It's cold.'

Everything felt vivid, yet he felt nothing.

Representing the East Country, Destroyer707.

'This is the weight of the crown.'

Realizing his emotions, he stopped and sneered.

"Heh."

His lips trembled.

'I'm… feeling fear?'

Death Princess whispered.

"What are you doing? Everyone's watching. If you won't do it, I will."

"I'll do it."

Taking a deep breath, Destroyer707 opened Low Gear's core and reached out.

As his palm touched it, the gauge for changing Low Gear's user quickly filled.

"Whoooa!"

Feeling embarrassed, Death Princess whispered.

"You don't have to shout."

As Strongest Code Name smiled, a message appeared on Low Gear's screen.

User change complete. Code name Destroyer707. Transmitting Low Gear's power to the East Country.

All East Country users, who had been waiting for this moment, raised their hands.

"We won!"

Bullets pierced the sky, and Destroyer707's team hugged and jumped in joy.

As Yolga's son turned, he saw Yahweh2, who had arrived with a smile.

'Good.'

Seeing Fermi's satisfied expression, he sighed in relief.

'Now help me out too.'

The Solar War has ended. By operator privilege, all users will revive fully charged.

The world was enveloped in light.

As East Country users gathered at the Solar Palace, West Country users revived at the outskirts of the plateau.

The Solar War rankings are being tallied.

The rankings, considering accumulated damage and user destruction counts, were displayed from 1st to 100th place.

Yahweh2 was not on the list.

"What a shame. He was the star of this Solar War."

"It's because it ended too early. If it had lasted over 12 hours like before, he would have made the rankings."

Shirone didn't mind, and shortly after, he received a communication from the Operator.

It's me. I've revived.

I see.

As promised, I'll help you with your task. But first, I need to announce something. I'm going to delete my code name.

Shirone couldn't respond.

Even being away for just 10 days in reality, a lot changes in High Gear. It's hard to maintain rankings. To work with you in reality, this is the right move.

Now that Shirone had grown attached to High Gear, he felt uneasy.

Sorry, because of me.

It's okay. My undefeated record is broken anyway. When I come back, I want to start fresh too.

With that, the communication ended, and the Operator made an announcement to the world using her administrative authority.

"This is the Operator."

Hearing the voice from the sky, all East Country users at the Solar Palace came outside.

"There were many issues in this Solar War. Most notably, my defeat."

It was quiet.

"Looking back, a lot has happened in High Gear. Memories that are irreplaceable."

With a tone of farewell, even the rankers reminisced about their time with her.

"I want to start anew. But before that, I have something to tell you all."

The announcement paused.

It was a significant decision for the Operator, so users were willing to wait as long as needed.

"From this moment on, I…"

At that moment.

An event will occur shortly.

A system message interrupted her voice.

"What?"

As users murmured, the horizon began to distort horribly.

'It's tearing through the code.'

New episode. Dr. Martin's Revenge. He will descend to steal the sun forever.

From the grotesque crack on the horizon, a giant eye stared at them.

"An eye?"

The next moment, the eye broke through the landscape, revealing Dr. Martin's face made of scrap metal.

"The time has come! Foolish creatures! You will pay for betraying the gods!"

The ground shook from the sound alone.

As users struggled to grasp the situation, the operator's meeting room became chaotic.

"Stop it quickly! We didn't even announce it! And what's Dr. Martin's Revenge?"

"This wasn't the planned event. The only one who could do this is Number Seven."

"Whatever it is, end it!"

Black Circle Mark said.

"Most of the administrative authority is blocked. It's a well-crafted program. We can't break through immediately."

"What?"

The operators checked.

Accessing the code with their authority, they only received denial messages.

"This madman…"

In a dark space filled with hundreds of screens, Number Seven stood alone.

Having confirmed everything happening in High Gear, he entered the final approval code.

"This ends the service."

Dr. Martin's machine, made from all High Gear designs, invaded the world.

Watching the Operator's face on the screen, Number Seven bit his lip.

"You're quitting?"

What about everything we've done?

"No, you can never quit. So watch closely. The world of High Gear…

The world you loved the most.

"See how it abandons you."

As Dr. Martin tore through the landscape and slammed his fists into the ground, a thunderous noise erupted.

"Finally, my grand ambition is realized! I will destroy Low Gear! Immortality ends here!"

His lower body was not visible.

Only his upper body, having pierced through the code, sat on the horizon like a rising sun.

"What is this new event? How will we generate power if Low Gear is destroyed?"

Starting the new episode.

Dr. Martin's face, patched with various metal structures, twisted.

"Die."

A beam from one eye scraped the ground, burning West Country users.

"Dodge!"

Despite quick reactions, 42 users vanished, and shortly after, Little Witch received a communication.

After receiving the report, she spoke to the Operator.

"Connection is impossible."

"What?"

"If you die, you're ejected from High Gear. I don't know why, but reconnection is impossible."

Finally sensing the gravity of the situation, the Operator received a message from an admin.

It's serious! Number Seven plans to shut down High Gear. The admins are tied up.

"This…"

Before she could voice her anger, Dr. Martin's second beam scraped the ground.

"Ugh!"

Though fewer died, the West Country's camp was in disarray.

Yahweh2's voice was heard.

"Operator!"

Sliding to a stop, Shirone arrived with East Country users.

"What's going on?"

"It's an emergency. One of the admins has rebelled. If this continues, High Gear's service will end."

All their conversations were relayed to users across the continent through the forum.

Dr. Martin shouted.

"Kahahaha! Yes, you wouldn't dare challenge me! Then I'll destroy the Solar Palace right now!"

Users murmured.

"How is this fair? I worked so hard to gather parts! We have to stop him!"

"How? Can't you see the level?

Level 3,300? And…

Users checking the durability through augmented reality were dismayed.

72,000,000/72,000,000

"We can do it."

Shirone said.

"If East and West Country users unite, we can take him down. We can't end it like this!"

"Yes. We'll fight."

"All hands to battle stations! We will defeat Dr. Martin and protect the Solar Palace! All users watching this video, come to the Zeta Plateau!"

Click! Click!
