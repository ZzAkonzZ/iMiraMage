Chapter 854: Ugly Woman (1)

Tranquil waves crashed onto the white sandy beach.

Perhaps it was because the armies of Hell were sweeping across the world, but the current calmness of the landscape, which was everything, felt thrilling.

"It's beautiful."

To Seongeum, looking beyond the sea, the sight was more beautiful than the ocean itself in Shirone's view.

"Space is an illusion..."

If the existence called 'country' is erased, what meaning would the size of the world we live in have?

"I've thought about it."

Seongeum turned to look at Shirone.

"Time is an illusion, so is space. But looking at the sea like this, what does it matter?"

Isn’t it just beautiful?

To someone beyond the planets, the sea might just be disgusting regurgitated matter, but to Seongeum, it was beautiful.

"That's the heart."

Planting the heart in a world of illusions.

"That’s right."

The reason Shirone enjoyed his time with Seongeum was because she understood him.

"But Shirone, I don’t have a profound philosophy towards the world like you or Nane. All I can do is prevent the destruction of my homeland."

For Jincheon alone.

"Seongeum, tell me. After spending a day with me, what will become of you? What are you planning to do?"

Seongeum changed the subject.

"Shirone, do you think I could be a good mother?"

"Huh? A mother?"

"I want to have a child. Someone who looks like me but resembles my beloved more."

Seongeum cradled her belly with both hands as if she was already harboring life.

"How much distance can I place between me and my child?"

Shirone dared not answer.

"Let's go see the children, Shirone. I want to do that."

Before hearing his response, Seongeum grabbed Shirone’s hand and cast an ether wave.

They arrived at the playground of Yama.

While noble ladies sat chatting in a corner, children were frolicking around.

"Ha ha! Don’t stand there!"

Despite the ongoing war outside Yama, the children playing in the playground were simply cheerful.

Even as Shirone and Seongeum entered the playground, the ladies did not recognize the face of the empire's princess.

"Ha ha, children are truly at ease."

That there were still people who could laugh in Yama was an endless joy for Seongeum.

"These are my children. The children I must protect."

She approached the children, said something to them, and began playing ball with them.

Shirone burst into laughter at the sight of her mercilessly spiking the ball against the clumsy children.

‘Come to think of it…’

Seongeum did not distance herself from the children.

"Hey! Eat this!"

Just then, a child entered the playground and scooped up a handful of dirt, throwing it at the other children.

"Don’t do that! You’ll get your clothes dirty!"

"Ah! Ah!"

Despite his friends’ protests, the child kept throwing dirt until the end, causing Shirone to frown.

"There he is again, Mrs. Jang’s son."

"I thought he wouldn’t come today. But it's awkward to take the kids and leave now."

The ladies murmured and criticized, but no one stepped forward to stop him.

Shirone surmised that the noble family named Jang held a higher rank than them.

"Stop that. The other kids don’t like it."

Seongeum admonished gently, but the dirt-throwing child just looked at her mockingly.

"Who are you to tell me? I play however I want in the playground."

"Ah, ma'am?"

Seongeum, dumbfounded, flicked the boy on the head.

"There’s no limit to what you can say to a woman who has not yet married."

"Ow!"

Holding his forehead, Mrs. Jang's son glared at the children hiding behind Seongeum.

"Boring."

Although the children could not speak up because of their parents' rank, hiding their disdain was too much for them.

Mrs. Jang's son walked to a corner of the playground and rolled a large rock.

Ants scurried about, and looking at them with a mix of disgust and awe, he began to stomp on them relentlessly.

"Die! Die!"

Watching the child, Shirone was seized by an indescribable discomfort, probably because he felt a strange kinship with the ongoing events in the world.

"Stop it!"

Shirone yelled, but the child's eyes were already filled with a mysterious madness.

"Stop it, I said."

Unable to bear it, Shirone pushed the child’s shoulder, and he fell with a thud.

"Hey, shit! Who are you?"

Shirone, with his Western looks, could never be a noble of Jincheon.

"Even a creature has a precious life. No, there is no such thing as a creature in this world."

"You’re joking. You’re not even a noble yourself. Do you know who I am?"

As a radiance burst from Shirone's eyes, the child froze in shock.

"Uh, uh…"

It was the wrath of Yahweh that would have made a demon cringe.

"What’s going on?"

As a fierce-looking woman approached from afar, the ladies' faces turned pale.

‘Mrs. Jang. This is trouble.’

The child began to cry upon seeing his mother.

"Mom! This man… Wah!"

Holding her child, Mrs. Jang glared at Shirone and snapped.

"Who are you? Why are you bothering our child?"

"He was killing ants in the playground."

"What? Ants?"

Mrs. Jang’s face turned incredulous.

"You made our son cry just for that? And kids will be kids. What does he know?"

"That’s the problem."

The truly terrible thing is not knowing.

Shirone sadly watched the child crying in his mother’s arms.

‘When this child goes home…’

His mother will massage his tired legs, and his father will spoon-feed him.

"I don’t want to," Harvitz said to the sky.

"I don’t want to go back."

Who was he talking to?

The 4 Generals looked at each other and murmured.

"He does this sometimes. Who is he talking to in the sky?"

"It must be a delusion. Honestly, neither Harvitz nor we are quite sane, are we?"

Natasha placed a hand over her mouth.

"Maybe he’s feeling the presence of a god…"

Balkan muttered.

"If this world is a lie…"

"Huh?"

Reading the crowd, sometimes he had chilling thoughts.

"That means there’s another world out there, and we are just virtual personalities connected to that outside world."

Balkan looked around at his friends.

"What would Harvitz be in that outside world?"

A child.

Also, a child who has clearly realized that this world is fake has entered here.

‘What kind of life would he live?’

Just a playground.

‘Children don’t play losing games.’ Because it’s not fun.

‘Why doesn’t Harvitz die? What is the reason for his near invincibility, piercing extreme probabilities?’

Was it designed this way?

‘Who exactly? Can one manipulate their own probabilities? But if that were possible…’

The world would already be overflowing with a second Harvitz, a second emperor, a second set of wealthy people.

Smode asked.

“Why stop talking? So what about Harvitz?”

Balkan snapped out of his reverie.

"No, just had that thought."

Assuming an outside world that doesn’t exist was already a zero credibility argument.

‘Even Buddha fights only to eliminate the suffering of this world. He doesn’t know the outside world.’

Balkan looked back at Harvitz.

‘But what exactly are you seeing?’

Maybe Harvitz is…

“I want to play more.”

A deep, dark loneliness swirled in Harvitz’s eyes as he looked up at the sky.

‘I want to have fun.’

"Teach them what is truly important. Otherwise, they’ll live in loneliness forever."

Mrs. Jang was livid.

"Who are you to teach anyone? Do you even know who I am? If I just open my mouth at the office—"

"Just take him away already!"

Shirone shouted angrily, startling Seongeum, who turned to him with a shocked expression.

Take him away, please!

It wasn’t the child he was angry with.

"What a disrespectful brat!"

The already ill-tempered Mrs. Jang suddenly stood up and slapped Shirone across the face.

The loud smack echoed, but it wasn’t Shirone’s face that turned—it was Mrs. Jang’s.

"Ouch! What...?"

In the face of the inexplicable phenomenon, Mrs. Jang couldn’t even muster anger. At that moment, Seongeum approached them.

"Follow his words."

Mrs. Jang turned her head, and the moment she saw Seongeum, her face contorted in shock.

"Princess!"

Though she had only seen Seongeum from hundreds of meters away, the eyes of a noble could never forget the bloodline of the emperor.

"This humble one greets the princess!" Seongeum waved her hand dismissively.

"Take your son and leave. The times are chaotic enough..."

As Mrs. Jang left with her son, the other ladies hurriedly returned to their homes as well.

Seongeum turned to Shirone.

"Let’s leave too. I think we’ve had enough fun for today."

"Now tell me what you’re thinking. What will happen to you when we return to the capital?"

"You already have an idea why I’m not saying anything." She paused. "It’s something that will help you. But if I tell you, you won’t be able to make a choice."

"I can still choose."

"It won’t be a good choice."

"That’s still for me to decide." Seongeum sighed deeply.

"Shirone, the demons are killing people. If there were a way to stop this, you’d do anything, wouldn’t you?"

"Of course."

"What if I told you I’ve thought of something extraordinary to win this war? Could you marry me?"

"Yes."

It was an unexpected question, but there was no such thing as an impossible situation for Shirone’s resolve.

"I’d do anything. Of course, I love Amy, but beyond that, nothing else matters."

Seongeum looked at Shirone with a pained expression before a soft smile crossed her lips, and she took a step back.

"That’s enough."

The space around Shirone was suddenly taken from him, and Seongeum’s voice, growing faint as she moved away, lingered.

Goodbye, Shirone.

"Wait!" But Seongeum had already left.

"Damn it! What the hell is going on?"

Spreading his wings, Shirone soared into the sky, flying at full speed toward Yama.

Compared to Seongeum, his speed was slow.

"Seongeum! Where is Seongeum?"

As he approached the palace, shouting, Anchal stepped forward to block him.

"Stop! No one is allowed to approach!"

"What is this madness? How can you justify killing someone just to win the war?"

"It was the princess’s proposal! Aren’t you also making sacrifices for the sake of the world?"

"But this isn’t sacrifice—it’s suicide! And Seongeum dying won’t end this war!"

Shirone pushed past Anchal, shoving her shoulder.

"The spiritual realm will be closed!"

He froze in his tracks.

"… What?"

"You know the demons are endlessly produced. But if the spiritual realm is closed, they won’t be able to replenish their forces."

"How is that even possible?"

"The princess is going to activate the Immortal Function on equipment developed by Jincheon Space Agency. Using the power of the Ether Wave, she’ll consume the entire realm of Hell."

Closing the spiritual realm meant that no matter what atrocities humanity committed, demons could no longer cross into reality.

We can win.

His heart pounded.

"Wait."

A sudden and horrifying thought crossed his mind.

"What nonsense is this? What happens to Seongeum then? Do you know what will happen to her?"

"Yes. The princess’s mind will…"

Anchal clenched her teeth and lowered her head.

"She will wander through a horrific Hell… forever."
