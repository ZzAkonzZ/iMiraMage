Chapter 494:

Through the Valhalla Action, the laser shot into the sky instantly reversed cause and effect, concentrating the magic of Amgu 6,000 kilometers above the sky.

Amgu borrowed the omnipotence of divine particles, absorbing mass at a terrifying speed, and finally, a spear of light with a mass of 10 tons was born.

The planet's gravity pulled the 10-ton mass strongly, and the light, containing immense kinetic energy, began to plunge straight towards the spire of Aravot.

Time left until the destruction of heaven: 18 minutes and 42 seconds.

'No! At this rate…'

Ikael stared blankly at the sky.

The Valhalla Action had instantly transported Shirone to the entrance of Aravot, and the thickness of the laser shot from there was enough to pierce the sky.

If Shirone's magic was true, heaven would be destroyed.

'How can I stop this?'

While she worried about the safety of heaven, the Archangel of Annihilation, Faiel, cared about nothing.

He was flying rapidly towards Shirone, aiming to eliminate the impurity before him.

Ikael knew Faiel intended to kill Shirone.

But even though Faiel's fundamental concept was annihilation, his reckless actions were puzzling.

'What is at the end of this war?'

Faiel's message hinted at the true intention of Anke Ra, who had banned angelic activities and reinstated Ikael as the archangel.

"Shirone…"

Faiel had already closed more than half the distance to Shirone.

Having intended to kill him from the start, if left unchecked, Shirone would vanish into nothingness.

"Ugh!"

As she thought this, Ikael's halo began to vibrate at a terrifying speed.

She had resolved to stay focused, but in the face of Faiel, the embodiment of absolute death, she finally realized.

There might be something greater than heaven in her lost memories.

Superpower Amplification Array Ataraxia.

Ikael clenched her fists and expanded her halo.

Kiiiiing!

The sight of light pouring into the massive magic circle, hundreds of meters in diameter, was heavenly.

She mobilized all her mental strength to reveal the fundamental concept and poured that immense amplification power into one thought.

'I must regain my memory!'

The fragments of memory erased by Anke Ra.

What remained at the end of the broken thoughts were small signals that couldn't even be called language, but Ataraxia amplified them over 200 million times, filling her mind with perfectly clear images.

"Huh?"

As the only remaining image swept through her body, it began to restore the torn memories.

The thoughts of the archangel advanced as fast as light, and finally, like a new bridge connecting a broken path, all thoughts unified into a single history.

"Ah…"

Clear tears flowed down Ikael's cheeks like light.

Aaaaaaaaah!

The most noble angel's sorrowful cry shook Aravot.

'How could I forget?'

The miserable realization pierced her heart like a dagger, and her mind spilled out like blood.

Shirone.

And Goffin.

Compared to her lifetime as the first and leader of all angels, the time she spent with those two was fleeting.

But for Ikael, it was undoubtedly the brightest and most beautiful moment in her time.

With that memory alone, she could exist.

Losing that memory meant she had no reason to exist.

The result she chose for Shirone had ultimately ruined everything.

'Ra…'

Where is Ra leading the entirety of this world?

"Shironeeeeee!"

Ikael snapped back to reality and spread her wings of light.

Though she had exhausted most of her mental strength to regain her memory, she squeezed out the last bit of strength and flew towards Shirone.

Through her vision, she saw Faiel had already reached Shirone, gathering the energy of annihilation in his hand.

"Are you rushing towards your own death? Well, that is the limit of a mere human, a Nephilim."

Shirone gritted his teeth and glared at Faiel.

The time required to reverse causality with Valhalla Action was a full 10 minutes.

If he had only cast Divine Punishment, the risk wouldn't have been this great, but the time needed to move to the exact coordinates to hit Aravot's spire was much longer than expected.

'No, this is enough.'

He hadn't saved Feope, who had sacrificed an almost eternal lifespan for him, and Ikael, whom he had hoped would be his lifelong mental sanctuary, had almost killed him.

Having completed his mission, he thought nothing else mattered now.

'Amy, I'm sorry. Everyone…'

Thinking of the faces of his precious people left in his hometown, Shirone awaited his impending death.

"Disappear, insignificant being!"

Faiel's face, hidden under his hood, twisted grotesquely as the darkness composed of particles flared like flames.

As the power of annihilation surged to engulf Shirone, Ikael caught up with Faiel just in time.

"Nooooo!"

Boom!

As Ikael activated her power, the concepts of annihilation and amplification clashed, shaking the area.

'Ikael?'

Shirone looked up at Ikael's face, which was two heads taller than his.

Though she wasn't sweating, her complexion was pale, and her eyes were weary.

Even so, Ikael forced a smile for Shirone, but it soon turned into an expression of sorrow, apology, and deep yearning, conveying her poignant emotions.

"I'm sorry."

Ikael's eyes conveyed everything words couldn't express.

"I'm sorry, Shirone…"

Seeing Ikael's tears, Shirone's eyes also filled with tears.

The same substance, perhaps, creating a comforting illusion that made him want to stay in it forever, began to rehydrate Shirone's war-dried emotions.

"Shirone, you…"

Boom!

Before she could finish, Ikael's body was flung to the side.

In Shirone's view, where Ikael had disappeared, Faiel stood with a furious expression.

"Ikael. Fallen, filthy archangel. How far will you desecrate the existence of angels?"

Shirone struggled to move, but there was no way to violate the principle of equivalent exchange.

"It doesn't matter."

Faiel turned back to Shirone.

"Both ugliness and nobility disappear all the same."

As Faiel attacked Shirone again, Ikael, who had fallen to the ground, sprang up and blocked the way once more.

"Ugh!"

Having exhausted her strength to remember Shirone, it was the only thing she could do.

But Faiel no longer cared.

He poured the power of annihilation onto Ikael, intending to overwhelm her with sheer force and concept.

The dark smoke, composed of particles, enveloped Ikael like swarming insects, and her halo gradually lost its light.

"Huuu!"

She tried desperately to endure, but the shock of her angelic presence being erased was evident on Ikael's face.

'Ugh, ugh! Ugh!'

Shirone wept as he watched Ikael.

Unable to move a finger, unable to cry out, he could only watch as the one he loved died, feeling the most miserable emotion a human could experience.

"It's okay, Shirone… It's okay."

Ikael's smile was beautiful.

The most beautiful smile in the universe.

Armin, Siena, and Kuan waited for Etella to arrive in front of the portal to Inglis.

Zulu, who had gone to retrieve the Metagate, could be met outside, but they needed the disk to extract coordinates from the Akashic Records.

"She's unusually late. I hope nothing's happened."

Etella knew time was of the essence, so even if Yameng had broken their promise or failed to keep their contract, she should have arrived at Zebul by now.

"Let's wait a bit longer. She's a good person but not so naive as to lose her sense of judgment. She'll be here soon."

Siena still believed in Etella, but she also had a bad feeling.

A bishop of the Karsis Monastery.

Heaven was full of powerful beings, making it impossible to move around comfortably with ordinary skills, but at least she should be able to avoid fights and complete her mission.

Even when Gaold first formed the team, he prioritized Etella over herself, proving her capabilities.

'What happened?'

As Siena's thoughts grew more anxious, a low, rough growl echoed from the corridor of Inglis.

"Growl!"

All three heads turned simultaneously.

The shadowy figure emerging from the corner was clearly not a normal enemy.

"What is that?"

It was a creature they had never seen before.

But anyone in the world would have felt the same.

Garas, with no clear species characteristics, manifested its appearance through the biological traits of its mother, with its only constant traits being its protein bow and immense reproductive drive.

The black forms varied, but as they were originally Yameng's subordinates, they had some uniformity.

Standing 2 meters tall, with black skin covered in mucus, long knees bent backward, and a spine ending in a spiked tail.

"Kekeke! Reproduction!"

With human traits incorporated, they had no problem speaking.

The difference from the King of Reproduction born in Laisis's headquarters was that they were all strong beings with a telomere level of 5 or higher.

Seeing Garas's eyes, Siena shuddered.

It was a different kind of fear from killing intent, a terrifying fear she instinctively felt as a woman.

Ice Cloud.

A freezing mist rose around Siena.

The rapidly cooled air froze into ice crystals, scattering like glass powder on the floor.

The fact that the combative Siena cast a defensive spell before even engaging in a single exchange shows how shocked she was.

However, the fear was soon controlled by the mage's cold rationality, and her eyes, glaring at the wicked lust demons, burned with fighting spirit.

'How arrogant.'

The person most disgusted by Garas's nature was Kuan.

Already burning with a desire to kill, he gripped the hilt of his sword more tightly than usual.

"I'll handle this…"

As Kuan moved to stand in front of Siena, Armin stepped in first.

"Stand back. There's no need to waste your energy here."

With Armin's Stop, any enemy could be defended against.

However, from Siena's perspective, Armin's overprotection against an enemy that was neither an angel nor a Mara was just annoying.

Moreover, the way Garas looked down on her was infuriating.

"Don't worry. This is nothing…"

"Kiaaaa!"

Dozens of Garas let out a screech.

The deafening sound, mixed with hundreds of emotions, was a unique characteristic that no other creature could imitate.

Exposed to the overwhelming desire that emotionally dominated humans, the three tensed up.

Garas's movements, fueled by the powerful life force of telomere level 5 and extreme desire, were explosive.

Ice Dagger.

Ice daggers formed from the freezing cloud around Siena and scattered in all directions.

The spells cast by a certified 5th-level mage were relentless, and the approaching Garas were repelled.

Ting! Ting ting!

As the Garas hunched their bodies, their carapaces hardened, shattering the ice daggers.

Though they were pushed back by the impact, they didn't suffer fatal injuries.

Even Kuan's sword strikes were deflected by the chitinous resilience.

'Damn it!'

Though not as strong as Ringer's armor, the fact that a mere creature could block his attacks was humiliating for a swordsman.

Absolute Zero.

As Siena's most powerful spell, Absolute Zero, was cast, the Garas began to freeze one by one.

Kuan's sword became effective, shattering the frozen Garas into pieces.

In the midst of the cleared view, a man stood.
