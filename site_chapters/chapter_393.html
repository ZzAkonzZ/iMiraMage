Chapter 393: Hill Capture (2)

Shirone's team had decided on their positions the night before.

With his excellent movement skills, Shirone was the forward, while Nade and Iruki took on the roles of left and right wingers.

The versatile Amy was the linker, and the less offensive Maya was naturally suited for the guard position.

In the Screamer team, the main player of the martial artist killer strategy, Screamer, was the forward.

Luman, with crowd control, and Pony, with water power, were the left and right wingers, the utility Suavi was the linker, and Aider was the guard.

The two teams didn't even exchange glances. Regardless of their usual relationships, they were just competitors now.

Going further, Screamer approached Shirone with his chin raised, knowing the importance of an early psychological battle.

"Hey, are you ready? Ready to get smashed, I mean."

Since the survival test, Shirone's stock had risen even higher, which only excited Screamer more.

With Maya on the team, the balance was the worst. Defeating Shirone in this match would solidify his position in the graduating class.

"You must be nervous, having a hole in your team."

"And you're not embarrassed to say that, being the biggest hole yourself?"

To Shirone, Maya was no longer just a fellow graduate. He couldn't forgive Screamer for insulting her.

"Heh, I'll finish this 5-0. Just wait and see."

Following the instructor's call, the six teams moved to their designated battlefields.

Arin, following Canis, suddenly remembered something and looked back at the Screamer team.

"......"

A giant snake was arrogantly coiled.

It was always a grotesque first sight, but today it was even worse.

"Canis, shouldn't we tell Shirone?"

"Forget it. Shirone is a competitor in the graduating class, just like us. It's not like we've formed an alliance."

Arin didn't ask twice. She too was putting everything on the line for the hill capture and had no room to worry about others.

As they crossed the threshold of the battlefield, Canis added a word.

"He's not someone who will go down easily."

Shirone's team and Screamer's team entered the second battlefield and briefly reviewed their strategies in their respective camps. The thing that Screamer's team found most troublesome was, of course, Ataraxia.

"Listen up. If Shirone uses the magic circle that defeated Dante, we charge no matter what. We just need to cut off Shirone. It seems to take over a minute to cast, so we'll have a chance."

Pony asked.

"What if we fail? If they come out with Guardian Force, we could be countered."

Guardian Force was an extreme defensive formation where all members protected the flag, a tactic Shirone would likely choose if he cast Ataraxia.

"In that case, we retreat. Dig a bunker on the hill and hide. With Luman's crowd control, it will be quick."

Aider spoke.

"So, we start by giving them 1 point?"

"Giving 1 point is fine. He passed out after using it once last time. He can't use such a large-scale magic every round. If we wear down Shirone's mental strength, it's to our advantage."

Even if the power was strong, if it took over a minute to cast, there were many ways to counter it. If possible, they would cut it off, but if not, they would just hide.

"Alright, let's get those 20 points easily. That's why we formed this team. Let's crush them in the first round."

Screamer's team put their hands together and shouted their battle cry.

Meanwhile, Shirone's team was also finishing their review. Iruki gathered everyone and spoke in a low voice.

"We'll use Ataraxia depending on the situation. They'll likely focus their utility on Screamer. We should take the early lead with Sidewinder."

Sidewinder was a strategy where the wingers ran wide along the lines, putting pressure on the enemy team trying to concentrate in the center.

As the announcement for the start of the first round was heard, the ten members scattered to their positions through spatial movement. Of course, once the 2000 system was activated, spatial movement would result in disqualification.

Shirone waited at the 10-meter mark on the central line.

The battlefield map was symmetrical, with various cover set up, so he couldn't see his teammates' faces. The only person he could see was Screamer, 20 meters ahead.

Wearing tight training clothes and warming up, Screamer was quickly moving his feet and then relaxing them. There was no sign of tension in his calm warm-up.

'Iruki will face Luman, and Nade will face Pony. Should I go into Iruki's line?'

Although the numbers were equal at 5 vs. 5, strategies and tactics would create constant 1 vs. 2 or 1 vs. 3 situations. The key was to lead local battles advantageously through choice and concentration.

-10 seconds until the start of the first round.

As the lights came on in the battlefield, engine noises were heard.

The lights on the 2000 bracelets came on, and Screamer lightly stepped and twisted his body diagonally. Then, as he quickly moved his feet back and forth in place, it looked as if he was floating in the air.

"Alright! Let's have some fun!"

4 seconds. 3 seconds. As the countdown dropped, Screamer's legs moved even faster.

From a distance, it looked like he was flailing, but from 20 meters away, Shirone could see the speed of the schema, making his hair stand on end.

Could human legs move that fast? It looked like a tightly compressed steel spring ready to launch.

"Uoooooo! Uoooooo!"

Screamer's body, shaking his limbs, became blurry. At the same time, the 2000 system activated, and everyone entered the Spirit Zone.

-The first round of hill capture begins.

Bang! Bang bang bang! Bang! Bang!

Colorful fireworks exploded above Screamer's head. Muscle enhancement, agility enhancement, nervous system enhancement, concentration enhancement, muscle endurance enhancement, and more—Suavi's 20 utility magic combat packages were hitting him relentlessly.

With the high-speed force, a specialty of utility magicians, Screamer's movements became even faster. As his limbs reached an invisible speed, a tearing screech erupted from his mouth.

"Kyaaaaaa!"

Screamer Martial Arts - Dragon Kick.

With his shoulders seemingly dislocated, Screamer stretched his arms out to the sides and flew forward with his right leg extended like a cannon.

'Spear to spear?'

A tactic to attack the opposing forward and pressure the entire line.

Seeing Screamer flying like a giant ballista, Shirone reflexively cast a spell.

'Radiance!'

As the curtain of light pulsed, Screamer's physical bullet pierced the mass barrier and struck Shirone.

"Ugh!"

Shirone, who blocked his chest with both hands, flew over 5 meters and fell to the ground.

Even though Radiance had dampened the force, the impact pierced his solar plexus, leaving him breathless.

'This is the martial artist killer strategy.'

The strength of a creature cannot be analyzed by the sum of its numbers.

Just as increasing muscle strength also increases speed, the twenty buffs were each cooperating, creating a synergy effect that exceeded the numbers.

Bang bang bang! Bang bang! Bang bang bang bang!

Suavi continuously applied buffs. The average duration of her active buffs was 2 seconds. The key was rotating the 20 types with precise timing.

Amy stood in front of Shirone. In a spear-to-spear situation, the linker was supposed to back up the central line.

"Wadadadadada!"

Screamer's appearance, flying in with teleportation, was terrifying. His posture changed like a pinwheel with each explosion of light.

"Wada! Wada!"

Screamer's rapid punches omitted the frames of the process. A side kick with his full weight sent her flying in her defensive stance.

"Kyaaaaaa!"

Screamer clenched his fists and trembled. His upper body, with his shirt torn by Radiance, bulged with defined muscles.

With the central line broken, Screamer charged towards the hill without hesitation. Maya tried to attack, but before she could cast a spell, her arm was twisted behind her back, and she was held by Screamer.

"L-Let go!"

"Heh, why? Is this your first time feeling muscles like these? Don't be shy."

Screamer grabbed the flag. The Spirit Zone disappeared, but with the schema, there was no problem subduing Maya.

As the pole was pulled out, the 2000 system shut down.

-The first round ends. The second round will start in 5 minutes.

Coming down from the hill, Screamer planted the flag at the bottom and shouted at Shirone's team.

"Hey, isn't this too easy? With such a weak guard, can you even protect the flag properly?"

The martial artist killer strategy was much stronger than Shirone had expected. Screamer's skills were impressive, but Suavi was the real problem.

A skilled user who could rapidly switch between 20 types of buffs. Without cutting her off, it would be difficult to gain the upper hand.

Shirone looked at Maya. She was the key figure who could counter the martial artist killer strategy.

Maya nodded vigorously. Although she felt burdened by the attention, she was willing to do anything for Shirone.

'I'll do my best, Shirone!'

As the second round began, Screamer once again pushed forward with spear-to-spear.

As Shirone retreated into his own territory, Screamer's confidence soared. Even the great Shirone seemed afraid of him.

"Hahaha! No guts at all...!"

Aaaah!

Screamer's steps faltered. When Maya's sound magic hit, it felt like an earthquake in his head.

"Ugh!"

Suavi frowned.

Maya's "Requiem of Death" was a spell that broke the enemy's concentration. However, with her skill level only at stage 2, she shouldn't have been able to produce such power.

'Her voice is different.'

Unlike before, when she used classical vocal techniques, Maya was now singing the Requiem of Death as if it were a song. The range was short, but the amplification was incredible.

Without the buffs, Screamer's power significantly weakened. Shirone's team, which had pulled back the line early on, defended solidly.

As the battle turned chaotic, Screamer retreated to his own territory.

Maya's amplification was impressive, but the range wasn't wide, so Screamer planned to cut off Shirone from their own territory.

"Suavi! Buffs!"

Crossing the central line, Suavi's buffs hit again.

With enhanced leg strength, Screamer shot forward at incredible speed.

Shirone's back, advancing 60 meters into enemy territory, quickly came into view.

"Kyaaa!"

Screamer charged with a Dragon Kick.

Amy, twisting her body to dodge Screamer, shouted.

"Shirone! Behind you!"

"It's too late!"

At that moment, Shirone's body glowed pale and exploded in eight directions.

Scatter Movement!

"Ugh!"

As a beam of light struck his abdomen, an anti-magic force of immense strength hit. Even with the schema's muscle enhancement build, the impact was overwhelming.

Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang!

Every time the Scatter Movement burst forward, photon cannons spread across the battlefield.

It wasn't just a movement technique. It was a powerful crowd control skill that tore through Screamer's team's formation.

"Ugh!"

Aider barely dodged the barrage of photon cannons. With Amy backing him up and blocking the path to the flag, Shirone easily grabbed and pulled out the pole.

-The second round ends. The third round will start in 5 minutes.

Although the flag should have been placed back in its original position at the end of the round, Shirone carried it down the hill. He then planted it in front of Screamer's feet, scoffed, and teleported back to his own territory.

"That bastard...!"

The Scatter Movement, combining movement, attack, and crowd control techniques, was a magic only Shirone could perform.

'What? Divine Particle?'

Is there only a god in the world of magic and no devil? What are they doing not catching a guy like that?

Pony approached, looking at Shirone's team's hill.

"Calm down. What happened? Maya's sound magic was too strong."

That was the reason for their loss in the second round. But Screamer couldn't understand how the magic had amplified in such a short time.

Seeing Maya laughing among her teammates, he recalled the strategy and tactics session, feeling even more frustrated.

'Damn it! How did they do it? That talentless girl...'

Shirone's team was in high spirits. The counter to the martial artist killer strategy had worked perfectly.

"Maya! You were amazing! This round is yours."

"I just did what I was told..."

Iruki's advice to exclude classical vocal techniques had hit the mark. Maya, with her innate talent for singing, had abandoned technical skills and focused on extreme amplification.

Of course, this approach had its limits.

The reason sound magicians learn classical techniques is that they are the most specialized in handling the voice technically. Ultimately, fighting with only amplification works at the student level.

But Maya willingly accepted Iruki's suggestion.

She wanted to bring victory to those who fought Screamer for her.

Shirone's team was not a one-man team.
