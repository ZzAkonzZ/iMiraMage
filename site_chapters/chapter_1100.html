Chapter 1100: Beginning of Destruction (1)

Those who knew Havitz were horrified.

'The ultimate evil of the era.'

The fact that Satan had allied with Uorin meant that the demons were also under Kashan's control.

'Damn it.'

Tension filled the air.

'Havitz's ability is Vanishing. Despite extensive research and analysis…'

The conclusion was that it couldn't be stopped even if known.

'It's like releasing a dog into a rabbit pen.'

Of course, no one at the Holy War considered themselves a rabbit.

Lupist asked Shirone,

"Did you know?"

"I had some suspicion. But I didn't expect him to reveal himself so openly."

Shirone looked at Uorin.

'What Havitz ultimately wants is the destruction of the world. Does she really desire that?'

As if hearing his thoughts, she turned her head towards Shirone.

'Finally, he's looking at me.' Her heart swelled with emotion, but she managed to hide it even without High Gear.

"What is the meaning of this?"

King Udulai of Corona scolded.

"Queen of Kashan, at a time when we should be uniting the world and promoting peace among nations, you summon Satan? Do you truly wish for the destruction of humanity?" It was a question on everyone's mind.

"Of course not."

Uorin smiled.

"What we are doing now is a world war where all races compete for supremacy. Strategic alliances are fundamental, aren't they?"

"Do you think such sophistry will work? This is a gathering of the top leaders of each country. Who would attend where Satan is present?"

"That's why I'm introducing him like this."

As Uorin finished speaking, Havitz, who had smoked his drug Asker down to the stub, dismounted.

"Ugh!"

Just his movement caused the guards of Tormia and Corona to stir.

'Vanishing is dangerous.'

It's not just disappearing.

There is no way to stop what happens outside everyone's perception.

'Havitz is here. Havitz is here.' Knowing it was futile, the guards endlessly imprinted Havitz's presence in their minds.

"Well, we meet again, Queen of Kashan," Havitz said.

"I need to do some history searching. But this woman made an interesting proposal."

The guards' focus reached its peak.

'Havitz is here. Havitz is here.'

"After hearing it, I thought I might have something to say at the Holy War. Anyway, so…"

As Havitz turned around,

'Havitz is here. Havitz… huh?' Everyone in the procession, including the guards, blinked in confusion.

'Havitz? What's that?'

While everyone forgot about Satan, only a few, including Shirone, wore serious expressions.

"I have a proposal."

The smell of Asker wafted, and Havitz, mounted on Uorin's words, continued.

'Damn. We missed it!'

The few who realized it late shuddered.

"During the Holy War meetings, I won't use Vanishing. Will you let me join then?"

Pony spoke,

"Do you think we would accept that proposal? Even if we did, how would we verify that you are keeping your promise?"

Uorin said,

"How about I stake my life on it?"

As soon as she spoke, the leaders of Tormia and Corona stirred.

Albino of Yongrae ordered,

"Find out if she has a daughter."

"Yes."

If Mitochondrial Eve had secretly given birth to a daughter, the Queen's life would be meaningless.

While the think tank busily moved, Uorin gave a shy smile.

"Don't worry. I'm a virgin."

"Ahem. Ahem."

The elderly officials cleared their throats.

"In other words, there is no other Mitochondrial Eve. If I die, the Queen dies too. If you want verification, we can follow official procedures for an investigation."

Gando's expression darkened, and Kido, standing guard, bared his sharp teeth.

'Damn! Do we have to go this far?' Even if it was an unavoidable choice, he didn't like allying with Havitz.

'He's the one who made you kneel. You are the Queen, the noblest among humans…'

At least in Kido's eyes.

'Is that guy really that good?' Feeling the gaze, Shirone turned his head and saw the goblin standing at the center of the guards.

'Kido.'

A precious friend who had endured hardships together.

'You're doing well.'

When Shirone smiled in greeting, Kido also slung his spear over his shoulder and chuckled.

'You've become quite gallant.'

He had heard countless stories about Yahweh.

'But I've also made it.'

When Kido pointed to his captain's badge and shrugged, Shirone grinned broadly.

'Haha.'

And then… their gazes crossed again.

'I hope things go well.'

Though they walked different paths according to their beliefs, they were still friends.

He wanted to believe that.

"Anyway, the decision will be made after the twelve nations gather."

Uorin continued,

"For now, this is my proposal. I will take responsibility for and control Havitz's actions. If he breaks this rule, I swear to give my life."

Udulai spoke,

"I'm not belittling Kashan, but claiming your life is worth more than the lives of the leaders of eleven other nations seems a bit self-important, doesn't it?"

"Then what should we do? Will the eleven nations boycott the Holy War without Kashan?" The Holy War is the ultimate business.

"I don't think anyone wants to scatter and fight to the death again. In that sense, I believe there is some justification for Havitz, the leader of the demons, to attend the Holy War. If you can't accept it…"

Uorin coldly declared,

"I will release Havitz. Freely."

"…Am I a beast?"

As Havitz muttered, Shirone's eyes calmed.

'This is the biggest problem.'

He was never someone who could be controlled.

'Even without Kashan, Havitz can come to the Holy War. The leaders of each country must have run simulations and prepared countermeasures.'

Just as Shirone had trained Wizard.

The reason for the discomfort isn't Havitz, but rather that Kashan has taken control of the demons. It's the displeasure of seeing someone else break a taboo they couldn't due to pride.

The balance of power is shifting towards Kashan.

"Regarding that matter…" Udulai spoke heavily.

"I think it would be best to gather the opinions of the eleven nations. We will officially raise an objection at the Holy War."

As the traditional powerhouse Corona took a step back, Uorin gracefully bowed her head.

"Thank you for your consideration. Then…"

The Kashan procession turned their horses, and as if on cue, Tormia and Corona stepped back.

"Empire of Kashan! Enter the inner castle!"

Hearing the general's shout, Albino laughed heartily.

"Haha! We took a hit right from the start. The Queen is not to be underestimated."

On the other hand, Rai's expression was grim.

"This is no laughing matter. The ability Havitz used earlier is on a different level from what we've heard."

The mere fact that he felt a sense of incongruity showed how keen Rai's senses were.

"The risk of the king's assassination is a realistic possibility. I'll set the alert level to the highest."

"Stay calm. The Royal Guard is the king's strength and face. Overreacting isn't dignified."

"It's better than being beheaded with our eyes open. Other countries won't be different from us."

"Hmm."

Albino twisted his beard.

"Perhaps that's Tormia's strength."

"Excuse me?"

"Just saying."

Ignoring Rai's question, Albino's expression turned cold the next moment.

'Pony can die.'

Unlike other countries, Tormia didn't rely heavily on their king.

'She's smart but politically unreliable. Even if Pony is killed by Havitz, Tormia won't be easily shaken. On the other hand…'

Other countries were different.

Especially the Kashan Empire, where Uorin was the entirety of power.

'It's a fortress that's hard to breach, but once breached, it collapses uncontrollably. That's Kashan.'

Albino's lips twisted.

'A virgin.'

Who would have thought the term "biological purity" would come from Mitochondrial Eve's mouth?

'Haha, the Queen of Kashan must be quite desperate.'

His mismatched eyes, reminiscent of Iruki, glared sharply at Uorin.

'So, where should I start?'

With Kashan's procession gone, Tormia and Corona faced off from the sides.

Shirone spoke.

"Let's yield."

Lupist agreed with him.

'Corona is deeply connected to Shirone. There's no benefit in antagonizing them now.'

Knowing her role, Pony spoke.

"The Ivory Tower fought against the crisis of humanity. I believe the participation of the Kingdom of Corona is a noble decision. Tormia respects that and will yield the way."

Udulai responded.

"As expected, you are a wise king. Refusing here would only half acknowledge Queen Pony's consideration, so I'll go first, even if it means swallowing my pride."

Even though it was a gesture of returning the favor, it felt like excessive praise.

They were conscious of Shirone.

'There's no benefit in antagonizing the Five Stars. The factional fights in the Ivory Tower are also intense.'

Many stars supported Xing's New World proposal, but Yahweh's influence was still strong.

The stars participating in the Holy War couldn't hide their awkwardness, proving that fact.

On the way to the inner castle gate, Lampa's satellite, Chongyi, bowed to Shirone.

"Ah, Five Star. Hello…"

Lampa stopped him.

"Don't act rashly. We are participating as Corona's think tank. Avoid personal interactions."

Rashly?

"How can I? I made eye contact with the Five Star." Lampa knew as well.

'The stars of the Ivory Tower are supranational beings. If Chongyi respects someone, it's not something to stop.'

But this time was an exception.

'We declared neutrality and left the Ivory Tower. Showing special feelings for Yahweh here could turn the Ivory Tower into a political arena.'

Lampa apologized in his heart.

'I'm sorry, Five Star. Please scold this old man later.'

Of course, Shirone understood their situation and remained calm even when Chongyi avoided his gaze.

'Lampa, Chongyi, Tzuoi, Musso, the Ten Mages. All belong to the System Surveillance Department.'

No stars or satellites from other departments were visible.

'The head of the System Surveillance Department is Lampa, a three-star resident. It must have been hard to endure the power struggle among the Five Stars. Choosing neutrality despite that means…'

It was a subtle nuance.

'Thank you.'

Even the neutrality of the System Surveillance Department's departure would have hit Sing.

'Lampa's bold decision balanced things. But it also means the internal power struggle in the Ivory Tower is intense. I should go…'

Shirone's thoughts were interrupted when he saw a familiar face in Corona's procession.

'Armin.'

Armin of the Radiant Eyes was walking with Keira.

'Yes. The Ivory Tower isn't just about the stars. The residents' abilities are also top-notch.'

32% of the world's top 100 dangerous individuals were from the Ivory Tower.

'Guido's group.'

Experts known even by name among card game players continued endlessly.

'Intelligence.'

The land of intelligence.

"Kingdom of Corona! Enter the inner castle!"
