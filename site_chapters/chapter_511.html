Chapter 511: Questions Left Behind (1)

"Ugh! How dare such a lowly creature… dare to… to me!"

The angels who had been hiding in various places in the temple under Anke Ra's command were helpless against the Garas.

The Garas, who had inherited the traits of the nocturnal subordinates through the Fuzix Machine and were now mercilessly breeding with the current angels, had become completely different creatures.

They had undergone such tremendous evolution that it could be called a great explosion.

Leaving offspring in the bodies of the highest beings in heaven, the angels, was the optimal choice for the preservation of the species.

The already strong desire for species preservation soared endlessly, and as a result, the reproductive abilities for all species except angels were lost.

The Garas, who craved only angels, transformed into giant tadpole-like forms and increased their numbers through self-cell division.

Just by touching their bodies, the angels were fertilized with the Garas' seeds, and even when removed by force, they somehow transmitted their information through self-destruction.

"Rather than suffer this disgrace…!"

The angels chose to self-destruct, and as a result, their numbers gradually decreased.

"Kiaaaak!"

A group of Garas filling the corridor was burned to ashes by the light emitted from the Archangel of Light, Rael.

"Crack! Crararack!"

The Archangel of Disintegration, Satiel, who was in charge of the rear, disintegrated the last remaining Garas' body below the molecular level.

Although they knew that Garas couldn't touch their bodies, the sight of them flying towards them was still terrifying.

"Disgusting creatures. How dare they desecrate angels?"

"They're not called extraterrestrial isolation species for nothing. Anyway, the situation is serious. We've already lost half of the angelic forces."

Anke Ra is waiting for Satan to be destroyed.

Realizing this, the two archangels' task was to ensure the survival of as many angels as possible.

While planning for the future was important, if Satan's power grew any stronger, they wouldn't even be able to reset and it would be the end.

"Pathetic."

What angered them the most was that most angels were choosing to self-destruct.

'We were too arrogant.'

Recalling the human battles that found hope in despair, Satiel reconsidered the meaning of the sacredness symbolized by angels.

Boom!

At that moment, a loud noise came from the gate of Aravot, which was connected to the temple.

Satiel's face twisted as he looked down.

The rebels were entering Aravot.

"Advance! Advance! Victory is within reach!"

"Destroy Ra!"

Just an hour ago, the rebels were on the verge of defeat, but the tide of war had turned sharply.

As the angels self-destructed, their followers, the Maras, disappeared.

Grasping that small spark of hope, the rebels broke through the walls of Aravot.

'Hope is in sight!'

In everyone's eyes, there was a determination to win the war and avenge their fallen comrades.

But the rebels did not know.

Even at this moment, God's punishment was falling towards the center of heaven.

Time left until the destruction of heaven: 3 minutes and 21 seconds.

Satan wandered around, destroying all the buildings in the temple.

He was so enormous that he didn't need to target anyone specifically.

Wherever he went was ruin and the end.

"This is fun."

The pitiful world seen from above, the weak and pathetic beings that crumbled at a mere touch.

"Hehehe. Hahaha!"

Satan's laughter was more intense than thunder, and the atmosphere trembled, causing the mountains and trees to shake.

"How big do you plan to get?"

Ikael's triangular Mara, Ashur, was watching Satan's rampage with the giant corps commander, Girshin.

"With sheer strength, he's on par with Ymir."

Moreover, being a being outside the law, there was nothing they could do until Ra's will was activated.

Even Girshin didn't boast in the face of Satan's power.

"He's certainly strong. I wouldn't be able to match him. But…"

Girshin turned to Ashur with wide eyes.

"Don't you dare compare his power to that of the Lord."

Ashur's signal was powerful enough to disrupt the balance of the triangular Mara, but for Girshin, the strongest was always Ymir.

True strength is one that cannot be relativized.

The only one who had reached that level in heaven was Ymir.

"Step aside. I'll handle this."

Etella blocked Miro's path as she approached Satan.

Miro's face, which she had checked like a thief, was chillingly cold, unlike before.

"What is it? Do you have something to say?"

"Ah, I…"

Etella quickly regained her composure but couldn't continue speaking.

Even with the Thousand-Hand Avalokitesvara Thunder Strike, she couldn't even make a dent in Satan's body.

The will to crush any evil was shattered in front of Satan.

Miro looked down at Etella's damaged fists.

A person's hands wouldn't get to that state without swinging with extreme determination.

"I don't blame you. You can't fight with those fists anyway, can you?"

Etella bit her lip and swallowed her tears.

'What kind of follower of good am I? What kind of monk fighting for the world?'

The sense of helplessness was as overwhelming as her strong faith.

Miro passed through the path Etella had opened.

She clasped her hands together, pushed them out, and her shoulders and elbows cracked.

As she twisted her neck from side to side and approached, Satan turned his head.

'What is this?'

Although the distance was too great to see with his eyes, Satan could clearly feel Miro approaching.

Her aura was fundamentally different from Etella's. Satan's unrestrained malice flared up like fire.

"Hehehe! What is this?"

Boom!

When Satan stomped his foot, a radius of 100 meters was scorched.

Sain, who had taken Etella to safety, quickly cleared the dust cloud with his Iron Wheel Eye.

"Where's Miro?"

When Miro was nowhere to be seen, Sain's face turned pale as he expanded his Spirit Zone.

"She's crazy."

Through his shared senses, Sain realized Miro's position was under Satan's foot.

"Huh?"

Satan lifted his foot with a puzzled expression, revealing Miro, who was forming a hand seal with her right hand.

The sole of Satan's foot, which had stepped on Miro, was dented, but it was nothing compared to his enormous body.

"How amusing, human. How dare you oppose me, the incarnation of evil."

"Amusing? This?"

Miro, who had been glaring coldly at Satan, suddenly relaxed her expression and sneered.

"No, the truly amusing thing is your ugly face."

Grrrr.

A rough sound came from Satan's throat as he bared his teeth.

"Hahahaha!"

But the next moment, his anger turned into mad laughter, causing all the humans on the ground to cover their ears.

"Insignificant human!"

At some point, Satan abruptly stopped laughing and glared at Miro with eyes burning with flames.

It was the first time he had shown murderous intent towards a single target while rampaging through the temple.

"We have to stop him!"

Sain grabbed Etella's shoulder as she tried to leap forward.

"Wait. Miro must have a plan."

"I know how strong Miro is. But scale magic won't work on Satan."

Satan was a being outside the law.

The only thing that could destroy that absolute chaos was a will strong enough to change the law.

"You still don't know Miro well."

Sain looked back at Miro.

"She created the dimensional wall to protect humanity. She was the only one who could do that. But Miro wasn't originally that kind of person. While Miro's space-time might have been a great blessing for us, it was more like a shackle for her."

Etella found it hard to believe.

Miro's space-time was the pinnacle that scale magicians could reach.

If even that level was just a shackle, could such a person be called human?

"Then what kind of person is Miro?"

"Honestly, I don't know. I don't know how to define that wild woman."

Etella's face turned incredulous.

"But one thing is certain."

Sain looked at Miro, who was standing in a three-curve stance, emphasizing the curves of her body.

"The Miro I know is someone who can do everything humans dream of."

Even in front of Satan's overwhelming body, Miro was not intimidated but wore a relaxed smile.

"Hehe, I'm quite determined."

"Ooooooh!"

Satan roared and raised his fist above his head towards her.

This time would be different.

If he crushed her with all his might, even the great Miro would be nothing more than a speck.

With that confidence, he was about to slam his fist down when his movement halted, and his eyes widened.

A mysterious aura was emanating from Miro's body.

The aura, shimmering like a mirage, gradually condensed in the air and finally began to materialize.

The face of the avatar, glowing with a serene blue light, looked peaceful, with a gentle smile on its lips.

Transcendence of the Mind - Prajna.

Etella muttered with a dazed expression.

"Unbelievable."

The avatar's afterimage was growing larger and larger.

Growing, growing, endlessly growing, it was beyond gigantification and could be called expansion.

The avatar rose to Satan's height.

As Satan watched in silence, his head slowly began to tilt towards the sky.

Finally, Miro's avatar surpassed the limits of visibility and extended into outer space.

The final size reached approximately 128 times the size of a planet.

The entire sky was filled with Miro's avatar, visible from anywhere in the hemisphere.

"Ah, ahhh…"

Arius, kneeling, shed tears of awe and raised his hands towards Miro.

This is Miro.

In her presence, any human becomes a mere insignificant being.

The avatar of Avalokitesvara overlooking the entire world.

Even Arius, one of the Seven Demonic Sages, who was known as a villain, willingly lowered himself to the status of a dog.

'This ends now.'

Miro, releasing her three-curve stance, drew a large circle with her hands, and the gigantic avatar mimicked the same motion.

As the avatar's arms, stretching from one end of the sky to the other, moved, the world seemed to ripple.

"Thousand-Hand Avalokitesvara…"

Etella's voice trembled.

The avatar's two arms, descending from above, left afterimages as they split at the smallest units of time.

The sight of countless afterimages trailing like tails and rushing towards Satan was the greatest realization of her life.

'The power of the avatar…'

Miro, focusing all her willpower, clasped her hands together and opened her eyes wide.

'Compress it into a single point!'

Prajnaparamita - Ultimate Strike.

There was no sound, no smell, no form.

The only thing Satan could perceive was that 180 million palms were flying towards him from both sides.

Realizing the severe danger, Satan's eyes darted around rapidly.

He didn't know what the outcome would be, but he didn't want to be hit by that technique, even if it meant selling his soul.

'I have to avoid it!'

But where?

The moment he realized it, the strength drained from Satan's body.

"Damn it…"

Rumble!

"Graaaah!"

As the avatar's palms collided with the planet in between, 180 million shockwaves surged simultaneously.

Feeling the shock that crushed his entire body, Satan trembled madly.

The force of the gigantic avatar compressing into a single point and striking was a sensation that could only be experienced at the end of the world.

Each cell in Satan's body swelled like a balloon, and his red skin became bumpy like embossing.

His body, distorted to the point of losing its original form, exploded with a bang and began to shrink rapidly.

"Kieeeek!"

An evil scream spread through the sky as if tearing it apart.
