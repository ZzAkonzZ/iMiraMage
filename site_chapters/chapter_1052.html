Chapter 1052: Fine Adjustments (3)

The moment the demons saw Oris protecting Shirone, their shock was beyond imagination.

"How could you…"

Despite their hatred for Yahweh, the seven generals were the most respected demons in the city.

Oris's eyes were filled with fear, as if the choice was unbearable even for her.

'I hate Yahweh too. I really hate him.'

But Yupi…

Even if he deceived her, she loved the lie that allowed her to laugh with Yahweh.

"Go."

Oris pushed Shirone away.

"Go anywhere. It's better to die fighting than to die a dog's death here."

The demons screamed.

"What are you doing, Oris! Do you know what you're doing right now?"

She had no room to argue against the common sense, system, and righteousness that supported this world…

"I believe you."

There are also inexplicable logics.

"You must have your reasons too."

Even if you try your best, sometimes everything just falls apart.

"So please… just go." Damn world.

There probably isn't a way to make everyone happy in the human world.

"Oris."

Isn't it thrilling just to imagine?

That imagination was so beautiful and exhilarating that Shirone couldn't give up.

"I'm sorry."

A smile briefly appeared on Oris's surprised face.

And a moment later, her back lit up and hellfire began to burn from her back.

"Ah!"

The fire spread instantly, and her body was destroyed without a chance to do anything.

Lete's voice was heard.

"Shameful, my child."

Without even looking at her, Shirone reached out to where Oris's memory remained.

Nothing was caught.

As Shirone slowly stood up, golden smoke began to rise fiercely from his body.

"Lete, do we really have to go this far?"

"What are you talking about? Oris was ultimately killed by you. Doing nothing is the way to help us."

Lete was an administrator. Therefore, unlike other demons, she did not harbor special hatred for Yahweh.

That made him even angrier.

"Lete."

As Shirone gritted his teeth, the demons around his blocked her path.

"Yahweh, how far will you corrupt us? How far will you look down on us?"

"I don't want to fight with you." That was what Oris left for Shirone, even as she faced annihilation.

"Hmph! Nonsense. You always say that. And then you'll massacre us again."

"Move. I want to talk to Lete." Shirone's voice was earnest, but the demons felt more ignored.

After exchanging glances for a moment, they rushed in without anyone leading.

"Kill Yahweh!"

To Shirone, who was surrounded by the Miracle Stream, all movements were clear.

'Is it this again?'

His heart didn't move easily.

'How long…

Killing, killing, and killing again, until there's no one left to oppose.

'Is that victory?'

Is that really the fulfillment of belief?

In Shirone's clear eyes, the faces of the demons trembling with anger appeared.

"Die…"

The moment the leading demon raised a large axe over 2 meters and swung his arm.

Boom!

A fire pit fell from the sky and exploded, burning the surrounding demons to ashes.

"Ahhh!"

As the demons screamed with their limbs torn off by the shockwave, Lete frowned.

"…Is it you?"

A woman, burning like a heated stone, was holding a red club in both hands.

Shirone tilted his head.

"Son Yu-jung? Why are you here?"

Burning almost to the point of white light, she revealed her fangs and shouted.

"You called me! What are you doing now? You chant incantations to me but grovel to the demons?"

As Shirone frowned slightly, Son Yu-jung, who came to her senses, flinched her shoulders.

"Well, it's just… I guess my true feelings came out, yes."

"Ah! Ah! Son Yu-jung! It's so hot, I'm going to die!"

Richela's face groaned from her waist, but the heat couldn't be reduced.

'That's real.'

Lete was a strong opponent, enough for Son Yu-jung to unconsciously grip her weapon tightly.

'The ghost with the hair (Itaka) next to her is quite something, but this woman is on a different level.'

She wanted to fight.

"Here I go!"

As Son Yu-jung bared her fangs and increased her firepower, Shirone spoke.

"Stop."

Just the feeling of the incantation gently tightening her neck made Son Yu-jung gasp and lift her head.

"Ugh!"

As the fire spirit weakened and the heat subsided, Richela finally breathed out.

"I have something to talk about with Lete."

"…Yes."

Without any will to resist, Son Yu-jung stepped back, and Shirone approached Lete.

"Lete."

His voice was as earnest as soothing a child, but Lete's eyes didn't waver at all.

"Stop calling me and say what you want to say. I'll listen to at least one word."

"Just one question."

As strength entered Shirone's eyes, the Miracle Stream surrounding his body soared as if ascending.

Lightning struck.

"Who am I?"

As the pillar of light gradually expanded its radius,

the demons began to retreat.

"Ahhh!"

The temperament that overwhelmed the entire city was completely different from the Yahweh they imagined.

Itaka raised her head vertically and looked up at the light piercing through the cloud of fire.

'Was it this much?'

Although the other seven generals might have felt the same, she gave up the thought of fighting Yahweh at this moment.

"Answer."

All the demons' gazes focused on one place, and Lete just kept her mouth shut.

Depending on the answer, the city of Laviette would be purified in an instant.

"In the end, it's a threat…"

"Lete."

Shirone interrupted.

"I asked who I am." As the ascending flow of light became rougher, the demons felt burning pain.

"Ahhh!"

Only Lete could endure it, and finally, she answered.

"Yahweh."

Knowing it wasn't the exact answer, she added with a twisted nose.

"…You are."

"Lete?"

The residents, as well as the seven generals, widened their eyes at the sight of her using an honorific for Yahweh.

But it was natural.

"The Buddha is no longer here. The balance of the world is broken, and hell is now in my hands. No matter how much you try to corrupt me, there's nothing left to shake."

Lete understood exactly.

"The highest administrator is gone, so I must serve you. What do you want from me?"

"Cleanse the demon realm."

"Impossible. Do you want me to kill all my children?"

"At least eliminate the demon realms of the legion commander. Then I won't stir up the underworld."

Lete's mission was to maintain the system of hell, so it wasn't a bad proposal.

'The problem is that it's not a good proposal either.'

Dismantling the demon realm was a complicated task, and it also weakened Satan's power.

But accepting it here would shake Shirone's anger and hell.

At that moment, a voice like a pig being slaughtered was heard.

"Oink oink. Boss?"

Just hearing the voice made Lete turn her head fiercely.

Monoras, looking like a beggar, was standing with Deputy Director Void.

"You bastard…!"

As soon as Void saw the sparks in Lete's eyes, he quickly bowed his head.

"I'm sorry! I told him it was urgent business, but he insisted on going to the gambling city…"

That alone painted the whole picture in her mind.

"Boss!"

Monoras approached with an aggrieved expression.

"I was tricked! Damn it! Those guys are complete pros! How could they get that hand with a 2% chance when I bet all my assets! Oink oink!"

As Lete stared at Monoras's shameless appearance, Void closed his eyes tightly.

"I'm sorry! I should have ended it there, but the chief of staff even bet the shares of the Fire Management Company…"

Monoras, suddenly remembering, continued.

"Right, my shares! I lost all my shares collected through all sorts of corruption! Boss, please go and get them back."

"Monoras."

Lete approached slowly.

"Yes. Oink oink."

As Lete, who was staring at Monoras with a pale face, reached out her hands, her palms touched his chest, and she murmured with a still dazed expression.

"I'll get them all back."

"What? Really? Oink oink."

"Of course, I'll get them all back. It's a good thing you came quickly. I'll let you have fun, so just do me one favor."

"…I don't want to work."

"I'll even let you into the boss's office. You can come in anytime."

"Oh?"

Monoras's eyes sparkled for the first time, but he soon made a disinterested face.

"If it's a really troublesome job, I won't do it."

"Do you see Yahweh over there?"

Monoras, rotating from his waist, stared at Shirone, who was enveloped in light.

Lete said.

"Kill him."

Monoras's eyes narrowed, and the corners of his mouth lifted.

"Oh, thank goodness."

Before the first tremor even ended, Monoras rushed at Shirone.

Boom boom boom boom boom!

The whole city shook, and Shirone, feeling the eeriness, retreated at an incredible speed.

"Because it's easy."

At that moment, Son Yu-jung, engulfed in flames, blocked the way and twisted her face.

"Kiieee!"

It wasn't for anyone.

Without time for rational thought, the battle monkey's instincts were activated.

'How strong is he?'

Feeling overwhelmed, Son Yu-jung's weapon bloomed like a flower and attacked.

"Kuul!"

As Monoras swung his arm, a crack appeared in the stone monkey's avatar.

"What…?"

Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang!

When she briefly regained consciousness, she was bouncing and flying several kilometers like a ball.

'Ymir?'

If he had to compare, it was like that.

Monoras, who had deflected Son Yu-jung, caught up with Shirone's speed and threw a punch.

"Kuul!"

The wind pressure was visible, and the shape of the giant fist distorted the space as it pushed forward.

'Hand of God!'

As the fist of light gathered in the sky met the wind pressure head-on, a spherical shockwave occurred.

Aaaaaaaah!

As half of the city was blown away, the screams of the demons faintly echoed.

"Ugh!"

Experiencing a single clash, Shirone had the same thought as Son Yu-jung.

'Ymir.'

But as Yahweh, he delved a bit deeper.

'Ymir's Demon.'

Just like in reality, there are mysteries in the underworld that have not been revealed.

'One of the biggest topics. Many demons searched for Ymir's Demon but never found it.'

Lete had monopolized it.

"To the boss's office… to the boss's office…" Monoras mumbled, flaring his nostrils, then shouted as if his jaw would drop.

"I'm going in!"

Shirone felt puzzled by the seemingly low and impulsive personality.

'Is this Ymir's emotion?' Giants cannot reproduce.

'No way?'

The moment Shirone, who suddenly realized, widened his eyes, the second strike was delivered.

Boom!

A force more than ten times stronger than the first shockwave swept across the vast plains of hell.

Monoras's eyelid lifted.

"Oh?"

The reason for his surprise was that he didn't know there could be something his fist couldn't break.

"Shirone, are you okay?" Rian, who had blocked Monoras's fist with his sword, was already standing in front of him.
