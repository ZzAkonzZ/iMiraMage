Chapter 593:

“Gasp!”

Lucan’s eyes widened at the unexpected situation.

Meanwhile, his comrades’ faces calmed down again.

‘Yolga…’

Yolga was a good person.

“If you want…”

Yolga guided Lucan’s hand to her chest, but he couldn’t bring himself to touch her.

“W-what are you doing? Aren’t you sorry to your husband?”

Lucan hastily pulled his lips away and stared blankly at Yolga’s face.

Seeing her cry with a face full of the world’s sorrow made his mind go blank.

“I’m really sorry.”

Yolga was such a good person.

‘Invincible kindness.’

Edgar looked at Yolga’s back with awe.

If Miro had an inhuman determination to achieve her goals, Yolga had an abnormal kindness that was almost eerie.

‘That’s why we dedicated our lives to Yolga.’

That’s why they willingly entered the upper echelons of Istas with her.

Lucan, who had been staring blankly at Yolga, began to cry.

“Sorry…”

A voice on the brink of death leaked out.

“I’m sorry. I’m really sorry.”

Yolga didn’t calculate her emotions.

“It’s okay. It’s all my fault. Blame me.”

That’s why she could embrace all ugliness.

‘Absolute sincerity. Can a person without a hint of pretense truly exist?’

That was exactly who Yolga was.

Lucan kept apologizing as he took his last breath.

It was a seemingly beautiful scene, but his comrades knew the taste of his tears.

It wasn’t tears of repentance or regret for spitting at Yolga.

‘If evil instills fear by surpassing human courage, extreme goodness also instills fear.’

Lucan was terrified by the pure goodness Yolga exuded.

“Yolga, here.”

Her friend and comrade, Mustang, handed her a perfumed handkerchief.

“Thank you.”

Wiping the spit from her face, Yolga pulled her hood halfway down and stood up.

“Let’s go. We need to find Miro.”

Swordsman Nine spoke.

“We failed to persuade Miro earlier. If we meet again, we’ll have to fight.”

“…We have no choice.”

Determination was etched on Yolga’s face.

“But I’ll persuade her to the end. Miro is my sister.”

“…”

The reason her comrades remained silent was that Miro, like Yolga, had a strong sense of identity.

“Let’s go quickly. Before the closed loop of time completely shuts.”

At that moment, the warehouse door burst open, and her comrades simultaneously took combat stances.

But soon their expressions turned blank, and Mustang, placing a hand on her slim waist, asked.

“What’s this, who are you?”

Shirone, who had been rushing towards the research society, stopped when he saw Yolga’s group.

“Who are you…?”

If they were from Mars, he would have to be prepared for battle.

“We’re not enemies.”

Fortunately, Yolga approached first, showing no hostility.

She was a stunningly beautiful woman, but there was an eerie feeling lurking in the back of his mind.

‘She looks kind, but something’s off.’

He had met many good people, but this was the first time he felt this way.

“Nice to meet you. I’m Yolga.”

She was Fermi’s mother.

‘So this is Yolga.’

Shirone recalled Anchal’s words.

She was the one who opposed Goffin’s departure to prepare a successor and set out to stop Miro.

‘Except for the height, she really resembles Fermi.’

Could people with the same disposition be so polar opposite?

Even her spine-chilling kind smile reminded him of Fermi.

“Who are you?”

“Ah.”

Realizing he had taken too long, Shirone grasped Yolga’s hand.

“I’m Shirone.”

Yolga tilted her head curiously at the unfamiliar name.

Given their similar physical ages, it was inevitable for Shirone to feel a bit excited.

‘What am I thinking?’

She wasn’t a friend but Fermi’s mother.

“Okay, Shirone. How did you get here?”

Shirone then realized that Yolga was still focused on the core issue.

She was so innocent that she had forgotten the current situation.

‘Get a grip.’

Anyway, it was a serious matter from now on.

Now that he realized he was Hexa, Yolga’s group was also his enemy, aiming for his life.

“You’re hiding something. It’s okay. Won’t you tell me?”

‘What’s with this person?’

She smelled similar to Miro.

“You know me. But I don’t know you. Maybe you’re from the future. If the closed loop of time has reopened.”

Even though he hadn’t said a word, Yolga kept digging into the core.

It felt like her voice was a drill boring into his mind.

“It’s okay. I’m not asking to harm you. But if you really are from the future, there’s one thing I want to ask.”

For the first time, Shirone met Yolga’s gaze and felt a shiver run through his heart, realizing one thing.

‘I see. This person has also surpassed the human category.’

Yolga asked.

“Do I die here?”

Shirone didn’t know how long he remained silent, but he realized he couldn’t hide it forever.

“Yes.”

That much was true.

“Although the death of someone named Yolga isn’t known, you’re definitely not in the future I live in.”

“I see. In other words, Miro eventually succeeds.”

Contrary to his expectations, Yolga wasn’t disappointed.

Knowing her own death, how could she be so calm? Shirone had more important matters to address.

“Can you stop here?”

Yolga snapped out of her thoughts.

“Stop?”

“Miro will save this world in the future. It’s not a perfect victory, but her judgment was correct.”

Regardless of Hexa’s death, Shirone conveyed the truth.

Yolga nodded in understanding, but Mustang’s face turned red.

“Hmph, that’s just Miro’s future. If it’s Yolga, she could end the war with Heaven.”

Edgar and Nine shared the same sentiment.

‘Goffin is essential, but even if he leaves, Yolga should be the savior.’

Yolga could undoubtedly achieve the great unification of humanity and fight Heaven with full force.

She had the qualities to become the unprecedented ruler of the world in human history.

“Okay, Shirone.”

Yolga didn’t hate Shirone because she was a good person.

“Even so, there must be a special reason you came here. Is this world in danger again?”

Although it wasn’t a precise answer, Yolga’s insight was extraordinary.

“I can’t answer that.”

If Yolga, who realized the future, actively intervened, the upper echelon’s events would spiral out of control.

“Don’t worry. I’ve already accepted death. Instead, I have one request. Take me to Miro.”

“To Miro?”

“Yes. And you compare me and Miro. Decide who should remain for this world.”

“But I’m not qualified to do that.”

“No. You’re from the future. And if you think Miro is more necessary than me…”

Yolga always spoke sincerely.

“I’ll take my own life.”

“Yolga! What are you saying!”

Her comrades shouted in unison, but she was resolute.

“I didn’t come here without resolve. Goffin is still needed. And Miro is my beloved sister. If Miro becomes the successor, everyone she loves will suffer.”

The fact that all the family members except Miro were secretly assassinated was no longer a secret 19 years later.

“What do you think? Can you make a difficult decision for this world?”

“I…”

Shirone hesitated.

With Hexa’s life in danger, he should refuse, but talking to Yolga made him feel like even life didn’t matter.

‘Yolga is sincere.’

100% sincerity had the power to move people’s hearts.

“Okay. I’ll decide.”

He thought it might be better for Hexa’s future if he had the choice.

“Where is Miro?”

“I know.”

She would be at the Supernatural Psychic Research Society.

No one knew how the events would twist, but it was certain Miro would come there.

Event Coordinates

Time: 12:32 AM

Location: Istas Warehouse No. 71

“Search thoroughly! Spread out!”

Following the orders of Mars’ captain, Nickel, the forces from Units 1 to 4 split up like water parting.

“Damn it! We’re in the most danger!”

The leader of Unit 1 was unhappy about this.

Even though they were praised as the elite, everyone knew that Unit 1 was essentially cannon fodder.

“Regroup on the second floor!”

The seven members scattered, and Lucan headed west, his assigned area.

“What the…?”

As he separated from his comrades and moved to the next warehouse, a figure in a black mask suddenly appeared.

‘An enemy?’

Lucan reflexively swung his main weapon, a chain sickle, but the masked figure swiftly moved to the side with a crackling sound.

‘A wizard. Electric type. Who is this?’

The figure was quite skilled.

Lucan immediately took a defensive stance, swinging the chain and positioning the sickle diagonally.

“Identify yourself.”

‘Damn it, what’s going on?’

The masked figure, Lycan, was getting frustrated.

He needed to place the "Last Scream of the Yeager Family" in the vault of the designated warehouse, but the structure of Istas had started changing.

‘Fighting these guys isn’t worth it.’

Lycan had never heard of the Mars unit, but from what Fermi had said, they were clearly top-tier fighters in the kingdom.

‘In that case…’

Lycan drew a dagger from his waist.

Seeing the silver blade crackling with electricity, Lucan’s eyes gleamed.

‘An assassin.’

An assassin who also used magic.

“Arrogant bastard.”

Lucan wasn’t very old either, but he had never been outmatched in talent since childhood.

As the two highly skilled individuals clashed head-on, a spine-chilling battle ensued.

The chain sickle changed its trajectory naturally, and the warehouse flashed with Lycan’s electric attacks.

In the energy-saturated state that seemed ready to explode, Lycan’s dagger flew and embedded itself in the wall.

Using Skima’s sensory functions to the maximum, Lucan made a precise movement to get behind Lycan.

‘This is the end.’

A perfectly timed, flawless slash aimed for the back of Lycan’s neck.

‘Impressive skill. But…’

Lycan’s lips curled into a smirk under the mask.

‘Too old-fashioned.’

What would have been an unreactable strike 19 years ago was something Lycan had been trained to counter since childhood.

Without even looking back, Lycan ducked, and Lucan’s sickle grazed the back of his head.

‘Take this.’

Responding to Lycan’s electricity, the daggers flew out and rushed towards Lucan.

The warehouse shook with the exploding electricity, and through the rising smoke, Lucan wrinkled his nose.

“Cheap tricks…”

Lycan had already disappeared.

“Damn it! I’m late for the assembly!”

His comrades had likely already searched their designated areas and reached the second floor.

But Lucan didn’t yet know that the 15-minute delay caused by his fight with Lycan would ultimately save his life.
