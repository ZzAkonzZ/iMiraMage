Chapter 469:

Gaold opened his eyes.

Dust floated in the sunlight streaming through the window.

Another new morning had arrived.

A morning that felt new every day.

Gaold got out of bed and washed himself.

The slender body reflected in the mirror was smooth, without a single scar.

By human standards, it wasn't particularly impressive, but he liked it. What importance did muscles have for someone who believed in God?

'God? Do I believe in God?'

A sudden question crossed his mind.

Come to think of it… why were there no scars?

"What?"

Gaold tilted his head in confusion and splashed water on his body.

His thoughts drifted away without settling on anything, and before he knew it, he was getting dressed, ready to greet the refreshing morning.

Just as he realized that only one thought wasn't drifting away, the door opened, and Miro entered.

"Hey! How can you still be sleeping? Today is the day of the combat evaluation!"

Gaold hurriedly turned his body to hide his bare skin.

"Whoa! This is the men's dormitory!"

Miro, looking at Gaold with a puzzled expression as he buttoned his shirt with a flushed face, said,

"Why so shy? We've already seen everything."

"Huh? Seen everything?"

"Why are you acting like this all of a sudden? We were together last night too."

Gaold blinked.

'Were we?'

He couldn't remember at all.

Come to think of it, it felt like he couldn't remember anything.

Miro sighed, placing her hands on her hips, and shook her head as if she had no choice.

"You're so coy. I can't believe I'm dating such a softie…"

With that, Miro playfully smiled and lunged at Gaold.

"Hey, wait!"

Before Gaold could say anything, she kissed him, pulling him onto the bed.

As they sank into the bed together, Gaold heard a beautiful song.

Miro's breath tickled his neck, and her hand slipped inside his half-buttoned shirt, caressing his skin.

Miro's soft voice whispered in his ear.

"We have about… 10 minutes left."

He heard his heart pounding.

It was the shock of his heart beating for the first time after being still for a while.

'It hurts.'

Only after regaining his senses did he realize he hadn't felt anything until now.

'It hurts.'

His nerves became sensitive, and the pain of simply being alive surged through his entire body.

"Ugh!"

All he felt was pain, and Gaold gritted his teeth.

Miro was no longer there.

From the chest she had touched, thin, delicate black smoke was rising.

I just wanted to be happy.

"Sob!"

A sob escaped through his clenched teeth.

Not power, not fame, not material things, just the small happiness that anyone could have, that's all I longed for.

"Ugh!"

As his heart raced, the black smoke rose even more, filling the ceiling.

His heart.

Gaold's heart, which had denied the entire world, looked down with grotesque eyes and a twisted mouth.

The only thing you wanted! The world took even that away from you! You have nothing left! She's there! Miro is there!

"Sob!"

Gaold's eyes, which had been showing only the whites, returned to normal.

In the pain amplified 2 million times, his briefly lost consciousness returned to reality, and the delusion that hadn't existed sank into his black heart, becoming fuel that burned and spun madly again.

"Aaaah!"

What Gaold saw was neither giants nor fallen angels, but just blood and flesh, and the shattered scenery.

Even in his unconscious state, he fought.

He had become a creature that existed only to advance, and nothing could change his momentum now.

"Stop him! If he breaks through here, it's dangerous!"

The giants, given special orders, ignored everyone else and charged at Gaold.

Hundreds of giants converged on Gaold in a triangular formation, but they were grotesquely scattered the moment they collided with him.

Pain sensitivity increased by 4 million times.

"Aaaah!"

Even as he felt transcendent pain, Gaold crushed them.

In a situation where it was impossible to distinguish between friend and foe, nothing could remain standing on the ground.

It was as if he had fallen into a vat of pain, the agony gnawing at his entire body and burrowing into his brain, delivering a burning discomfort.

'It doesn't hurt! I can endure it!'

Gaold repeated only one thought.

Pain was just pain.

No matter how painful, it was just a signal sent by the brain.

Compared to 'not being able to meet Miro'…

"It's nothing!"

All the enemies within 100 meters ahead were crushed as if rolled over by an invisible force.

The blood, turned to dust, sprayed into the air, filling it.

In the distance, he saw the exit of the capital. He was more than halfway there.

"Let's go! Faster!"

Gaold shouted, holding onto his consciousness that was about to fly away again.

His eyes were already half white.

"Sob! Damn it…!"

Kangnan bit her lip, holding back a sob.

'It must hurt. No, it's beyond just hurting.'

If this continued, Gaold would break.

Like that time, or maybe this time, he might never come back.

"Wait! Let's regroup here first…!"

"Don't stop Gaold."

Sein caught up to Kangnan.

"Don't stop him? If he goes any further, it's really over."

"I know. But if he stops, it's over right there."

"What do you mean…?"

Sein glared at Gaold's rapidly receding back.

"His stride is faltering. His mind is wavering. He's feeling his limits. If his momentum breaks even once…"

Sein gritted his teeth.

"He'll never get up again."

"……"

Finally, as they exited the capital of Lakia, they saw paths stretching out in all directions.

Paths symbolizing the seven sins.

Without thinking, Gaold chose the path right in front of him.

It was the 'Path of Desire.'

"Lord Surt! The enemy has exited the capital and entered the Path of Desire!"

At the messenger's report, not only Surt but also Kariel and Uriel turned their heads.

Only Miro continued to glare menacingly at the unseen distance.

"They've already broken through the capital? How is that possible!"

Surt shouted in anger.

Aiming for reinstatement in Heaven, he was furious to receive such a disgraceful report in front of Kariel.

The strongest unit, including fire giants, fallen angels, and Maras, was blocking Gaold's party.

And yet, they had already broken through the capital?

Uriel spoke.

"I find it strange too. Unless the path was deliberately opened, such speed is impossible."

Surt thought the same.

Either the path was opened, or the enemy crushed their forces at a comparable speed.

But his pride denied both assumptions.

"That's absolutely impossible. My subordinates are strong warriors who do not fear death!"

"Anyway, they'll be here soon. What will you do, Kariel?"

Kariel remained calm.

Even if they were mere humans, they had come to rescue the great Miro. He had expected them to be formidable from the start.

"Answer the call, Tiara."

A brilliant light flashed in the air where Kariel stood, and a beautiful and noble fairy appeared.

It was Tiara, the Fairy of Dominion, ranked second among the 72 classes of fairies.

"You called, Archangel."

"Request support from the Ministry of Internal Affairs. You know the situation."

Tiara coldly looked at the seven paths of sin beyond the drawbridge.

"I have a rough idea."

"Stop them. Coming this fast means they've given their all. But if you destroy their hope, humans are creatures that can't move."

"…Understood."

Without another word, Tiara flew towards the Path of Desire.

As the commander of the Ministry of Internal Affairs, things would get complicated if humans rescued Miro.

Miro bit her lip.

A grand noise was faintly approaching from the other side of the Path of Desire.

It meant they were almost there.

So now was the only chance to change her mind.

'Don't come, Gaold. You need to go to the temple. If you don't adjust the coordinates in Ingris, you can't stop the final war.'

This wasn't the time to come and rescue someone as powerless as herself.

'Sein, at least you should stop Gaold. You know this is madness.'

"They're coming!"

A giant on the wall shouted.

No one thought they would lose, but the giants felt a chill they couldn't explain.

"They're already here?"

Everyone's eyes focused on the Path of Desire stretching beyond the plains.

It was still just a dot, but that dot was crushing the army as it approached.

"Just a little more! Just a little more to go!"

Sein looked back and encouraged the party.

Even he, with his strong rationality, couldn't hide his excitement at this moment.

He hadn't really believed they would make it this far.

'That fool…'

Things that no one thought possible, things believed to be impossible, were unfolding before their eyes.

'We just need to reach the drawbridge! From there…'

The Law of Dominion.

Boom!

At that moment, an immense mental force crushed everyone.

"Ugh!"

Sein looked up at the sky.

A fairy with wings of light was looking down at them.

Armin spoke.

"She's too high. Stop magic is impossible."

Too high?

And yet, the fact that her form was visible at that distance meant she was enormous.

'Scale magic…'

Sein bit his lip until it bled.

There were limits to a fairy's power, so it wouldn't be like opening an infinite dimension like Congob. But if it was magic that utilized the relativity of scale itself, it was entirely possible with a fairy's mind. The anti-magic currently being used was far beyond what Gaold had faced 20 years ago at Alpheas Magic School.

"Ugh!"

Sein tried to break the mental magic with his Iron Wheel Eye.

But the more he tried, the stronger Tiara's dominion became, and eventually, everyone was pressed flat against the ground.

"President…!"

Kangnan, her face pressed to the ground, looked up in shock.

Even Gaold, whom she had trusted, was on his knees, trembling.

Tiara's dominion suppressed not only their bodies but also their minds.

The moment his momentum was lost, Gaold's dark heart was crushed without a trace.

The momentum was gone.

'Is this the end…?'

Sein, unable to contain his frustration, grabbed a handful of dirt.

They had poured everything into this, running without even thinking of stopping, and had come this far.

But it was only to the Path of Desire.

"It's over."

Kariel said with a smirk.

Everyone was stuck at the exact point where the Path of Desire ended.

"It must be quite miserable. At times like this, shouldn't you at least pretend to be sad?"

Miro still didn't respond.

Boom.

At that moment, a distant rumble was heard.

Boom.

As the ground shook rhythmically, Kariel's gaze turned to Gaold.

And without a word, his mouth slowly opened.

"Ugh!"

Gaold used all his strength to lift his body.

The heart had no physical form, so it had no limits, and the faint smoke bypassed all control and sprouted again.

The sprout grew into a thick stem, branching out into many more, providing Gaold's heart with immense fuel.

Boom!

Due to the nature of the magic that suppressed targets by scale, Tiara in the sky shrank every time Gaold moved.

"Ugh!"

Gaold grimaced and poured his strength into his body.

Though it was still just a black speck, Miro was there, beyond the distant wall.

"I'm coming."

As Gaold took a difficult step forward, Sein couldn't hold back his tears any longer.

'Yes. You're going, Gaold.'

Miro was there.

'Now, even I can see Miro.'

As Tiara's dominion weakened, Sein barely managed to stand.

'To be honest, Gaold, what you're doing isn't love or anything. But that's why I placed my hopes on you.'

A love so pure it was almost grotesque.

'If it's an equivalent exchange of pain and desire, you have every right to desire. No one can blame you. So go.'

Sein's Iron Wheel Eye spun at a terrifying speed, creating two massive halos in reality.

Iron Wheel Eye - Equilibrium.

Clang, clang, clang, clang!

As the Law of Dominion was ground down by the gears, Tiara in the sky screamed.

Having used all his strength to break the magic, Sein shouted at Gaold.

"Go!"

Boom!

Gaold's body shot forward like an arrow again.

The immense heart that had risen from his chest enveloped him.

I am your heart, your desire. Go, Gaold!

"Raaaah!"

Gaold roared like a demon as he charged.

Every nerve twitched, blood tears flowed from his eyes, and his blood-soaked tongue hung down to his chin.

The distance to Miro was now 10 kilometers.

"Lord Surt! They've exited the Path of Desire! They're about to enter the drawbridge!"

"I know! Prepare for full-scale assault!"

All of Surt's elite troops descended to block the drawbridge.

As Gaold's form became clearer, Miro slowly stood up.

'He's really… coming?'

Thump, thump.

Cold sweat dripped down Miro's forehead as she listened to the sound of her own racing heart.
