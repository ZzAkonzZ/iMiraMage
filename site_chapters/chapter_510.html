Chapter 510: The Blowback of Evil (4)

"There exists a concept that is symmetrical to the Akashic Records, which we thought encompassed everything. A concept outside the law. In other words, perfect chaos."

"I see."

The smile on Uorin's lips disappeared.

"Because we couldn't understand that chaos, Ra reset everything."

When Gando looked at him with a puzzled expression, Uorin walked to the table and took out some documents.

A thick file was placed in front of Gando.

Anke Ra Erasure.

Perhaps one of the highest security secrets in the world, passed down only to the official successors of Terraje.

As Gando found it difficult to even look at it, let alone touch it, Uorin gestured for him to read it.

"Read it. I need your help."

"Yes, then…"

Gando didn't have the ability to absorb the entire 200-page document.

"Just read the last three pages."

As Gando obediently followed the instruction, each word hit him like a shockwave.

He swallowed hard, and his hands trembled.

"This is…"

"Yes. This is the full story of the incident where Anke Ra tried to reset the world and erase himself."

"But Ra still exists, doesn't he?"

"That's where the variable occurred. As long as Satan isn't eliminated, the same result will repeat no matter how many times we reset. Therefore, the Anke Ra Erasure will only be implemented after Satan's destruction. That's why Ra decided to repeat the world once more, even after winning the final war."

Uorin continued his explanation.

"Even ordinary humans retain faint traces of the reset. That's what déjà vu is. Terraje, as Mitochondrial Eve, can almost perfectly restore that déjà vu."

"It's called the base event."

"Yes. Based on the analysis of that base event, we are currently living in the world after the second reset was implemented. In other words, we are at the midpoint, repeating all processes exactly the same, heading towards the point when the second reset was implemented."

Gando imagined a hypothetical timeline in his mind.

Once a reset occurs, the world goes into an 'off state.'

Then, with some amplification, the world opens up and returns to the point when the reset was done, completing a cycle.

'We are currently at the midpoint of that cycle. In other words, we haven't yet reached the point when Anke Ra did the second reset.'

That point is probably just before the final war ends.

But with Terraje's intervention, the future is changing.

"When is the final war supposed to happen originally?"

"Six months from now, during the first reset period, the Goffin Erasure. Originally, by now, the archangel Kariel would have destroyed the space-time of Miro, and Miro would have died. But as you know, the second reset, the Anke Ra Erasure, is different. Because I added a variable."

One of those variables was Gando.

The reason he was created in Mistra's womb during the Anke Ra Erasure, and raised as a spy in Tormia from birth, was precisely…

"To send Gaold to heaven. By sending him to heaven, a different future was created from the Goffin Erasure."

"Yes. After Ra attempted the second reset, hoping for Satan's destruction, Terraje pondered this for hundreds of years. During that time, my predecessor Mistra recalled the events of 20 years ago during the Goffin Erasure."

Memories of that time surfaced in Uorin's mind.

Gaold, who had forced his way through the law's barrier to prevent Miro from being sent, his hair turning white.

The monstrous scream he let out still seemed to echo in Uorin's ears.

"That's when I realized. This is it! If this man can sacrifice everything for Miro, he might bring us a special variable."

Uorin snapped his fingers and smiled.

Of course, it wasn't something she had experienced directly.

Satan's endlessly growing face pierced the sky.

Even the highest mountain ranges in heaven only reached his shoulders.

"Ooooooh!"

If a mountain had life and will and moved, how much destruction would this world have to endure?

Satan truly felt like a great mountain.

Everything below his eyes seemed so insignificant.

Rumble!

With each step Satan took, the structures of the temple crumbled like sandcastles.

Etella, who was striking repeatedly at an altitude of 700 meters, was no longer in his sight.

"Huh! Huuuuh!"

Cold tears flowed from Etella's eyes as she floated in the air, rapidly punching with both fists.

Helplessness.

She was pushing the waves with all her might, but as Satan's body grew larger, the shockwaves were futilely dissipating.

'I won't give up! No, I can't give up!'

Her resolve, strengthened after losing to Arcane, was never to succumb to the power of evil again.

Crack! Crack!

Even as her bones began to break after thousands of punches, she didn't stop attacking.

More frightening than her body breaking was her faith breaking.

"Why, whyyyyy!"

Does evil grow endlessly stronger?

Is this world truly ruled by evil, not good?

-If everyone were good…

She heard her master's voice.

"There would be no chaos in the world. But someone will always try to use that chaos to break the peace. The methods of evil are easy, pleasurable, and efficient."

Etella, kneeling and listening intently, looked up.

A fourteen-year-old child.

A girl with a baby face and large glasses, but her eyes shone with the will of good.

"But the biggest reason they choose evil is different."

"What is it, Master?"

"Because it looks strong."

Etella blinked at the unexpected words.

"Yes, you can't understand. But that's because you are truly strong. A strong person doesn't waver in the face of evil. It's the weak who seek other methods. So always remember, Etella. If one good can block a hundred evils, then good is stronger than evil."

"Yaaaaaah!"

The speed of the Thousand-Hand Avalokitesvara Thunder Strike increased.

At the moment when her fists were so shattered they could no longer be used, a strike filled with rage hit Satan's body.

'Powerful Strike!'

Boom!

Satan's skin rippled like waves.

But the waves receded as if time had reversed, and with a bang, the shock pushed Etella away.

"Why…!"

Etella's body fell from hundreds of meters high.

"Whyyyy!"

In the face of an unacceptable reality, Etella trembled with extreme helplessness.

'I'm sorry, Master. I'm not strong.'

In the terrible feeling of her faith collapsing, Etella's body accelerated towards the ground at a terrifying speed.

Iron Wheel Eye - Equilibrium.

As the air resistance increased tremendously, her falling speed significantly decreased.

With a thud, Etella crashed into the ground, sparks flying in her eyes, but it wasn't enough to tear her limbs apart.

'How?'

As she waited, unable to grasp the situation, Sain ran over from afar and looked down at her.

"Are you okay?"

"Mr. Sain."

"Sorry I'm late. I had business in Ingris."

Etella turned her body and stood up, supporting herself with her elbows on the ground.

The pain from her fists came belatedly, but it wasn't the time to worry about that.

"Mr. Armin was supposed to go to Ingris…"

"Yes. But the reason we went wasn't for the coordinates."

"We?"

Only then did Etella look beyond Sain's back.

Arius, with his eyes bandaged, was crawling like a dog, and a woman holding his leash was walking towards them.

"Miss Miro."

"Is that Satan? He's really ugly."

Miro, shading her eyes with her hand, looked up at Satan as if she were watching an animal at the zoo.

"Ultimately, the names good and evil are just boundaries defined by humans."

Uorin turned from the window.

"Even before humans called it evil, it already existed. Some kind of energy? Or the concept of chaos."

"That chaos materialized is Satan."

"Yes. A creature born from the Akashic Records defines what is outside the Akashic Records. That's why Anke Ra can't defeat Satan, but humans can. However."

Uorin returned to his seat and sat down.

"Only if the power to define good is stronger than the power to define evil. What will humans define as right between good and evil?"

"If evil wins, there will be no reset, and we will have no future. Was Gaold the variable the Empress planned to destroy Satan?"

Uorin shook his head.

"Heaven is not so easy. Even though they made a mistake in terms of the alliance, they still lost even with the full power of the Holy War. Of course, Gaold is an excellent magician. But he is not an irreplaceable magician. Gaold was just an opportunity to create a variable. The true variable that can cause a ripple in heaven is different."

A face of a person came to Gando's mind.

"Then…"

"Adrias Miro. She died in vain during the Goffin Erasure, but I thought Gaold could save her. I intended to change the future through her."

"Can she succeed?"

"I don't know. We can only believe. But if she can't do it, no one can. Anyway, Miro is…"

Uorin smiled in the sunlight.

"The strongest 'Prajna' humanity has produced."

"What business did you have in Ingris?"

Sain briefly answered Etella's question.

"To understand Ra's will."

Early this morning, Sain, who had assigned each party member their tasks, reunited with Miro, who had defused the bomb in his mind.

Before coming to heaven, specifically when he entered Miro's space-time at Alpheas Magic School's Istas, he wanted to confirm the source of the discomfort he felt.

When he entered with the expectation of a reunion and found that Miro was not there, Sain sensed a strange distortion.

A feeling of unease, as if nothing he chose was real.

At the time, no one could explain that feeling, but he thought Miro might be different.

As expected, as soon as Miro heard Sain's words, she sensed something and investigated the Akashic Records of Ingris.

Finally, they realized the true meaning of this war and what flow they were being swept into.

"It's the second reset. After the Goffin Erasure, another reset occurred, and we are currently on that timeline. This is the source of the discomfort I felt in Istas."

Miro added,

"Sain adjusts physical and emotional factors with his Iron Wheel Eye. That's why he could sense the subtle discrepancies in this world more clearly than others."

Sain continued,

"The problem is that the world of the first reset and the current world might be completely different. Why Istas? Because in the world of the first reset, the event of us visiting Istas didn't happen. In other words, an event that didn't occur in the previous world is happening now. What that means is…"

Miro picked up the explanation,

"Someone intentionally triggered the event of sending Gaold to heaven. To free me. I can roughly guess. There aren't many who have the ability, power, and intellect to calculate this far."

"Terraje."

Etella had also heard the secrets of Terraje from Gaold's group before coming to heaven.

"Yes. They must have been planning everything since the Day of Judgment of the 20. The purpose is singular."

Miro looked up at Satan, who was destroying the world.

"To defeat that monster. Anke Ra gave humanity a chance through the reset, and Terraje created that possibility. So, I feel very bad."

Miro pursed her lips and started walking.

-Miro, let's stop… and go back.

For a brief moment, the face of a man flashed through her mind, and Miro's eyes turned fierce.

"These bastards are playing with me?"
