Chapter 744:

As lightning-like flashes struck the 3,599 altars spread across the world, the earth shook violently.

When Nane's will seeped in and red light began to leak through the cracks in the bricks, confusion erupted among the occupying forces in various countries.

"Commander! The altars are opening!"

The lieutenant shouted, but the commander checked the map one last time before fastening his sword to his waist.

"Strange."

According to the guide of the Holy War, which included the Tri-Imperial System, the Seven Star Kingdoms, and the Two Kings, there were still 23 hours left until the altars were supposed to open.

"Or perhaps it's expected?"

The altars, towering tens of meters high, were constructed so quickly that the kingdoms had no time to intervene.

A history made in a day.

Many kingdoms attempted to destroy them, but the result was annihilation.

Eventually, the matter was handed over to the Holy War.

'But the Holy War is not supranational. They, too, will act in their own national interests.'

The lieutenant reported.

"According to urgent intelligence, the Kingdom of Iron made a final attempt to destroy the altar but failed. We have received information that their elite forces were annihilated last night."

It was none other than the elite forces of the Seven Star Kingdoms.

"Report to the kingdom. The current time is 14:47. The altars are opening earlier than expected."

Similar reports were likely being sent from many countries, with the final gathering point being the Holy War.

"Understood."

As the bricks of the altar disassembled as if losing gravity, an ominous light began to spread.

"What, what is that?"

As the light spread, the scenery changed, revealing a horrific world made of entrails, muscles, and bones.

"Arus! De Mehahe!"

As the veil lifted, beings with melted eyelids and jagged teeth like nails emerged.

Residents of the other world, Arus.

Meaning loneliness, they were demons that used their sense of smell to chew up everything around them.

"Ahhh! Run away!"

As the light from the altar quickly engulfed the troops and Arus appeared nearby, the soldiers lost their will to fight.

"Damn it! All troops retreat! Pull back to the 'guideline'!"

The Holy War had informed the nations of the response plan, and the measured radius was 2 kilometers.

'Too late. They will completely take over in 30 minutes.'

The demons were taking over the world.

Shirone, looking down at the planet from the Great Temple, felt a wave of sadness.

"In the end..."

It was happening because he couldn't stop it.

"It opened earlier than expected. Amita's decision made Nane impatient."

Just as humanity knew Nane's intentions, Nane also knew humanity's movements as if looking at the palm of his hand.

"Nane doesn't want to drag out time out of sympathy for life. He was aiming for a perfect moment with no chance of counterattack."

"An acceleration of enlightenment."

"Yes. According to my calculations, if Nane's karma score exceeds ten million decimal places, even Shirone will struggle to stop it."

A seemingly insignificant one percent at ten million decimal places made a tremendous difference in the ultimate realm.

"The time to reach that is roughly 48 hours. The Holy War likely predicted this based on similar information, but it seems they didn't account for the variable of the Ten Elders."

Amita's presence was felt in the fact that the timing could be advanced by more than 20 hours.

"How long will it take?"

Thousands of red dots spread across the continent were growing rapidly, visible to the naked eye.

Just seeing this speed from space made Shirone shudder to think what people on the ground were experiencing.

"That red light is called the psychic realm, which erases the veil between the other world and the real world. It will cover the entire planet in about a day."

"Is there a way to stop it?"

Great Star shook her head.

"It's not something that can be physically destroyed. It's a system, not a material."

"The Akashic Records."

"Yes. Only beings with the highest authority can directly handle the Akashic Records. In biological terms, it's a law operating at the tenth sense, the state of formlessness."

Anke Ra was a being with the tenth sense, and that authority had now fully transferred to Nane.

"When the altars were built, Luber tried to enter La Enemy's dream. But Luber, too, is a product of the Akashic Records. He was erased upon connection."

Because he was outmatched in authority.

"Therefore, to destroy the altars, Nane, who inherited Anke Ra’s dream, must do it directly..."

Nane destroyed one of the altars in the Kingdom of Iron.

"Or we must infiltrate a concept not in the Akashic Records to destroy it."

"...I have to do it."

"Yes. Only Shirone is detached from the whole. Therefore, this is your first mission. Seal the altars."

Shirone thought of the 3,599 altars.

"I can't make it in time. By the time the sealing is done, no one will be left alive."

"They will buy you time."

As Shirone looked puzzled, Great Star projected the landscape of a giant ice continent onto the ceiling of the Great Temple.

"These are people you know."

As the radius of the psychic realm rapidly expanded, 680,000 people died worldwide in just 10 minutes.

The problem was that the other world and the real world used the same space, only separated by a veil.

In a situation where the walls of the royal castle, ancient weapons, and any concealment facilities were useless...

"I approve the Zion Project."

18 minutes and 42 seconds after the altars opened, representatives of the Tri-Imperial System, the Seven Star Kingdoms, and the Two Kings signed the documents at the Holy War.

The documents were sent to the hands of the Valkyrie's top information mage and crossed the continent at the speed of light.

"Miro, the approval has been granted."

It was transmitted to the Eternal Contemplator Armin and then to Miro, the representative of Zion.

"Too late! Politicians, as always!"

To operate the sanctuary of the law enforcers, organized with the support of 48 countries worldwide, their help was needed.

"Let's start. The longer we delay, the greater the damage."

Nodding at Kuan's words, Miro stared at Nane beyond the Antarctic continent and spoke.

"We will gather everyone's will. From now on, we will minimize the area of the psychic realm."

As torches ignited on the 700 totems arranged in two rows behind her, the ice land blazed like fire.

Ancient silence.

"......"

Between the two rows of pillars stretching towards the continent, 37,000 law enforcers from around the world stood in dense formation.

Looking up at Miro's face, which filled the ceiling of the Great Temple, Shirone felt a lump in his throat.

"They haven't given up."

Veron was gone, but many people still stood against Nane.

"Did Miro plan this?"

"Yes. When Anke Ra reset the world for the third time, she seemed to be the only one who foresaw it."

Hexa, created by Goffin to prevent the world's reset, and the one who carried it through was Miro.

"Although Istas's memory was erased with Goffin, the circuit of insight still remains."

Anke Ra wanted to become a god, but simply repeating resets could never transcend the whole.

"Only those who can close it themselves are gods."

In the end, Miro's foresight was correct.

"The more intense the resistance, the more the world will plunge into pain, but this has bought us time."

Great Star looked at Miro and spoke.

"She protected the world from the heavenly army for 20 years. She is undoubtedly the strongest in humanity when it comes to defense. This time, Nane won't have it easy."

Shirone clenched his fists.

"We can still fight more."

It was a fact he could be sure of because it was none other than Adrias Miro.

"Humans are not your exclusive property, Nane."

As the incarnation of Avalokiteshvara filled the Antarctic sky, Miro raised her hands.

"Ooooooo."

As if conducting the law, her gestures focused the consciousness of the many monks seated behind her.

Leaders of 984 sects, including the Yore Shinkyo, the Karsis Monastery, and the monks of the Eastern Zhongchendong, known as the Ivory Tower of the Seeker World, were all gathered in one place.

'These are almost all the good in the world.'

Nane might not be evil.

"But that is also for us to define."

To activate the seesaw of the law, it was necessary to designate Nane as evil.

'It's shrinking.'

Shirone watched from space as the area of the psychic realm rapidly contracted.

'This is Adrias Miro.'

She was the teacher who had awakened the meaning of life in Shirone.

"Push a little more!"

At Miro's command, the law enforcers moved in unison, each strengthening their will.

Simultaneously, the torches on the 700 totems exploded, ascending into massive pillars of fire.

The heat was enough to blow away the Antarctic cold, but those already in samadhi didn't even feel it.

'What a monstrous being.'

Miro's eyes gleamed fiercely.

'How can a human body do this...'

Although she had to inevitably oppose Nane due to the seesaw of the law, his will was so strong that it inspired awe.

'Yes, you are closest to being a god. Take it all!'

The size of the torches was gradually diminishing.

'Honestly, this is too much!'

The terror of the psychic realm lies in breaking down the barrier between the other world and the real world, which share the same space.

The defensive structures built by humans lose their meaning, and thus the primary task of the Zion Project was to reduce the area of the psychic realm to less than one kilometer.

'The boundary must not collapse.'

If only a hole is made, even if the demons invade, humans can still fight a human war within their territory.

"Damn it!"

Suddenly, a curse burst from Miro's mouth.

'Demons are not good.'

That's why they are called demons, but they are also beings created by humans in the other world.

"Miro, you must forgive."

As Miro's mind wavered at the extreme of concentration, Armin hurriedly spoke.

"If you collapse, it's all over."

Miro felt as if Armin's voice was coming from a distant place.

'Even when the world annihilated the Adrias family, I did not waver. But this...'

Isn't this too unreasonable?

'Why is suffering the lot of the good?'

Armin shouted.

"Get a grip! Shirone has already absolved everyone's sins. This is the one chance we got!"

"Huuuu!"

Miro's sequence, which had hit a plateau, began to surge forward at a terrifying speed again.

'Shirone, how could you forgive?'

Just as Nane's merit was astonishing, Shirone's philanthropy was equally remarkable.

'I can't be outdone by my disciple!'

Finally, as Miro's sequence reached the unimaginable realm of (10^{64}), the flames of the totems pierced the sky.

"I will never lose."

The area of the psychic realm was shrinking back towards its original point of creation.

The radius of the psychic realm: 17.4 kilometers.
