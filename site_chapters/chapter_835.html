Chapter 835: Ivory Tower Grand Assembly (3)

"An assembly, you say?"

Shirone asked as he ushered Minnie and Ariana into the living room.

"Yes. It's a directive from the Grand Star herself. All residents who have received the title of 'Star' must gather with their satellites."

"I see…"

As he poured the tea, Shirone's expression was serene.

"Did you know about this?"

He had been so immersed in meditation that he hadn't received the notification.

"No, but I had my suspicions." Currently, Habitz was extreme, but the moment the "Law Killer" project failed, Shirone intuitively felt it.

"I can’t kill him."

No matter the scenario, achieving the desired outcome was impossible because it lay beyond the scope of the law.

It was a rational decision for the Grand Star to call everyone together now as the armies of hell were engulfing the world.

"You must be having a tough time."

Minnie spoke worriedly, seeing Shirone's more haggard face than before.

"No department in the Ivory Tower fights like you, Shirone. You need rest."

Of the five departments in the Ivory Tower, the Universal Integration Department had the broadest mandate.

"The best rest is resolving the problem at hand. I don't want to run away."

Ariana's eyes were weary.

'As stubborn as I heard.'

The state of Yahweh itself wasn't something humans could handle without immense stress.

Even Miro, known as humanity's strongest constructor, couldn’t extend beyond the boundaries of goodwill.

'How is that even possible?'

While pondering this, Shirone looked at her and smiled.

"I'm really okay."

Color rose in Ariana's cheeks.

'He’s cute.'

She felt a strong desire to pin him down and overwhelm him.

'Me liking him means...'

As befits a fantasy pair, Minnie activated her scale magic.

Her body grew gigantic, reaching the ceiling as if magnified.

"It seems unavoidable. We must take drastic measures to recover from the fatigue of the Five Stars."

Shirone gaped upward, puzzled.

"Drastic measures?"

As she opened the front of her suit jacket, revealing her cleavage, she said to Shirone,

"Come in. When you're buried in a woman's chest, you won't think of anything else."

It indeed looked spacious enough for Shirone to fit.

"No, I…"

Although known for enjoying risqué talk, this was his first encounter with such brazen behavior.

"There's some logic in that."

Ariana stood up and, like Minnie, opened her jacket as she approached.

"Trust us. It’s scientific. Buried in a woman’s bosom, all worries will disappear."

Shirone, realizing this wasn't a joke, felt a sudden fear.

"Why is everyone doing this?"

"Minnie, catch him."

A giant hand blocked his view as it approached.

"Wait! Just a moment!"

Instinctively, he jumped onto the table, but another hand caught him.

Ariana shouted.

"Got him. Put him in!"

"Stop, stop!"

As Mini was pulling Shirone towards her, the door opened, and the dream architect, Luber, entered.

"Great Star."

Mini and Ariana froze, and Shirone blushed as if caught doing something embarrassing.

"Oh, Luber..."

Luber glanced around the room and smiled.

"Am I interrupting something?"

"N-No! Please help!"

Mini gently set Shirone down and released her scale magic.

"Ahem, so this is..."

Luber turned to Mini.

His smile remained, but his eyes turned cold and dark like a snake's.

"I told you to show respect to the Great Star, didn't I?"

The two women immediately straightened up.

"We're sorry! We thought he seemed very troubled lately, so we thought... with our chests..."

"Hmm."

The oppressive atmosphere around them dissipated.

"That's true."

Luber approached Shirone and continued.

"But the Great Star's troubles are not something you can solve. This time, you made a mistake."

Mini hung her head in shame.

"We're sorry."

Luber's unusually strict demeanor indicated he understood the gravity of the Grand Assembly.

'Hmm.'

In reality, Luber was very tense.

'Even a 2-star should know better. Playing around like this...'

As the only one in the Integrated Universe Management Department who had experienced a Grand Assembly, he didn't appreciate the frivolous atmosphere.

'What happened?'

After observing Shirone for a moment, Luber blinked and then turned his head.

"Monga, come out."

From behind Luber, a boy with an egg-shaped white face and large round eyes stepped out.

He was about the size of a five-year-old, and his eyes, which took up half his face, were somewhat eerie.

"Hello. I'm Monga, the dream architect."

Luber introduced him to Shirone.

"This is my satellite, Monga. Like me, he's a dream architect. He handles various tasks for me."

Mini's eyes widened.

'Luber's satellite?'

Even while working together in the Integrated Universe Management Department, she had never heard of Luber's satellite.

'Information about dream architects is scarce even in the Ivory Tower. Yet he's participating...'

Ariana also sensed it.

'This Grand Assembly is no joke.'

Those with the title of Star, especially the Five Great Stars, never back down.

Their actions to uphold their beliefs are nearly limitless, so having more allies is always better.

"A dream architect?"

As Shirone showed interest and looked at Monga, the boy's face broke into a mischievous smile.

As he rolled his eyes up to meet Shirone's gaze, the scenery began to distort.

'What is this?'

As time gradually slowed, only Monga opened his mouth wide and let out a strange scream.

"Kraaa!"

The thought that it was ridiculous lasted only a moment before the boy's face dissolved into black liquid and engulfed Shirone.

'Must... avoid...'

Like a dream, the moment he thought, his movements slowed, and the black liquid covered Shirone.

'Even the Great Star is simple.'

Only dream architects can directly invade the REM realm, the boundary between consciousness and unconsciousness.

Unlike other mental mages, they have the terrifying advantage of being unavoidable.

'In dreams, I'm in control.'

Nightmare, demon.

One of Monga's abilities is to absorb the dreamer's anxious emotions and grow in size.

Grrrr.

The inner beast opened its eyes, and all the resentment Shirone had been holding in began to burn.

'What is this?'

It was growing endlessly.

Even Monga lost control for a moment in the face of the immense anger he had never encountered in any human dream.

'Isn't he supposed to be Yahweh? What kind of philanthropy is this?'

Tears flowed as he faced the resentment that could destroy the entire universe.

'This is dangerous. This human might die.'

Luber had told him not to hold back.

'Oh, whatever!'

Removing the nightmare filter, Shirone's suppressed emotions expanded at the speed of light.

"Aaah..."

Monga realized how large he had become and was terrified.

Even though there were no limits in dreams, he couldn't measure the distance from one end of himself to the other.

'Does he live with this?'

He wondered.

'How can he endure it?'

In a normal situation, Shirone would have already stopped breathing, and the dream world would have closed.

'Where is he?'

Although Shirone was trapped in Monga's grasp, he was too large to locate.

After focusing for a while, Monga finally sensed Shirone buried in the darkness.

Even while experiencing a terrible nightmare, Shirone calmly kept his eyes closed.

'Is this Yahweh's spirit?'

It's not that he lacks anger.

'He's just handling it.'

Although Monga had designed many nightmares, this was the first time he felt something so eerie and terrifying.

Shirone's eyes slowly opened.

"Love."

In a moment that couldn't be called time, a thin flash pierced through the demon's darkness.

Until then, Monga hadn't realized.

'Huh?'

A stimulus surged from a distant place.

"What? What?"

As Monga realized, the flash expanded at the speed of light, burning the demon and the entire world.

"Aaah!"

The fleeting dream ended, and Monga, screaming, flew back and hit the wall.

"Aaah! Aaah!"

While Mini and Ariana looked bewildered, Luber's expression was serious.

'Monga, who designs nightmares, was terrified.'

What could have scared him so much?

The answer seemed to be in Shirone's calm, forward-looking eyes.

"I'm sorry. I won't do it again."

Seeing Monga unable to meet Shirone's gaze, Luber nodded.

'I understand.'

Monga had seen something in Shirone's heart that should never be revealed to the world.

'A precarious double-edged sword.'

If philanthropy were broken for any reason, this world would end.

"You've incurred Yahweh's wrath, Monga."

Monga pouted, feeling Luber's indifferent attitude.

Feeling a sense of responsibility, Luber confessed to Shirone.

"I'm sorry. I was overly cautious before the Grand Assembly."

"It was a terrible nightmare."

Despite his words, Shirone's eyes were calm.

'If it's like this, the Grand Assembly is worth a try.'

Mini and Ariana, who had arrived earlier, also seemed to relax a bit.

"I'll explain. This is the second Grand Assembly of the Ivory Tower since the birth of humanity. We'll likely discuss the current situation, but also..."

Luber sighed and continued.

"The atmosphere of the meeting will likely be to punish you, Shirone."

Mini asked, "Punish? Who would punish the Great Star?"

"Of course, not the Grand Star. But all the Five Great Stars from the five departments will be there. With the failure of ‘Law-Killer’, the situation has worsened, and they'll surely bring it up."

"I'm prepared."

At Shirone's words, Luber nodded.

"The current Great Star can certainly handle their attacks. And take this."

Luber handed over ‘Law-Killer’.

"It's something you registered. You must be fully prepared for this meeting."

It was also a good opportunity to connect with Minerva, who was expected to be their only ally at the Grand Assembly.

"But one person hasn't arrived yet?"

"I heard there are four stars in the Integrated Universe Management Department."

"Sorry!"

The door burst open, and a woman with dark skin entered.

Mini put her hands on her hips and said, "Why are you so late?"

"Yes! I'm late! I'm really sorry!"

She wore a shabby martial arts uniform tied with a belt, looking like a country girl.

Shirone, looking at her plump thighs like frog legs, asked Luber, "Who is this?"

"Yes. This is Kira, a 1-star resident of the Integrated Universe Management Department. She's an undead mage."

Kira bowed at a right angle.

"Hello! I'm Kira! And this is..."

A moment later, a giant skeleton over two meters tall entered and placed a hand on Kira's shoulder.

"This is my satellite, Etron! I found him in the mountains!"

The skeleton clacked its jaw in greeting.

"Shirone, Mini, Kira, and I. The four of us are in the Integrated Universe Management Department. There are a total of 12 stars."

"12 stars?"

"The total number of stars varies by department. It's a measure of prestige, but don't worry. Some departments don't have 4-star members, so it's not an absolute measure."

'It seems our department isn't the highest.'

Luber addressed the stars.

"All 27 members of the Ivory Tower, including the Grand Star, will be present. Never let your guard down."

"Yes!"

The stars and satellites responded energetically.
