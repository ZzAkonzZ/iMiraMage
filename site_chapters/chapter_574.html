Chapter 574:

"This is… impossible."

Eden's defensive magic, which had never been broken even in the graduation exam, was shattered.

'He's come back as a monster?'

Before Hershey could even process this, Shirone charged at Kaiden.

"Stop him!"

In that moment, he realized it was impossible.

Having lost the Kang to Shirone, none of the alliance team could harm him.

Jincheon Sorcery - Sweet Honey.

As Anchal's illusion magic unfolded, the scenery around Shirone turned lewd.

"What the…!"

Though not clearly defined, the Gestalt evoked obscene imagery.

The dense metaphors embedded in the trees, rocks, and even the grass bypassed reason and struck at instinct.

"Now! Run!"

Taking advantage of the moment, Pisho moved a giant spider to grab Kaiden and Eden and escape.

As the illusion of Sweet Honey faded, Shirone's face was flushed.

The pounding of his heart echoed in his ears.

'Anchal…'

It wasn't just a simple illusion to confuse his vision.

Anchal, a master of illusion magic, used Gestalt to stimulate the primal human psyche.

'It's unfortunate, but destroying Eden's hand was a significant gain. At least we bought more than a day.'

Moreover, with Kaiden injured, it would be difficult for him to participate in the game immediately.

'We need to rethink our strategy.'

A signal flare for gathering shot up above Shirone's head.

Midnight.

The alliance team, having failed in their plan to eliminate Shirone, gathered at their hideout with somber expressions.

Kaiden, with bandages around his waist, sat weakly, while Eden knelt in a corner of the forest, praying and crying continuously.

"This is troubling."

Pisho, riding a giant spider, descended from the top of a lush tree.

"We barely collected any Scrambles. At this rate, we'll fall behind on the fourth day too."

Hershey clicked his tongue.

Having lost the Kang to Shirone and had all their cards destroyed, a full-scale confrontation was impossible for the time being.

'The enemy will definitely call a Kang.'

They had to avoid losing their Master Cards, so collecting a few Scrambles was their only achievement for the day.

"What about the injured?"

Anchal spoke up.

"Kaiden is injured. Even with healing magic from the research members, it will take at least a day."

If not for Anchal's illusion magic, both Kaiden and Eden would have lost their Master Cards, so it was fortunate it ended this way.

Hershey said to Anchal,

"Thanks to you, we survived. This strategy was my mistake. Stopping Shirone's movements with an illusion was effective."

At that time, Anchal had also lost the Kang and couldn't harm Shirone.

"Honestly, it was a gamble. The definition of harm was ambiguous. But since secondary traps were allowed, I took the risk, expecting disqualification."

'Hmph, they must have already tested this.'

Despite this, Anchal's complaints were about the rewards after the Scramble Royal.

'If we win, the underground research groups will compete again. The priority for investigating Istas will be determined by how much each contributed to the victory.'

Though a powerful party of research group leaders, the alliance team had its limits.

"I can fight."

Kaiden said, grimacing.

"I don't need healing magic. I'll go after Shirone again tomorrow."

His obsession was almost pitiful.

"No, get the healing magic. We still have four days left, so there's plenty of time to recover."

"I said I can fight. This injury is nothing…"

"Kaiden, that's an order."

Hershey was Fermi's proxy.

"Then I refuse the order. Not even Fermi can control me."

Hershey's gaze turned cold.

"Admit it. If Shirone had hit you with the magic that broke Eden's barrier, you'd be dead. It wouldn't just be your ribs; your whole body would be gone."

'Damn it!'

Having already lost, arguing was pointless. Kaiden clenched his teeth so hard it felt like his jaw would break.

"Lord, forgive my reckless behavior. Grant me the teachings of your infinite love…"

Eden's voice, praying in a trance, pierced the silence.

Hershey, watching her, turned back to Kaiden.

"We can't stick to the same strategy if you can't stop Shirone's dominance."

But it wasn't something anyone else could do either.

"I'll do it."

Eden, having finished her prayer, approached.

"You?"

"I'm going to collect White Cards again. If I create and protect Infinity (○○○○○○) in the shortest time, we'll win, right?"

"But you were also defeated. Another failure is too risky."

"No, it was the magic that broke, not my faith."

"I understand how you feel, but…"

Eden's eyes flashed fiercely.

"I can do it. I'll be different from now on. But I have a condition."

"Hmm."

Even as a team, they were ultimately competitors in the graduation class.

Just as Anchal had a sealed ability, Eden likely had a hidden trump card she didn't want to reveal.

'Setting a condition means she's ready to see this through to the end.'

Hershey, making his judgment, said,

"Alright, let's hear it. What's the condition?"

"If I protect the Infinity hand until the seventh day, I get the priority for investigating Istas."

Hershey's expression turned troubled.

"Hey, that's… even I can't…"

"No, I'm proposing this to everyone here. The number of hands is already starting to diverge. But I can definitely create and protect the Infinity hand. It's a much better choice than losing the game and paying the price."

If Eden truly had a hidden card, the chances of a successful comeback were high.

'Eden will give it her all. It's a matter of religious conviction.'

Hershey looked around at everyone and said,

"Let's vote. It has to be unanimous to pass. Raise your hand if you agree."

Though everyone in the alliance team had their own calculations, they eventually started raising their hands one by one.

'Even if the priority is slightly delayed, it doesn't matter. She'll never find it anyway. If she succeeds, we get the reward, and if she fails, nothing changes. It's worth a try.'

Finally, Kaiden raised his hand, and Hershey said,

"Alright, it's settled. We'll stick to Eden's strategy. Do it right this time."

Anchal asked,

"Then who will mark Shirone? Even if Eden collects Infinity, if we can't stop Shirone, it will be a tie, and the next highest hand will decide the winner."

This was the biggest problem.

Even if Eden collected Infinity, if they couldn't stop Shirone, it would be a tie, and the next highest hand would decide the winner.

Hershey, searching for the right person, spoke up.

"Frings."

With Kaiden injured, he was the team's only combat mage.

"No, I won't mark Shirone."

Frings said, stroking his mustache.

"I can't break him. He's too straightforward."

Pisho said,

"Sure, he has a soft side. If we exploit that weakness…"

"It's not softness."

Hershey's face turned cold as he stared into the darkness.

"It's a bit… humiliating."

This was also as Fermi had predicted, but it didn't feel good.

-Kaiden alone won't be enough.

Before the Scramble Royal began, Fermi had said this.

"To stop Shirone, you'll need double and triple traps."

Hershey hadn't understood his words.

"In the first half of the Golden Wheel's simulation, he was only ranked seventh. We hold the top five spots."

"That was in the first half."

"Will it change? Besides, Kaiden will use his sword in this Scramble Royal. You know his swordsmanship skills."

Fermi had acknowledged that.

"Of course, Kaiden might overpower Shirone. Without Armand, mages are weak against physical force."

"Then what's the problem?"

"But he'll… eventually find a solution."

"Don't you dislike Shirone?"

Fermi had laughed.

"I don't dislike anyone. I just make them dislike me. Anyway, Shirone won't stop. If his growth rate is accelerating, it's safe to assume he's always ahead of expectations. It's a defensive investment."

The key was that Miro had taken Shirone.

"Then what if Kaiden doesn't work?"

Fermi had pointed at Hershey with his index finger.

"In that case, do this."

Snapping out of his thoughts, Hershey said,

"Alright, we'll give up marking Shirone. Instead, Frings, you have a job to do."

"If it's something I can do. What's the strategy?"

A cold smile spread across Hershey's face.

"If they want to act like old men… we'll give them a real old man."

The faces of Shirone's team, gathered at their hideout, were generally bright.

"Great, we've turned it around! Let's keep this up!"

Amy, the last to join the team, was the most excited.

Everyone, guessing the reason, laughed as Neid turned to Shirone and said,

"Perfect timing. With one use of your time manipulation, you destroyed over ten cards."

"Still, it's a bit disappointing. I wanted to keep the momentum until the fourth day, but they forced a confrontation."

Iruki said,

"They'll be more cautious with Kangs from now on. But we can use that tactically. Anyway, let's keep pushing. We broke Eden's barrier too."

"It's a shame I didn't see the full exposure… ouch!"

Sabina smacked Neid on the back of the head.

As everyone enjoyed the cheerful atmosphere, one person remained unusually quiet—Dorothy.

Noticing her slow reaction since the third Scramble summoning, Iruki asked,

"Dorothy, what happened?"

"Huh? Nothing."

"I want to ask, why did you retreat so quickly in the third round?"

Dorothy replied in a sharp voice, "It was dangerous."

"I'm not criticizing you. I trust your judgment. But if there's a problem, we should address it…"

"I said it's nothing!"

Everyone looked at her in surprise, and Dorothy quickly realized her mistake and lowered her head.

"Sorry, I'm really sorry."

Amy said, "It's okay. It's normal to get sensitive when the battle heats up."

Neid added, "Yeah. Let's rest well today and do our best tomorrow. We can win this."

As Shirone's team disbanded after a day's battle, only Iruki remained.

Scramble Royal, Day 4.

"The second Scramble has been summoned."

Having already collected six cards, Shirone focused on destroying the opponent's Scrambles.

The nearest coordinates were in the forest near the Integrated Training Ground.

"Let's go!"

Excited by their lead, Shirone quickly covered the distance and found the glowing Scramble.

"Got it!"

At that moment, the ground shook, and a giant beetle, three times the size of a human, emerged.

'Pisho!'

-Only participants can collect Scrambles.

As the beetle swallowed the Scramble and turned to flee, Shirone immediately launched Homing Photon Cannons.

-However, the location of the Scramble can be moved by factors other than participants.

This had already been confirmed by Dorothy's experiment, and the relocated Scramble's coordinates wouldn't be recorded on the Master Card.

'I have to catch it before it escapes underground.'

Of the Homing Photon Cannons Shirone launched, four struck trees, and the remaining three hit the beetle's exoskeleton.

Screeching in pain, the beetle collapsed, and as Shirone ran towards it, a faint light began to emanate from its exoskeleton.

Insect Magic - Explosive Bug.

The beetle had evolved to have a self-destruct function.

Usually applied to smaller insects, this one had a blast radius of five meters.

"Ugh!"

The beetle's fragments struck Shirone's body.

'This isn't improvisation. It's an ambush.'

Knowing the Insect Magic series, he was certain.

Insect mages use their "mental ecosystem" to induce specific evolutions from the egg stage.

To evolve a beetle of this size with a self-destruct function would take at least two hours.

'What are they planning?'

Expanding his Spirit Zone to find Pisho, Shirone's eyes widened.

'There's another person?'
