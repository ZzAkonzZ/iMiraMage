Chapter 842: Participation (2)

A silence fell over the Great Holy War of the Ivory Tower. The movements of the Law Slayer, reflected by Gaia's power, were enough to make even the Five Great Stars shudder.

“Is that the Law Slayer?”

It was moving so fast that it could only be seen from a high-altitude view, piercing through the demons.

“I thought it twisted the law.”

“Of course it does.”

Minerva, the original owner of the Law Slayer, smiled bitterly.

“The fuel is different. It can kill in the most straightforward way, so there's no need to change the law.”

“Yahweh's wrath…”

Freed checked the ground again.

He frowned as he saw Shirone running along the path opened by the Law Slayer.

‘Sick of it?’

The stars of the Ivory Tower rarely had the opportunity to see each other's faces throughout their lives.

They just observed the world from high above and did what they wanted according to their beliefs.

‘How long has it been since the grand assembly was convened…’

Freed's mind was filled with Shirone's actions, who had stormed out of the Ivory Tower, feeling disgusted.

“Underestimating people.”

Freed tore his lips and stood up.

“I'm going to Kashan.”

Minerva turned her head.

“Are you saying you want to fight? Didn't the System Inspection Department say it didn't matter who won?”

“No need to mock. This grand assembly, Shirone enforced it. It might have been the only way to break my conviction.”

By pointing upwards, Shirone had made the remaining beliefs lose their persuasiveness.

‘Damn kid. Take it easy.’

The five Great Stars, who had reached their destination through the fiercest competition among humanity.

But if one of them ran again, the remaining four had no choice but to run as well.

Xing muttered softly.

“Do we have to go up again?”

Unable to tolerate the illusion of something higher than herself, she stood up.

“I’m going to Kashan too.”

The sudden situation stirred the emotions of the stars, but the Grand Star smiled gently.

‘Thank you, Shirone.’

It's not a choice anyone can make.

It was like asking a climber who had reached the summit of Mount Tai to fly in the sky this time.

‘It will be tough, but you have to lead.’

When Amanta also joined the trip to Kashan, Minerva spun her jet and approached them.

“Should we jump?”

Even the Great Stars of the world couldn't fall from space without Material's power.

“We'll jump from the Corona Kingdom.”

“But the terminal in Kashan must have been occupied and sealed by the demons, right?”

“We can go to the Molton Kingdom. From there, we'll advance north to the capital of Kashan. For us, two hours should be enough.”

‘It's risky…’

Minerva, who was observing the war situation in Kashan, hid her true feelings and joked.

“Is Shirone going to finish everything at this rate?”

Freed snorted.

“It's an army of ten million. The Law Slayer is targeting Habitz, but it has its limits. Besides, there's Amon, the commander of the 7th Legion.”

That was the most concerning part.

“The commanders from the 1st to the 10th legions are direct descendants of Satan. In any case, they are equivalent to the Great Stars. It's not something that can be handled while cutting through an army of ten million.”

The Ten Commandments of Chaos, the illogical essence of demons born from Satan.

The Law Slayer howled.

Its speed was so fast that the sound seemed to come from all directions, and the demons were stunned.

“What is this! This…”

A demon with a hole in its chest fell to the ground with a thud, not even knowing how it was hit.

Shirone's eyes sparkled as he followed behind.

‘I can see it.’

The imperial city of Aganos in Kashan.

But the problem was that the number of enemies was increasing as he approached.

‘The durability of the dagger is reaching its limit.’

Although he was attacking the weakest parts according to the law, he had already pierced through over 100,000 muscles.

“Argh! Save me, save…”

A thud and a shockwave erupted from inside the retreating demons' camp.

“Grrr!”

The demons were torn apart, and dust spread within a radius of tens of meters.

“Pathetic.”

Division Commander Volcano.

A demon with lower fangs reaching up to its cheekbones and four thick arms.

“Don't run away! It's just a weapon made of iron! If you keep killing and killing, it will eventually break!”

The demons' confusion disappeared instantly.

“Die! Worship Satan with your death! Show the detestable Yahweh what you're made of!”

As Volcano's demonic energy spread, 300,000 troops simultaneously targeted Shirone.

For an ordinary human, just that much hostility would be enough to stop their breath.

‘A strong one.’

Even now, the Law Slayer was killing demons, but no one was running away anymore.

“Yes! Being afraid of Yahweh! This is a disgrace to demons! Attack!”

As the brigade commander shouted, the demons' switches were turned on in unison, following the command system.

“Kill!”

The enemies swarmed Shirone, who had been running without looking back, leaving no room to escape.

It was like watching a swarm of mayflies, and as they formed a sphere around Shirone, a curtain of light spread out.

‘Multiple Radiance.’

With a roar, the demons were crushed, but the number of enemies seemed infinite.

‘There's no end!’

Fireballs flew from the sky, and ice spikes wrapped around the ground like a torrent, attacking Shirone.

Lightning, strong winds, heat, and poison mixed, and thousands of tridents and tens of thousands of arrows rained down.

The one-sided bombardment, without even thinking of a counterattack, was a characteristic of demons who didn't care about their own deaths.

‘Stop!’

Time stopped, and all the demons within a 2-kilometer radius froze.

‘Where do I start?’

As soon as the thought ended, the scenery shook, and twelve figures in black robes emerged from the ground.

Time outside the law.

The priests of hell, who appeared by squeezing through the 0.666-second gap of each second.

“We are the Shiok. We govern the prison of time. Yahweh, we have come to seek your forgiveness.”

“Forgiveness?”

Shirone asked coldly.

“We will give you anything you want. So why not calm your anger?”

‘So this is it.’

He recalled the incident where the terrorist Mortasinger disappeared in front of him during the World Beauty Contest.

“What I want is for all demons to repent.”

“It is possible.”

“No.”

It was with such sweet whispers that Mortasinger was dragged to hell.

“You need to be punished.”

The Shiok spoke in a murky voice, unlike before.

“Don't try, Yahweh. The world already belongs to evil.”

The twelve priests, who had opened black books, began to sing a sinister dirge.

“Aaaaaa.”

The twelve slid away, moving outside the radius of the stop spell.

The roar of the enemies, who had been held in the air, erupted again, and despite the stop spell, the attacks continued.

“Damn it!”

To evade, he had no choice but to release the spell, and the flash of spatial movement soared into the sky.

“There! Catch him!”

As he deviated from the trajectory heading directly to Aganos for the first time, the demons chased after him.

‘Block it for now!’

Yahweh's radiance flowed through his body, and the ground around Shirone crumpled into a vortex.

“Grrr!”

Demons with crushed legs flailed their arms, their upper bodies sticking out.

He extinguished fire with water, neutralized lightning with iron, and melted ice spikes with flames.

‘Split.’

At Yahweh's command, the ground split in two, and thousands of demons fell.

‘Close.’

Rumble!

As the gap in the ground gradually narrowed, chilling screams erupted.

“Kieee!”

Yahweh's defense, the only thing that had ever blocked Nane's attack, known as the Sermon.

There were over two hundred types of attacks, but none could wound Shirone.

‘I can hold on.’

Shirone, defending with Yahweh's power, retrieved the Law Slayer, which was ravaging the surroundings.

As expected, the length of the blade was halved.

‘If I pierce the armor in this state, it will break.’

That was exactly what Volcano was aiming for, and having achieved his goal, he charged.

“Yahweh! Is this all you've got?”

Shirone gripped the handle of the Law Slayer tightly.

‘Material!’

As black matter synthesized and clumped onto the Law Slayer, it transformed into the shape of a giant monster.

Its name was Salsu.

‘This will do.’

Even if the blade wore out and disappeared, Shirone's anger would not dull.

“Grrr!”

Salsu, equipped with the Law Slayer as its engine, roared and lunged at Volcano.

“With Satan's power!”

The moment Volcano grabbed Salsu's head with his four arms, his eyes widened.

“This, this is…!”

A powerful shock, enough to shake the very essence of the demon, shattered his bones.

“Argh!”

Volcano's body exploded.

“Hah. Hah.”

As Shirone, breathing heavily, regained his composure, another 10,000 troops gathered around him.

But in front of the black monster exuding a menacing aura, no one dared to approach.

‘It's not easy.’

Shirone, riding on Salsu's back, looked at Aganos.

Salsu's face, absorbing Yahweh's wrath, elongated like a blade.

“Let's go.”

As Salsu kicked off the ground, a hole was punched through the encirclement.

“Damn it! Chase them!”

The demons hurried to follow, but Shirone had already passed through the outer gate of the capital.

‘Wait for me, Habitz!’

“This way!”

Kido, carrying Uorin on his back, ran through the complex paths guided by the Wind Guards.

‘Just a little further and we can escape.’ Just as hope seemed within reach, an overwhelming killing intent seized them.

“Ugh!”

Kido rolled on the ground, risking Uorin's safety, as a black afterimage slashed through the air.

‘This killing intent alone could kill us.’

Kido looked back at Uorin.

“Are you okay?”

“…Yes.”

Though Uorin had survived, her voice indicated the situation was dire.

Standing where Kido had looked up was a demon swordsman in black armor.

“And who are you?”

The presence of this demon was qualitatively different from those they had fought before, causing Kido's legs to tremble involuntarily.

“Commander of the 7th Legion, Amon.”

Despair flashed in Uorin's eyes.

‘He's finally moving.’

“Protect the Empress!”

The twenty Wind Guards leading them converged from the sky and charged at Amon.

Moving like a storm, they created a barrier of bodies and swung their swords wildly.

The air vibrated, and the overlapping afterimages gradually became transparent, revealing Amon's figure.

“Damn it…”

The twenty Wind Guards were reduced to pieces and fell to the ground.

“My weakness is…”

Amon sheathed his sword and approached.

“I don't bother hiding my killing intent.”

That's why Kido survived, but the fate of the Wind Guards who approached Amon was tragic.

“Are you the Empress?”

Uorin glared at Amon.

“How did you find me?”

The answer came from behind Amon.

“Do you know why I came to Kashan instead of Zetaro?”

Smoto, walking with a sneer, pointed to his eyes.

“To find a fleeing rat, you need my eyes.”

It was the power of the Perfectionist.

“It's unfortunate. Even if you abandoned the empire, you didn't want to be caught by us. So what will you do now? Will you commit suicide here?”

“I won't die.”

If she were going to die just because her body was broken, she wouldn't have abandoned a billion citizens.

‘Shirone, please…’

If only she could see him one more time.

“It's been a while, Empress.”

As Amon and Smoto simultaneously stepped aside, Habitz, covered in blood, appeared.

‘Habitz!’

Uorin ground her teeth.
