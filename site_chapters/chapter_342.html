Chapter 342: The Protagonist of Life (2)

Talent and all that, it was just an excuse from the beginning.

Their minds were filled with magic from the start, leaving no room for success or honor.

They wanted to win. They didn't want to lose in competition. And ultimately, they wanted to become the greatest mages.

Dante's eyes sparkled as he watched the Inferno. It was a powerful spell he hadn't seen during the graduation exam.

'She used eidetic memory to lower entropy. With the red eyes, it must be close to zero percent. This might work.'

"Dante, Dante."

Liria, who was kneeling, called out to Dante in a trembling voice.

"What's wrong? Is there a problem?"

"Can we really... do this? Can we succeed?"

"Why are you asking me that?"

Liria turned her head to look up at Dante. Her tearful face showed significant inner conflict.

"You always make the right choices! So tell me! If you say it, I'll feel reassured."

Dante understood her feelings. In battle, a mage’s cold rationality is as important as a swordsman's conviction. Fear comes from uncertainty, not crisis. When you can objectively analyze the situation, your comrades can muster the courage to fight.

"It's not optimistic. But it's not pessimistic either."

It was literally a coin toss. And this was the minimum probability for a mage to attempt something.

"There's a 50 percent chance Shirone will push through. A 50 percent chance my defensive magic circle will hold. And a 50 percent chance Amy's spell will work on the enemy. Add those three together, and you know what that means?"

Liria, mumbling Dante's words, suddenly widened her eyes and lifted her head as if realizing something.

"Then, 150 percent?"

Dante gave a bitter smile.

"...Don't become a mage."

The remaining distance to the Pakyojin installation point was 220 meters.

Shirone fired the photon cannons in a trance. The flashes shooting beyond his hands were blinding. The magic amplifiers embedded in his hands were glowing red-hot.

"Raaaargh!"

The Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara's body bent and was pushed towards the ground.

Remaining distance: 200 meters.

Jocre, watching the massive Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara about to fall, felt his legs tremble.

'Is that Shirone...?'

The person who overwhelmed Dante, the kingdom's best defender, with firepower.

He had once thought Shirone was just at a student level. He couldn't bear the self-deprecation otherwise.

But the photon cannons he saw with his own eyes had a power that even most professionals couldn't wield.

They might not be superior to professionals in every aspect, but at least in one area, they had already reached a professional level.

That was the students of the five great academies.

Remaining distance: 170 meters.

"It's in! I'll create the second barrier!"

Dante trapped the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara in a hexagonal mansion. The magic circle only integrated information on the inside, so attacks from outside didn't apply. Meanwhile, the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara, trapped in the narrow walls, struggled violently.

"Ugh!"

Dante's face contorted. He was pulling the mansion containing the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara to the ground, but the resistance was stronger than expected. Especially the power of the fireballs spreading in all directions, which could destroy the highest durability defensive magic circle in less than 30 seconds.

Remaining distance: 120 meters.

Dante shouted with a face full of veins.

"Amy! Now!"

"Got it!"

Amy jumped up and swung her hands, sending the massive Inferno away from her. The slow-moving fireball crossed the sky and struck the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara with pinpoint accuracy, following the coordinates of her eidetic memory.

"Raaaargh!"

As the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara's body burned, Dante felt the pressure ease slightly. Fire-based magic had the advantage of continuous impact over photon cannons.

Remaining distance: 40 meters.

Liria shook her head and shouted.

"It's still not enough! We can't trap it in Pakyojin like this!"

The blazing Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara scratched and screamed inside the mansion. Eventually, the magic circle, reaching its durability limit, shattered like glass.

Liria's face filled with despair.

"It's over. The demigod will escape into the world."

"No, it's not over."

Liria's gaze turned to the sky. Shirone, gathering light in his hands, was looking down at the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara from above. As the light grew, it was like another sun had appeared in the sky.

"Yaaaargh!"

Shirone fired the photon cannon with all his might. A massive flash, one meter in diameter, struck the side of the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara.

Remaining distance: 30 meters.

"Raaaargh!"

The Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara flailed its arms desperately. But the mass, which even its law couldn't withstand, kept pushing in.

Remaining distance: 20 meters.

"Now! Start!"

Liria clasped her hands and prayed.

For evil to be vanquished. For a world where everyone can live happily. If she could create a paradise without hunger, murder, or adultery, she wouldn't mind if her body was torn into a thousand pieces, or if she was hated by demons and fell into hell to suffer forever. She would become the fertilizer for the world. If everyone could hold the will of good, she would gladly enter the dirtiest place and roll her body.

She shed tears like a waterfall. Her will, surpassing human limits, drew the energy of the totem embedded in the ancient ruins. As the sealing formation disappeared, a 10-meter diameter Pakyojin took shape.

"In the name of Liria, I command you! Evil, sleep here!"

Liria's eyes burned golden. The flowing tears evaporated instantly, and a pillar of light made of law pierced the sky.

"Raaaargh!"

As the photon cannon's flash pushed, the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara screamed its death throes, flailing its dozens of hands.

Remaining distance: 10 meters.

"Yaaaargh!"

Shirone felt the output of the photon cannon decreasing. But as he drew more power through mental transcendence, the flash amplified, completely enveloping the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara.

Remaining distance.

0 meters.

As the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara entered the Pakyojin's domain, Liria focused all her will on one point.

"Screeeech!"

The strongest sealing magic of the Akeanis Shrine.

Okwang Pakyojin.

Two arms made of light twisted like braids and grabbed the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara, pulling it to the ground.

"Raaaargh! Raaaargh! Raaaargh!"

The Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara resisted desperately. There was no reason for the law of evil to exist in a space filled with the will of good. The fact that it was resisting was clear evidence of a demigod.

'But it's over now! Just a little more...!'

Just as Liria thought it was over, she heard footsteps. Vivian ran past her towards the Okwang Pakyojin.

"Ice Queen!"

Dante's face twisted.

"That fool!"

Liria shouted in shock.

"Don't approach! You'll be dragged in!"

Pakyojin was different from the sealing formation.

It concentrated the will of good in a narrow area, so even a slightly evil-leaning person would fall into the abyss.

Vivian heard Liria's voice clearly. But she never stopped running.

"This is the end. This is the final act of my play."

It was a life where no one paid attention to her. But the Ice Queen gave her hope that she could be the protagonist. Even if the reality was a monster, even if her kindness and consideration stemmed from ignorance, it was the only spotlight for Vivian.

Tears streamed down Vivian's face.

What would happen if she entered Pakyojin? Perhaps only the silence after the play ended would continue forever.

But she wasn't afraid. She would meet the Ice Queen. She would hold her hand and bow to the audience. She would shout to the world, thanking them for watching a wonderful performance.

"Yaaaargh!"

As tears filled her eyes, blinding her, Vivian kicked off the ground and threw herself into the Okwang Pakyojin.

"Vivian! Come back!"

Jocre cried out in anguish.

She was a sensitive child. Perhaps her heart had broken in the face of an unbearable situation.

But it couldn't end like this.

It was all his fault.

If he hadn't entered the Ice Queen's nature while drunk, if he hadn't cared about Amy, she wouldn't have made such an extreme choice.

"Vivian!"

Jocre's desperate cry couldn't reach Vivian. It was Amy who caught her. Amy, throwing herself to grab Vivian, narrowly avoided the sealing formation and fell. At the same time, the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara fell into the Okwang Pakyojin as if being buried. The sound of hundreds of nails scratching the ground echoed ominously.

"Raaaargh!"

The force tearing the ground sent chills down the spines of those watching. But even that formidable power couldn't overcome the principle of the Okwang Pakyojin.

It was the mysterious and profound power of the law.

The Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara sank into the abyss of the law, leaving only thousands of nail marks.

Then, Vivian, who had been watching with tears, turned to Amy and shouted.

"Let go! Let me go! I want to go in too!"

"Are you crazy? You'll die if you go in there!"

"Since when did you care about me? Do you know how I feel? You've lived your whole life as the protagonist, do you understand my miserable feelings?"

"So you're going to die because of that? Because you're miserable?"

"No, I'm going to make you suffer until the end! I'll make sure you never succeed! At this moment, I'm the protagonist!"

"Don't talk nonsense, idiot! What kind of protagonist gives up on life so easily?"

Amy's voice pierced Vivian's eardrums.

"Fighting is hard for everyone! But you fight to the end to become the protagonist! If you give up, if you give up..."

Amy's voice choked up, and she lowered her head. She, too, had always wanted to be the protagonist more than anyone else.

"From that moment, you become nothing."

The feeling of being thrown out of the spotlight of the world.

While everyone looked at the successful candidates, Amy cried alone in her room.

No one looked at her.

But now she understood.

You have to fight anyway.

Even if what awaits is a bad ending, those who don't fight to the end don't deserve to be the main character.

Vivian, not understanding why Amy was suddenly so agitated, wore a blank expression and opened her mouth to say something.

"I..."

At that moment, the ground began to shake. Vivian turned her head, and everyone looked at the Okwang Pakyojin.

As soon as Shirone landed on the ground, he shouted.

"It's not over yet!"

Dozens of hands rose from the Okwang Pakyojin. As they bent simultaneously and pushed the ground, the face of the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara reappeared.

"I am the ruler of the world!"

Shirone’s party's faces turned pale. The Okwang Pakyojin's power hadn't completely subdued it yet, and they didn't know what would happen as time passed.

"Did you catch the law user? You're lucky."

Shirone turned his head in surprise.

A woman with curly blonde hair, dark skin, and a tight-fitting suit was standing there.

It was Ahoya Kangnan, the secretary-general of the Magic Association.

"Who are you?"

Kangnan took off her high heels as she walked towards the Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara. Passing by Shirone’s party, she said,

"Step back. You might get hurt."

Liria shouted.

"Wait! You can't just go in! If you can't handle the law, you'll be dragged into the Pakyojin's power!"

Kangnan showed no hesitation and pushed her body into the Okwang Pakyojin. She didn't know whether she leaned towards good or evil. But the conviction she had lived with as a Ram Muai since birth was not weak enough to be shaken by the law.

"Raaaargh! Raaaargh!"

The Thousand-Armed Avalokiteshvara scratched the ground repeatedly, like a person drowning in the sea, rising and falling.
