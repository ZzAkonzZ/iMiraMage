Chapter 828: Anger (4)

Heaven, Sixth Heaven, Zebul.

In the Great World War where Kariel resided, eight subjects, including Smile, were trapped.

Humans selected by special criteria.

The first giant was a fusion of Gaia people, and there was no rejection reaction when performing the Elixir of Life.

This was because they had all achieved an integrated mental system to the extent that individual distinctions were impossible.

Thus, the giant king Ymir, known as the ultimate in living beings, was born.

However, as the Ultima System weakened, the success rate of the Elixir of Life also began to decline significantly.

'If there is no point in distinguishing individuality, the strength of integration comes only from quantity.'

Conversely, if even one individual remains, the complete dismantling of the Ultima System is impossible.

Currently, it was difficult to create even a sixth-stage giant, and Anke Ra gave Kariel a special order.

Analyze the Ultima System.

To completely annihilate the Gaia people, it was necessary to understand their mental system.

'Was her name Smile?'

Kariel looked back at the blue-haired woman trapped in the physics machine's glass sphere.

'Just an ordinary human.'

There was no fear in her calm acceptance of reality.

'It's not that her mind is strong, or that she has good endurance, or that she understands fear.'

The Ultima System is not like that.

Among humans, there are strong individuals who have pushed their traits to the extreme, but such integration is impossible.

'Ultimately an ordinary human? No, even such a definition is just a concept that hinders the Ultima System.'

The moment you define something, it becomes impossible to integrate the whole.

'Yes, this is something beyond definition.'

Therefore, there is no way to define Smile.

'Just accept it.'

Kariel approached the glass sphere.

"Aren't you afraid?"

If he obtained the primal substance through the black elixir, he could know everything about Smile, but the fear that he might not find it made him ask.

"You know what the Elixir of Life is. Ten, a hundred, a thousand. Do you think you can be found among them?"

Smile's eyes wavered for a moment but soon regained a calm aura as if swept away by a great river.

"Being eaten by a wild beast or being buried in the ground after death is no different."

"...Is that so?"

No matter what you designate in this world, if you trace it back endlessly, everything is one.

'I think I understand something.'

There is no distinction between friend and foe.

The position where you can contemplate everything as one is probably outside the universe.

'So, are you trying to escape the photon system?'

As Kariel activated the physics machine, the liquid of the black elixir, known as the Philosopher's Stone, began to fill the glass sphere.

"Lord Kariel."

At that moment, an angel flew into the Great World War.

"An intruder disturbing Heaven has crossed the Third Heaven and entered the Fourth Heaven."

"What?"

Judging by the intruder's path, they were breaking through the barriers clockwise from the First Heaven and heading here.

"You still haven't dealt with it?"

The first report clearly stated that it could be stopped at the Second Heaven.

'No, but... it's too fast.'

It had only been 20 minutes since the report, so at this rate, they would arrive within 30 minutes.

As the black liquid touched her ankles, Smile stood up in the glass sphere to buy time.

It wasn't a struggle for survival.

'Brother.'

If the intruder who broke through Heaven's walls was anyone, it had to be Ogent.

'You shouldn't come.'

She already knew her brother's heart.

'You've struggled a lot.'

Ogent is a timid person.

He felt that love could only be achieved by keeping someone close and cherishing them.

'You think too much.'

Couldn't they have lived their whole lives knowing each other's hearts?

If they had entrusted themselves to the flow of life, couldn't they have shared a passionate love at some point?

Humans can't do that.

'Now, find your happiness, brother. You shouldn't push yourself into unhappiness anymore.'

Anguish.

It was the reason why all humanity couldn't be unified.

Kariel asked.

"What happened?"

In a reprimanding tone, the angel hurriedly bowed his head.

"I'm sorry. It was my misjudgment."

The fact that he made a mistake in evaluating a mere human stimulated the angel's sense of shame.

But he couldn't deny the truth, so he bit his lip and raised his head.

"The intruder reported 20 minutes ago and the current intruder are completely different humans."

Kariel tilted his head.

"Are you saying the person changed?"

"That is..."

The angel, who had been lost in thought for a moment, finally gave up and confessed his honest feelings.

"I don't know what it is anymore."

Fifth Heaven, Matei.

"Weaklings! The strongest giant unit can't stop a single human!"

Under the command of the giant corps commander, hundreds of giants swung their swords at Ogent.

Boom! Boom! Boom!

The sight of the blades continuously hitting the ground felt like the sky was collapsing from Ogent's perspective.

At the moment when the giant blade was about to crush Ogent, the giant's eyes widened.

"What strength...!"

Despite the force comparable to a falling mountain, Ogent was holding the blade and enduring it.

"Ugh!"

Hot steam shot out from between Ogent's tightly clenched teeth.

Crack. Crack.

Bones twisted, and muscles coiled like ropes, transforming him into something other than human.

'Cut!'

As Ogent deflected the giant's weapon and advanced, the faces of the surrounding giants fell cleanly.

"What is that?"

It's obsession.

"Stop him! Crush him!"

As the giants ran, shaking the ground, Ogent fell into thought.

'I'm already finished.'

Everything had become a mess.

'I will disappear without ever being happy.'

Even now, his anger was growing endlessly, and eventually, it would swallow everything.

'I don't care if I die.'

He didn't feel it was unfair. He had lived for over twenty years, and there were countless lives shorter than that in the world.

'But Smile.'

She wouldn't understand.

'I don't know how to live as cool as you.'

Calculating, analyzing, thinking about what would happen if this or that happened.

'There's no right answer.'

A pitiful life wandering and crying in the middle of an unknown life.

'That's me.'

Is life beautiful?

'Good for you, being happy.' Where should the petty heart that no one in the world can congratulate go?

"Aaah!"

The anger, hot as a furnace, began to twist not only his mind but also his body.

"Gag! Gag! Gag!"

Vomiting biological waste that surged from within, he swung his sword like a madman.

'I've reached it!'

At the peak of anger where he didn't even feel like himself, he realized another level.

"Gag! Gag!"

Save me.

'Did you see? This is the strongest swordsmanship of humanity!'

No giant could match him.

'Does this look beautiful? Does this look cool?'

Struggling to survive like an octopus thrown into boiling water is the fate of life.

'But I still want to live.'

Struggling while soiling himself, but never being able to get out is the truth of life.

"I'm so angry I'm going crazy!"

As Ogent swung his sword, the wall of the Sixth Heaven, Zebul, collapsed with a bang.

The angels flying from afar suddenly stopped and widened their eyes.

"What...!"

Ogent's body, which had reached the angel's realm, had already collapsed from its human form.

"Huff! Huff! Huff!"

The process of anger burning his skin, twisting his muscles, and melting his bones was truly horrific.

'It's okay.'

His breath was stuck in his throat, and biological waste kept pouring out of his mouth.

'I have to swing my sword.'

That's all he knew how to do.

'If I keep swinging, swinging, and swinging...'

Surely, a good day will come.

"Even someone like me will have good things happen!"

Not knowing where his limbs were, Ogent swung his sword at the approaching Mara.

The action, driven by the sole intention to cut, was horrific by human standards, but...

"He's not human."

To the angels, his desperate struggle strangely appeared beautiful.

"He is life itself."

Ogent cried out, sobbing.

"Give me Smile!"

"Kill him."

Thinking it was dangerous to leave him as he was, the angels gave the order, and hundreds of Mara attacked.

At the same time, Ogent's body burst into flames, cutting down all the surrounding Mara.

"How can he do that?"

The law of this world never grants infinite power to a single entity.

"Destroy his weapon!"

A giant three-horned Mara snatched Ogent's sword, broke it, and threw it away.

"Enough! No more..."

'Cut.'

As the Mara's face twisted, his body was precisely split in two.

The angels' eyes sharpened.

'I see.'

Idea.

'He is becoming a sword.'

It was regrettable that they hadn't stopped him just 30 minutes ago.

"We can't let him reach it. If he progresses further, it will threaten the very existence of Heaven."

All the angels' halos transformed into their unique forms.

"Launch a full-scale attack!"

As the judicial halos filled the sky, the angels' unique concepts focused on Ogent.

In the bombardment that shook Zebul, Ogent's roar pierced through the smoke.

"Aaah!"

As the smoke cleared and Ogent's figure emerged, the angels watched in stunned silence.

"This can't be..."

"Ugh."

The perfectly regenerated body resembled a human, but its nature was far from human.

His face twisted like a demon, and his muscles contorted grotesquely.

"Is it indestructible?"

If it had reached the level of a concept, destruction was impossible.

"No, it's just solid. As long as it has a physical body, it can't reach the Idea."

"Then what is that?"

"A Yaksha."

It was the strongest Yaksha in history.

"Smile!"

As Ogent's roar erupted, the angels blocking his path were split in half.

Boom!

With the Law of Maha imbued, a huge hole was punched in the wall of the Great World War, and Ogent entered.

"You've finally come."

Kariel, who had been watching from above, greeted him with an annoyed expression.

"Smile."

The moment Ogent saw Smile submerged in the black liquid, his muscles tightened even more.

Although he didn't outwardly show his anger, the scenery shook with each step he took.

"Foolish man."

Despite becoming a being comparable to an angel, he was committing suicide just to find one woman.

"I commend you for making it this far. But you will never get what you want."

That is the life of a human.

"Give me Smile."

Ogent had given up being human.

"Judicial Halo."

As Kariel's trademark Simulacrum accumulated, all the electronic devices in the Great World War flickered.

'The stronger it gets, the stronger it becomes, endlessly growing stronger, the cannon of matter.'

A concept approaching the theory of a perpetual motion machine, which humans could not fathom, gave birth to the cannon.

"It is enough to destroy you."

It probably was, but Ogent didn't care and raised his arms as if holding a sword.

"Cut."

The hardest sphere from the cannon of matter was fired.
