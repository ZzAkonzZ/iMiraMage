Chapter 820: The World They Live in (4)

"...Magic?"

Meyer muttered with a dazed expression, and the captain of the guard slowly shook his head.

"No, Your Majesty. It borrows the power of the mind but is fundamentally a physical technique."

It was called Divine Transcendence.

"Physical? Can a human produce such destructive power? You've never shown me anything like that."

The answer was simple.

"Because I can't do it either."

Meyer's gaze turned to Rian.

'I've heard he's the best among the new swordsmen, but to surpass even the Shinjang?'

He was a talent needed by the Kingdom of Jive.

"I'd like to have a private audience with him. Convey my message."

The captain of the guard looked troubled.

'If he could be swayed by money, honor, or power, he wouldn't be here.'

He was a swordsman even the emperor of the empire couldn't have.

"Rrrr!" Hundreds of bats, making beastly sounds, reformed into Nike's shape.

'I almost perished.'

If it weren't for the bat transformation, his body would have been completely scattered.

"You've had the greatest luck of your lives."

As Nike retracted his whip and backed towards the wall, Xenia took a step forward.

"Where do you think you're going!"

The hunters blocked her.

"Calm down. It's a relief if he retreats now."

"Move! That bastard killed my parents..."

"I know. But darkness will soon come. You haven't fought just for a day or two, have you?"

She couldn't argue with the hunter's words.

"Damn it!"

Even as the hunters tensed, Nike's gaze was fixed solely on Rian.

'Jinma must have given special instructions.'

The order was to infiltrate the royal palace, kill the hunters, and assassinate King Meyer.

'And if possible... capture the Knight of Maha.'

'If possible?'

Nike's face twisted with rage at his failure and pointed at Rian.

"We will meet again."

As Nike disappeared, Rian pulled his greatsword from Shovel's body, which had already dried up and turned to ash.

"With this, the internal spy has been eliminated. There will be no more leaks."

As he turned to leave, Benoff hurriedly followed.

"Wait. Are you just going to leave?"

"There's no benefit in staying here. I'll act alone from now on."

Though the law wouldn't recognize it, no one could refute him after witnessing the Yacha's power.

"Wait a moment."

The captain of the guard spoke loudly enough for everyone to hear.

"I have a proposal. How about conducting joint operations between the hunters and the royal security forces?"

It was a strategy to prevent bureaucratic interference by having the guard directly manage it.

"Joint operations?"

The hunters murmured.

It was clear that the strategy was to recruit Rian, but having the kingdom's support would make things much easier.

One of the hunters shouted,

"If it's joint operations, what support can you provide?"

Having already received the king's approval, the captain of the guard spoke confidently.

"Anything to drive out the vampires. Guns, bullets, weapons, drugs, and operational forces."

Xenia frowned.

"But there's a condition."

As expected, the captain of the guard walked straight to Rian and extended his hand.

"Knight of Maha. I promise all support on the condition that you join this operation."

It was part of the recruitment process.

"I dislike complications. I plan to hunt vampires alone. It will benefit the kingdom."

The captain of the guard couldn't let Rian go.

"If that's the case, the support will be nullified. You know the vampires' strength. Are you saying you don't care if they die in vain?"

"Die in vain?"

Rian sneered.

"Do you think you're being rational?"

The captain of the guard didn't answer.

"If you can endure it, you're not truly angry. Don't judge them by ordinary standards."

The hunters' eyes sharpened.

Feeling their gaze, the captain of the guard realized his mistake but couldn't back down.

"You're making the captain of the royal guard look too foolish. I could forcibly detain you."

"I'm too stupid to know how to turn back."

Rian said as he sheathed his greatsword.

"So I can only move forward."

If they blocked his path, it meant the route from Kashan's end to Jive through the desert could be connected.

"I was wrong."

The hunters began to change their minds.

"It's not about weapons or numbers. I fought them even when I had nothing."

"Right. I can't work with people who set conditions. That's not what I need."

The hunters shouted in unison.

"We'll follow you. Lead us."

The decision was made, and Rian bowed lightly and descended the stairs.

"I understand your intentions."

King Meyer said.

"I made an unreasonable proposal. I officially approve the hunters' activities. So this time, support the royal palace as hunters."

From Jive's perspective, the vampires needed to be driven out as soon as possible.

"I've heard that special weapons are needed to fight vampires. Let's make a deal. If you share your knowledge, we'll mass-produce them with the palace's funds."

This time, Rian couldn't find an excuse to refuse.

'Xenia.'

The only thing holding him back was the promise he made to her before coming here.

Xenia met Rian’s eyes and pouted.

"The team name will be Genocide."

Smiling, Rian turned to Benoff.

"I'll take the hunters to the hideout first. We'll contact you with the meeting's outcomes through the chief."

"Alright. I'll be waiting."

It was best for Benoff, who had a connection with Rian, to take charge of the royal palace side.

The hunters who arrived at the Genocide hideout were impressed by the advanced technology.

"Wow, modified like this?"

Fowler said as he loaded a heavy gun with two barrels on the sides.

"Mega Gun Z-88. Gas-powered. Ergonomic design. There's nothing better for tearing vampire flesh."

With no need to worry about supplies, Fowler set up targets and demonstrated.

"Let's blast away!"

Though the vibration reduced accuracy, the speed of twelve silver bullets per second was powerful.

"And this is a shotgun..."

As he excitedly introduced the firearms, other hunters listened to explanations in their fields.

One hunter, examining the Anticell drug, said it was ten years ahead of its time.

Only three hunters specialized in metal weapons.

Xenia said,

"We can get Obscura B from the palace, but there's no need to change weapons."

For those who used metal weapons, the user's physical ability was more important than the weapon.

"I don't plan to change my familiar weapon. Just make spares for damage. Let's focus on planning."

Regardless of the country, most hunter organization leaders specialized in metal weapons.

A hunter wielding an axe said,

"I don't understand. How could we not track the Lord's location with a Silver Born?"

"I don't know. We searched Rodenin thoroughly but couldn't detect the Lord's presence."

A hunter wielding a spear said,

"You said a 2-kilometer radius? Then there's only one answer. They're hiding outside that radius."

Rian asked,

"Is that possible?"

"Underground. If they're 2 kilometers deep, even Xenia can't detect them."

"Ah."

"Relying solely on the Silver Born's ability was the blind spot. So, I have a method I usually use."

As Xenia turned, the spear-wielding hunter grinned.

"Hybrid hunting."

Even past midnight, the club house's interior was filled with loud music.

Genocide's first team, including Rian and Xenia, arrived in front of it.

'Club House, Blood Knight.' The name alone suggested a vampire den, but such places were common in Rodenin.

"Are there hybrids here?"

"If there are purebloods, there are hybrids. After investigating for a few days, this place seems the most likely."

Katain checked his syringe.

"It's definitely a blind spot. Even a Silver Born can't detect half-demons. But could there be a connection to purebloods?"

"Something is happening in the city. If it's related to the Lord, purebloods alone can't handle it."

The spear-wielding hunter seemed confident.

"Hybrids can reproduce, unlike purebloods. So their activities are more depraved. Be prepared."

Listening closely, they could hear the groans of many people mixed with the music.

"They don't die and can reproduce, so there's no taboo. Hybrids are like a kind of STD."

Fowler scolded,

"Hey, stop calling hybrids, hybrids."

Genocide used the term half-demon because Xenia wasn't a pureblood either.

"Ah, sorry. My mistake..."

The spear-wielding hunter apologized hastily, but Xenia shook her head and approached the club house.

"It doesn't matter."

Even if she was a hybrid.

"When I couldn't kill vampires, hating myself was my only solace."

That's what true anger is.

"I see. But things will be different from now on."

Feeling solemn at Xenia's words, the hunters checked their weapons and stared at the door.

"Shall we begin?"

As Rian opened the door, the pounding drum sounds escaped outside.

Arriving at the vampire hideout, Nike entered the main building, drawing the attention of the Almas.

"You called, great Jinma."

Ranked third in the Ten Lords, White Ghost Yacha Faust walked around the large hall, reading a book.

"Have you recovered?"

Nike bowed his head apologetically.

"I'm ready to kill anyone."

"Really? Even the Knight of Maha?"

Nike's eyes filled with killing intent.

It had taken him four days to recover after being struck by the law of the fist.

"There are no enemies in the darkness you bestowed upon me."

"You must be prepared for annihilation."

As Jinma's aura emanated, the numerous Almas hanging upside down from the ceiling trembled.

"Soon, the three Lords will gather in one place. When that happens, the world will be ours. However...

The only obstacle is...

"If the bloodline of Ogent does not exist."

"I do not understand, Jinma. No matter how strong he is, he cannot compare to the current Lords."

Of course, this included himself in the darkness.

"Do not underestimate humans. When they are weak, they seem endlessly fragile, but when they are strong, they grow beyond their limits."

"With all due respect, I have never seen a human surpass the power of a vampire."

"I have."

More than ten thousand years ago.

"Before I realized my incarnation... when a bat hung upside down in the cave of heaven, casually observing the world of light.

"One human fought against the king of giants."
