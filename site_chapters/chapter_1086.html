Chapter 1086: Solar War (4)

East Country Starting Point

In a temporary tent at the base of the Zeta Plateau, 600 meters above sea level, Shirone opened his eyes.

He was inside Mid Gear, undergoing rapid charging.

'It's been 20 minutes.'

The delay here is significant, which is why there's a penalty for dying.

As he stepped outside, numerous third-team guild members were tending to the revived users.

There were three starting points in total, but over 100 users were here alone.

"Yahweh2."

Destroyer707 and his team, who had awakened almost simultaneously in Mid Gear, approached.

Death Princess's right arm was missing.

"You died too. Sorry, we should have protected you better."

Thinking that all users were watching them, they forgot their original mission.

'We should have died instead of Yahweh2.'

Realizing that a momentary lapse in judgment had led to this result, they felt dejected.

"It's okay. If we made a mistake, the West Country did too. It's not about being perfect. It's about how quickly you control variables."

They lifted their heads.

"If the West Country makes two mistakes while the East Country makes only one, that's an advantage. Let's focus on winning. The East Country isn't so weak that our withdrawal would tip the scales."

Encouraged by Shirone's words, Destroyer707 and his team nodded.

"Yeah. We won't worry about the broadcast anymore."

Golden Wheel's third team approached with a cartload of supplies and began repairs.

Death Princess's right arm was reattached, and auxiliary items were distributed to others.

'It's a massive resource battle.'

Large guilds had multiple copies of the same parts to prepare for death penalties.

Armor-piercing rounds and grenades were replenished, and each member received a backup charger.

Strongest Code Name looked at Yahweh2, who was listening to Golden Wheel's third team.

'Is he okay?'

No enemy is perfect.

In the relative chaos of war, is there any statement more confidence-boosting?

'But it's not something just anyone can say. How many battlefields must one experience…'

To speak with such certainty?

Golden Wheel's third team said,

"The order is to return as quickly as possible. Use boosters to ascend the plateau."

How you ascend the 600-meter height greatly affects total power consumption.

'The initial strategy is rapid deployment.'

Since the momentum battle wasn't over, Shirone agreed and left the tent.

"Let's go!"

Dozens of users activated rocket engines simultaneously and ascended the plateau.

Yahweh2. Joining the battlefield.

As he communicated with the guild, Aegis responded.

It's a melee. If you can't arrive within 7 minutes, it's better to flank and hit the center.

Checking the satellite video, he saw prominent West Country users gathering.

At the center was Yolga's son.

'He's fighting well.'

Even under the combined attack of the Operator and Little Witch, he skillfully bought time.

'The Operator is outside. Tactically, the best move is to strike the Solar Palace.'

Fermi had left the choice to Shirone because he didn't know his current state.

Joining. Going turbo.

A response came shortly after.

Approved.

As Yahweh2's machine accelerated, the distance from Destroyer707 gradually increased.

"Wow, he's faster than us now."

Strongest Code Name chuckled.

"It was bound to happen. Let's do what we can. Win the Solar War."

Death Princess said,

"Yeah. Whether we're on the broadcast screen or not."

Exchanging glances, they nodded and activated their boosters.

Meanwhile, an all-too-familiar code name appeared in front of Shirone, heading to the battlefield's center.

"You bastard!"

West Country ranker Bishamonten's machine blocked Shirone like a steel wall.

As all kinds of firepower spewed out, Shirone quickly dodged.

'Damn, of all times.'

Aegis's requested time was now 3 minutes.

"Kahaha! What's wrong? In a hurry? But you'll die by my hand…"

Before he could finish, Destroyer707 rammed his machine into Bishamonten's side.

With a thud, the massive machine was pushed back, and Strongest Code Name and Death Princess joined in, firing.

"Yahweh2! Go! We'll handle this!"

"But…"

Even if the three of them combined forces, they couldn't match West Country ranker Bishamonten.

Destroyer707, clinging to the massive machine, met Shirone's eyes.

"We won't make the same mistake twice. Right?"

Even if they were bullet shields, it didn't matter.

Turning forward, Shirone withdrew his lingering gaze.

"Da Vinci."

As his shoulder opened and energy output increased, the scenery rapidly shifted.

Upon reaching the target point, over 30 rankers were in fierce combat.

'The Operator.'

Ignoring them, Shirone charged.

Aegis had died.

It must have been the 7 minutes she mentioned, and Yolga's son was facing the Operator and Little Witch.

The broadcast screen captured Yahweh2 again.

"He's here."

As users' attention focused, Little Witch's eyes widened sharply.

'I'll take him.'

Even in a simulation, Shirone dying to someone else was unpleasant.

As she charged at Yahweh2, Yolga's son grabbed her wrist.

A clash of outputs.

"Hnnng!"

As she was pulled by the engine's power, a clawed hand flew at her.

Dodging by tilting her head, Little Witch weaponized her left hand and attacked Yolga's son.

Boom! Boom! Boom!

As sparks flew and they distanced, Yolga's son maintained a defensive stance and said,

"Let's restrain ourselves. We're not the main characters here."

"You…"

As Little Witch began to speak, a horrific noise erupted from the Operator's direction.

"Collapse."

Turning to the familiar sound, she saw numerous rankers colliding in mid-air.

Yolga's son smiled bitterly.

"That's tough."

The legendary technique that always ensured the Operator's survival in critical situations.

'Well… the one who got it is a monster.'

The black sword was a crafted item.

To gather all the components of the 7-stage process, one would have to circle the High Gear world dozens of times.

In the rain of machine fragments, the Operator aimed at Yahweh2.

"I'll finish this for sure."

Having cleared the rankers with Collapse, she intended to show the difference in skill this time.

Shirone activated Michelangelo.

Everyone is fighting.

Even if it's not reality, the hearts of the friends who sacrificed for Shirone were real.

I am fighting too.

Finally, he felt like he was living in this world.

Therefore.

Resonance.

Shirone's senses merged into the array of codes forming the simulation.

Therefore.

He and the world were not different.

As the light sphere created by Michelangelo trembled, the entire landscape began to shake.

Users from both the East and West Countries stopped fighting, their faces filled with astonishment.

"What? What?"

On the broadcast screen, it looked like the sun.

Above Yahweh2's head floated a golden sphere over 10 meters in diameter.

"Ugh!"

The Operator urgently braked.

'What is that?'

Even as the original designer of High Gear, she had never imagined a photon of that size.

As Yahweh2 fired the flash, the Operator's AI output the attack information.

Barrage level, 879.

The direct trajectory, defined solely by speed, was chilling.

'Dodge…'

Before the thought finished, the flash struck directly, and the shockwave swept the area.

Boom!

The operator's meeting room analyzing the scene was silent.

Someone, feeling frustrated, snapped.

"What's the number? Tell me quickly."

Candy Cane Mark lit up.

"2.1 million force."

The atmosphere grew even more stifling, and the silence stretched longer than expected.

'It's not an illegal program. But Michelangelo shouldn't have this power. It means it can be used infinitely.'

A clear balance breaker.

'Should we take emergency measures?'

If the outcome of the Solar War hinged on Michelangelo alone, users would complain.

'But on the other hand…'

Patching during the Solar War could also cause problems later.

"Let's watch for now."

Gear Mark said.

"We approved the Operator's play to prepare for such unexpected situations."

Where they turned their gaze again, a clear crater had formed.

Solar War users were mesmerized by the screen.

"My goodness…"

Even in the video taken from altitude, the massive depression was visible to the naked eye.

"Phew."

At the center, the Operator knelt on one knee, her black sword embedded in the ground.

"She blocked it."

In that fleeting moment, what they saw was a massive expansion of a black magnetic field.

Fermi muttered.

"Did she use all remaining output for Collapse?"

Though separate from High Gear's power, the recharge time was much longer than Michelangelo's.

'Then this is the chance.'

Even without Collapse, the Operator was powerful, but at least the win rate was now realistic.

East Country users shouted.

"Go, Yahweh2! Defeat the Operator!"

With the cheers of a thousand in his heart, Shirone leaped into the crater.

The Operator's eyes lit up.

'Power is low.'

Having fought to the end without dying, her power was lower than other rankers.

Cleanly annihilating enemies and returning leisurely was her trademark, but now she faced an enemy.

'19%. I can hold out for now. If I destroy Yahweh2 and head to the Solar Palace…'

The next moment, she bit her lower lip and started running towards the Solar Palace.

The broadcast screen buzzed.

"What, what?"

The Operator was fleeing.

It was an unprecedented sight in High Gear history, and the shock was immense.

"Is it because of her undefeated record?"

Indeed, one loss would be painful.

But fleeing to protect that one loss was far from honorable.

Shirone chased the Operator.

'It's not about honor.'

The reason she kept her undefeated record…

'Because this place is everything. Because the world she escaped to must be real.'

She thought defeat meant death.

'How much must she hate going back.'

Shirone understood the wounds she bore but also knew.

Death isn't something so light.

'Resonance, Da Vinci.'

As the metal wings activated, Yahweh2's machine shot forward like a bullet.

The Operator gritted her teeth and ran.

'I won't die.'

This world is reality.

The emotions of her constantly fighting parents, the guilt of abandoning them, were mere illusions.

'I must not die. The moment I die… I'll realize.'

Where reality is, and who she truly is, knowing all along.

Her heart was anxious.

Even the machine she prided as the strongest in High Gear couldn't carry her heart to the Solar Palace.

And at that moment, something swiftly passed by and blocked her path.

'Yahweh2.'

In her rigid thoughts, the black sword swung, and Shirone's machine jumped through codes.

'Double jump?'

From far to near.

Dodging the black sword by a millimeter, Shirone activated Raphael and Da Vinci simultaneously.

Boom!

Yahweh2's rocket-like punch pierced the Operator's chest directly.

"Ugh…"

Her expression was terrified, almost too real for a simulation.

As the entire continent fell silent, Shirone, with his fist embedded, spoke.

"Operator."

Her eyes slowly moved.

"Now it's one win, one loss."

Seeing Yahweh2's smiling face, the broken High Gear began to beat rapidly.

Thump, thump.
