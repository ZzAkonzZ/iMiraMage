Chapter 635:

The moment Amy's fist connected with Nade's jaw, she knew it had landed perfectly.

'Got him!'

In that brief instant, she twisted her waist with all her might, causing Nade's head to snap back and his body to spin.

"Grrr!"

Just as Nade had unleashed a lifetime of rage, Amy's punch carried the weight of countless moments of determination.

"His magic power level…!"

The percentage on Elizabeth's screen dropped rapidly, as if water was being siphoned out.

Considering it was at 1.2 billion magicals, the speed was chilling.

When the percentage fell below 3%, it seemed like the end.

"Aaaargh!"

But Nade didn't lose consciousness.

Baikal, guessing the reason, showed emotion for the first time, slamming his knee in frustration.

"The reaction speed of electricity."

Amy's punch had delivered a shock, but Nade's reflexes twisted his body faster than the shockwave, nullifying the impact.

As Amy took a step forward, still in her punching stance, electricity surged from Nade's spinning body.

It was a brilliant move, and for a moment, there was a glimmer of hope, making Baikal bite his lip.

'It was nearly perfect. Be proud, Amy!'

It was a counter that surpassed human senses, but if it had gone just a bit deeper…

If she had extended just 0.1 millimeters more, she might have achieved the unprecedented feat of defeating the magic-transformed monster.

"Grrr!"

As Nade roared and released powerful electricity, Amy sighed.

'Get a grip, idiot.'

At the same time, a massive anti-magic force struck her mind.

-Karmis Amy. Final graduation rank: 4th. Remaining participants: 3.

Amy collapsed forward, and Nade, having released the electricity, fell beside her, looking up at the sky.

But Nade's mental strength was also weakened.

"What's his magic power level?"

"4 million magicals."

"It's dropped significantly. He was on the verge of losing consciousness."

"Yes. But even in a dazed state, having 4 million magicals is abnormal."

Shirone and Iruki, panting from different positions, had the same thought as they looked at the fallen Nade.

'Just sleep. Please, just sleep.'

Then blue electricity enveloped Nade's body.

As he regained consciousness, his magic transformation bloomed, and the rising numbers were grotesque.

"20 million, 50 million, it's surpassing 100 million magicals."

"…Something's off."

The electricity surrounding Nade shot up into the sky, forming a giant human shape.

Kaaaang!

The sound waves created by the electricity shook the entire colosseum, and everyone covered their ears.

"Grr! What is this!"

-Die! Disappear!

"Is his consciousness manifesting as magic power?"

With each shout, the expanding electric giant looked like a thunder god, exuding overwhelming presence.

-I…

The thunder god crossed its arms and shouted with the loudest voice.

-What did I do wrong!

Electric bundles spread in all directions, and a transparent wall seemed to form at the edge of the 2000 system's boundary, rubbing against the air.

"This… this is…!"

The pale-faced audience couldn't even blink as they watched the blue-white currents spreading before them.

Each one bore Nade's face, twisted in agony.

-Answer me! Why must I be unhappy? Why do I have to bear everything?

As Bolum looked at his wife, who was staring at Nade's electric face, he saw the same anger reflected in her.

-Answer me!

As the electricity concentrated and a giant mouth opened to release a voice, Teria shouted,

"Because you're pathetic!"

The audience turned to look at her.

"Dear."

Bolum tried to stop her, but only death could halt her rage.

"What kind of tantrum is this! I did my best too! I gave you everything you asked for! I sent you to magic school with what little we had! That's why you could take the exam!"

-I didn't want success!

"Don't talk nonsense! What parent wants their child to fail! Does that annoy you so much? If you pitied me, how could you do this!"

The unexpected argument between mother and son made the audience murmur.

"The West family. I heard they fell, but they're really broken."

"It's their family matter. Stay out of it."

Teria, losing her composure, shouted,

"Do you think you're the only one suffering? I don't want to live either! I'm sick of taking care of you and your father!"

-Why… did you abandon me?

Compared to the previous thunder, it was a small rumble, but Teria fell silent for the first time.

"That was… in that situation…"

-You didn't even apologize.

"Was it my fault? I… because of that…"

-Should I tell you what happened that day?

Teria's body trembled.

Despite seeing the numerous burn scars on Nade's body, she had never asked what had happened.

"I don't want to hear it. It's already over."

Electricity flowed like tears from Nade's face on the transparent wall.

-I think so too. It's over and can't be undone. So…

As the face receded, the electricity returned to Nade.

-Watch closely from there, see what kind of monster I've become.

Nade, who had been hanging limply under the thunder god's form, opened his eyes.

"Shirone. Iruki."

The voice came from Nade, not the thunder god.

"I'm sorry, but I have to win."

Despite controlling his magic transformation, Nade's face remained cold.

"I told you to do that from the start."

Shirone and Iruki weren't upset.

Whether it was betrayal or not, if it was a decision Nade made on his own, they could accept it as part of the graduation exam.

"Magic power level: 240 million."

Though it was higher than Ataraxia's physical system, it was a more human number compared to his previous rampage.

"The strongest instinct and a slightly less strong reason. Which do you think is worse to face?"

Elizabeth felt no need to answer.

Shirone, Iruki, and Nade slowly moved towards the center of the colosseum.

A triangular formation.

A perfect balance where no one could act first, making the battlefield's atmosphere heavy.

"Hey, come to think of it, the final three…"

Mark said.

"They're from the Supernatural Psychic Science Research Club, right?"

"Oh, they are. First to third place."

The teachers had mixed feelings.

"Those troublemakers."

Siena grumbled, recalling the trio's antics from their advanced class days.

"They never let things go smoothly until the end."

"They were unique kids. Eccentric, but pure. They gave me headaches, but thinking about them leaving makes me sad."

Etella's words made Siena smile bitterly.

"…They are students I'll never forget."

After circling the massive colosseum, the three established their strategies.

'Ataraxia.'

As the colorful magic circle formed before Shirone, the scouts murmured.

"He's going for the win right away."

"Probably. If they fight with future plans in mind, they'll be eliminated. They have to play their strongest cards."

'Nuclear Fusion.'

At the same time, Iruki began to draw up a detonation equation of unprecedented power.

The best outcome was to eliminate both opponents simultaneously.

For that, an explosion that could dominate the entire colosseum was needed.

'Watch closely, see what your broken son can do.'

Nade's thunder god compressed into a sphere, emitting white lightning.

He intended to destroy the graduation exam, the mages, the wealth and glory—everything.

And he would tell Teria.

Everything you could have had is now gone forever.

As the sphere of lightning floated like a divine object, the audience gulped.

"The power is unimaginable. Someone will be eliminated here."

Iruki glanced at Shirone.

'It's tricky. Should I detonate first?'

But if he did, Shirone and Nade's magic would focus on him.

'The best outcome is Nade attacking Shirone, and I take them both out. Then it's over.'

As soon as he finished thinking, Nade's lightning split into two.

"Seriously."

Iruki smirked.

'Being too close is a problem.'

Shirone also anticipated Nade's move.

'Good. If Nade targets Iruki, I'll attack Iruki too. Then I'll dodge Nade's attack.'

Nade's lightning speed was unimaginable, but Time Explosion could dodge it.

'Now it's about timing.'

Who would move first?

Before he finished thinking, Nade's nature twisted.

'Nade!'

As the other two reacted, both bolts of lightning flew at Shirone.

'Damn, it didn't go as planned.'

Pretending to target both but actually aiming to eliminate Shirone first.

The danger of a less strong reason.

'Then…!'

The massive beam passing through Ataraxia swallowed the lightning and rotated towards Iruki. As expected, Nade appeared before Iruki, casting Spark Magic.

'Heh, fool. Don't I know you? Thanks to you…'

Iruki's lips curled into a grin.

'It was a fun school life!'

Nuclear Fusion!

A massive explosion erupted from the colosseum's center, sweeping through the entire colosseum.

Albino, judging the power by the brightness, muttered in a daze.

"Is he… an idiot?"

In the psychological battle, Iruki's final choice was to blow everything up with an explosion even he couldn't handle.

'A human weapon indeed.'

As new explosions continued to form at the blast's center, the scouts could only watch the scene filling the 2000 system's barrier.

"In reality, they'd all be dead."

As the explosion, like a sun before their eyes, disappeared, the audience's gaze wandered over the colosseum.

All three lay motionless on the ground, causing murmurs to ripple like waves.

"What… what happened?"

-Mercodine Iruki. Final graduation rank: 3rd. Remaining participants: 2.

As the 2000 system's announcement played, Nade staggered to his feet.

"Brute."

Iruki looked up at the sky and said,

"That's a matter of perspective. You can blow up too. No one will say anything here."

If not for his magic power level over 200 million, Nade would have been lying next to Iruki.

"…I will."

As Nade moved away, Iruki, reflecting on 19 years of life, smiled contentedly.

"Ah, refreshing."

The audience held their breath, but the 2000 system's announcement didn't come.

"He amplified the mass wave to cancel the explosion. He planned to defend and attack simultaneously."

"Ugh, my head."

The audience's gaze focused on one spot.

"I knew it, but…"

Slowly getting up, Shirone rubbed his shoulder and grumbled.

"You guys are really too much."
