Chapter 1073: The Only Chance (3)

Destroy-02

In the urban jungle of skeletal buildings, creatures ranging from level 20 to 80 inhabit the area.

Thus, Destroy-02 is not only a place for rapid leveling but also a battleground where East and West factions clash.

"Haha! You idiot! Why? Are you going to show off like last time?"

From Chaos1MinuteBefore to Chaos5MinutesBefore, five West Country users surrounded Strongest Codename, who was equipped with the Hundred General set.

"Damn it! These guys…!"

Strongest Codename tried to get up, but Chaos3MinutesBefore's barrage blew off his left leg.

"Ugh!"

The Chaos gang was dragging out the time, enjoying themselves instead of finishing off Strongest Codename.

Hostile actions between East and West were naturally accepted, so there was no need to be cautious.

'The Hundred General set is tempting too.'

There was a rumor that the more parts you destroyed, the more loot you got upon death.

It was like a superstition, but from Strongest Codename's perspective, it wasn't necessarily bad.

"You won't be so arrogant for long. Once my friends arrive, you guys…"

Chaos5MinutesBefore burst into laughter.

"Haha! Do you think a bunch of level 10s can beat us? Right?"

Chaos1MinuteBefore pondered.

He was a user equipped with the Amara integrated type, known for its efficiency at level 70.

With a round body like an egg and stubby arms, the firepower of its machine gun, firing seven rounds per second, was outstanding.

'He seems to have something up his sleeve. Is there a large guild backing him?'

West Country users could only confirm the codenames of East Country users.

Rankers could get detailed information through the rankings, but that was out of reach for them.

"He's bluffing. Crush him."

In Destroy-02, no one could surpass the Amara integrated type.

Chaos3MinutesBefore said, "Let's destroy his limbs first. Leave only his face and torso."

"Haha! I like that."

Still angry from his encounter with Yahweh2, Chaos5MinutesBefore aimed his bolt gun.

"I'll make you crawl like a bug."

Just as the gun was about to fire, Yahweh2's voice echoed from the other side of the city.

"Stop!"

Chaos5MinutesBefore turned his head.

"Haha! Finally, the hero arrives! Where's your sense of justice…"

Silence fell.

The codenames were clear, but their machines looked almost like AI.

"…What? They came with strange parts."

Their forms exuded the aura of predators among insects.

"Is that…?"

Their slender limbs handled tons of weight like feathers, the pinnacle of mechanical engineering.

"Grim Reaper."

It was a product of Metal Genome, recognized as a masterpiece by intermediate users over level 200.

"Is it fake? How can they all be equipped with Grim Reapers?"

Seven times more expensive than the Amara integrated type, its cost-effectiveness was the worst.

'Equipping that at level 20… they become literal reapers.'

"Crazy. They bought Metal Genome to catch us? What will they wear at level 70?"

"Wait. Hold on."

Chaos1MinuteBefore had just seen new news on Atogram's free bulletin board.

Title: The King of Electronic Warfare, Aegis, Appears in Atogram!

Clicking the link, he found over 200 recorded videos taken by users.

Below a photo of Aegis bowing to Yahweh2, detailed information was attached.

Thus, this reporter tracked the movements of Yahweh2's group. As a result…

Parts shop. Who is the manager? Sweeping up goods.

Presumed VIP.

Quickly skimming the article, Chaos1MinuteBefore spoke in a modulated voice.

"They're from Golden Wheel."

"What?"

The Chaos gang looked at Yahweh2.

"Check the Atogram bulletin board. It's a big deal right now."

Their pupils moved rapidly.

"Yeah, it's true."

Golden Wheel was the only guild in East Country that could counter the Ascension Guild of West Country.

"…This sucks."

Chaos1MinuteBefore made a decision.

"Let's back off. There's no benefit in messing with Golden Wheel. Fighting them will only embarrass us."

Of course, but Chaos5MinutesBefore had already suffered too much.

"It's not over yet."

He aimed at the back of Strongest Codename's head and shouted.

"Give me back my bolt gun! If you hand it over, I'll let him go!"

Destroyer707 snorted.

"You're talking nonsense! Do you think that's a threat? Go ahead and kill him!"

The matter was already settled through communication.

"Damn it! This is driving me crazy! Can't we do something? Even if I have to clean my codename, I won't let them…"

Just then, Shirone shouted.

"Let my friend go! If you send him over safely, I'll forget everything that happened."

"Huh?"

The Chaos gang was more surprised than Destroyer707's group.

'What nonsense is this?'

If he died, he could just start over.

Of course, there was a chance of losing parts upon death, and the Hundred General set was quite expensive…

'But they're from Golden Wheel.'

Would those covered in Metal Genome's Grim Reaper care about the Hundred General set?

Destroyer707 looked at Shirone.

"Hey, what are you doing? Strongest Codename can just die. It'll drive them crazy."

"Yeah, probably. But…"

Shirone looked at Strongest Codename, lying with a broken left leg.

"Their misfortune doesn't make me happy."

"I don't want to see Strongest Codename die. Sure, he can start over, and there will be times to use the system, but dying feels bad, doesn't it?"

Even in a virtual world.

"If there's a chance to save him, I don't want to give up. Winning without anyone dying is the best. Their feelings don't matter to us."

Destroyer707 gaped.

'He's really strange.'

But if there was a way to avoid Strongest Codename's death, it couldn't be better.

"So, what are you going to do?"

"Leave it to me."

What he realized at Mid Gear.

If this simulation also had the relativity of time in the world of electricity, 'I can do it.'

As Shirone pushed his concentration to the limit, Chaos2MinutesBefore muttered.

"What's with them? They don't seem to want to fight."

"Heh, now I get it."

Chaos5MinutesBefore grinned wickedly.

"The honor of Golden Wheel. What happens when a guild member gets wrecked by nobodies like us? If we record it and post it on the bulletin board, it'll cause a huge uproar."

"Oh? You're right. The West Country will mock them endlessly. We'll be heroes."

"Exactly. If things go south, we can just clean our codenames. We win this fight."

Chaos5MinutesBefore shouted, "Hey! Give me back my bolt gun! Or bring me a million galaxies! Then I'll let this guy go!"

Destroyer707 shouted back, "Idiot! Why would we give you a million galaxies?"

"Fine? Then I'll break all his limbs! I'll record it and spread it around, okay?"

"Damn! Now that you mention it…"

There was a political issue at hand.

"Now."

As Shirone, who had been intently watching the front, took a step forward, the Chaos gang shouted.

He stopped again, but Shirone's senses had already penetrated deep into the world.

'The mind is the world.'

Like a bat sensing its surroundings, he began extracting signals embedded in the scenery.

'I can feel it.'

Even the gaps were a torrent of information coded into electrical signals.

Shirone jumped between the codes.

"Choeni Bardo."

It was a kind of memory jump, akin to traveling through a wormhole in reality.

"Huh?"

As Yahweh2 suddenly disappeared, Chaos5MinutesBefore manipulated his AI frantically.

"Behind! Behind!"

Turning at his comrade's terrified scream, he saw Yahweh2 already holding Strongest Codename.

"You… you…"

Click! Click!

Shirone's right upper arm opened, and a claw-like output device emerged.

'Energy beam.'

A thick beam struck the solar plexus, reducing the armor's durability from 500 to 400 to 300.

In two seconds, the armor would burn out.

"Ahhh!"

As Chaos5MinutesBefore twisted and fell, his comrades raised their guns and fired.

"Kill him!"

Gunfire erupted, and Shirone, carrying Strongest Codename, kicked off the ground and sped away.

"Damn! How is he so fast?"

Like a black wasp in flight, only a dark blur was visible, and sparks flew harmlessly to the ground.

Shirone's friends joined the fight.

"Alright! Let's go!"

Chaos2MinutesBefore and Chaos3MinutesBefore were burned by energy beams.

"Ahhh! This is annoying!"

Despite the output limits based on level, the durability of Metal Genome was evident.

"Haha! This is awesome!"

Durability 5,687/7,000.

No matter how much they were hit, the armor showed no signs of breaking, and in the meantime, Chaos4MinutesBefore fell.

"You bastards!"

The Amara integrated type aimed forward, its motor roaring as it unleashed a barrage of bullets.

Ratatat!

As Destroyer707 rolled on the ground, sparks covered his armor, reducing durability to 4,544.

Hey, you okay?

Death Princess communicated.

Yeah. These parts are good up to level 60. As long as I don't take direct hits, I'm fine.

"Come on! Bring it on! You bastards!"

Amid the frenzied gunfire, fragments flew, and in the hazy view, Yahweh2 emerged.

"I'll handle this!"

Chaos1MinuteBefore also spotted Yahweh2.

"That guy…"

Approaching as if jumping through space, even the AI couldn't track him.

'He's using a strange option.'

Like a black wasp hunting, Shirone circled the Amara integrated type quickly.

'He jumps through space. But he can't freely choose his position.'

He moved along the codes.

"Here!"

Predicting the movement path, Chaos1MinuteBefore twisted 60 degrees and fired.

'Got him!'

Yahweh2 dodged between the bullets, closing in.

"What the hell? Damn…!"

Moving nimbly in the slowed time, Shirone activated the selective option.

'Turbo.'

Receiving the doubled output, he charged forward, raising his left hand.

'If I take him down, we win.'

A flat output device emerged, and an energy cutter, shaped like a blade, activated.

Chaos1MinuteBefore screamed in terror.

"Ahhh!"

Sparks flew from the Grim Reaper's armor, and its durability rapidly decreased.

'6,871, 6,123, 5,231…'

Shirone's AI approved the penetration judgment as he deflected the barrage with his body.

'Now!'

The energy cutter pierced the Amara integrated type's armor, but the Grim Reaper's left arm was also shattered.

With a loud crash, the over one-ton machine tilted backward.

"Huh? Oh no?"

Specialized for attack, its low center of gravity made it hard to get up once down.

Thud!

As the ground shook and the gunfire ceased, the half-destroyed Chaos gang looked bewildered.

"Huff, huff."

With his left arm torn off, Yahweh2 stood on Chaos1MinuteBefore's stomach, aiming his gun.

"Surrender."

The Amara integrated type struggled briefly before lowering its output in surrender.

Vroom.

As the intense noise subsided, a voice was heard.

"…I surrender."

"Apologize for attacking Death Princess. And don't interfere with us again."

"I wasn't planning to. If you have apologies to make, do it. We lost."

"Tch."

Chaos5MinutesBefore turned his head.

"Sorry. Is that enough?"

His machine, damaged in the battle, had only half its face and torso left.
