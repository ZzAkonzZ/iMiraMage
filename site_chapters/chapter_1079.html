Chapter 1079: Fully Armed (1)

Ruby Mix and Yagwi, realizing it was no longer a joke, hesitated and stepped back.

"How… how did you do that?"

Of course, they knew.

A sudden disconnection during an attack meant an accident had occurred in reality.

But the problem was how the little witch had found Taedaecheon in the real world.

'Even the operators don't disclose users' personal information. No, there's not even personal information to disclose. This is Under Coder.'

While their minds were racing, the little witch received a long-distance communication.

Your Highness, I've woken him up. Should I kill him?

It was code name 987392.

A soldier of Kashan, secretly lurking in High Gear, responsible for relaying communications.

No, just scare him a bit. But his attitude should be different when he reconnects.

Understood.

The communication was cut off.

'I didn't expect to use this method.' Uorin, who had joined the Ascension Guild, had placed surveillance on the key figures of High Gear.

It wasn't out of any ulterior motive, just something easy to do.

'The situation is different now.'

To enjoy the Solar Battle with Shirone, she still had to stay with the Ascension Guild.

At that moment, the door opened, and Taedaecheon entered.

If disconnected within the guild, the starting point was each person's Mid Gear.

Ruby Mix and Yagwi quietly made way for Taedaecheon to approach the little witch.

His knees slowly bent.

"I was wrong…"

"Ah, it's fine."

Uorin extended her hand.

"Why did you do that? We were getting along fine, and now it's awkward. I don't have any ill feelings towards you."

Taedaecheon, straightening his knees again, asked.

"What should I do?"

"Just continue as you were. Just don't interfere with what I'm doing. Anyway, I plan to win the Solar Battle too."

"Understood."

"Speak casually. It's awkward otherwise."

"Okay."

Ruby Mix and Yagwi just watched.

'Taedaecheon is the most belligerent in the Ascension Guild, what happened to him outside?'

The little witch pointed her finger.

The intensity of the pressure was different from before, and the guild members' upper bodies flinched.

"Phew."

She said.

"Get out."

Those who had been unable to move for a while slowly turned around and left in a line.

The little witch looked up at the ceiling.

"I have to win."

If Shirone asked for help, she would kill everyone in High Gear to help.

'That won't happen.'

There was nothing to stop her, who had endured eons just to possess Shirone.

'Even if it means selling my soul to the devil.'

She burst into laughter and asked herself in a self-deprecating voice.

"Do I even have a soul?"

Democracy, midnight.

High Gear's charging is complete.

The door of Mid Gear opened.

Slowly opening his eyes, Shirone looked at the now darkened interior of the charging station.

It had only been a fleeting moment, but it felt like he had returned from a long dream.

'Brain in a vat.'

Of course, there were no memories.

Turning his head, she saw the mafia group still guarding nearby.

Sona approached.

"Are you done? Then let's get going."

Shirone, who had exited Mid Gear, turned on the selective option and asked.

"Where do we go to get Donatello?"

"The Moon's Womb. It's also called Moon Island. It's an island in the sea, so you'll need a booster. Equipping the constant speed mode in the selective option will help."

Skillfully changing the options, Shirone left the charging station to find many people gathered.

They had gathered after seeing the bulletin board.

"Wow! It's really Michelangelo!"

Interest was at its peak as it was a legendary item rarely seen even in videos.

A reporter appeared without fail.

"Hit the Hit, a private reporter from the Western Kingdom! As far as I know, you're currently level 100, so why did you equip Michelangelo? Wasn't it a waste?"

The mafia intervened.

"Tch! Who says otherwise? Anyway, we just need to get paid. We'll upload the verification video, so watch it."

It was to ensure that Golden Wheel couldn't change their words later, but the reporter didn't care.

What mattered now was Yahweh2's stance.

"Please say a few words. All High Gear users are focused on Yahweh2's actions."

"Uh…"

As Shirone began to speak, the murmuring crowd fell silent as if by magic.

"I just equipped it without much thought. I got it from hunting, so I planned to use it."

The reporter was dumbfounded by the bland answer.

"More importantly, I have something to say to the operator."

"Of course!"

With the following words, the atmosphere heated up again, and the reporter checked the recording status once more.

"To all the users watching the news, it seems Yahweh2 is about to make a shocking declaration."

It was a well-known fact that Gold Wheel and Ascension were involved in Yahweh2's actions.

Moreover, it was shocking that an Eastern user had obtained Michelangelo in a Western hunting ground.

"You can say anything. All High Gear users are supporting you. Is it a declaration for the Sun Battle? Or a personal challenge?"

"When I first came here, I had a communication with the operator. We had a bit of a fight. Said some bad things."

People murmured.

"Communicated with the operator?"

Countless stories were generated in their minds, gradually coming together.

The reporter's shoulders trembled.

'Personal grudge. That's why they joined Golden Wheel. For revenge! Now it all makes sense.'

Today's top news was undoubtedly his.

"But as I played, I began to understand the operator to some extent. The reason for his confidence, and why he was angry with me."

"Huh?"

"Of course, there are still beliefs I can't compromise on, but I want to take this opportunity to correct a misunderstanding. Operator, I'm sorry for that time."

Shirone looked straight at the reporter as if the operator were right in front of him.

"I'm having fun. You really… created an amazing world."

People's expressions turned blank.

"That's all. I'm in a hurry, so…"

As Shirone left, the mafia group quickly followed. The reporter, who had been standing there for a while, snapped back to reality and gave a closing comment.

"This is shocking in another way. Yahweh2 apologized to the operator. What could have happened between them? And how will the operator respond to Yahweh2's statement…"

Ascension Guild.

How will the operator respond to Yahweh2's statement? With the relationship between the West Country and the East Country in turmoil, the Solar War is approaching.

The operator was sitting on the runway, listening to the news with her wrist resting on her bent knee, her eyes flashing.

This is Hit the Hit, and I will continue to track the situation between the two guilds and report back to you. This concludes today's emergency news.

The light in her eyes dimmed as if it were fading away.

After a moment of thought, she stood up and walked to the end of the runway.

She muttered softly.

"Shirone."

The person who inspired Michelangelo.

Her figure, revealing a slender neck, disappeared as if evaporating into the night sky.

A delicate silhouette floated in the center of the moon.

West Sea.

The mafia group, flying over the sea with rocket engines ignited, was silent.

Their feelings were complicated.

'We're going, but what are the chances of finding Donatello? This could be a wild goose chase.'

The mafia approached Shirone.

"Let's be clear. We'll look around for just one day, and if we don't find it, we'll contact Golden Wheel to settle the Michelan first."

"Got it."

Shirone nodded obediently.

The mafia group was puzzled by his easy acceptance.

"Really? This is being recorded."

"I said I got it."

If there were no mistakes, it would end in one go.

'It's not a perfect random number.'

Getting the item as the designer intended meant there was a pattern.

'Understanding the system of this world.'

In reality, this is called enlightenment, usually considered the state of Buddha.

'Here, it would be becoming a programmer.'

In any case, if it wasn't as random as Havitz, Shirone could read it.

"We're almost there."

Despite using the constant speed option, the power dropped to 70% upon reaching Moon Island.

"Wow."

But it was truly beautiful.

"Isn't it pretty? Even if you don't catch Maginus, users gather here for fireworks."

It was a ring-shaped island with a freshwater lake in the center and a giant moon in the sky.

"The Moon's Womb."

Hence, Moon Island.

"The hunting ground is underground, beneath that lake. Once you go under the lake, you're cut off from the outside like in Ergos' sanctuary, so you need to set the selective option here."

After discussing the options, they turned off the boosters and landed near the freshwater lake.

Would you like to start the Maginus event?

Rumble!

The ground shook, and the center of the lake split open, revealing a deep chasm.

"What happens if we go inside?"

Sona answered.

"An ice-made space appears. Maginus moves by freezing the sea to create tunnels."

Having roughly heard the information, Shirone was the first to fly into the chasm.

The event is starting.

How deep did they fall?

They had already passed the thickness of the island, indicating they were deep in the sea.

"Creature, why do you reject eternal life!"

Suddenly, the surroundings brightened, revealing a scene like being trapped inside a diamond.

Marine creatures were moving beyond the walls.

"Kiaaa!"

A giant machine resembling an eel opened its jaws, and small fish-like machines quickly darted out.

"Shoot! If you miss even once, it's over!"

While the mafia group caught the small fish, Maginus turned and pierced through the ice wall.

"Follow it!"

A massive amount of seawater rushed in but quickly froze due to the internal cold.

'Ah, so this is how it works.'

Maginus' specialty was spraying cold air, creating ice tunnels in the sea to kill users.

'Its only attack ability is physical. But this…'

Following the complex ant-nest-like tunnels at high speed was dizzying.

"Be careful. If you deviate from the route, it's just the sea. The pressure will crush your machine. You must enter the tunnel."

The effective distance with Maginus, who created complex tunnels like knots, was only 200 meters.

With seawater rushing in from behind and the weakened ice walls continuously breaking, the power gradually decreased.

"Ah, I can't hit it!"

Especially for the low-level Shirone, the power dropped below 30%.

'There's no time.'

After analyzing Maginus' pattern, the conclusion was that there was only one last chance.

'Here.'

Maginus, bypassing the deep sea in a figure-eight shape, charged towards the tunnel where Shirone was.

'It's trying to break through.'

It was a strategy to shorten the effective distance by breaking its own tunnel.

"Dodge! If you collide, you'll die!"

The mafia group shouted, but Shirone now had a weapon to fight.

"Michelangelo."

As the lens on his palm activated, particles of light began to compress at an incredible speed.

'This isn't enough.'

Recalling the senses of reality, Shirone increased his concentration, and the sphere of light grew explosively.

The mafia group was shocked.

"Th-that…!"

With a light large enough to cover a person's torso, Shirone turned.

At the same time, Maginus broke through the ice wall and charged.

"Kiaaa!"

"Come."

Donatello.
