Chapter 906: Face to Face (3)

"Time remaining until purification: 65,400 hours."

It had been an almost eternal period since Goffin fell into hell.

'Such an immense amount of time.'

Shirone felt dizzy.

If Omega hadn't been in thousandths, experiencing Goffin's time might have driven him mad.

Goffin muttered,

"Time flies."

His body was emaciated, and his face was gaunt with sunken eyes.

But his mind remained intact, and that fact alone gave Shirone chills.

'Although 600 quintillion hours haven't actually passed.'

Time was a matter of perception, and hell was a place where that perception was maximized.

'Intense sensations accelerate the flow of time.'

In other words, the more intense Goffin's suffering, the shorter the purification time.

'That's the nature of time.'

Shirone recalled disassembling moments of time infinitely in the state of non-form.

The bizarre signals he saw when he delved deeper beyond each 0.666 seconds.

'Those must be special signals affecting the relativity of time and sensation.'

It was the truth of time.

'Ultimately, what the creator of this world wants is not for evil to be punished but for efficient data purification.'

Shirone's eyes filled with sorrow.

'Is God indifferent?'

With the same question in mind, Goffin reached the end of hell, blocked by a black wall.

Staring intently at it, the darkness of the wall seemed to move as if alive.

"The end of space."

Though called a wall, it was actually the entrance to a void world.

It was also the final gate where emotions from the underworld reached after purification.

'Ozente Guy.'

Shirone guessed what fate awaited Rian's older brother, who had passed on his link and disappeared.

'He went to an eternal void world, but it might not be as terrible as it seems.'

In reality, Jin Seong-eum would be wandering hell without even a chance for purification.

"The infinite wall."

Hearing a voice behind him, Goffin turned to see an angel with black wings like a shadow approaching.

Recognizing Lucifer, the lord of hell, Goffin raised his hand with a rare smile.

"Are you here to see me off?"

After fighting fiercely for over 600 quintillion hours, they no longer felt like strangers.

But Lucifer ignored Goffin's joke and turned his gaze to the infinite wall.

"Why are you in such a hurry? You only have 65,000 hours left until purification."

"I can't stand being here any longer."

Of course, that was true.

But it wasn't something expected from Goffin, who had endured 600 quintillion hours calmly.

Lucifer asked,

"Are you still clinging to false hope?"

Since falling into hell, Goffin had searched for a way back to reality but found it impossible.

Unlike traversing through dimensions via astral projection, those subject to purification couldn't escape the underworld.

'That's how this world is made.'

Goffin looked back at the infinite wall.

"Hope is a good thing. If I break through this void realm, I can return to reality. Then even hell would have to go on strike."

From Lucifer's perspective, it was madness.

"Why are you so obsessed with reality? Hell has already been devastated because of you. You could live happily here."

"Yes, that's true. I've spent a lot of time here. It must have been an incredibly long time. But looking back..."

Goffin shrugged his shoulders.

"Even that eternal time feels like a moment." "Isn't that right? Time seems to flow endlessly, but when you reach the end of the future..."

When only the past remains,

"All memories become just one feeling contained in zero seconds. How long you've lived doesn't matter."

Therefore,

"Beings that can't transcend infinity are illusions. Me, you, and your sibling..."

Goffin pointed at Lucifer.

"Ikael too."

Originally there were nine archangels.

Eight concepts forming the world and one opposing concept.

The archangel of chaos, Lucifer.

"Of course Anke Ra didn't create you. You weren't born. You're negative energy belonging to nonexistent time and events."

Outside the law.

"In this vast cycle, you and angels are number two in the law. When angels' concepts weaken, chaos strengthens..."

Goffin winked as he spoke,

"But that doesn't mean you can meet Ikael."

Lucifer wrinkled his nose at Goffin's piercing words but knew arguing would only hurt himself.

"So you're gambling on breaking through the infinite wall? If you fail, you'll face eternal annihilation."

"I don't care."

Goffin's eyes turned serious.

"Even after 600 quintillion hours pass, all that remains is emptiness. If God is truly indifferent, that's fine too. It means I can become God."

Unlike his comrades who chose reincarnation, he remained as his signal for this reason.

Goffin walked towards the wall,

"Shall we get going?"

He would break infinity.

"Wait."

At Lucifer's words, Goffin turned around,

"It's not like we're saying goodbye forever."

Lucifer shivered at Goffin's ability to smile even while heading towards annihilation,

"Do you really think such a being can exist? A God who loves this world instead of being indifferent?"

"Hmm... It's difficult because that's not what God is. But if I were God..."

Looking up at the ceiling Goffin said,

"Wouldn't it be nice to plant some hope? Even if it's just one signal."

Lucifer remained silent for a long time,

A Messiah...

If he could really achieve that...

"I will send you back to reality."

Surprised by Lucifer's unexpected words Goffin raised an eyebrow playfully,

"Oh? That took quite some time. How did you hold back all this while?"

"It's not about methods. There's no way to stop purification. But because of that I can send you."

Goffin realized,

"No way... You..."

“Yes. As long as purification happens it’s fine. I’ll take your place in annihilation since no one else in hell can replace your data.”

"Hmm."

Resting his chin on his hand Goffin asked,

"You know what kind of pain you're talking about right? It's different from what I endured."

"In exchange... there's a condition."

Goffin chuckled,

"I figured."

"If you truly want to give meaning to this empty world, share that meaning with us too."

It can't just end as mere signals...

"I understand your sentiment."

Goffin showed a troubled expression,

"You’re different from humans. No links exist between us. You're just part of this world's system."

"Ikael."

Lucifer spoke firmly,

“Give her meaning not as part of a system but as beings like yourselves. That’s my condition.”

“Ikael…”

Of all beings why her?

“I thought you hated her?”

In fact, Ikael, who represented the beginning of the law, and Lucifer, who represented chaos outside the law, had never met.

Like how existence and non-existence cannot merge.

But because of this, Lucifer hated and envied Ikael more than anyone.

He still remembered.

The moment of creation, when the archangel Ikael was born smiling amidst a brilliant explosion.

The first emotion he felt then was,

'Why am I...

...born so hideously?'

Through the early stages of Omega, Shirone knew how deep Lucifer's resentment ran.

'Yin and Yang.'

Two extremes that split at birth.

Coming out of his recollection, Lucifer spoke with a hollow feeling.

"I was jealous. Even though we were born in the same place, why am I so hideous?"

It wasn't about appearance; it was a matter of concept.

"Why do I hate Ikael? Why do I think I'm hideous and Ikael is beautiful?"

"So did you realize?"

"Maybe."

Fighting with Goffin for over 600 quintillion hours had changed Lucifer's heart too.

"Because I couldn't have her."

Lucifer smiled sadly.

"All I could do was hate. But not anymore. If everything is empty..."

If emotions are just signals too,

"At least let my last act be love. Love is better than hate. Isn't it?"

Even though they referred to each other the same way, Lucifer saw beauty in Ikael.

Shirone doubted his senses.

'Love?'

Hearing the word love from the lord of hell?

'Ah...'

Suddenly, he remembered Demon Commander Igor's last words.

'This was it.' - Not all demons lack repentance.

Goffin asked,

"What will happen to hell?"

"Just because I'm gone doesn't mean demons will disappear. The control will just shift to humans. I'm curious what kind of hell they'll create..."

Lucifer shrugged.

"If most humans are like you, living here won't be so bad."

"What if humans fall?"

"Hmph, you don't need to worry about hell when you're going back to reality. A human-made lord will rule this world. Let's call him Satan for now."

"Satan."

Humans, please restrain yourselves.

"Go. Once my purification ends, you'll return to reality. The lord of hell repenting will strengthen your Ultima even more..."

Lucifer's demonic eyes gleamed.

"Anke Ra is strong."

Goffin nodded.

"I know. But I'm not alone. All Gaia will attack together."

"Ikael won't help you."

Goffin knew how conservative and proud she was too.

Lucifer's fangs glinted.

"But promises must be kept."

With the fate of the universe at stake, Goffin couldn't break his promise lightly either.

"I'll do my best. I'll never abandon Ikael under any circumstances."

Lucifer was finally satisfied.

"That's enough." As he closed his eyes and smiled thinking of Ikael, a cold voice spoke,

"Time remaining until purification: 764 seas 1 quadrillion 428 trillion 9 quadrillion 984 trillion 1 billion 176 million 3 thousand 329 hours."

It was the price of repentance.

After leaving the infinite wall, Goffin disappeared from sight after about a day.

"...How much time is left?"

Lucifer asked the sky,

"Time remaining until purification: 764 seas 1 quadrillion 428 trillion 9 quadrillion 984 trillion 1 billion 176 million 3 thousand 329 hours."

Not even an hour had decreased.

"Heh, I see."

In hell, time was converted into sensation, and the lord of hell couldn't feel pain.

"How embarrassing."

As Lucifer looked up at the sky with a wistful gaze, Ikael's image appeared.

'Ah...'

As vast as the distance between existence and non-existence, it was a great love.

"If only I could see you once more."

Reaching out towards her in front of him but grasping nothing,

Tears flowed down Lucifer's face as he smiled beautifully for the first time in his life.

"Ikael."

As heart-wrenching pain surged through him,

"I love you."

That pain was fully converted into time,

"Aaaah!"

Exploding Lucifer's body into black ash and instantly pushing him into the infinite wall.

In a place where only silence remained,

"...Purification is complete."

A woman's voice softly echoed.
