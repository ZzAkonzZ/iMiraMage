Chapter 1084: Solar War (2)

But because of that…

'It's annoying.'

Shirone wanting to win the Solar War meant he wanted to become a user of this world.

Shirone would love everyone.

'And I…'

Love Shirone.

'It has come to this. There's no choice.'

On this planet, the only one Mitochondrial Eve could love was Shirone.

'I will take him.'

Even if it means selling my soul.

I will not become one of the countless things Yahweh loves.

"Let's go. We're heading west."

A user from Ascension's second team asked, puzzled.

"We've just taken the center. If we go west, there will already be other users there."

The results of the Solar War become points, which are tallied into the Solar War rankings.

That's why capturing strategic points is important.

"I want to do it right. This Solar War will be tough. I'll do my best to make sure the West Country wins."

If Shirone does it, she will too.

"Yes! I will give my life!"

The determination of the Little Witch, known for her free-spirited nature, ignited the enthusiasm of Ascension's second team.

'Anyway, words are just…'

Having driven many to death in reality, she felt no particular emotion.

"Let's go! For the West Country!"

In any case, boosting their morale was necessary for victory.

Meanwhile, in the East Country, Shirone and Golden Wheel were engaged in fierce combat.

Explosions erupted, and bullets with various characteristics rained from all directions.

'Surviving is hard, let alone fighting.'

The initial battle was a firepower war.

Even if you die, you can revive at the starting point, so everyone pours out their firepower.

Users from both the East and West Countries, starting with the lower levels, began to die one after another.

'In the Solar War, users' states can be classified into three categories. First, those who keep fighting without dying.'

Currently, this included Shirone and other rankers.

'Second, those who have died.'

Those who have no impact on the battlefield until they return to the starting point.

'Third, those who join from the starting point.'

This group is crucial.

'How quickly they join and how many join can completely change the outcome.'

Controlling their speed and position is the skill of those who keep fighting without dying, i.e., the rankers.

"Hehehe! Die, die! Everyone die!"

An East Country user was shooting at allies, unrelated to the war.

Being an Amara type meant his level wasn't even 100.

"Oh? Parts, parts!"

Seeing loot on the ground, he aimed for easy gains but was blown up by a grenade.

'…Fourth, those who act unrelated to the war.'

Adding another category, Shirone could roughly sketch the flow of the Solar War.

'In this war, the weight of a ranker's life is immense. If one ranker dies, a gap forms instantly. Then the number of the second category, the dead, increases…'

If delays in reinforcements continue, the Solar War will be lost.

'It's gradually calming down.'

Currently, over 2,000 had died, and the ratio between the East and West Countries was evenly matched at 5 to 5.

The most intense initial clash.

Only those who survived the total firepower remained, marking the true beginning of the Solar War.

The special unit to the east wall! Wild West, cover from the rear!

Found the second team of the Guardian Guild! Sending coordinates!

As expected, real-time communication continued from Golden Wheel's command center.

With users over level 400 from the West Country clashing, local battles finally erupted.

'No dying. It's impossible not to die at all, but at least until reinforcements return…'

At that moment, he realized.

'Wait, so…'

The Operator.

Her legendary undefeated record in High Gear history included the Solar War.

'Never… died even once.'

Realizing how astonishing this record was, a chill ran down his spine.

Shirone smiled.

The competitive spirit he had once considered someone else's business began to boil again.

"Take down the rankers."

Golden Wheel's changed strategy had only one expectation for Yahweh2.

"Yahweh2!"

Though not from Ascension, code names seen on the ranking board charged at him.

Raphael's strong armor deflected armor-piercing rounds.

Though he had practiced dueling, the rankers' skills in real combat were on another level.

In Donatello's all-around vision, something translucent shimmered.

'Stealth.'

Quickly twisting his body, he dodged a real bullet, and the rankers attacked simultaneously.

"Got him…!"

With bat-like senses, Shirone activated Metal Wings Da Vinci.

Flying at supersonic speed, he punched before the stealth user could react.

His right arm, covered in Raphael's metal, pierced the user's High Gear.

"Ugh!"

As stealth deactivated, the ranker's machine, wrapped in electricity, exploded violently.

'Got one.'

Killing a ranker was significant.

As the rankers who failed the stealth operation hid, a warning sounded.

Attack detected.

Through Donatello's all-around vision, a terrifying flash was seen approaching.

Flying quickly into the sky, a shockwave erupted where the flash struck.

"Michelangelo…"

Realizing the power, Yahweh2 saw a member of Ascension standing before him.

User code name, Mystic.

Her upper body was that of a beautiful woman, but her lower body was a monstrous spider's legs.

'A top 24 ranker in dueling.'

Holding Michelangelo, Mystic spoke.

"I saw the video. Yahweh2, you're famous. But Michelangelo is originally from the West Country."

Light smoke swirled above her palm, rapidly compressing into a sphere.

"Let's see who wins!"

Both users moved simultaneously, shooting flashes as they dashed across the battlefield.

'Incredibly fast.'

Mystic's monstrous form excelled in ground combat, tracking Shirone.

"Hohoho! Is that all you've got?"

With 307-level output, he couldn't match Mystic's speed, which exceeded level 450.

"Ugh!"

Jumping through codes, the ranker's AI immediately tracked Yahweh2's position.

"Hoohoo."

Dozens of light spheres were fired simultaneously from her Michelangelo.

'Multi-shot?'

Mystic, blocking the space for Shirone to jump through codes, charged with a euphoric smile.

'I've already analyzed the video.'

A chain explosion occurred, but when the smoke cleared, Yahweh2 was nowhere to be seen.

'Damn! Da Vinci.'

Clicking her tongue and twisting her upper body, Mystic was about to activate Michelangelo again.

A massive sphere of light, enough to make even the most battle-focused stop fighting, appeared.

"W-what is that?"

Yahweh2 was holding Michelangelo up to the sky, and a 4-meter diameter light sphere vibrated menacingly.

"More."

As Shirone's eyes widened, the light sphere explosively grew even larger.

Users, purely curious about its power, forgot about winning or losing.

"Want to see what the original can do?"

Michelangelo was a weapon modeled after Shirone.

"This is Photon Cannon."

Stepping forward and swinging his arm with all his might, a massive flash shot out.

"Dodge…!"

As the spider legs bent sharply, the flash struck and exploded.

Boom!

Within a radius of several dozen meters, no users remained as if they had never been there.

"Phew."

Witnessing the terrifying power, East Country users fired bullets into the sky and shouted.

"Alright, let's go! This time, we'll win! We'll take the Solar Palace!"

The disappearance of two rankers became a weakness for the West Country, and the East Country pushed in endlessly.

Aegis, checking the satellite video, communicated.

Sending all revived forces from the starting point to the east. Push in as much as possible.

As East Country users followed, Shirone's High Gear pulsed like a heartbeat.

'We'll win. We will win.'

Gradually… his heart was getting into it.

Meanwhile, in the operator's meeting room.

"What on earth is that?"

Ten operators, excluding the Operator and Number Seven, were watching the Solar War.

It was the biggest event in the world they created, and they were ready to respond to any immediate errors.

Candy Cane Mark spoke.

"Michelangelo's quantum collection function is basically equal to the total photon count of the world. But as it passes through specific sections, the difficulty increases, so the collection time lengthens. The current power is clearly abnormal."

Black Circle Mark spoke.

"It took about 2.3 seconds to reach 190,000 force just now. This means Yahweh2 has a completely different adaptability, aside from having special senses."

Gear Mark spoke.

"The biggest interest in this Solar War will be Yahweh2. Though he was in the spotlight from the beginning."

Demon Mark spoke.

"After the Solar War, a new event will be updated. Whether Yahweh2 will be a poison or a medicine…"

"Come to think of it, Number Seven isn't here?"

"He always does as he pleases. But he never misses the Solar War, especially when the Operator is active…"

Gear Mark summarized.

"Well, he's probably busy with the upcoming event. Let's focus on our tasks. The most important thing is that this Solar War ends smoothly."

The operators' attention returned to the battlefield.

Solar Palace.

'Shirone.'

Still kneeling, the Operator found Yahweh2 in the satellite video.

His actions, defeating rankers, resembled the visions seen in people's dreams.

'But he doesn't come.'

Yahweh entered High Gear solely for a duel with her.

To save people in reality, nothing here would matter.

'He's fighting the Solar War.'

Seeing him fiercely battling numerous users, against her expectations.

Thump. Thump.

High Gear pulsed like a heart.

'He's living in the world I created.'

Her hands clenched involuntarily.

'I want to fight.'

Yahweh2, rapidly approaching the Solar Palace, had already defeated seven rankers.

'Ascension's first team defending the inner castle will be different. But I want to fight. Right now, I want to feel you.'

In the quiet stillness, the Operator's machine gracefully stood up.

As she opened the Solar Palace doors with cold footsteps, golden sunlight poured in.

The noise of battle surged in, and she turned her head east.

The Operator's AI had a search range of over 2 kilometers.

Yahweh2's coordinates were marked.

"…I'm coming."

Leaning forward into a sprinting stance, she lifted her head and pushed off the ground.

High Gear output level 487.

Within seconds, she reached her destination and turned towards Yahweh2.

Users, sensing the unreal speed, turned their heads, but only the sound remained.

Shirone's Donatello flashed.

'She's here.'

Before the thought finished, the Operator's fist flew towards Shirone's face.

Boom!

The horrific sound of the collision made users from both the East and West Countries turn simultaneously.

Yahweh2, activating Raphael and Da Vinci simultaneously, blocked the punch.

But the reason users were shocked was different.

"W-what?"

The Operator.

A machine that shouldn't have appeared on the battlefield yet was right before their eyes.
