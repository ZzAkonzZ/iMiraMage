Chapter 1022: Electric Civilization (2)

Among the refugees aboard the three hundred ships, confusion was evident on their faces.

"Did we survive?"

No one had expected to live when the drones attacked, but a bizarre phenomenon had occurred.

A spatial barrier fluctuated and suddenly thousands of bombs had all detonated.

Shirone felt relieved inwardly.

'Trying it out for real turned out to be quite tricky.' Slate of Hand was not magic, but rather a trick-like skill.

This trick occurred at the quantum level, seemingly disregarding even the laws of physics.

Poine's eyes became fierce.

'Such a method...'

If a magician deceives the eyes, Shirone deceives cognition.

'At the moment when everyone in the audience is certain of the future, the mind becomes something predictable, hence a law.'

And if it’s a law, Shirone could twist the event they perceived.

'But the reason it's a trick... is because once people’s perception returns, the event itself regains its logical probability.'

"Heh."

If someone had doubted a different future, the three hundred ships would never have disappeared.

"A magic trick with 20,000 lives at stake." It was a moment of artistry, brought about by focusing everyone's perception squarely in the blind spot.

"Certainly not sane, that."

With that said.

"What will you do? Are you going to die at my hand, or wait for the Messiah to come?"

As Poine glanced over at the pirate ship, the face of Jacra on deck was distorted.

"Hmm."

"Captain, what should we do?"

If the opponent was a first-class dragon, pretending to deliberate seemed the human thing to do.

"Is there a need to wait?"

Electricity coursed through Jacra's eyes, and a powerful magnetic field encased him like a sphere.

"Let’s go, boys!"

The crew, realizing his sincerity, activated the machinery, and the pirate ship began to turn transparent.

"Stealth? Pathetic." A high-level technique, but nothing a dragon couldn’t handle.

"Raaah!"

Poine tucked her wings and dove as only a blurry outline of the pirate ship remained.

However, what met her teeth was not the pirate ship, but the salty ocean water.

"Pfoo!"

"Kahahaha! Here, here!"

Jacra’s pirate ship, now six kilometers away, revealed itself.

"Come at me, lizard."

Probably some kind of electric energy.

It was technology handling energy at a level never achieved by current humanity...

"Raaah!"

"Dissolve."

Jacra burst out laughing.

"Puhahaha! See that? The lizard’s lost it!"

The pirate ship, maneuvering through teleportation powered by electric energy, was agile regardless of size and mass.

"Maybe it’s time we withdrew? We've made our point."

As one person came to mind.

"How, how could this be?"

Maika's artifact.
