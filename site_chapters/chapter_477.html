Chapter 477:

Gaold, with his head raised towards the sky, accepted the situation without a shred of doubt.

In the 20 years of working on the project, had he ever truly believed this was possible?

But because he had never gone a single day in those 20 years without thinking about today's situation, he felt that what had to happen had simply happened, rather than feeling doubt or anxiety.

"We must escape."

That necessity quickly shifted Gaold's thinking.

From now on, there was no need to look back.

The only task left for him was to get out of this place.

"Ugh!"

Gaold's concentration delved deep into the end of his mind, reaching the realm of death, and the immense Air Press crushed Uriel.

Even the archangel of destruction was momentarily immobilized by its power.

That alone was enough for Gaold at that moment.

"Run at all costs!"

Sein landed behind Gaold, and the rest of the group followed immediately.

Turning his body one last time, Gaold saw Kariel frozen in place within his rotating gaze.

As long as the Stop magic wasn't lifted, not even an archangel could transcend time.

But the hordes of fallen angels madly charging beyond Kariel would be different.

"We're getting out at top speed!"

As soon as Gaold finished speaking, the mages cast teleportation spells and sprang out.

Being top-tier mages in their respective fields, the flashes quickly left the plains and finally entered the drawbridge.

Sein cut Miro's collar first.

Arius, attached to the collar, was cumbersome, and if they started to speed up, it could choke her and put her in danger.

Arius rolled on the ground, hurriedly regained his balance, and moved forward straight.

Though he had lost his sight, he was once one of the Black Line's Seven Magical Sins, and escaping alone was not a problem for him.

"Activate it!"

At Sein's command, Kangnan kicked open the door to the engine room.

She found a large operating lever that seemed impossible to pull without being a giant, and with a kick, the iron handle rang and pushed forward.

While it wouldn't have much effect on the flying fallen angels, it could temporarily block the approach of the ground-walking Maras.

Additionally, raising the drawbridge had the advantage of blocking the enemy's long-range attacks.

When they came out, Gaold's group had already entered the drawbridge.

Rumble!

The center of the drawbridge split into two and began to rise slowly towards the sky.

Raising a drawbridge 1 kilometer wide and 4 kilometers long was impossible with human technology.

"Miro! Recapture Miro!"

The fallen angels charged, targeting only Miro.

They knew that if they captured her, everyone's movements would be restricted.

Having forgotten even the noble memories of their original angelic status, the fallen angels became a single spear and surged onto the drawbridge.

The bridge was quickly filled with enemies, and another battle ensued on the increasingly tilted bridge.

Even the fallen angels' full-force attacks were terrifyingly powerful.

Gaold's group painfully realized how crucial Armin's Stop magic had been in this project.

"But now we can't use Stop. We have to do it ourselves."

Stopping time didn't mean canceling the event.

The current Stop field was cast on Kariel, and if the field was lifted, Miro's head would explode without any chance to intervene.

Meanwhile, instead of chasing Gaold, Uriel flew to the fortress wall to check on Kariel, who was trapped in the Stop.

"Kariel…"

Miro's issue was secondary to Uriel.

After all, Kariel had planned all this, and Uriel had helped him from a somewhat detached position.

But seeing Kariel, Uriel experienced significant mental shock.

There was no radiance to be found in the archangel frozen in time.

"A mere human has incapacitated an archangel."

The Nirvana Shield spun with a whistling sound, but Uriel couldn't bring himself to destroy it.

After all, he would also be frozen, trapped in the field.

"It can't be reversed immediately."

To accelerate and reverse the stopped time at the speed of light, the help of the archangel of light, Raiel, was needed.

"But…"

Uriel turned his head towards Gaold's group, who were moving far away.

"I must retrieve what was taken."

As Uriel's body turned into light, he flew in a parabola to the center of the bridge, several kilometers away.

Boom!

When Uriel landed, the vibration was transmitted to everyone's feet as if they were standing on a wave.

They were currently 1 kilometer away from the drawbridge's entrance.

Unless Gaold's group were pushovers, it would be difficult to pursue them once they left the one-way path.

"I will destroy your will."

As Uriel spun the Nirvana Shield, Gaold applied the Air Press.

Once again, the atmosphere burned, and a fierce explosion erupted on the bridge.

"Ugh!"

Gaold's group was thrown in all directions by the shockwave.

Even without seeing it, the result showed that Gaold was overpowered.

Gaold, with his strength drained, staggered while keeping his gaze fixed on Uriel.

He had to protect them.

Even if it meant dying here, he couldn't let Uriel pass.

With each step Uriel took, the pressure doubled and quadrupled.

When the energy pressing down on him was enough to stop his heart, Uriel halted.

"That's just like you, Kariel."

"Kariel?"

Hearing that name, Gaold suddenly felt fear.

As he turned around in surprise, he heard Sein's voice.

"Miro!"

He saw Miro flying high into the sky, having escaped from Sein's arms.

Her arms and legs were ablaze with flames manifested by the Seal of Fire.

Seal Magic - Prison of Fire.

On the opposite drawbridge, which had risen to a 30-degree angle, the fire giant mage Urotas shouted with his hands raised.

"Ha ha ha ha! I did it! I did it, Lord Kariel! Remember the name of Urotas!"

After dropping Kangnan onto the fortress wall and receiving orders from Kariel, he had hidden himself.

It was Kariel's trump card to recapture Miro if things went wrong.

"No!"

The sense of despair that surged as they watched Miro, caught in the seal magic, quickly move away was unbearable even for the best party.

Gaold took a step forward and then fell to his knees.

His mind was filled with thoughts, but they were all jumbled and black.

In that darkness, he found one possibility.

"Dog!"

When Gaold shouted, Kangnan immediately turned her body.

"Bite!"

Kangnan, who had kicked off the ground, quickly moved her legs, aiming at Miro crossing the sky.

While some fallen angels returned towards Miro, others charged at Kangnan.

Thump! Thump!

Kangnan calculated their movements, hearing her heartbeat reach her eardrums.

Not a single pause was allowed.

In the scene of enemies rushing like leaves swept by a storm, Kangnan shook her upper body.

She narrowly avoided the dangerous attacks as if she were in a spinning blade and broke through the fallen angels' formation, jumping and stepping on them as she charged.

"Hmph!"

The drawbridge's angle had now risen to 45 degrees.

For a normal human, not only would their speed decrease, but it would also be impossible to run on such a slope. However, Kangnan's speed did not decrease at all.

"Faster, faster!"

Bang! Bang! Bang!

As she kicked off the ground with powerful leg strength, the stones shattered, and fragments flew backward.

Miro had already flown to the other side, and all she could see was the sky.

"Ugh!"

Kangnan gritted her teeth and put more strength into her legs.

Despite the uphill slope, her speed increased, and finally, she kicked off the broken end of the drawbridge with all her might, which had tilted to almost a cliff level.

"Yaaaah!"

Boom!

As the end of the bridge exploded and shattered, Kangnan's body flew.

A blue river sparkling in the sun flowed 1 kilometer below, and she ran through the air with nothing to catch her body.

She saw Miro disappearing beyond the opposite drawbridge.

And beyond that, the giant Urotas was aiming his fiery gaze at her.

As the Seal of Fire was engraved on her heart, the blood flow that had been circulating rapidly throughout her body suddenly braked, causing a shock.

In her mind, which had turned white, Kangnan's consciousness flowed into a time of purpose and mission she had never forgotten.

"Kangnan?"

A man had asked the last descendant of the wolf tribe, who wandered the sewers with broken fangs.

"The soul of a wolf."

And he had smiled gently and said,

"It's a wonderful name, Kangnan."

"Yaaaah!"

Kangnan let out a monstrous cry and moved her limbs.

With a pop, the flames of the seal exploded, and her heart started beating again.

Urotas's hideous face, looking at her in astonishment, was getting closer.

Kangnan was not the strongest warrior of the Rammoai. But she was the best warrior.

"I am the last descendant of the wolf tribe!"

The voice of the master who had passed on the secret technique echoed in her ears.

"Do not rely on fangs. Have jaws that can crush anything."

Crunch!

Kangnan's stomach, which had stretched like a bow, began to harden like a stone.

And finally, her body, shot like an arrow, passed Miro and approached Urotas.

Rammoai Strike Technique - Arang's.

"Hmph!"

Kangnan tightened her abs with all her might, pulling her elbows and knees together as if curling up.

When her attack hit Urotas's crown and jaw, there was a cracking sound as the giant's rock-hard face shattered into pieces.

It was the jaw of a wolf crushing the giant.

Thud!

Almost simultaneously with Urotas's fall, Kangnan landed, grimacing in pain.

Her abdominal muscles were completely torn, making it impossible for her to stand.

She gritted her teeth and twisted her body. Then, she caught the flying Miro and activated her external force.

"Ugh!"

The drawbridge's angle was approaching 70 degrees, and if it tilted any further, everything would be over.

Clinging almost vertically to the bridge, Kangnan and Miro were able to look into each other's eyes for the first time.

Miro didn't know who Kangnan was.

But the emotions in her eyes in that brief moment said it all.

"So, this child is…"

Even while being swept up by centrifugal force, Miro shook her head.

She shouldn't throw herself. What really needed to be thrown was…

When Miro signaled that she couldn't do it, Kangnan gave a dry smile for a fleeting moment.

But then, like a warrior of the wolf tribe, she wrinkled her nose and let out a battle cry.

"Yaaah!"

With all her strength, she threw Miro into the air. With a whoosh, Miro's body traced back the path she had flown and began to leap over the bridge.

"Ugly duckling…"

Finally, Kangnan's feet left the drawbridge, and she began to fall into the abyss below.

"If you call me a mutt one more time…"

Kangnan, her body flipping upside down, could no longer hold onto her fading consciousness and slowly closed her eyes.

The last thing she remembered was the faces of numerous fallen angels pouring down from the sky.
