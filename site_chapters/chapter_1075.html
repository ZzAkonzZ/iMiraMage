Chapter 1075: Raid Control (1)

West Country ranker, Little Witch.

Shirone knew her well, having kept track of rankers as he leveled up.

'If she knows me…'

It means they met in some way in reality.

Little Witch turned her eerie gaze to Shirone's friends behind him.

'What, what's this?'

Destroyer707 and his group were familiar with Little Witch, but meeting her eyes gave them chills.

'She's just a machine, but this feels weird.'

Shirone also sensed the strange atmosphere emanating from Little Witch.

'I get it.'

She was Mitochondrial Eve.

Having reached the level of a bat in High Gear, Shirone could read the spirit contained in matter.

'This also means…'

Uorin was using this world to understand the real world.

"Everyone… please step outside for a moment."

Destroyer707 looked puzzled, but Shirone didn't take his eyes off Little Witch.

"Fermi, you too."

Yolga's Son shrugged and left the room, followed by Destroyer707 without a word.

Once the door closed, Shirone asked, "You…"

Little Witch put a finger to her lips.

"There's no need to talk about reality. All our actions and conversations are logged."

Technically, the designer can check everything that happens in High Gear.

'The only thing they can't check is the mind.'

The quantum world is unobservable.

"Alright. Why are you here? You're from the Ascension Guild. This is East Country's hideout."

"I requested it. I wanted to meet you. You're planning to defeat the Operator, right?"

Little Witch laughed dryly.

"Haha. That girl is really concerned about you. But it'll be tough. She might seem like a teenage girl, but she's the strongest in High Gear."

"So, what do you want to say?"

"Shall I help you?"

Shirone read the madness in Little Witch.

"Are you going to betray the Operator?"

"Don't say such hurtful things. I might use people, but I don't betray them."

Thinking about their differences, Shirone realized there was no need and shook his head.

'Empress of Kashan, Uorin.'

The moment you define her, she will exploit that gap to control your mind.

"So how will you help? Even you can't beat the Operator here."

"Still naive, I see." That's why she liked him.

"You know who I am. The Operator probably suspects too. Just as you have special senses, I have a trump card."

"A trump card?"

Little Witch mimicked a gun with her hand, aiming at Shirone.

"Real-world murder. Want to know who the Operator is? I guarantee you'll never find out. Even a dreamer can't trace the undercoder's floor. But I can."

History search.

"If you say the word, I'll help. We can drag the Operator here and unearth the apocalypse."

'Fermi, you bastard…'

He didn't know how much information they exchanged, but it was clearly a mutually satisfying deal.

He had no complaints, but dealing with Uorin was unsettling.

"What deal did you make?"

Little Witch blinked for a while before speaking.

"I don't know much. The situation with the apocalypse, and in return, I…"

"Not that."

Shirone interrupted.

"What deal did you make with Havitz?"

Silence fell.

"You disappeared in Kashan. You must have met and made some deal. Otherwise, it's strange you're still alive."

"External matters aren't for here. You have things you don't want to reveal too."

"Of course. But once you mentioned real-world murder, that doesn't matter. I need to know what happened with Havitz. It's related to the Holy War, right?"

"Why don't you ask?"

Little Witch's voice was tinged with sadness.

"Come to Kashan and ask me. Do you think I won't tell you? Why? Because you'll learn what Mitochondrial Eve is, and now you don't want to see my face?"

'Why?'

Yahweh wasn't narrow-minded, but after learning everything through Omega, his heart didn't move.

Little Witch said, "To be clear, it's not my fault. I did my best. Don't blame everything on me."

She still had no expression, but Shirone felt like she was crying.

She placed a card on the table.

"It's a pass for West Country's raid hunting grounds. Yolga's Son said you'd need it."

That was the deal.

"And again, I won't talk to you here. If you want to hear, come to Kashan. That's the only condition."

As Little Witch left, Shirone checked the card she left.

'West Country Free Pass.'

A moment later, Yolga's Son entered.

"Did the conversation go well?"

There was no need to report to Fermi, so Shirone said nothing.

Fermi shrugged and approached.

"Well, good. You got it. With that, you can get two of the four legendary items. If you're lucky or use tricks."

"What is this?"

"It's literally a pass. Many hunting grounds in High Gear are monopolized by specific guilds. They issue passes and sell them to users from other countries. The Free Pass lets you enter all hunting grounds occupied by West Country."

Shirone took the West Country Free Pass.

"But I can't avoid conflicts with users. I'm from East Country. Do I have to party with them?"

"No choice. To beat the Operator, you need three things: skill, level, and equipment. Skill aside, our guild can help with the level. But there's still a 183-level gap. Especially above level 300, each level difference is huge."

"You need equipment to bridge that gap."

"Yes. To face the Operator, you need four essential items: the energy gun Michelangelo, the strong armor Raphael, the metal wings Da Vinci, and the liquid eyes Donatello. These are called the four legendary items. Equipping them will narrow the gap."

Shirone understood.

"What do I need to find in West Country?"

"Michelangelo and Donatello. West Country won't like it, so you need to go now. 'Myth' level raids adjust difficulty based on the average user level. It's always extremely difficult, though."

"Adjusting difficulty means?"

"Like Mid Gear, it's a hunting ground isolated from the outside. Once inside, even other Ascension Guild members can't enter. I can't guarantee what happens inside."

"Got it."

Shirone stood up.

"What about Destroyer707?"

"He'll be useful to Golden Wheel. We'll level him up to Magnan for the Solar War. You focus on finding the legendary items."

Time was tight for both, so Shirone immediately left the room and exited the bar.

Destroyer707 asked, "Did the conversation go well?"

Fortunately, he didn't seem too discouraged by Little Witch's cold demeanor.

"Yeah. It looks like I need to go to West Country."

After briefly summarizing the conversation with Fermi, Destroyer707 nodded.

"Hmm, that's certainly a clever approach. I think it's the only way to stand a chance against the Operator."

Death Princess added, "It's really difficult, so the success rate is fifty-fifty. Plus, the chances of getting legendary items are extremely low, so you probably won't get them on the first try. But I'll pray you do."

Shirone had a rough idea of the structure.

"Alright, then…"

Recalling the time left until the Solar War, Shirone followed Aegis outside.

"Taking the train from Atogram is the fastest. You need to be at least level 200 to be faster than the train. I'll guide you."

When they reached the square, reporters swarmed, but Aegis's electronic interference kept them at bay.

After buying tickets and boarding the train, everyone entered Mid Gear.

"Ah."

This method would prevent the journey from feeling tedious.

Some users stayed in their seats to enjoy the journey, but they were all from West Country.

"Hey, it's Yahweh2. Did you get on the wrong train?"

To avoid suspicion, Shirone entered the Mid Gear capsule.

'Energy charging.'

Wires connected, and even with his eyes closed, he saw blue electricity in his vision.

Charging complete.

They were in West Country.

The capital, Democracy.

It was the first city for users who left Atogram and chose West Country.

Now able to distinguish parts, Shirone saw a wide range of levels.

Most were from West Country, so he couldn't see guild marks, but there were a few East Country users.

'There are more people here than in Atogram.'

The rumor that 40% of West Country users gathered here wasn't an exaggeration.

The scenery was a mix of half-hologram buildings and numerous advertisements.

Following Fermi's instructions, they reached the square where people were recruiting for raid parties.

Recruiting for the Gambler's Sanctuary! Minimum level 280.

Going to the Valley of the Cursed. Need 3 more. Preference for those with Aegis option activated.

The street was quiet, but the wide-area communication echoed in Shirone's ears.

'Dizzying.'

After adjusting the frequency, he found silence and used the filtering function to input 'Ergos.'

Looking for challengers for Ergos. Low levels welcome. Contact codename Mafia.

Ignoring other voices, Shirone responded to the first information he received.

He contacted the man.

Yes. Are you interested?

I am, but I'm new. Can I learn as I go?

Hmm, what's your level?

Level 100. I'm equipped with Suicide Speed Machine. All selective options are unlocked.

Mafia responded positively, perhaps appreciating his effort to not seem like a beginner.

Let's see. Where are you?

I'm under the clock tower.

We'll come to you.

After waiting about a minute, a group pushed through the crowd from the east side of the square.

'Codename Mafia.'

There were other codenames, but being from West Country, he couldn't get detailed information.

"Hey, it's East Country."

Realizing the same, Mafia's group stopped.

"Wait, that's… Yahweh2? Didn't you guys check the bulletin board? It was crazy yesterday."

"No, I was hunting all day. What happened?"

"That guy's from Golden Wheel."

A man with the codename DexGamsung approached Shirone.

"Hey, did you get on the wrong train? Why are you in Democracy? You should be in East Country."

"I came here on purpose. I heard I could get Michelangelo by defeating Ergos. Can we team up?"

DexGamsung looked at his friends and burst into laughter.

"Haha! This guy's a real oddball. Are you stupid? How can East and West hunt together? There's no hostile action judgment, but you think we won't stab you in the back?"

"That's why I'm asking. Let's do it together."

DexGamsung, looking baffled, tapped his silver metal head.

"Just go. What nonsense is this? If we team up with you, they won't even issue a pass."

"I have a pass."

"Huh?"

Shirone pulled out a card.

"Here, take a look."

Leaning in to examine the card, DexGamsung's AI displayed the information.

West Country Free Pass. Approved by Ascension Guild.

He stood still for a long time, as if frozen.
