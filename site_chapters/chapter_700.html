Chapter 700:

Unable to track Ra Enemy, Uorin traced the movements of all humanity except Ra Enemy.

"History search isn't a big deal. It's just a way of sorting through my memories into a series of categories."

However, the scope of those memories encompassed all of humanity, which was remarkable.

Most humans can only pass on fragments of their lifetime experiences to future generations, but Terraza's memory transfer had no information loss.

"In short, no human in my memory has ever encountered the real Ra Enemy. Therefore, the only places left are those I haven't experienced myself, namely the world's three great labyrinths. If it's not there, then it's not on this planet."

Shirone understood.

'Regions with a very low exit frequency compared to the entry frequency. But that means…'

Even if they encountered the real Ra Enemy, the chances of escaping were extremely low.

"Although I said I'd vote for you if it meant betting my life, honestly, you're behind the other competitors in terms of progress."

To be a candidate for the Ivory Tower, the speed of judgment had to be similar, and the rest was a matter of disposition.

"Nane has already chosen one of the three labyrinths and is heading there without anyone's help. Jin Seong-eum has the powerful soldiers of the Jincheon Empire, her direct guard unit, Sambogun. They are top-notch in skill and have the authority to approach within three steps of her. And you, Shirone, have… the Knight of Maha and the King of Goblins."

As Kido awkwardly cleared his throat, Uorin smiled with her eyes.

"I know Rian and Kido are strong, but this time the opponents are formidable too. You'll have to work hard."

"It's okay. There are three labyrinths. I don't think they'll find what you couldn't, Uorin."

Uorin's point was also consistent.

"Ra Enemy is in one of the three labyrinths: Jessica, Andre, or Paitaros. So you still have a chance, Shirone. Do you have a map?"

When Rian handed over a world map, Uorin spread it out on the table.

"Do you know the difference between a maze and a labyrinth?"

"Um, a maze is a path, and a labyrinth is a space?"

"Close. But more precisely, a maze has an exit, and a labyrinth does not. It's not a place designed to confuse someone from the start."

Uorin pointed to the Kingdom of Molton, which bordered Kashan to the south.

"First, Jessica."

As the largest empire, Kashan bordered three kingdoms to the south: Jaive, Molton, and Bornai.

"Jessica is named after its first discoverer. It's located 87 kilometers from Molton's royal castle, in a forest adjacent to the Mediterranean. You'll probably find it easily because it's so huge."

"What do you mean by huge?"

"It's literally too big. Another name for Jessica is the Giant's Ruins. It's believed to have been made by giants before humanity settled on this land. No one has fully explored the underground space of the pyramid, but archaeologists estimate it's as large as a small kingdom."

"Hmm, finding it is a problem, but so is getting out."

"Among the three labyrinths, it's the most popular. It's called a labyrinth because it can't be conquered, but you can find valuable relics and sometimes ancient weapons without going too deep."

None of the people talking to Uorin were unaware that giants came from heaven.

"Next, Andre. It's in the Akkad Desert."

Terraza pointed to the desert southeast of Kashan's territory.

"The Akkad Desert is the largest desert in the world. The southern desert belongs to the Kingdom of Paras, but Andre is in the northern desert, within Kashan's territory."

Tormia wasn't a very large country, so introducing one of the three great labyrinths within its territory felt unfamiliar.

"Andre is also known as the Prison of Time and Space. As far as I know, it's the most unique and hardest to escape among the three labyrinths."

"Why is it called the Prison of Time and Space?"

"The flow of time and space is completely different from reality. The survival rate is one in a thousand. According to records, it could be the past from 3,000 years ago or the primitive era from 10,000 years ago. Some might have even visited future worlds."

"I see. So every time you enter, a different time and space unfolds? This is tough. Survival depends on luck?"

"Not exactly."

"Huh?"

When he looked up, Uorin was smiling playfully.

"It's actually a famous tourist spot. Thousands of people visit it every year."

"What do you mean?"

"A long time ago, someone isolated the time and space of Andre individually. So, even if you enter Andre, you no longer experience the mixing of time and space. Instead, you'll find 19,000 circular steel doors densely installed there. Each one is an entrance to a different world."

"There are 19,000 independent worlds?"

"Yes. Each world is sealed in a different time and space. Of course, tourists can't open the steel doors, but no one tries to enter anyway. Since the steel doors appeared, no one has returned. So no one knows how each world has developed. Some might have already perished."

"That's why it's called a prison."

"It's a kind of penitentiary. Time and space that broke the rules and ran wild were imprisoned."

At this point, there was one question.

"Who did such an incredible thing?"

"I don't know. No one knows."

Shirone's expression hardened.

"Maclaine Goffin."

"Yes. The fact that you can't find it in any history means it's already been erased. Now you understand? Andre is Goffin's relic."

Shirone swallowed hard and said,

"Ra Enemy probably isn't here either, right?"

He wanted to believe that.

"Haha. That could be, but Anke Ra isn't an ordinary creature either."

With a serious expression, Uorin looked at the map again and pointed to the Middle East, the Kingdom of Paras, to the left of the southern Akkad Desert.

"Lastly, Paitaros, also known as the Pit of Corruption."

Kido stuck out his tongue and raised it.

"Just hearing the name makes me not want to go."

"If Andre is unique in terms of time and space, Paitaros is unique in terms of law. All the corrupt energy of this world gathers there. Just like rainwater flows from high to low places, Paitaros is a legal lowland."

Rian spoke briefly.

"It must be dangerous."

"Even if they're not Ivory Tower candidates, those with low karma go mad as soon as they enter. Moreover, numerous incorporeal beings born from the murky energy threaten explorers. Of course, the demon ranks range from at least B to triple S."

According to what they learned in school, demons are known to be born from the collective consciousness of ancient people.

A collection of murky desires, pleasures, and taboos.

And now that Shirone had realized the direct path, he could understand another fact.

"Demons are the rulers of the reverse world, right?"

"That's right. Reality and unreality are born like twins, indirectly influencing each other through a thin veil. But sometimes that veil is lifted, or the collective consciousness of humans becomes so strong that it crosses into reality."

"Just hearing about it gives me chills."

It was Kido's realistic assessment.

"Anyway, Ra Enemy is in one of these three labyrinths. To summarize, Jessica is as vast as a kingdom, Andre has countless worlds, and Paitaros is literally a paradise for demons. You can choose from these."

Uorin smiled like a merchant selling all good and cheap goods.

Shirone looked at her with weary eyes, but it was time to make a choice.

"Let's choose. The Giant's Ruins, the Prison of Time and Space, the Pit of Corruption. Which one will it be?"

After a moment of silence, Kido spoke.

"Can I be honest? I just want to go home."

He had never missed his deceased sister so much.

"It's important where we go, but shouldn't we also consider where Ra Enemy might be hiding?"

Rian made a rare, sharp point.

"Oh, right. Hmm."

Shirone looked at the map again.

"The Giant's Ruins are related to Anke Ra, and the Prison of Time and Space is the perfect place to hide."

Kido said,

"But wouldn't Paitaros be comfortable for Ra? Ra moved the army of the reverse world in Radum too."

In the end, it wouldn't be strange for Ra Enemy to be anywhere.

"What about the other competitors? Do we know where they went?"

"They haven't decided for sure yet. But based on their location, Jin Seong-eum seems to be heading to Jessica."

"The Giant's Ruins."

"Jin Seong-eum is an Unlocker. Her magic controls space with ether waves. The wide radius doesn't matter to her."

"She played to her strengths."

If they couldn't guess Ra Enemy's location, it was an excellent decision.

'That leaves Andre and Paitaros. Which one will Nane choose?'

In a three-way competition, Shirone wanted to avoid competing with Nane for a single labyrinth.

"Shirone, don't overthink it. This isn't a competition where tricks work. It's about fighting with your strengths. Nane and Jin Seong-eum have achieved great heights, but you have senses that surpass human limits."

"Shibulsangpokmae (Simultaneous Perception of the Past, Present, and Future)."

"Yes. While senses get stronger with training, acquiring them in the first place is a different matter. You can't give ears to someone who doesn't have them. But you're different."

Uorin raised both index fingers side by side.

"There are said to be 11 senses through which living beings perceive the world. Humans have only five senses, but the abilities of angels are theorized to be based on senses that surpass human understanding. That's why humans can't comprehend them."

Even Arius couldn't fully understand Ataraxia.

"The Valhalla Action that the great wizards are repaying their debts with is also an extreme manifestation of these senses."

"Valhalla Action?"

Thinking about it, the core principle was similar to Shibulsangpokmae.

"Some use their eyes to paint, while others use them to hunt. Even the same sense can function very differently depending on how it's developed. So, having an additional sense is a huge advantage."

Shirone felt a bit more confident.

"You probably inherited the senses of an angel at birth."

'Ikael…'

Thinking of her made his heart ache.

"Even so, it usually ends there, but you've opened the seventh sense, Bat Sense. You've already unlocked the final sense, known as the Ultimate Sense. Even Anke Ra doesn't have that sense."

'Goffin.'

Shirone pressed his lips together firmly.

He had no cause, but the two who designed him might have passed on something even greater.

"I don't know how many senses you can unlock, but just training your current senses will be enough to compete with the other Ivory Tower candidates."

Unlocking senses is on a different level from learning new magic.

'Indeed, just realizing Bat Sense…'

Feeling concepts like mind and concentration as tangible substances had greatly strengthened Shirone's mental power.

It was confirmed when he fought the Lord of Fear, Igor, and the accumulation time of Ataraxia completely disappeared.

'This is just the beginning. I can become stronger.'

As confidence filled Shirone's eyes, Uorin smiled and asked,

"So, what's your choice?"

Looking at the map again, Shirone no longer hesitated.

"Here."

Rian and Kido followed his gaze.

The Prison of Time and Space, imbued with Goffin's breath.

I will be where you seek.

"Ra Enemy is here."

It was the labyrinth Andre.
