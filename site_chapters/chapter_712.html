Chapter 712:

The laws that flowed into the underground labyrinth were layered like liquids of different densities.

Although there were seven layers in total, Nane had no way of knowing that yet.

As Nane gazed at the scene embodying extreme human suffering, another question arose in his mind.

'What is a human?'

The acts committed on the first underground floor might be the most powerful violence against human dignity.

"Ahhh! Please stop!"

Monks following the will of goodness, mages with cold rationality, and knights of conviction all screamed in unison.

'Why do they suffer?'

The sight of humans struggling in a hell created by humans was the irony of this world.

'In this empty world that disappears when you close your eyes.'

"Hehehe, it's been a while since a newcomer arrived."

The residents of the underworld living on the first underground floor of Pytharos approached with excited faces.

None of them had intact limbs, as if they were sewn together like rag dolls, and they held grotesque hooks.

The tools, which seemed to be used for pulling meat in a butcher shop, were performing their roles faithfully amidst the flames.

"Welcome, human. I will destroy your mind."

Nane did not understand the language of the residents.

When you understand the principles of the world and what you must do, the meaning of language disappears.

"Send these poor souls outside."

The residents, who had long enjoyed the voices of humans, understood Nane's words.

"Outside?"

The residents stuck out their tongues and burst into laughter.

"A very interesting toy has arrived. Do you know what those who said that ended up saying?"

A resident with bulging eyes mimicked a human.

"Please save me!"

The residents laughed uproariously, but Nane just walked calmly.

"The truth I seek lies at the end of hell."

Ra Enemy would be waiting for Nane in the deepest part of Pytharos.

"Isn't it my karma to save them from the human-made hell?"

A resident of the underworld extended a hook.

"If you can endure it, I will let you go. But I guarantee, you will also beg with tears of blood."

"There is no pain I do not know."

When someone raised a hook and struck his back, sparks flew in Nane's eyes.

'Why was I born?'

Fate, the wheel of law.

'What transcends the great cycle of reincarnation?'

Karr value 99.999 percent.

The group led by Seong-eum entered Jessica's pyramid and looked up at the ceiling with clouds.

"There is a sky."

It was an artificial sky.

Mun-gyeong, the leader of the Three Treasures, pointed to the endlessly extending horizon.

"It's really vast. The rumors were not exaggerated."

To a giant, it was just a city, but by human standards, it was the size of a kingdom.

"Size is meaningless to me."

To Jin Seong-eum, who had realized the ether wave, space was like an infinite rubber band that could be stretched and shrunk at will.

"Is La Enemy really here?"

"It doesn't matter. We will search the entire world."

As long as La Enemy existed, it would eventually appear before her eyes.

"Even the city of giants will be enough to see."

When Seong-eum focused her mind, the ether wave transmitted, and numerous objects were detected.

"First, one step."

Ether Wave - Shrinkage.

The scenery ahead crumpled and rushed in, and the distance of nearly 4 kilometers was instantly compressed before her eyes.

"Who are you!"

A group of people with weapons drawn glared at Seong-eum from beyond the boundary where two different landscapes met.

Having arrived at Jessica's first base camp in one step, she looked around and spoke.

"I am Jin Seong-eum, princess of the Jincheon Empire. From now on, I will occupy Jessica, so please cooperate."

As the guards of the Three Treasures waited for a response with solemn expressions, the hunters who had set up camp blinked.

"Princess? Jincheon Empire?"

The leader of the hunters shrugged.

"Hehehe. Did you hear that, guys? She says she's a princess."

Although they did not disbelieve Seong-eum's words, titles from outside had no relevance in the labyrinth of Jessica.

"Hey, clueless princess. Do you know where you are? Do you know how many people we killed over a year to take this base camp?"

Seong-eum turned to see a mountain of bones, at least 200 bodies.

"That's unfortunate."

"What?"

"My destination is the end of the labyrinth. I have nothing to do with you who are lingering here. Tell me what you know, and I will pass quietly."

"This is driving me crazy."

Even without recalling the hunters' saying, "Information is almost everything," there was a code of conduct.

"Guys, it's been a while since we had a workout."

As the hunters sensed the killing intent, the soldiers of the Three Treasures drew their weapons, but Seong-eum spread her hands and stepped forward.

"Do not interfere. This is my fight."

That was the condition, and as the Three Treasures hesitated, the hunters rushed at Seong-eum, swinging their swords.

"Die, foolish woman!"

Dozens of swords flashed.

"Ugh!"

As the slashing ended, all the hunters except the leader had their heads severed.

"How…?"

The leader could not believe what he saw.

The swords that should have cut Seong-eum suddenly disappeared and reappeared behind each hunter, cutting their own necks.

'Invincible.'

The word floated in Mun-gyeong's mind.

'If you bend space, any attack is useless. The person who will unify the world is Jin Seong-eum of Jincheon.'

Having dealt with the hunters, Seong-eum walked towards the trembling leader with a pale face.

"Tell me what you know."

"Eek!"

"If you share information honestly, I will spare your life."

The leader, looking at the bodies of his slaughtered comrades, spoke with a resigned expression.

"This is the ruins of giants."

According to their investigation, Jessica was composed of ten zones spreading out like concentric circles.

"The structures get larger as you cross the boundaries. From the sixth stage, they get smaller again, but we haven't confirmed that. However, the quality of the ancient weapons unearthed will be significantly higher."

"I am not interested in ancient weapons. But the ten zones seem to represent the ten stages of enlightenment."

"That might be true."

It was natural to think of Ymir, the only one in heavenly history to break through the ten stages.

As Seong-eum passed the leader, she spoke.

"I will spare your life as promised."

Relieved to be alive, the leader soon realized he had lost everything as he looked at his beheaded comrades.

'You bastards! If you leave me alone, do you expect me to be beaten to death by your families?'

Having fought countless battles over a year and built trust deeper than brotherhood, it was not a joke that they had cut their own throats.

'This is driving me crazy!'

Anger surged.

'Yes, endure it! How did I survive? Just endure like trash… like a maggot…'

The sword on the ground tempted him, and he unconsciously leaped.

"Do you think I'll live!"

As the leader, with a sword imbued with his soul, struck vertically above Seong-eum's head.

Ether Wave - Bend.

As space distorted, the central part of the blade contained within it bent high like a mountain.

"…"

Landing on the ground in a striking posture, the leader trembled as he looked at the blade bent to Seong-eum's height.

"Damn it…! What is this!"

He wept bitterly, waiting for a meaningless death.

"Are you resentful because your comrades died?"

As Seong-eum slowly turned, the leader looked up with a tearful face.

"Kill me!"

"No."

Seong-eum knelt on one knee before the leader and gently touched his head.

"Live."

The leader's face went blank.

"I said I would not kill you. Whether you seek revenge or dedicate your life to it, I will not kill you. That is Jin Seong-eum, princess of Jincheon."

There would be no one else in the world who could promise not to kill no matter what harm was done.

'How high is this woman?'

Rising again, Seong-eum sensed the next boundary with the ether wave and cast shrinkage.

As space compressed again, she took the second step to explore Jessica.

Watching the scenery snap back like a rubber band, the leader cleansed his heart of resentment.

'Revenge…'

He could not even dream of it.

In the labyrinth Andre-World No. 847.

Shirone, who had arrived in the city called Codename, was being chased by numerous people with numbers engraved on their foreheads.

"An uncountable person! Catch him!"

Among the 19,000 worlds, some seemed to have evolved independently without interference from other worlds.

And Codename was a world of perfect inequality.

"How dare you reject number 427,621!"

Every human in this city was assigned a number based on their value at birth, and no two numbers were the same.

In other words, all humans from the greatest number 1 to number 427,620 were ranked by their numbers.

'What a crazy world!'

The pursuers, armed and chasing Shirone, had code numbers in the 230,000s, corresponding to the middle class of this city.

Casting teleportation, Shirone climbed to the rooftop of a brick building and jumped into an alley.

A beggar leaning against the wall flinched and examined Shirone's forehead.

"A code…"

Although it had only been a short time, Shirone also checked the beggar's forehead and saw the number 417,631.

"You bastard! You're inferior to me!"

The beggar grabbed Shirone's clothes.

"Give it to me! It's all mine! Give me your food! And from now on, work for me! Bring me money!"

At that moment, the pursuers, hearing the beggar's voice, clanked their armor and filled the alley.

"There he is! Catch him!"

"Eek! Number 230,000!"

The terrified beggar flattened himself on the ground and bowed his head.

"Please spare me! I am a lowly beggar! Please don't kill me!"

As Shirone adjusted his collar and ran down the alley again, the pursuers ignored the beggar and continued chasing him.

'What does the number even mean?'

According to the investigation so far, numbers in this city were absolute, and even murder was not considered a crime.

However, in special cases, a new number could be assigned through exchange or acquisition.

Exchange was when a superior human swapped numbers with an inferior one, usually decided through a duel.

Acquisition was much more extreme, where an inferior human could behead a superior one and register it with the authorities.

'If I keep running like this, it will never end.'

In this place, being an uncountable person was a crime in itself, and everyone who saw Shirone chased him with burning eyes.

"Move! Move!"

The reason it was difficult to escape was due to the strange abilities used by the residents of Codename.

'I can't sense the Spirit Zone…'

When knights leaped high into the sky and threw flames at Shirone, pillars of fire erupted from the ground.

"Idiot! What if you hit a superior!"

"I'm sorry! Please just spare my life!"

Laughing at the ridiculousness, Shirone was about to leap when a shop door suddenly opened.

"Damn…!"

Using Armand's body enhancement ability, he stopped abruptly, startling a girl who screamed shrilly.

"You scared me! What is this?"

Although Shirone was annoyed by Codename's rules, he couldn't help but glance at her forehead.

'This is driving me crazy!'

On her prominent forehead was the number 97.
