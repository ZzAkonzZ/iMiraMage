Chapter 623:

"Gasp!"

Shirone's pupils fully dilated, and his mind expanded massively as if it were sucking in everything in the world.

In the amplification that could be called an explosion, he felt like a giant swallowing the world.

"What... is that?"

No one at the school had ever seen the power of Ataraxia's body technique before.

With just one scatter movement, the colosseum was divided into eight parts.

The area where the flash passed was clean as if erased with an eraser, and the sight of explosions without a focal point flickering in spherical shapes reminded one of a planet's explosion.

Although Shirone's body hadn't changed, the participants felt as if they were facing a giant entity.

Wyverns wrapped in shining chains had their necks and spines broken simultaneously and crashed to the ground, while ogres were crushed by photon cannons falling like meteors.

"Impact! Report it!"

As Baikal shouted in frustration, Elizabeth hurriedly regained her composure and spoke.

"Ah, yes! Single impact of Photon Cannon: 2.3 million crash! Magic power level...!"

"It currently exceeds 170 million!"

"170 million?"

He had increased his power, changed systems, and now completely transcended the scale itself.

"Evaluator, at this level..."

"I know. My eyes aren't crooked."

Even Baikal had to acknowledge it now.

'This isn't a power that can originally be wielded. And yet... he's enduring?'

Thinking about how much a human mind could contain sent chills down his spine.

Even as everyone was mesmerized, the number of creatures known as kings of their species was rapidly decreasing.

'Sonic Explosion!'

Casting magic directly on coordinates rather than targets caused some monsters to be swept away by a storm of mass and disappear.

The 5th tier was almost annihilated, and even the number of 4th-tier creatures dwindled to double digits. The participants finally began to see the battlefield from a scout's perspective.

'Do they think we're fools?'

Initially thinking they would tire themselves out, they realized they would end up as Shirone's sidekicks if this continued until the first evaluation ended.

As participants changed their stance and charged towards the enemies, Fermi cast one of his trump cards, Super Eruption, an earthquake magic spell.

"Why?"

'That's right. It can't be maintained for long anyway.'

If Ataraxia was a magic amplification array, Shirone's massive mind would inevitably hit its limit. Sensibly thinking about it, that time wouldn't be long.

The aftermath when the mental expansion of body technique ended was unimaginable.

Therefore, Shirone would want to skip as many stages as possible in this state. Fermi just needed to block that desire.

'Enjoy your much-desired rest. It's troublesome if only you stand out.'

When creatures were annihilated, 30 seconds were given. During that time, Shirone's amplified mental power would calm down.

As Super Eruption stopped activating, the 2000 system restored the ground to its original state.

While participants blankly watched the cleaned colosseum, Fermi clapped his hands and spoke.

"Well then, let's take a short break..."

-Entering stage 8 of endurance survival.

"What?"

Fermi stopped speaking, and participants looked around at the corners of the colosseum.

"Grrr!"

"I won't be swayed by you anymore."

Fermi said with a rotten smile.

"It won't change anything anyway."

Without a word, Shirone placed his hand on the Basilisk's face.

'Dark Sphere.'

As a black sphere formed, the monster's limbs trembled violently before being disassembled into codes and sucked into darkness.

Already 3rd-tier creatures had taken over the colosseum.

They weren't as large or grotesque as 4th-tier creatures but instead proved they were demons based on mental domains rather than biological functions—magic-based beings.

A little further away, succubi were shooting pure magic storms, pounding on Eden's barrier.

"She's enduring both the physical strength of the vampires and the magic of the succubi. It's impressive."

Despite the praise, Elizabeth's voice lacked liveliness.

He had witnessed countless graduation exams, but it was the first time he felt like the standards themselves were collapsing today.

'In the end, everyone is watching only one person.'

Not just the spectators but even scouts from other countries were only talking about participant number 27.

The demons surrounding Eden exploded with one photon cannon shot, surpassing Binder team's combined strength.

"With that kind of power, strategy and tactics become unnecessary."

Making the value of skills meaningless was precisely the essence of Unlocked Scale Magic.
